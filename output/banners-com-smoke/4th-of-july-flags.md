<h2>Polyester-Nylon Flag Material for 4th of July Flags</h2>

<h3>Dye-Sublimation Printing on Fabric Flags</h3>

<p>All 4th of July flags are printed using a direct dye-sublimation printing process. This means the ink dyes the fabric rather than sitting on top of the material. This allows the flag material to act in its natural state rather than being weighed down or made more rigid with ink adhered to the surface. This is an important factor when selecting properly manufactured flags.</p>

<p>The dye-sublimation printing process creates an eye-popping display. You can add full-color images, custom logos, graphics, and text to your 4th of July flag.</p>

<p><strong>Our fabric flags are a polyester-nylon blend. They are NOT the same material as a standard U.S. flag.</strong></p>

<h3>Temperature Sensitivity and Sizing for Fabric Flag Material</h3>

<p>Our fabric material is temperature sensitive, and due to the printing process it can expand and contract in response to heat or cold. Because of this, exact sizing cannot be guaranteed for any fabric product due to temperature fluctuations. If the size needs to be precise, such as for installation into an existing frame or hardware, please communicate this to your customer service representative to discuss your specific application.</p>

<h3>4th of July Flag Lifespan and Care</h3>

<p>With proper care and installation, custom-made 4th of July flags have an outdoor lifespan of 3 to 6 months. To help increase the lifespan, take your flag down and store it indoors during inclement weather.</p>

<h3>Prorated Warranty for Custom Flag Orders</h3>

<p>A prorated monthly warranty is available for standard flags. For example, a standard 3x5 flag that fails 2 months after proper installation will be replaced with 33% of the replacement cost covered for a new 3x5 flag.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom 4th of July Flags</h2>

<h3>What material are your 4th of July flags made from?</h3>
<p>Our 4th of July flags are made from a durable polyester-nylon blend. Each flag is printed using a dye-sublimation process where the ink dyes the fabric rather than sitting on the surface, creating vibrant, long-lasting colors. Please note that this fabric is not the same material as a standard U.S. flag.</p>

<h3>How long do custom 4th of July flags last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper care and installation, custom 4th of July flags have an outdoor lifespan of 3 to 6 months. To extend the life of your flag, take it down and store it indoors during inclement weather.</p>

<h3>Do your 4th of July flags come with a warranty?</h3>
<p>Yes, a prorated monthly warranty is available for standard flags. For example, a standard 3x5 flag that fails 2 months after proper installation will be replaced with 33% of the replacement cost covered for a new flag.</p>

<h3>Can I get a double-sided 4th of July flag?</h3>
<p>Yes. Double-sided 4th of July flags are printed on two separate layers of fabric with a blockout liner between them. This ensures your design is seen in the correct orientation from both sides. Single/reverse flags, which show a mirror image on the back, are also available.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for custom flag artwork?</h3>
<p>The recommended file format is an .eps file with fonts converted to outlines. High-resolution .pdf and .jpg files (typically 300 dpi or higher) are also accepted. If you are unsure about your file quality, our graphic design team is available to assist.</p>

<h3>How quickly can I receive my 4th of July flag order?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 2 to 5 business days depending on the option selected. Total delivery time includes both production and shipping. Shipping methods include Standard (3&ndash;5 days), Three Day, Two Day, Overnight, and UPS Overnight AM. Emergency same-day rushes may also be available &mdash; contact customer service for details.</p>
