<h2>Finishing & Mounting Options for Custom Accounting Banners</h2>

<h3>Heat-Welded Hems & Grommets</h3>
<p>Hems consist of one inch of the vinyl banner material folded over and heat-welded together to form a strong, durable edge. Grommets are typically added every 2 to 4 feet for secure hanging. We recommend hems on all accounting banners to increase longevity.</p>

<p>You will have the following hem and grommet options:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Top and Bottom Only Hems</strong> — Standard finishing for vinyl accounting banners.</li>
<li><strong>All Four Sides Hems</strong> — All 4 sides of the banner are heat-welded and grommeted for maximum durability.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Reinforced Hems</h3>
<p>Reinforced hems feature a PVC-coated webbing that is heat-welded to the perimeter of the accounting banner, providing additional strength for heavy-use or outdoor installations.</p>
<p><em>Reinforced hems are not available with pole pockets.</em></p>

<h3>Reinforced Corners</h3>
<p>A triangular piece of vinyl is applied to each corner of the accounting banner to improve strength and prevent tearing at the grommet points. Each corner is grommeted for secure mounting.</p>

<h3>Pole Pockets</h3>
<p>Pole pockets create a sleeve along the top, bottom, or sides of the accounting banner, allowing a dowel or pole to run through for support. Available in three sizes:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>2" Pole Pockets</strong> — fits a pole 3/4" in diameter</li>
<li><strong>3" Pole Pockets</strong> — fits a pole 1 1/4" in diameter</li>
<li><strong>4" Pole Pockets</strong> — fits a pole 1 1/2" in diameter</li>
</ul>
<p>Selecting pole pockets will not increase the finished size of your banner.</p>
<p><em>Pole pockets are not available with reinforced hems or corners.</em></p>

<h3>Double-Sided Banner Finishing</h3>
<p>Double-sided accounting banners can be finished in sizes up to 150 square feet. Larger sizes are available — contact our team for a custom quote.</p>

<p>To learn more, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/vinyl-banner-finishing-options">vinyl banner finishing options</a> page. We do not recommend wind slits in banners. For more information, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/wind-slits">wind slit banners</a> page.</p>

<h2>Accounting Banner FAQs</h2>

<h3>What sizes are available for accounting banners?</h3>
<p>Custom accounting banners are available in sizes ranging from 1' x 1' up to 8' x 50'. If you need a larger size, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/oversized-banners">oversized banners</a> page or contact us for a custom quote.</p>

<h3>What material is best for an outdoor accounting banner?</h3>
<p>For outdoor use, we recommend our 13 oz matte vinyl or 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl for maximum durability. If your accounting banner will be displayed in windy conditions, our <a href="https://www.banners.com/mesh-banners">mesh banner material</a> allows airflow to reduce wind resistance.</p>

<h3>Can I include my CPA credentials and firm logo on the banner?</h3>
<p>Yes. You can fully customize your accounting banner with your firm name, CPA credentials, logo, phone number, website, email address, and any financial services you offer. Use our online designer or upload your own artwork.</p>

<h3>How fast can I get my accounting banner?</h3>
<p>We offer production turnaround times as fast as 1 business day. Combined with standard shipping, most accounting banners are delivered within 3 to 7 business days depending on your location and selected production speed.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for accounting banner artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept .eps (preferred, with fonts converted to outlines), .pdf, .tiff, and .jpg files. For best results, files should be 300 dpi or higher resolution. If you are unsure about your files, our graphic designers will review them and contact you with recommendations.</p>

<h3>Are accounting banners suitable for indoor use?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Vinyl accounting banners work well indoors for office lobbies, waiting areas, and trade show displays. For a premium indoor look, consider our <a href="https://www.banners.com/fabric-banners">fabric banner material</a>, which produces a refined, high-end finish.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided accounting banner?</h3>
<p>Yes. We offer double-sided finishing for accounting banners up to 150 square feet. For larger double-sided banners, please contact our team for a custom quote.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design assistance for accounting banners?</h3>
<p>Yes. You can choose from our library of accounting banner templates, upload your own print-ready file, or select our Professional Designer option and our in-house graphic design team will create your layout based on your instructions.</p>

<h3>How are accounting banners shipped?</h3>
<p>Unless otherwise specified, accounting banners ship via UPS Ground with 1 to 3 day delivery to 90% of the United States. Expedited options including Two Day, Overnight, and UPS Overnight AM are also available.</p>

<h3>What finishing options are available for accounting banners?</h3>
<p>We offer heat-welded hems and grommets (top and bottom or all four sides), reinforced hems, reinforced corners, and pole pockets in 2", 3", and 4" sizes. Visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/vinyl-banner-finishing-options">finishing options page</a> to learn more about each option.</p>
