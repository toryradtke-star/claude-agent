<h2>Vinyl Material Options for Advent Banners</h2>

<p>Every Advent banner is printed using state-of-the-art, large-format printers for vibrant, full-color results. All banners are manufactured and <a href="https://www.banners.com/banner-printing">printed in the USA</a>. Choose from six customizable banner materials to match your church's indoor or outdoor display needs.</p>

<p><em>Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature-sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (e.g., a 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please contact customer service to discuss your specific application.</em></p>

<h3>13 oz Matte Vinyl</h3>
<p>Our most popular banner material. The 13 oz matte vinyl has a low shine and rich color gamut, making it an excellent choice for Advent banners displayed both indoors and outdoors.</p>

<h3>13 oz Gloss Vinyl</h3>
<p>The 13 oz gloss vinyl material has a high shine that produces vibrant, crisp colors and graphics. This option gives your Advent banner a polished, high-end appearance.</p>

<h3>18 oz Heavy-Duty Vinyl</h3>
<p>Our toughest material. The extra heavy-duty 18 oz matte vinyl has a low luster, blockout coating for extra durability and print quality — ideal for Advent banners that need to withstand extended outdoor display.</p>

<h3>Mesh Material</h3>
<p>Made with 8 oz PVC polyester mesh featuring a criss-crossed breathable scrim that allows 37% airflow. Mesh is ideal for outdoor Advent banner installations in windy conditions.</p>

<h3>Fabric Material</h3>
<p>Made with a premium nylon/polyester blend called Banner Soft. This fabric is ideal for indoor Advent displays and has a low shine that produces a high-end look and feel within your church sanctuary.</p>

<h3>Adhesive Vinyl</h3>
<p><strong>Standard Tack Vinyl:</strong> Can be used both indoors and outdoors, with an outdoor lifespan of 3 to 5 years when properly installed and maintained.</p>
<p><strong>Extreme Tack Vinyl:</strong> White calendered vinyl material with a high tack adhesive specifically designed to adhere to low energy surfaces — a great option for mounting Advent banners on challenging surfaces.</p>

<h2>Finishing & Mounting Options for Advent Banner Installation</h2>

<h3>Heat-Welded Hems & Grommets</h3>

<p>Hems consist of one inch of vinyl banner material folded over and heat-welded together to form a strong hem. Hems are recommended for all Advent banners to increase durability.</p>

<p>When selecting hems and grommets, the following options are available:</p>

<p><strong>Top and Bottom Only Hems</strong> — Standard finishing option for vinyl Advent banners. Grommets are typically added every 2–4 feet.</p>

<p><strong>All Four Sides Hems</strong> — All 4 sides of the banner are heat-welded and grommeted for maximum security during installation.</p>

<h3>Pole Pockets for Advent Banner Displays</h3>

<p>Pole pockets consist of 2, 3, or 4 inches of material wrapped around the sides, top, or bottom of the banner — ideal for hanging Advent banners from dowels or poles in your church.</p>

<ul>
<li><strong>2" Pole Pockets</strong> — fits a pole 3/4" in diameter</li>
<li><strong>3" Pole Pockets</strong> — fits a pole 1 1/4" in diameter</li>
<li><strong>4" Pole Pockets</strong> — fits a pole 1 1/2" in diameter</li>
</ul>

<p>Selecting pole pockets will not increase the finished size of your Advent banner.</p>

<p><em>Pole pockets are not available with reinforced hems or corners.</em></p>

<h3>Reinforced Corners</h3>
<p>Reinforced corners provide extra strength with a triangular piece of vinyl applied and grommeted at each corner of the banner — recommended for outdoor Advent banner installations.</p>

<h3>Reinforced Hems</h3>
<p>Reinforced hems feature a PVC-coated webbing that increases the overall strength and wind resistance of your Advent banner.</p>
<p><em>Reinforced hems cannot be combined with pole pockets.</em></p>

<h3>Double-Sided Advent Banner Finishing</h3>
<p>Double-sided finishing is available for Advent banners up to 150 square feet. Larger sizes require a custom quote.</p>

<p>For more details, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/vinyl-banner-finishing-options">vinyl banner finishing options</a> page.</p>

<p>Wind slits are not recommended for banners. For more information, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/wind-slits">wind slit banners</a> page.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Advent Banners</h2>

<h3>What material are Advent banners printed on?</h3>
<p>Advent banners are printed on 13 oz scrim vinyl as the standard material. You can also choose from 13 oz gloss vinyl, 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl, mesh, fabric, or adhesive vinyl depending on your display needs.</p>

<h3>Can Advent banners be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>Yes. Our vinyl Advent banners are rated for both indoor and outdoor use. For outdoor installations in windy areas, consider our mesh material option, which allows 37% airflow for added wind resistance.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for Advent banners?</h3>
<p>Advent banners are available in sizes ranging from 1'x1' to 8'x50'. If you need a larger or custom size, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/oversized-banners">oversized banners</a> page or <a href="https://www.banners.com/quotes">request a custom quote</a>.</p>

<h3>Do you offer Advent banner design templates?</h3>
<p>Yes. We offer a variety of Advent-themed templates that you can customize in our online designer. You can also upload your own artwork file or have one of our professional graphic designers create a custom design for you.</p>

<h3>How quickly can I receive my Advent banner?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 1 to 5 business days. Combined with UPS Ground shipping (1–3 days to 90% of the U.S.), most orders arrive within one week. Expedited shipping options are also available.</p>

<h3>What finishing options are available for Advent banners?</h3>
<p>Finishing options include heat-welded hems with grommets, pole pockets (2", 3", or 4"), reinforced corners, and reinforced hems. Pole pockets are ideal for hanging Advent banners from dowels inside your church.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided Advent banner?</h3>
<p>Yes. Double-sided finishing is available for Advent banners up to 150 square feet. For larger double-sided banners, contact us for a custom quote.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for Advent banner artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept .eps (converted to outline), high-resolution .pdf, .jpg, and .tiff files. Files should be 300 dpi or higher for the best print quality. If you are unsure about your file, our graphic designers will review it and contact you if adjustments are needed.</p>

<p>We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and checks. Your credit card will not be charged until you approve your order.</p>

<h3>Do you ship Advent banners internationally?</h3>
<p>Yes. For international shipments, customers must provide both shipping labels and customs documentation. We currently accept international shipping labels from USPS and UPS.</p>
