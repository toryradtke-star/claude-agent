<h2>Durable Vinyl Material Options for Advent Pole Banners</h2>

<p>Every Advent pole banner is made with premium materials and printed using state-of-the-art, large-format printers for vibrant, weather-resistant colors. All banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.</p>

<p>Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature-sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. The acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (e.g., a 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please contact a Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.</p>

<h3>13 oz Matte Vinyl Pole Banners</h3>

<p>Our high-quality 13 oz matte vinyl banner material is the most popular option for Advent pole banners. It features a low-shine finish and a rich color gamut that brings Advent designs to life.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Advent Pole Banners</h2>

<h3>What sizes are available for Advent pole banners?</h3>
<p>Advent pole banners are available in heights from 12" to 72" and widths from 12" to 36". The maximum print size is 72" height by 36" width. If you need a custom size not listed, <a href="https://www.banners.com/quotes">request a custom quote</a>.</p>

<h3>Are Advent pole banners printed on both sides?</h3>
<p>Yes. All Advent pole banners are printed double-sided so your message is visible from both directions. Each banner includes pole pockets at the top and bottom and grommets in all four corners.</p>

<h3>What material are Advent pole banners made from?</h3>
<p>Advent pole banners are available in two vinyl options: 13 oz matte vinyl (most popular, with a low-shine finish and rich colors) and 18 oz heavy-duty matte vinyl (extra durable with a blockout coating for enhanced print quality and weather resistance).</p>

<h3>What hardware do I need to hang Advent pole banners?</h3>
<p>Advent pole banners are designed for use with adjustable pole-mounted bracket systems. Two options are available: the <a href="https://www.banners.com/accessories/standard-hardware">Standard Bracket System</a> (recommended for banners up to 24" wide) and the <a href="https://www.banners.com/accessories/wind-deflecting-hardware">Wind Deflecting Bracket System</a> (recommended for larger banners and windy conditions).</p>

<h3>Can I customize the design of my Advent pole banner?</h3>
<p>Yes. You can choose from Advent-themed templates in our online designer, upload your own print-ready artwork files, or have our professional graphic designers create a custom design for you. Templates include religious and seasonal options with customizable backgrounds, clip art, text, and images.</p>

<h3>How long does it take to receive Advent pole banners?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 2 to 5 business days. Total delivery time includes production time plus shipping time. Standard UPS Ground shipping reaches 90% of the United States within 1-3 days. Expedited shipping options including overnight delivery are also available.</p>

<h3>What file formats are accepted for Advent pole banner artwork?</h3>
<p>Vector-based files (.eps with fonts converted to outlines) produce the best results. High-resolution .pdf and .jpg files are also accepted. Files should be 60 MB or smaller. For larger files, contact customer service for upload instructions.</p>

<h3>Are Advent pole banners weather resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes. Advent pole banners are made from durable vinyl material designed to withstand outdoor winter conditions. The 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl option provides extra durability with a blockout coating for extended outdoor use throughout the Advent and Christmas season.</p>
