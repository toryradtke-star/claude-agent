<h2>Corrugated Plastic Material for Advent Yard Signs</h2>

<p>All Advent yard signs are digitally printed in full color on 4 mm white corrugated plastic (Coroplast). This material features hollow horizontal flutes similar to cardboard, providing a lightweight yet rigid structure ideal for outdoor signage.</p>

<h3>Key Material Features</h3>

<ul>
<li><strong>Durable</strong> - 4 mm corrugated plastic holds up to wind, rain, and repeated handling</li>
<li><strong>Lightweight</strong> - easy to transport, install, and reposition around your church grounds</li>
<li><strong>Waterproof</strong> - withstands rain, snow, and moisture throughout the Advent season</li>
<li><strong>Full-color printing</strong> - CMYK digital printing delivers vibrant, eye-catching graphics</li>
<li><strong>Single or double sided</strong> - printed on one or both sides for maximum visibility</li>
</ul>

<p>The corrugated plastic sheets measure 4' x 8' with flutes running horizontally in the 96" direction. This material is the industry standard for yard signage and delivers excellent print quality for both text and full-color imagery.</p>

<p><em>Please note: all Coroplast yard signs are cut 1/8" on each side to allow for finishing. For example, an 18" x 24" yard sign will ship as 17.75" x 23.75". If your sign needs to be a precise size, please contact our customer service team to discuss your specific application.</em></p>

<h2>Finishing Options for Custom Advent Yard Sign Printing</h2>

<p>Select from the following finishing options to customize your Advent yard signs:</p>

<ul>
<li><strong>Edge finish:</strong> 1/2" white margin or full bleed (ink prints all the way to the edge of the sign)</li>
<li><strong>Print sides:</strong> Single-sided or double-sided printing</li>
<li><strong>Installation accessories:</strong> H-Stakes (10x15, 10x24, or 10x30), Beefy Stakes, Zip Ties 12" (black), and Zip Ties 36" (white)</li>
</ul>

<h3>Scoring for Oversized Advent Yard Signs</h3>

<p>All signs larger than 32" x 48" or any sign where the combined height and width exceeds 81 inches are automatically scored and folded for shipping. Scoring involves cutting through the top layer of the corrugated plastic without cutting through the entire piece, allowing the sign to fold into an optimal size for shipping.</p>

<p>If you prefer your oversized Advent yard signs to ship flat without scoring, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/oversized-signs-flat-shipping">oversized flat shipping page</a> or contact customer service at <a href="tel:13203915300">(320) 391-5300</a>.</p>

<p><em>Please note: when ordering yard signs with different designs, text, or imagery, each unique design is priced and processed as a separate sign.</em></p>

<h2>Artwork Requirements for Advent Yard Sign Printing</h2>

<h3>File Specifications</h3>

<p>For the best print quality, submit your artwork as an .eps file with fonts converted to outlines. We also accept high-resolution .pdf and .jpg files. Files should be 300 dpi or higher for optimal results. The maximum upload size is 60 MB. For files larger than 60 MB, call our team for instructions to ensure the fastest service.</p>

<p>If you are unsure about the quality of your files, our professional graphic designers are available to review and assist with your artwork at no additional charge.</p>

<h3>Artwork & Order Proofs</h3>

<p>Requesting an order proof allows you to verify your Advent yard sign design before production begins. Your proof will include a low-resolution image of your artwork along with a summary of your order details, pricing, billing, and shipping information. If you request a proof, your order will not enter production until you send your approval.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Advent Yard Signs</h2>

<h3>What material are Advent yard signs made from?</h3>
<p>Advent yard signs are printed on 4 mm white corrugated plastic (Coroplast). This material is lightweight, durable, and waterproof, making it ideal for outdoor display throughout the Advent season.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for Advent yard signs?</h3>
<p>We offer a wide range of standard sizes from 12" x 12" up to 48" x 96". If you need a size not listed, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/yard-signs/custom-sizes">custom sizes page</a> for additional options.</p>

<h3>Can I print on both sides of an Advent yard sign?</h3>
<p>Yes. All Advent yard signs are available in single-sided or double-sided printing so your message is visible from both directions.</p>

<h3>How do I install Advent yard signs?</h3>
<p>Advent yard signs are installed using wire H-stakes or beefy stakes, which are available as accessories during checkout. Simply slide the corrugated plastic sign onto the stake and push it into the ground.</p>

<h3>How quickly can I receive my Advent yard signs?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 1 to 5 business days depending on the turnaround option you select. Shipping time is additional and depends on your location and chosen shipping method. Most U.S. orders arrive within 1-3 days via UPS Ground.</p>

<h3>Do you have Advent templates available?</h3>
<p>Yes. We offer a library of pre-designed Advent yard sign templates that you can customize in our online design tool. You can also upload your own artwork or work with one of our professional graphic designers.</p>

<h3>What happens if my Advent yard sign is larger than 32" x 48"?</h3>
<p>Signs larger than 32" x 48" or where the combined height and width exceeds 81 inches are automatically scored and folded for shipping. If you prefer flat shipping, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/oversized-signs-flat-shipping">oversized flat shipping page</a> or call us at <a href="tel:13203915300">(320) 391-5300</a>.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for Advent yard sign artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept .eps (preferred, with fonts converted to outlines), high-resolution .pdf, .jpg, and .tiff files. Files should be 300 dpi or higher for optimal print quality.</p>

<h3>Can I request a proof before my Advent yard signs go into production?</h3>
<p>Yes. You can request an order proof that includes a low-resolution preview of your artwork and a full summary of your order details. Your signs will not enter production until you approve the proof.</p>

<h3>Do you offer Advent banners as well?</h3>
