<h3>13 oz Gloss Vinyl Advertising Banners</h3>

<h3>18 oz Heavy-Duty Vinyl Advertising Banners</h3>

<h3>Mesh Advertising Banners</h3>

<h2>Finishing Options for Custom Advertising Banner Installation</h2>

<h3>Heat-Welded Hems & Grommets</h3>

<p><img src="https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/291886d1-dd6d-4fdf-9f62-c5b6826dba17.jpg" alt="Advertising Banner Hems and Grommet Finishing | Banners.com"></p>

<p>Hems consist of one inch of the vinyl banner material folded over and heated together to form a strong hem. We recommend hems for all advertising banners to increase durability. These are a one-inch fold, heat-welded hem with grommets typically added every 2-4 feet.</p>

<p>You will have the following options when selecting hems and grommets for your advertising banner:</p>

<p><strong>Top and Bottom Only Hems</strong> - Standard finishing option for vinyl advertising banners.</p>

<p><strong>All Four Sides Hems</strong> - All 4 sides of the advertising banner will be heat welded and grommeted.</p>

<p><img src="https://web2printdata.blob.core.windows.net/w2p-cms-1/24-09-04-BANS-Top-and-bottom-hems-and-grommets-only-2.png" alt="Top and Bottom Grommets Only for Advertising Banners"></p>

<p><img src="https://web2printdata.blob.core.windows.net/w2p-cms-1/24-09-04-BANS-Top-and-bottom-hems-and-grommets-all-sides.png" alt="All 4 Sides Hems and Grommets for Advertising Banners"></p>

<h3>Pole Pockets for Advertising Banner Display</h3>
<p>Pole pockets consist of 2, 3, or 4 inches of material wrapped around the sides, top, or bottom of the advertising banner.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>2" Pole Pockets</strong> - fits a pole 3/4" in diameter</li>
<li><strong>3" Pole Pockets</strong> - fits a pole 1 1/4" in diameter</li>
<li><strong>4" Pole Pockets</strong> - fits a pole 1 1/2" in diameter</li>
</ul>
<p>By selecting pole pockets, there will be no increase in the finished size of your advertising banner.</p>
<p><em>Pole pockets are not available with reinforced hems or corners.</em></p>

<p><img src="https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/728828a0-b142-4abc-98ec-5b404600dca9.jpg" alt="Advertising Banner Pole Pocket Finishing | Banners.com"></p>

<h3>Reinforced Corners for Advertising Banners</h3>
<p>Reinforced corners provide extra strength and are grommeted to each corner of the advertising banner. A triangular piece of vinyl is applied to improve strength and durability in the corners.</p>

<p><img src="https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/Reinforced Corners for Vinyl Banners.jpg" alt="Advertising Banner Reinforced Corner Finishing | Banners.com"></p>

<h3>Reinforced Hems for Heavy-Duty Advertising Banners</h3>
<p>Reinforced hems are a PVC-coated webbing which increases the strength of the advertising banner. A thick, weblike material is heat-welded to the perimeter to provide additional reinforcement.</p>
<p><em>You cannot have reinforced hems with pole pockets.</em></p>

<p><img src="https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/Reinforced-Hems-for-Vinyl-Banners.jpg" alt="Advertising Banner Reinforced Hem Finishing | Banners.com"></p>

<h3>Double-Sided Advertising Banner Finishing</h3>
<p>We can finish double-sided advertising banners up to 150 square feet. Larger sizes require a custom quote.</p>

<p>To learn more, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/vinyl-banner-finishing-options">vinyl banner finishing options</a> page.</p>

<p>We do not recommend wind slits in advertising banners. For more information, visit our <a href="https://www.banners.com/wind-slits">wind slit banners</a> page.</p>
