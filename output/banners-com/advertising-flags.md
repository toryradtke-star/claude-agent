<h2>Dye-Sublimated Fabric Material for Advertising Flags</h2>

<p>All advertising flags are printed using a direct dye-sublimation printing process. The ink dyes the fabric rather than sitting on top of the material. This allows the flag to move and behave naturally in the wind without being weighed down or stiffened by surface-applied ink. This is an important factor when selecting properly manufactured advertising flags for outdoor use.</p>

<p>The dye-sublimation printing process produces an eye-catching, full-color display. You can include custom logos, images, graphics, and text on your advertising flag for maximum brand impact.</p>

<p><em><strong>Our fabric flags are a polyester-nylon blend. They are NOT the same material as a standard U.S. flag.</strong></em></p>

<p>Our fabric material is temperature sensitive. Due to the printing process, it can expand or contract in response to heat or cold. Because of this, we cannot guarantee the exact size of any fabric product due to temperature fluctuations. We are not responsible for any product received that does not precisely match a required dimension. If your advertising flag needs to fit within an existing frame or hardware, please communicate this to your customer service representative so they can discuss your specific application.</p>

<h3>How Long Do Advertising Flags Last?</h3>

<p>With proper care and installation, our custom advertising flags have an outdoor lifespan of 3 to 6 months. We recommend taking your flag down and storing it indoors during inclement weather to help extend its life.</p>

<h3>Advertising Flag Warranty Information</h3>

<p>We offer a prorated monthly warranty on our standard advertising flags.</p>

<p>For example, a prorated warranty on a standard 3x5 flag that fails 2 months after proper installation will be replaced, and we will cover 33% of the replacement cost for a new 3x5 flag.</p>

<h2>Finishing Options for Single-Sided & Double-Sided Advertising Flags</h2>

<h3>Single / Reverse Advertising Flags</h3>

<img src="https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/Single-Reverse-Sided-both-WEB-KB.jpg" alt="Single-sided and reverse advertising flag example showing front and back" />

<p>Single / reverse advertising flags are printed on a single layer of material. The ink fully permeates the fabric, creating a reverse image on the back side. When the flag is flipped, the artwork will appear mirrored.</p>

<h3>Double-Sided Advertising Flags</h3>

<img src="https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/Double-Sided-both-WEB-KB.jpg" alt="Double-sided advertising flag example showing matching front and back" />

<p>Double-sided advertising flags are printed on two separate layers of fabric with a blockout liner in between. This ensures that any wording and images always appear in the correct orientation from both sides — ideal for high-traffic locations where your flag will be viewed from multiple directions.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Advertising Flags</h2>

<h3>What materials are your custom advertising flags made of?</h3>

<p>Our advertising flags are made from a high-quality polyester-nylon blend. They are printed using dye-sublimation, a process that dyes the ink directly into the fabric fibers. This produces vivid, full-color graphics that resist fading and allow the flag to move naturally in the wind.</p>

<h3>Do your advertising flags have a matte or glossy finish?</h3>

<p>Our advertising flags have a matte finish, which provides excellent readability in both direct sunlight and overcast conditions. If you are looking for a glossier signage option, our <a href="https://www.banners.com/vinyl-banners">custom vinyl banners</a> include a gloss finish option.</p>

<h3>How long will my advertising flag last outdoors?</h3>

<p>With proper care and installation, custom advertising flags have an outdoor lifespan of 3 to 6 months. We recommend bringing your flag indoors during severe weather, high winds, or storms to help extend its useful life. We also offer a prorated monthly warranty on standard advertising flags.</p>

<h3>What is the production time for custom advertising flags?</h3>

<p>Standard production for custom advertising flags is 3 business days at no additional charge. A 2-day express production option is also available. With express production and overnight shipping, your advertising flag can arrive in as few as 3 business days from the time your design is approved.</p>
