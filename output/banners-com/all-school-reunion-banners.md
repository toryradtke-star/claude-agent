<h2>Reunion Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<h3>File Format Specifications</h3>

<p>Submit artwork files in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PNG formats for optimal print quality. Vector-based files (AI, EPS) provide the best results for text elements and logo reproduction on reunion banners.</p>

<h3>Resolution Requirements</h3>

<p>Image files should be provided at 150 DPI minimum at actual banner size for sharp, professional printing. Higher resolution files ensure crisp reproduction of school logos, photographs, and detailed design elements.</p>

<h3>Color Profile Guidelines</h3>

<p>For accurate school color reproduction, provide artwork in CMYK color mode. If you're working with specific school colors, include Pantone color references to ensure precise color matching on your reunion banner.</p>

<h3>Text and Font Considerations</h3>

<p>Use fonts that are easily readable from a distance, especially for outdoor reunion displays. Sans-serif fonts typically provide better readability on vinyl banners. Ensure all fonts are outlined or embedded in your artwork files.</p>

<h3>Design Layout Guidelines</h3>

<p>Include a 2-inch bleed area around your design to accommodate finishing processes. Keep important text and design elements at least 3 inches from banner edges to ensure they're not affected by grommets or hemming.</p>

<h3>Professional Design Services</h3>

<p>If you need assistance creating your reunion banner artwork, our in-house design team can work with your concept, photos, and text to create professional layouts that effectively represent your school and reunion celebration.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Reunion Banners</h2>

<h3>What sizes are available for reunion banners?</h3>
<p>We offer custom vinyl reunion banners in sizes ranging from 1'x1' up to 8'x50'. Popular reunion banner sizes include 3'x6', 4'x8', and 6'x10' for entrance displays and registration areas. Custom sizes are available to fit your specific venue requirements.</p>

<h3>Can you match our school colors exactly?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match your school colors precisely. Provide Pantone color references or official school brand guidelines with your artwork, and our printing process will reproduce your colors accurately on the vinyl banner material.</p>

<h3>How long do vinyl reunion banners last?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl reunion banners are designed to last for multiple events with proper storage. When used outdoors, they typically maintain their appearance for 1-3 years depending on weather exposure. Indoor banners can last much longer with minimal fading.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse my reunion banner for future events?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Vinyl banners are durable and can be rolled up for storage between reunion events. Avoid folding when possible to prevent creasing, and store in a cool, dry location for best longevity.</p>

<h3>What if I need my reunion banner quickly?</h3>
<p>We offer rush production and expedited shipping options for time-sensitive reunion events. Contact our customer service team to discuss timeline options that can accommodate your event date.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation services?</h3>
<p>While we don't provide installation services, all reunion banners come with grommets or finishing options that make installation straightforward. We can provide guidance on mounting options for your specific venue.</p>

<h3>Can you help design my reunion banner?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our in-house design team can create custom reunion banner layouts using your school logos, photos, and text. Simply provide your concept and materials, and we'll create professional artwork for your approval.</p>

<h3>What happens if my banner is damaged during shipping?</h3>
<p>All reunion banners are carefully packaged for shipping protection. If your banner arrives damaged, contact us immediately and we'll arrange for a replacement to be produced and shipped right away.</p>
