<h2>Athletic Department Banner Artwork Requirements for Printing</h2>

<p>To ensure your athletic department banner prints with professional quality, please follow these artwork specifications:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit athletic department banner artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD format. JPEG and PNG files are acceptable for simple designs but may limit print quality on large athletic banners.</p>

<h3>Resolution for Athletic Banner Printing</h3>
<p>Athletic department banner artwork should be created at 150 DPI at actual print size, or 300 DPI at half size. This ensures crisp text and graphics when printed at full banner dimensions.</p>

<h3>Color Mode and Profile</h3>
<p>Design athletic department banners in CMYK color mode using the SWOP color profile. RGB files will be converted but may result in slight color variations from your original design.</p>

<h3>School Logo and Brand Guidelines</h3>
<p>Provide high-resolution school logos and athletic department graphics in vector format when possible. This ensures logos remain sharp at any size on your athletic department banner.</p>

<h3>Text and Font Considerations</h3>
<p>Use bold, easy-to-read fonts for athletic department banner text. Minimum recommended text size is 2 inches tall for optimal readability from typical viewing distances in sports facilities.</p>

<h3>Bleed and Safety Areas</h3>
<p>Include 0.125" bleed around your athletic department banner artwork and keep important text and graphics at least 0.5" from the finished edge to account for finishing processes.</p>

<p>Our design team is available to review your athletic department banner artwork before printing to ensure optimal results.</p>

<h2>Athletic Department Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>What material is best for outdoor athletic facility banners?</h3>
<p>For outdoor athletic facilities, we recommend 15oz vinyl with UV-resistant inks and wind slits. Mesh vinyl is ideal for fence installations where wind is a concern. Both materials withstand weather exposure and maintain vibrant colors throughout sports seasons.</p>

<h3>Can you print school logos and athletic department graphics?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can print any school logos, athletic department graphics, mascots, and custom designs on your banners. Provide high-resolution logo files in vector format for best results. Our design team can also recreate logos from existing materials if needed.</p>

<h3>How large can athletic department banners be printed?</h3>
<p>Athletic department banners can be printed up to 8 feet wide by 50 feet long. Common sizes for athletic facilities include 4x8, 6x10, 8x12, and custom sizes to fit specific gymnasium or stadium requirements.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation hardware for athletic department banners?</h3>
<p>We provide various finishing options including grommets, pole pockets, and reinforced hems. Installation hardware such as bungee cords, zip ties, and mounting clips are available separately. Contact us for specific athletic facility installation recommendations.</p>

<h3>What file format should I use for athletic department banner artwork?</h3>
<p>Submit artwork in PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD format at 150 DPI at actual size. Include school logos in vector format when possible. Our design team will review your artwork and contact you if any adjustments are needed for optimal printing.</p>

<h3>Can athletic department banners be double-sided?</h3>
<p>Yes, double-sided athletic department banners are available for hanging installations in gymnasiums where both sides will be visible. Double-sided printing allows different designs on each side or the same design readable from both directions.</p>

<h3>How do I care for athletic department banners?</h3>
<p>Clean athletic department banners with mild soap and water using a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaning materials. For long-term storage, roll banners rather than folding to prevent creases. Indoor banners typically last 3-5 years, while outdoor banners last 1-3 years depending on weather exposure.</p>

<h3>Do you offer discounts for schools and athletic departments?</h3>
<p>Yes, educational institutions may qualify for volume discounts on athletic department banner orders. Schools with approved credit can also use purchase orders with Net 30 payment terms. Contact our sales team to discuss educational pricing options.</p>
