<h2>Artwork Requirements for Authorized Dealer Banner Printing</h2>

<p>Professional artwork specifications ensure your authorized dealer banners print with sharp graphics and accurate brand representation.</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong><br>
Submit authorized dealer banner artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats. JPG and PNG files acceptable at 300 DPI minimum resolution.</p>

<p><strong>Brand Logo Guidelines</strong><br>
Provide manufacturer logos and authorized dealer graphics in vector format when possible. Ensure you have proper licensing for all brand elements used.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications</strong><br>
CMYK color mode recommended for authorized dealer banners. RGB files will be converted, which may cause slight color variations from screen display.</p>

<p><strong>Text and Font Requirements</strong><br>
Use readable fonts for authorized dealer information and contact details. Minimum text size should be 1 inch for every 10 feet of viewing distance.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safety Areas</strong><br>
Include 2-inch bleed area around all authorized dealer banner edges. Keep important text and logos at least 3 inches from edges to avoid trimming.</p>

<p><strong>Design Review Process</strong><br>
Our design team reviews all authorized dealer banner artwork for print quality and brand compliance. We'll contact you if adjustments are needed.</p>

<p><strong>Professional Design Services</strong><br>
Don't have artwork ready? Our graphic designers can create custom authorized dealer banners using your brand guidelines and dealer information.</p>

<p><strong>Proof Approval</strong><br>
Digital proofs provided for all authorized dealer banner orders. Production begins only after you approve the final design and layout.</p>
