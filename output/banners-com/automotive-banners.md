</ul>

<h2>Automotive Banner Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What size automotive banner works best for dealership displays?</h3>
<p>For dealership storefronts, 3'x8' to 4'x12' automotive banners provide excellent visibility. Service center promotions work well with 2'x6' to 3'x10' banners. We can customize any size up to 8'x50' for your specific automotive marketing needs.</p>

<h3>How long do automotive banners last in outdoor conditions?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl automotive banners are designed for 1-3 years of outdoor display, depending on weather exposure and UV conditions. The 13oz vinyl material and UV-resistant inks ensure your dealership banners maintain vibrant colors throughout their lifespan.</p>

<h3>Can I use manufacturer logos on my automotive banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, authorized dealerships can typically use manufacturer logos on automotive banners. However, you should verify compliance with specific brand guidelines. Our design team can help ensure your automotive banner meets manufacturer requirements.</p>

<h3>What's the best finishing option for outdoor automotive banners?</h3>
<p>For outdoor dealership displays, we recommend hem & grommets for maximum durability. This finishing option reinforces the edges and provides secure mounting points that withstand wind and weather at automotive locations.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for urgent automotive promotions?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer 1-2 day rush production for automotive banners when you need them quickly for sales events or promotions. Same-day production may be available - contact our customer service team to check availability for your automotive banner needs.</p>

<h3>Can you design automotive banners for service center promotions?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our design team specializes in automotive marketing materials and can create effective banners for oil change specials, tire sales, brake service, inspections, and other service center promotions that drive customer traffic.</p>
