<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Backdrop Stand Hardware</h2>

<h3>What are the size limitations for the Backdrop Slider Stand?</h3>
<p>The frame adjusts from 81" to 120" in width and 41.5" to 96" in height. The recommended graphic size is 96"W x 94"H to accommodate the pole pockets and ensure proper fit.</p>

<h3>How much does the complete backdrop system weigh?</h3>
<p>The entire system including frame, graphic, and carry bag weighs approximately 16 pounds, making it easy to transport to trade shows and events.</p>

<h3>Can I use different sized graphics on the same frame?</h3>
<p>Yes, the telescopic design allows you to use multiple graphic sizes on the same frame. The adjustable poles accommodate various dimensions within the frame's specifications.</p>

<h3>What's included with my backdrop stand order?</h3>
<p>Your order includes: (1) single-sided graphic, (2) 4.5" pole pockets sewn top and bottom, (1) large slider frame, and (1) black nylon carry bag. Additional pole pockets can be added to the sides as an upgrade.</p>

<h3>How long does production take?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 1-5 business days depending on your selected turnaround time. We offer money-saving options for longer production times if you're not in a rush.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for artwork?</h3>
<p>We prefer vector files (PDF, AI, EPS) or high-resolution raster files (PSD, JPEG, PNG). Files should be 150 DPI minimum at full size with 0.5" bleed on all edges.</p>

<h3>Can you print double-sided graphics?</h3>
<p>Double-sided graphics are available as a custom option. Contact our customer service team for pricing and specifications for double-sided backdrop displays.</p>

<h3>What's the warranty on the frame?</h3>
<p>All backdrop stand hardware comes with a one-year warranty on the frame components. This covers manufacturing defects but does not include normal wear and tear.</p>
