<h2>A-Frame Sign Replacement FAQ</h2>

<h3>What material are replacement A-frame signs made from?</h3>
<p>Our replacement A-frame signs are made from 4mm corrugated plastic, a durable, waterproof material that withstands extreme weather conditions. This "plastic cardboard" construction provides lightweight portability with up to 2 years of outdoor durability.</p>

<h3>How long do A-frame sign replacements last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper care, our corrugated plastic A-frame sign replacements maintain excellent appearance and readability for up to 2 years of continuous outdoor use. The UV-resistant surface and weatherproof construction ensure long-lasting performance in all climates.</p>

<h3>Can you match specific colors for brand consistency?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer professional color matching services through our Color Match Proof System. This service ensures precise brand color reproduction for businesses requiring exact color specifications across their A-frame signage.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for A-frame sign replacements?</h3>
<p>We offer standard A-frame sizes including 18" x 24", 24" x 36", and custom dimensions to fit your specific A-frame holder. Custom sizing ensures perfect fit and professional appearance for any A-frame display system.</p>

<h3>How quickly can I get rush replacement A-frame signs?</h3>
<p>Rush orders can be completed in 1 business day with same-day shipping available for orders approved and paid by 10 AM CST. This allows emergency A-frame sign replacements within 24-48 hours.</p>

<h3>Do you provide design services for A-frame sign replacements?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional graphic designers create custom A-frame sign artwork. Simply provide your text, images, and design preferences, and we'll create print-ready artwork optimized for outdoor display and A-frame installation.</p>

<h3>What file formats work best for A-frame sign printing?</h3>
<p>EPS files converted to outlines provide the best print quality, followed by high-resolution PDFs, TIFF, and JPEG files. All artwork should be 300 DPI resolution in CMYK color mode for optimal results.</p>

<h3>Are digital proofs available before printing?</h3>
<p>Digital proofs are available for all orders and include artwork preview, order details, pricing summary, and shipping information. Proof approval is required before production begins to ensure complete satisfaction.</p>
