<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How durable is the Black Signicade A-Frame hardware?</h3>
<p>The Black Signicade Deluxe A-Frame is constructed from high-quality plastic designed for outdoor commercial use. It features weather-resistant construction that withstands rain, wind, and UV exposure while maintaining structural integrity.</p>

<h3>Can I get different artwork on each side of the signs?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can have completely different designs printed on the front and back of each Coroplast sign. This allows maximum flexibility for your advertising message and seasonal promotions.</p>

<h3>How easy is it to change the signs in the A-Frame?</h3>
<p>The signs slide easily into the A-Frame slots and can be removed and replaced quickly. This allows you to update your messaging, promote seasonal offers, or use different signs for various events without purchasing new hardware.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for custom artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept PDF, AI, EPS, and high-resolution PSD files. Images should be 150 DPI minimum at actual print size (36" x 24") and provided in CMYK color mode for accurate printing results.</p>

<h3>How stable is the A-Frame in windy conditions?</h3>
<p>The Signicade Deluxe features a wide, stable base design that resists tipping in moderate wind conditions. For extremely windy areas, we recommend bringing signs indoors or using additional weights for enhanced stability.</p>

<h3>Can I order additional signs without purchasing new hardware?</h3>
<p>Yes, replacement signs can be ordered separately once you have the A-Frame hardware. This is cost-effective for businesses that want to rotate messages or update signage regularly.</p>

<h3>What's the maximum quantity I can order?</h3>
<p>You can order up to 1000 A-Frame kits per order. Volume discounts are available for larger quantities, and our customer service team can assist with bulk pricing and shipping arrangements.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services if I don't have artwork?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional design team can create custom artwork for your A-Frame signs. Design services include layout creation, text formatting, image integration, and color optimization for professional results.</p>
