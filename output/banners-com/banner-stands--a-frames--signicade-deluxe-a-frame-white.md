<h2>A-Frame Sign Artwork Requirements & Design Specifications</h2>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements:</strong> Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats for optimal print quality. JPEG and PNG files are acceptable but may require resolution verification.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Standards:</strong> Artwork should be created at 150 DPI at full size (36" x 24") for sharp, professional printing. Low-resolution files may result in pixelated or blurry printed signs.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode Specifications:</strong> Design artwork in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted to CMYK, which may cause slight color variations.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safe Area:</strong> Include a 0.125" bleed area around all edges of your design. Keep important text and graphics at least 0.25" from the trim edge to ensure proper visibility.</p>

<p><strong>Font and Text Guidelines:</strong> Use fonts larger than 24 points for optimal readability from typical viewing distances. Convert all text to outlines or embed fonts to prevent font substitution issues.</p>

<p><strong>Design Templates:</strong> Download free A-frame sign templates from our website to ensure proper sizing and layout. Templates include guides for bleed areas, safe zones, and optimal text placement.</p>

<p><strong>Proof Process:</strong> Digital proofs are provided within 24 hours of artwork submission. Review proofs carefully for text accuracy, color appearance, and overall layout before approving for production.</p>

<p><strong>Design Services Available:</strong> Professional design services are available for customers who need artwork creation or modification. Upload reference materials and provide design instructions for custom design work.</p>

<p><strong>Color Matching:</strong> PMS color matching is available for specific brand color requirements. Provide PMS color numbers for accurate color reproduction on your A-frame signs.</p>
