<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What do people call an A-frame sign?</h3>
<p>A-frame signs are also commonly called sandwich board signs, sidewalk signs, or sidewalk sandwich boards. These portable advertising displays get their name from their distinctive A-shaped frame structure that holds changeable sign panels.</p>

<h3>What are A-frame signs made of?</h3>
<p>Our A-frame signs use 4mm corrugated plastic (Coroplast) sign panels printed in full color and paired with sturdy aluminum or plastic sign holders. The corrugated plastic material provides excellent durability, weather resistance, and lightweight portability for sidewalk advertising.</p>

<h3>Are A-frame signs waterproof and weather-resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes—these signs are completely waterproof and designed specifically for outdoor use. The corrugated plastic construction resists water absorption, and the signs are engineered to be weather- and wind-resistant for reliable performance in all seasons.</p>

<h3>Are A-frame signs double-sided?</h3>
<p>Yes—double-sided A-frame signs are available and recommended for maximum visibility from both pedestrian and vehicle traffic directions. You can use identical graphics on both sides or create different messages for varied advertising approaches.</p>

<h3>What is the largest A-frame sign size available?</h3>
<p>The largest standard A-frame sign size is 36" × 24", available with both Signicade and Signicade Deluxe frame systems. Custom sizes may be available for special applications—contact customer service for sizing options beyond standard dimensions.</p>

<h3>Can I order replacement signs for my A-frame frame?</h3>
<p>Yes—replacement signs are readily available and designed for easy installation in existing A-frame holders. This allows you to update your advertising message for new promotions, seasonal changes, or menu updates without purchasing new frames.</p>

<h3>Can I design my A-frame sign online?</h3>
<p>Yes—create your custom A-frame sign using our online design tool with templates, fonts, and graphics library. You can also upload your own completed artwork files during the ordering process for maximum design flexibility.</p>

<h3>Can I request a proof and what are your production times?</h3>
<p>Yes—request a PDF proof during checkout for design review before production begins. Production options range from 1–5 business days after proof approval. Orders approved after 5:00 PM CST begin production the next business day, and total delivery time includes both production and shipping time.</p>
