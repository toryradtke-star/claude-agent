<p>The carrying case is compatible with all Retractor Lite banner stands up to 36" wide, including the 24", 31", 33", and 36" models. The universal design accommodates the base, pole, and retracted banner components.</p>

<h3>How does this compare to the standard soft case included with banner stands?</h3>
<p>This heavy-duty carrying case provides superior protection with reinforced construction, secure snap closures, and enhanced durability for frequent transport. The standard soft case offers basic protection, while this upgrade delivers professional-grade security for your investment.</p>

<h3>Can I store multiple banner graphics in the carrying case?</h3>
<p>The case is designed to accommodate one complete banner stand system with retracted banner. Additional graphics should be stored separately to prevent damage and ensure proper case closure.</p>

<h3>Is the carrying case suitable for air travel?</h3>
<p>Yes, the compact design and durable construction make it suitable for air travel as checked luggage. The secure closure and protective construction help ensure your banner stand arrives safely at your destination.</p>

<h3>What are the carrying case dimensions?</h3>
<p>The case dimensions accommodate Retractor Lite banner stands up to 36" wide when fully retracted. Exact dimensions are optimized for the banner stand components while maintaining a compact profile for easy transport.</p>

<h3>Do you offer volume pricing for multiple carrying cases?</h3>

<h3>How quickly can carrying cases ship?</h3>
<p>Carrying cases are in-stock accessories that typically ship the same business day for orders placed by 2:00 PM CST. Express shipping options are available for urgent delivery needs.</p>

<h3>Can I order a carrying case for an older Retractor Lite model?</h3>
<p>The carrying case is compatible with current and recent Retractor Lite models up to 36". If you have questions about compatibility with older models, please contact our customer service team with your specific banner stand model information.</p>
