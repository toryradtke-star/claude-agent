<h2>X-Stand Banner Hardware FAQ</h2>

<h3>What banner sizes work with this X-stand hardware?</h3>
<p>The adjustable X-stand accommodates banners from 24" to 36" in width. Heights of 60" to 72" provide optimal proportions. Do not use banners below the minimum 24" width as this can damage the frame.</p>

<h3>What type of banner finishing is required?</h3>
<p>Banners need grommets or pole pockets to attach to the plastic hooks on the X-stand frame. Standard grommet placement works best for secure attachment and easy setup.</p>

<h3>How much does the X-stand hardware weigh?</h3>
<p>The complete X-stand hardware weighs only 2 pounds, making it highly portable for trade shows and business presentations. The lightweight design also reduces shipping costs.</p>

<h3>Can X-stand hardware be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>No, this X-stand system is designed for indoor use only. The lightweight construction is not suitable for outdoor conditions where wind resistance is required.</p>

<h3>How does the X-stand assemble?</h3>
<p>Assembly is tool-free and simple. Connect the fiberglass arms to the central plastic hub, adjust for your banner width, and attach your banner to the corner hooks. Setup takes just a few minutes.</p>

<h3>What's included with the X-stand hardware?</h3>
<p>The hardware kit includes the plastic support hub, banner hooks, and adjustable fiberglass arms. Banners are sold separately and can be customized with your artwork and specifications.</p>
