<h2>Artwork Requirements for X Stand Banner Printing</h2>

<h3>File Format Specifications</h3>
<p>For best print results, submit artwork as .eps files converted to outline. We also accept high-resolution .pdf, .tif, and .jpg formats. If you're unsure about your file quality, our professional graphic designers can assist with artwork optimization.</p>

<h3>Resolution Requirements</h3>
<p>Artwork should be created at actual size with 150 DPI resolution for optimal print quality. Images and graphics must be high enough resolution to avoid pixelation when printed at full banner size.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How long does production take?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 1-5 business days depending on your selected timeline. Rush production (1-2 business days) is available for urgent replacement banner needs, while standard production (3 business days) offers the best value.</p>

<h3>What materials are available for replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Choose from 13oz vinyl for general indoor/outdoor use, 15oz block-out material for premium displays, or banner soft fabric for wrinkle-resistant indoor applications. All materials are compatible with adjustable X stand hardware.</p>

<h3>Can I use my own artwork for the replacement banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can upload your own print-ready files in .eps, .pdf, .tif, or .jpg formats. We also offer template customization and professional design services if you need assistance creating artwork.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for X stand replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Standard size is 72" x 35" to fit most adjustable X stands. Custom sizing is available for non-standard X stand dimensions - contact customer service for custom measurements and pricing.</p>

<h3>How much do replacement banners cost?</h3>
<p>Pricing varies based on size, material, quantity, and production timeline. Use our online configurator to get instant pricing for your specific requirements. Volume discounts are available for multiple replacement banners.</p>
