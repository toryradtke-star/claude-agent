<h2>Giant Banner Display FAQ</h2>

<h3>What's included with the Giant Outdoor Banner Display?</h3>
<p>Each order includes the complete rotating hardware system, fillable base, mounting hardware, assembly instructions, and your custom-printed 120" x 42" banner.</p>

<h3>How do I set up the display for wind resistance?</h3>
<p>Fill the base with water or sand for ballast stability. The rotating mechanism allows the display to turn with wind direction, preventing tip-over while maintaining banner visibility.</p>

<h3>What file format should I use for my banner artwork?</h3>
<p>Vector files like .eps with outlined fonts provide the best quality. We also accept high-resolution PDF or JPEG files. Contact our team if you need help with file preparation.</p>

<h3>How long does production take?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3 business days after artwork approval. Express 2-day and economy 5-day options are available depending on your timeline needs.</p>

<h3>Can I get design help for my banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional graphic designers can create custom artwork for your display. We also offer templates you can customize, or you can upload your own ready-to-print files.</p>

<h3>What's the size tolerance for the finished banner?</h3>
<p>Vinyl material may vary up to 1/2 inch per linear foot due to temperature sensitivity during printing and finishing. Contact us if your application requires precise dimensions.</p>

<h3>Is the display suitable for permanent outdoor use?</h3>
<p>The system is designed for temporary to semi-permanent outdoor display. The rotating mechanism and weather-resistant materials handle normal outdoor conditions, but extreme weather may require temporary removal.</p>

<h3>Can I order replacement banners for the hardware?</h3>
<p>Yes, replacement banners can be ordered separately to use with your existing hardware system, allowing you to change messages for different campaigns or seasons.</p>
