<h2>Banner Artwork Requirements for Small Outdoor Frame Display</h2>

<p>To ensure optimal display quality with your small outdoor banner frame, custom banners must meet specific sizing and finishing requirements for proper frame attachment.</p>

<h3>Banner Size Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Recommended Size:</strong> 48" W x 36" H (allows proper frame fit)</li>
<li><strong>Maximum Size:</strong> 50" W x 37" H (tight fit on frame)</li>
<li><strong>Minimum Size:</strong> 44" W x 32" H (adequate display coverage)</li>
<li><strong>Aspect Ratio:</strong> Maintain 4:3 ratio for optimal appearance</li>
</ul>

<h3>Required Banner Finishing</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Grommets Required:</strong> Must specify grommets for ball bungee attachment</li>
<li><strong>Grommet Spacing:</strong> 12-18 inches apart around perimeter</li>
<li><strong>Hem Reinforcement:</strong> Recommended for durability</li>
<li><strong>Corner Grommets:</strong> Essential for proper frame tensioning</li>
</ul>

<h3>Artwork Design Guidelines</h3>
<ul>
<li>Design at actual print size with 300 DPI resolution</li>
<li>Include 0.25" bleed area around design edges</li>
<li>Keep critical text/images 1" from banner edges</li>
<li>Use high-contrast colors for outdoor visibility</li>
</ul>

<h3>File Upload Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Accepted Formats:</strong> PDF, AI, PSD, PNG, JPG</li>
<li><strong>Color Mode:</strong> CMYK preferred for accurate color matching</li>
<li><strong>File Size:</strong> Maximum 100MB per uploaded file</li>
<li><strong>Fonts:</strong> All fonts embedded or converted to outlines</li>
</ul>

<p><em>Note: Banners are sold separately from frame hardware. Design services available for custom banner artwork creation.</em></p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What banner sizes work with the small outdoor frame?</h3>
<p>The frame accommodates banners from 44" x 32" minimum up to 50" x 37" maximum. We recommend 48" x 36" banners for optimal fit and appearance. All banners must include grommets for ball bungee attachment.</p>

<h3>Is the banner included with the frame purchase?</h3>
<p>No, banners are sold separately from the frame hardware. You can order custom banners with grommets from our vinyl banner section, or use existing banners that meet the size and grommet requirements.</p>

<h3>How long does frame setup take at events?</h3>
<p>Complete frame assembly and banner installation typically takes 10-15 minutes. The aluminum frame connects quickly, and ball bungees allow fast banner attachment to grommets.</p>

<h3>Can the frame withstand outdoor weather conditions?</h3>
<p>Yes, the aluminum construction is weather-resistant and suitable for outdoor use. However, we recommend securing or removing the frame during severe weather conditions with high winds.</p>

<h3>What's included with the frame purchase?</h3>
<p>Each frame includes the complete aluminum frame hardware, ball bungees for banner attachment, professional carrying case, and assembly instructions. Only the banner is sold separately.</p>

<h3>Are replacement parts available?</h3>
<p>Yes, replacement ball bungees and other hardware components are available separately. Contact customer service at 320.391.5300 for replacement part orders.</p>

<h3>Can I order multiple frames for larger displays?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Multiple frames can be used together to create larger display areas. Volume discounts may be available for multiple frame orders - contact our sales team for pricing.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for custom banners?</h3>
<p>For custom banner orders, we accept PDF, AI, PSD, PNG, and JPG files up to 100MB. Artwork should be designed at actual print size with 300 DPI resolution and include grommets in the specifications.</p>
