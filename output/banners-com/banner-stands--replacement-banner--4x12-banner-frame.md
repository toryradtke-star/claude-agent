<h3>FAQ - Banner Frame Replacement Banners</h3>

<h4>What size banner do I need for my 4x12 frame?</h4>
<p>Our replacement banners are sized at 42" x 138" to fit standard 4x12 banner frame systems perfectly. This sizing ensures proper tension and professional appearance when mounted.</p>

<h4>How do I know which vinyl weight to choose?</h4>
<p>Choose 13oz vinyl for standard indoor displays and general retail environments. Select 18oz vinyl for high-traffic areas, trade shows, or displays that will remain mounted for extended periods.</p>

<h4>Can I reuse my existing banner frame hardware?</h4>
<p>Yes, our replacement banners are designed to work with your existing 4x12 frame hardware. The replacement process simply involves removing the old banner and installing the new one.</p>

<h4>What if I don't have print-ready artwork?</h4>
<p>No problem! You can either customize one of our templates using the online designer or work with our professional design team to create custom artwork from your concepts and ideas.</p>

<h4>How long will my replacement banner last?</h4>
<p>Indoor banner frame replacements typically last 1-3 years depending on environmental factors and usage. Both vinyl options are designed for extended indoor display life.</p>

<h4>Do you offer rush production for urgent replacements?</h4>
<p>Yes, we offer 2-business-day express production for urgent display updates. Combined with overnight shipping, you can receive your replacement banner within 3 days of order placement.</p>
