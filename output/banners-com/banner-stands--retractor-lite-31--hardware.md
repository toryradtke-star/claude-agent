<h2>Retractor Stand Hardware Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What's included with the Retractor Lite 31 Hardware?</h3>
<p>The hardware includes the aluminum base, shock cord pole system, snap rail for banner attachment, banner tape, and swivel feet. Setup instructions are also included. Banner graphics are sold separately.</p>

<h3>How much does the retractor stand hardware weigh?</h3>
<p>The complete hardware unit weighs 6 lbs, making it easy to transport to trade shows and events while still providing stable display support.</p>

<h3>What size banners work with this hardware?</h3>
<p>The Retractor Lite 31 Hardware is designed for banners that are 31" wide and up to 84" tall. The snap rail system accommodates standard retractable banner materials.</p>

<h3>How long does setup take?</h3>
<p>The shock cord pole system and snap rail attachment allow for setup in just a few minutes. No tools are required - simply extend the pole, attach your banner, and adjust the swivel feet for stability.</p>

<h3>Can I use multiple banners with one hardware unit?</h3>
<p>Yes, the snap rail system makes it easy to swap banner graphics quickly. You can have multiple banners printed for different events or messages and change them as needed.</p>

<h3>Is assembly required?</h3>
<p>Minimal assembly is required. The main components come ready to use - you simply extend the shock cord pole and attach your banner graphics. Detailed instructions are included.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between black and silver finish?</h3>
<p>Both finishes offer the same durability and functionality. Black provides a modern, professional appearance while silver offers a classic trade show look. Choose based on your brand preference.</p>

<h3>How stable is the base on different floor surfaces?</h3>
<p>The dual swivel feet adjust to accommodate uneven surfaces and provide excellent stability on carpet, tile, concrete, and other common floor types found at events and retail locations.</p>
