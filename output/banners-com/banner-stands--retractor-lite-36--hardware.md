<h2>Retractable Banner Stand Hardware FAQ</h2>

<h3>What's included with the Retractor Lite 36 Hardware?</h3>
<p>Your hardware package includes the complete aluminum base unit, shock cord pole system, snap rail for top banner attachment, banner tape for bottom mounting, and carrying case. Custom banner printing is available separately.</p>

<h3>What banner sizes work with this retractable stand?</h3>
<p>This hardware accommodates banners up to 36" wide. The display height extends to 83.5" when fully assembled, making it ideal for eye-catching trade show and retail displays.</p>

<h3>How portable is the Retractor Lite 36 system?</h3>
<p>At just 7.5 pounds, this aluminum construction is designed for easy transport between events. The shock cord pole system breaks down quickly, and everything fits in the included carrying case.</p>

<h3>Can I use this hardware with banners I already have?</h3>
<p>The system works with banners that have snap rail compatibility at the top and can accommodate banner tape attachment at the bottom. Contact our team to verify compatibility with your existing banner materials.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between black and silver finishes?</h3>
<p>Both finishes offer the same structural performance and durability. Choose based on your display aesthetic - black provides a sleek, modern look while silver offers a classic professional appearance.</p>

<h3>How stable is this retractable banner stand?</h3>
<p>The dual swivel feet provide excellent stability on various floor surfaces. The 15" depth base and 7.5-pound weight ensure your display remains secure in normal indoor environments.</p>
