<ul>
<li>Account for snap rail attachment at top (approximately 1" area)</li>

<li>Vertical orientation recommended for maximum impact</li>
<li>High contrast designs work best for trade show visibility</li>
<li>Large, bold text ensures readability from distance</li>
</ul>

<h3>Material Recommendations</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Standard Banner Vinyl:</strong> 13 oz. matte finish for indoor use</li>
<li><strong>Fabric Options:</strong> Wrinkle-resistant polyester for premium appearance</li>
<li><strong>Mesh Materials:</strong> Not recommended due to snap rail attachment</li>

<h3>Attachment Method</h3>
<ul>
<li>Top edge: Snap rail insertion (requires hem or grommets)</li>
<li>Bottom edge: Banner tape application for tension</li>

<h2>Banner Stand Hardware FAQ</h2>

<h3>What is included with the Retractor Lite 47 Hardware?</h3>
<p>The hardware package includes the aluminum base unit, shock cord pole system, two swivel feet, snap rail for banner attachment, banner tape, and assembly instructions. Banner graphics are sold separately.</p>

<h3>What size banner graphics work with this hardware?</h3>
<p>This system accommodates banners up to 47.25" wide by 78" tall. The snap rail attachment requires specific finishing on your banner graphics for proper installation.</p>

<h3>How much does the complete hardware system weigh?</h3>
<p>The Retractor Lite 47 Hardware weighs 10.25 lbs total, making it easily portable for trade shows and business presentations.</p>

<h3>Can I use this banner stand hardware outdoors?</h3>
<p>This hardware is designed for indoor use only. The lightweight construction and snap rail system work best in controlled indoor environments without wind exposure.</p>

<h3>How long does setup take for this banner stand?</h3>
<p>The shock cord pole system and snap rail design allow for setup in under 2 minutes. The swivel feet adjust quickly for stability on various surfaces.</p>

<h3>Can I order replacement parts for this hardware?</h3>
<p>Yes, replacement components are available including snap rails, banner tape, and pole sections. Contact customer service for specific replacement part needs.</p>

<h3>What shipping options are available for banner stand hardware?</h3>
<p>We offer ground shipping, expedited 2-3 day service, and next-day air delivery. Direct-to-venue shipping is available for trade show deliveries.</p>

<h3>Do you offer volume pricing for multiple hardware units?</h3>
<p>Yes, volume discounts are available for orders of 10 or more units. Contact our sales team at 866.419.7446 for custom pricing on large quantity orders.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for banner graphics?</h3>
