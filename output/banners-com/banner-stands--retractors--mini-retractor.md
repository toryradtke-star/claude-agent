<h2>FAQ - Mini Retractor Banner Stand Questions</h2>

<h3>What's included with the mini retractor banner stand combo?</h3>
<p>Your combo includes the mini retractor hardware with shock-corded support pole, protective base, and your custom printed 16" x 8" banner. No additional hardware or assembly is required.</p>

<h3>How does the retractor mechanism work?</h3>
<p>The banner pulls up from the base and the shock-corded pole extends to support the display. When finished, the pole folds down and the banner retracts into the protective base for storage and transport.</p>

<h3>What banner materials work best for retractor stands?</h3>
<p>We recommend 13 oz vinyl for most applications, 14 oz no-curl vinyl for photography and detailed graphics, or banner soft fabric for lightweight portability. All materials are compatible with the retractor hardware.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse the retractor hardware with new banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, the mini retractor hardware is designed for repeated use. You can order replacement banners separately to update your display graphics for different events or campaigns.</p>

<h3>How portable is the mini retractor banner stand?</h3>
<p>Very portable - the banner retracts completely into the base and the support pole folds down for compact transport. The entire unit is lightweight and perfect for trade shows and traveling presentations.</p>

<h3>What's the maximum viewing distance for the 16" x 8" size?</h3>
<p>The mini retractor is designed for tabletop viewing distances of 3-10 feet. Consider text size and graphics accordingly for your intended viewing environment.</p>

<h3>Do you offer custom sizes for mini retractor banners?</h3>
<p>The mini retractor hardware is specifically designed for 16" x 8" banners. For other sizes, consider our full-size retractable banner stands or custom signage options.</p>

<h3>How long does production take for mini retractor banner stands?</h3>
<p>Production options include 2-day express, 3-day standard, or 5-day economy timelines. Production begins after artwork approval and payment processing.</p>
