<h2>Replacement Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>How do I know what size replacement banner I need?</h3>
<p>Check your existing retractor stand specifications or measure your current banner. If you're unsure, contact our customer service team with your retractor model information, and we'll help determine the correct replacement banner size.</p>

<h3>Will my replacement banner work with any retractor stand?</h3>
<p>Our replacement banners are designed to work with standard retractor stand systems. For specialty or custom retractor stands, contact us to confirm compatibility before ordering.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between vinyl and fabric replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Vinyl offers durability and vibrant colors, ideal for frequent use and outdoor displays. Fabric provides a premium matte finish perfect for upscale presentations and corporate environments.</p>

<h3>How long will my replacement banner last?</h3>
<p>With proper care, vinyl replacement banners can last several years of regular trade show use. Fabric banners offer excellent durability but require more careful handling during retraction.</p>

<h3>Can you match specific brand colors on my replacement banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, our Color Match Proof System ensures accurate color reproduction for brand-critical applications. Contact our team to discuss color matching requirements for your project.</p>

<h3>What happens if my replacement banner doesn't fit my retractor?</h3>
<p>We guarantee proper sizing for standard retractor systems. If you experience fitting issues, contact our customer service team immediately for assistance and resolution.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional graphic designers can create custom artwork for your replacement banner or optimize your existing designs for best printing results.</p>

<h3>How do I install a replacement banner in my retractor stand?</h3>
<p>Each replacement banner order includes detailed installation instructions. The process typically involves removing the old banner and attaching the new banner to the retractor mechanism hardware.</p>
