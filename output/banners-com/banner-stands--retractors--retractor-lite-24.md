<h2>Retractor Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>What material works best for trade show retractor banners?</h3>
<p>The 14 oz No Curl Vinyl provides the best performance for trade show use, preventing curling and ensuring professional display quality. For premium presentations, Banner Soft Fabric offers reduced glare under trade show lighting.</p>

<h3>How portable is the Retractor Lite 24 system?</h3>
<p>The Retractor Lite 24 is designed for easy portability with included nylon carrying case. The lightweight design makes transport between trade shows and business locations simple for one person.</p>

<h3>Can I change graphics in my retractor banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, the graphics can be replaced when you need to update your display. Contact our customer service team for replacement banner pricing and specifications.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between Retractor Lite and heavy-duty retractor systems?</h3>
<p>The Retractor Lite 24 features a lighter duty mechanism perfect for occasional use and seasonal displays. For frequent setup/takedown or permanent installations, consider our heavy-duty retractor options.</p>

<h3>How long does production take for custom retractor banners?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3 business days, with 2-day express and 5-day saver options available. Production begins after artwork approval and payment processing.</p>
