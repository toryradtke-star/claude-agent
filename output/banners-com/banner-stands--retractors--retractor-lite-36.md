<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Banner Stands</h2>

<h3>What hardware is included with the Retractor Lite 36?</h3>
<p>Each order includes the complete Retractor Lite 36 retractable mechanism, custom printed banner, and nylon carrying case. Hardware is lightweight yet durable for trade show applications.</p>

<h3>How do I set up the retractable banner stand?</h3>
<p>Setup is simple: remove from carrying case, extend support feet, and pull banner up from base mechanism. Banner locks in place for stable display. Breakdown reverses the process for quick pack-up.</p>

<h3>Can I replace just the banner without buying new hardware?</h3>
<p>Yes, replacement banners are available for existing Retractor Lite 36 hardware. This allows you to update graphics while keeping the same display mechanism.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between vinyl and fabric materials?</h3>
<p>Vinyl materials (13 oz and 14 oz No Curl) offer durability for indoor/outdoor use with vibrant colors. Banner Soft Fabric provides an elegant matte finish ideal for upscale indoor presentations.</p>

<h3>How much does the complete banner stand weigh?</h3>
<p>The Retractor Lite 36 with banner weighs approximately 8-10 pounds, making it easy to transport to trade shows and events. Lightweight design doesn't compromise stability during display.</p>

<h3>Can I ship directly to the convention center?</h3>
<p>Yes, direct shipping to trade show venues is available. Contact Customer Service to arrange delivery timing and meet any special venue requirements for package receipt.</p>

<h3>What if my artwork doesn't meet the size requirements?</h3>
<p>Our design team can resize and optimize artwork files for proper banner stand dimensions. Professional design services ensure your graphics display correctly on the 78" x 36" banner.</p>

<h3>Is there a warranty on the banner stand hardware?</h3>
<p>Hardware includes manufacturer warranty against defects. Normal wear from trade show use is expected, but mechanism failures due to manufacturing issues are covered.</p>
