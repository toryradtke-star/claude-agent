<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Banner Stands</h2>

<h3>What's included with my retractable banner stand order?</h3>
<p>Your complete package includes the retractor hardware with double pole support, your custom printed 78" x 47" banner, and a protective carrying case for transport and storage.</p>

<h3>How long does production take for custom banner stands?</h3>
<p>We offer multiple production timelines: 2 business days for express orders, 3 business days for standard production, and 5 business days for economy pricing. Production begins after artwork approval.</p>

<h3>What file formats work best for banner stand artwork?</h3>
<p>Vector files like .EPS with outlined fonts produce the best results. We also accept high-resolution .PDF and .JPG files at 300 DPI. Our design team can help optimize your files if needed.</p>

<h3>Can I use my banner stand outdoors?</h3>
<p>The Retractor Lite 47 is designed primarily for indoor use at trade shows, businesses, and events. For outdoor applications, consider our weighted outdoor banner stand options.</p>

<h3>How do I set up my retractable banner stand?</h3>
<p>Setup is simple: extend the double poles, attach the top rail, and pull the banner up from the base. The retractor mechanism holds the banner in place. Full instructions are included with your order.</p>

<h3>What's your size guarantee for printed banners?</h3>
<p>Due to the temperature-sensitive nature of vinyl materials, we allow up to 1/2 inch variance per linear foot. For applications requiring precise sizing, please discuss your needs with our customer service team.</p>

<h3>Do you offer quantity discounts for multiple banner stands?</h3>
<p>Yes, volume pricing is available for orders of multiple banner stands. Business accounts also qualify for NET 30 payment terms and additional discounts.</p>

<h3>Can you ship directly to my trade show or event venue?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. We offer direct-to-event shipping to convention centers and venues. Provide the event details and we'll coordinate timing to ensure your banner stands arrive when needed.</p>
