<h2>Frequently Asked Questions - Table Top Banner Stands</h2>

<h3>What hardware is included with my table top banner stand?</h3>
<p>Each order includes the complete Retractor Lite table top hardware system with pre-assembled base, retraction mechanism, and mounting hardware. Your custom 36" x 24" banner arrives pre-installed and ready for immediate display setup.</p>

<h3>How portable is the table top banner stand for trade show transport?</h3>
<p>The Retractor Lite system is designed for frequent transport with lightweight aluminum construction. The compact retracted size fits easily in standard trade show transport cases, and setup requires no tools or additional assembly.</p>

<h3>Can I replace just the banner without ordering new hardware?</h3>
<p>Yes, replacement banners are available for existing Retractor Lite hardware. Contact customer service with your original order details to ensure proper banner sizing and mounting compatibility with your current hardware.</p>

<h3>What material works best for frequent trade show use?</h3>
<p>For frequent setup and breakdown, we recommend 14oz No Curl Vinyl for its anti-curl properties and durability, or Banner Soft Fabric for its wrinkle resistance and compact storage capabilities. Both materials handle repeated handling better than standard vinyl.</p>

<h3>How do I ensure my artwork fits properly on the 36" x 24" banner?</h3>
<p>Design your artwork to exactly 36" height x 24" width with 300 DPI resolution. Include a 0.125" bleed area on all sides for trim accuracy. Our design team can verify file specifications before production begins.</p>

<h3>Is overnight shipping available for urgent trade show deadlines?</h3>
<p>Yes, overnight shipping is available for orders completed before daily cutoff times. Combine express 2-day production with overnight shipping for fastest possible delivery. Contact customer service for same-day production availability on urgent orders.</p>

<h3>Can I order multiple banner stands with different artwork but matching design elements?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. We can coordinate multiple banner stand orders with consistent branding elements while customizing specific content for different products or services. Contact our design team to discuss multi-banner display coordination.</p>

<h3>What's the viewing distance recommendation for optimal text readability?</h3>
<p>Table top banner stands are typically viewed from 3-8 feet. Use minimum 24-point fonts for body text and 48-point or larger for headlines. High contrast colors and bold fonts improve readability in trade show environments.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services if I only have basic content and logo files?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional design team can create complete banner artwork from your logo, text content, and basic requirements. Design services include layout creation, color coordination, and optimization for table top banner stand display.</p>

<h3>What warranty covers the Retractor Lite hardware?</h3>
<p>Retractor Lite hardware includes manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Hardware replacement or repair is available for manufacturing defects. Normal wear from frequent use is not covered under warranty terms.</p>
