<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Retractor Banners</h2>

<h3>How does the telescoping height adjustment work?</h3>
<p>The telescoping pole adjusts from 62.5" to 79" high using a simple extension mechanism. This allows your banner display to accommodate different ceiling heights and venue requirements while maintaining professional appearance.</p>

<h3>Can I change banners without replacing the hardware?</h3>
<p>Yes, the snap rail header system allows easy banner interchange without disassembling the retractor hardware. Simply remove the existing banner and snap in a new banner for different events or messages.</p>

<h3>What banner material works best for trade shows?</h3>
<p>14 oz No Curl Vinyl provides the best lay-flat performance for professional trade show displays. The smooth matte finish and superior stability make it ideal for extended display periods.</p>

<h3>How much does the complete retractor system weigh?</h3>
<p>The complete telescoping retractor system with banner weighs approximately 15-20 pounds, making it portable for trade show transport while providing stable display performance.</p>

<h3>Can artwork be printed larger than 79" x 31"?</h3>
<p>Banners are printed at the maximum 79" x 31" size to fit the telescoping retractor system. The adjustable height feature accommodates the full banner size at any height setting.</p>

<h3>What file resolution is required for print quality?</h3>
<p>Vector files (.eps, .ai) provide the best quality. Raster files (JPG, PNG) should be minimum 150 DPI at actual 79" x 31" size for professional print results.</p>

<h3>How long do retractor banners typically last?</h3>
<p>With proper care, telescoping retractor banners last through dozens of trade shows and display events. The snap rail system and quality materials ensure long-term durability for repeated use.</p>
