### Replacement Hardware for Your Signs

Description/Features

Frame available in black or [white](https://www.banners.com/banner-stands/signicade-deluxe-a-frame/hardware-white)

Frame can be filled with water or sand for stability

A molded handle makes it easy to move

Folds flat for transport or storage

Tabs on the frame keep signs securely in place

Signs sold separately

Product Dimensions

Product Size: 27" W x 38.5" H x 19" D

Collapsible Dimensions: 27" W x 46.13" H x 3" D

Product Weight: 19lbs

The Black Signicade Deluxe A‑Frame Hardware is a durable outdoor A-frame sign stand built from weather-resistant plastic that won’t rust or splinter and folds flat for easy transport and storage. Its molded handle and optional water or sand fillable base provide stability and portability, while the frame’s secure tabs hold your [sign panels](https://www.banners.com/banner-stands/signicade-deluxe-a-frame/replacement-signs) (sold separately) in place for reliable sidewalk or curbside messaging.
