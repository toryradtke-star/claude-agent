<h2>A-Frame Sign Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What is an A-frame sign and how is it used for business advertising?</h3>
<p>An A-frame sign—also called a sidewalk sign or sandwich board—is a foldable, freestanding advertising display used in high foot-traffic areas like storefronts, strip malls, outdoor restaurants, and parking lots. The portable design allows businesses to attract customers with daily specials, promotions, and important announcements.</p>

<h3>Are Signicade A-frames suitable for year-round outdoor use?</h3>
<p>Yes, these A-frames are engineered for continuous outdoor use in all weather conditions. The frame is constructed from durable plastic that won't rust, splinter, or corrode, and can be filled with sand or water for enhanced stability during windy conditions. The printed signs feature weather-resistant inks and waterproof corrugated plastic material.</p>

<h3>Does the complete A-frame kit include one or two printed signs?</h3>
<p>Each Signicade Deluxe A-frame kit includes two (2) full-color printed signs sized 24" × 36"—one sign for each side of the frame. This allows for double-sided advertising with the option to display identical messaging or different content on each side.</p>

<h3>What color options are available for the A-frame hardware?</h3>
<p>Signicade Deluxe A-frames are available in black or white frame colors, allowing you to select the option that best complements your business branding and storefront appearance.</p>

<h3>Do you offer different A-frame sizes beyond the Signicade Deluxe?</h3>
<p>Yes, in addition to the Signicade Deluxe A-frame (24" × 36"), we also offer Simpo Sign A-frames (22" × 27") and Coroplast A-frame signs in multiple sizes including 12" × 18", 18" × 24", 22" × 28", and 24" × 36" to accommodate different advertising needs and space requirements.</p>

<h3>Can I order replacement signs for my existing A-frame?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Replacement signs are available for existing A-frame hardware, allowing you to update messaging seasonally or replace weather-worn signs while extending the life of your original frame investment.</p>

<h3>How stable are A-frame signs in windy outdoor conditions?</h3>
<p>Signicade A-frames feature fillable bases that can hold water or sand for increased stability. When filled, the frame can weigh up to 35 pounds, providing excellent resistance to moderate winds. The low center of gravity design further reduces the risk of tipping in outdoor conditions.</p>

<h3>What artwork specifications are required for A-frame sign printing?</h3>
<p>A-frame signs require artwork sized at 24" x 36" with 150 DPI resolution minimum. Acceptable file formats include PDF, AI, EPS, or high-resolution JPEG/PNG files. Include 0.125" bleed and keep important elements 0.5" from edges for optimal results.</p>
