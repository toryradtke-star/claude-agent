<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What is an A-frame sign (sidewalk sign)?</h3>
<p>An A-frame sign—also called a sidewalk sign or sandwich board—is a foldable, freestanding sign used to advertise in high foot-traffic areas like storefronts, strip malls, outdoor restaurants, and near parking lots.</p>

<h3>Are these A-frames suitable for outdoor use year-round?</h3>
<p>Yes. These A-frames are made from durable plastic that won't rust or splinter, and the frame can be filled with sand or water for added outdoor stability in various weather conditions.</p>

<h3>Does the Signicade A-frame include one or two printed inserts?</h3>
<p>The complete kit includes two (2) full-color printed signs, sized 24" × 36" (one for each side of the A-frame).</p>

<h3>What colors is the Signicade A-frame available in?</h3>
<p>The Signicade Deluxe A-frame is available in white only. The printed signs can be customized with any colors and designs.</p>

<h3>Do you offer other A-frame sizes or styles?</h3>
<p>Yes. In addition to the Signicade Deluxe A-frame (24" × 36" sign size), other A-frame sizes and styles are available. Contact customer service for additional options and sizing information.</p>
