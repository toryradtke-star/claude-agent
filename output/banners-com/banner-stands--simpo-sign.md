<p>Our A-frame signs use premium 4mm corrugated plastic (also known as Coroplast) for the printed sign panels. This material features a hollow flute construction similar to cardboard but made from durable plastic that provides excellent weather resistance.</p>

<ul>
<li>Lightweight yet sturdy construction for easy handling</li>

<h2>A-Frame Sign Questions & Installation Support</h2>

<h3>What is an A-frame sign and how is it used for business advertising?</h3>
<p>An A-frame sign, also called a sidewalk sign or sandwich board, is a portable, freestanding advertising display that folds into an "A" shape. Businesses use them in high foot-traffic areas like sidewalks, parking lots, and storefronts to advertise specials, services, or important information. The dual-sided design maximizes visibility from multiple directions.</p>

<h3>Are custom A-frame signs suitable for outdoor storefront use?</h3>
<p>Yes, our A-frame signs are specifically designed for outdoor use. The plastic hardware won't rust or splinter like metal or wood alternatives, and the corrugated plastic signs are completely waterproof. The frame base can be filled with sand or water for additional stability in windy conditions.</p>

<h3>How many printed signs come with the A-frame hardware kit?</h3>
<p>Each complete A-frame kit includes two full-color printed signs measuring 22" × 27". This allows you to display different messages on each side or create a cohesive design that's visible from both directions. Replacement signs can be ordered separately for seasonal updates.</p>

<h3>What color options are available for A-frame sign hardware?</h3>
<p>The A-frame hardware is available in white, providing a clean, professional appearance that complements any sign design. The neutral color ensures your custom printed graphics remain the focus while maintaining a professional business appearance.</p>

<h3>Do you offer other A-frame sizes for different business needs?</h3>
<p>Yes, in addition to our standard 22" × 27" A-frame signs, we also offer larger Signicade Deluxe A-frames that accommodate 24" × 36" signs. The larger size provides increased visibility for businesses that need maximum impact or have more detailed messaging requirements.</p>
