<h2>Banner Frame Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<h3>File Format Specifications</h3>
<p>Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, or PSD format at actual size. Files should be created at 150 DPI minimum for optimal banner frame printing quality.</p>

<h3>Design Area Guidelines</h3>
<p>Design your banner frame artwork to the exact finished size of 42" x 90". Include a 0.25" bleed area around all edges to ensure proper printing coverage.</p>

<h3>Color Requirements</h3>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files may result in color shifts during the printing process for your banner frame system.</p>

<h3>Text & Image Resolution</h3>
<p>Ensure all text is outlined or converted to curves. Images should be embedded at 300 DPI or higher for crisp reproduction on banner frame material.</p>

<h3>Professional Design Services</h3>
<p>Our design team can create professional banner frame artwork from your concepts, logos, and messaging. Design services ensure optimal results for your banner frame display.</p>

<h2>Banner Frame System FAQ</h2>

<h3>How long do banner frame systems last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Banner frame hardware is designed for permanent installation and will last many years with proper installation. The vinyl banners typically last 2-5 years depending on weather exposure and material choice.</p>

<h3>Can banner frames be installed on any surface?</h3>
<p>Yes, banner frame systems can be mounted on brick, wood, concrete, metal, and most flat surfaces. Different mounting hardware may be required based on the surface material.</p>

<h3>How difficult is it to change banners in the frame system?</h3>
<p>Replacing banners in an installed frame system takes just a few minutes and requires no tools. The banner frame is designed for quick and easy banner changes.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between 13 oz and 18 oz vinyl for banner frames?</h3>
<p>18 oz vinyl provides maximum durability for extreme weather conditions and high-wind areas. 13 oz vinyl is suitable for most outdoor applications and offers good durability at a lower cost.</p>

<h3>Do you provide installation instructions for banner frame systems?</h3>
<p>Yes, detailed installation instructions are included with every banner frame kit. We also provide customer support to assist with installation questions.</p>

<h3>Can I order replacement banners for existing banner frames?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Once you have banner frame hardware installed, you can order replacement banners as needed for updates, seasonal changes, or promotions.</p>
