<h2>Frequently Asked Questions - Replacement Banners</h2>

<h3>What materials are available for replacement banners?</h3>
<p>We offer premium 13oz and 15oz scrim vinyl with reinforced fiber construction, as well as Banner Soft fabric material with matte finish. Both materials are specifically designed for indoor banner stand applications.</p>

<h3>How do I know which size replacement banner I need?</h3>
<p>Measure your existing banner or check your stand specifications. We offer rotatable stand sizes (36"×62", 36"×78", 40"×75", 48"×72") and versatile banner stand sizes (72"×24", 72"×36", 90"×36"). Contact customer service if you need assistance determining the correct size.</p>

<h3>Can I get a replacement banner in a custom size?</h3>
<p>Yes, custom sizes are available. Contact us at 1-866-419-SIGN or request a custom quote for sizes not listed in our standard options.</p>

<h3>How long does production take for replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 1 business day (priority) to 5 business days (saver). Production begins after artwork approval and does not include shipping time.</p>

<h3>What artwork formats do you accept?</h3>
<p>We accept high-resolution PDF, Adobe Illustrator (.AI), Photoshop (.PSD), JPEG (300 DPI minimum), and PNG files. All files should include 0.25" bleed area and keep critical elements 0.5" from edges.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide professional design services, template customization, and have an extensive library of pre-designed templates organized by industry and application.</p>

<h3>How are replacement banners shipped?</h3>
<p>Replacement banners are carefully packaged in protective tubes to prevent wrinkles and damage. Shipping options include standard ground, expedited, and overnight delivery with tracking provided.</p>

<h3>What is the difference between scrim vinyl and Banner Soft fabric?</h3>
<p>Scrim vinyl features reinforced fiber construction for enhanced durability, while Banner Soft fabric offers a premium matte finish with vibrant colors. Both are suitable for indoor applications, with material choice depending on your specific display preferences.</p>

<h3>Can I order multiple replacement banners at once?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer volume pricing for multiple replacement banner orders. Contact our customer service team for bulk order discounts and business account options.</p>

<h3>What if my replacement banner doesn't fit my stand?</h3>
<p>We guarantee proper fit when you provide accurate size specifications. If you're unsure about sizing, our customer service team can help determine the correct dimensions for your specific stand system before production begins.</p>
