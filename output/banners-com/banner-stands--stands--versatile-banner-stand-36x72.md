<h2>Professional Versatile Banner Stand Hardware with Custom Vinyl & Fabric Banner Printing</h2>

<p>The Versatile Banner Stand provides excellent stability, easy adjustment and is capable of holding two different banners to create a double sided display. It breaks down into a compact, flat box for easy shipping and transportation. The horizontal support bars accommodate dowels for pole pocketed banners and can hold rigid substrates up to 6 mm thick.</p>

<p>Price includes the Banner Stand hardware and a 36" x 72" full-color printed banner with professional finishing. The printed banners feature a 2" pole pocket on the top and bottom for secure hardware mounting. Keep all important text and graphics at least 3" from the top and bottom edges.</p>

<p>Available with durable 13 oz vinyl material for indoor and outdoor use, or premium Banner Soft Fabric for professional trade show displays. Dowels and mounting hardware are included with every order.</p>

<h2>Banner Stand Material Options for Indoor & Outdoor Display Use</h2>

<h3>13 oz Vinyl Banner Material for Banner Stands</h3>
<p>Our high quality 13 oz vinyl banner material is the standard material we use for banner stands. It is rated for both indoor and outdoor use. You can select to have either (1) 13 oz vinyl banner or (2) 13 oz vinyl banners printed for the stand.</p>

<h3>Banner Soft Fabric Material for Trade Show Displays</h3>
<p>Our Banner Soft material is our fabric option for banner stands. The material offers a matte finish with vibrant print colors. The edges of the fabric will be cut with a hot knife to prevent fraying when rolled in and out of the retractor. We recommend you be very careful when retracting the banner as some fraying may still happen. You can select to have either (1) fabric banner or (2) fabric banners printed for the stand.</p>
