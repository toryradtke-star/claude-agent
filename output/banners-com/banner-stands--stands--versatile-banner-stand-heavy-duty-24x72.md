<h2>Banner Stand FAQ</h2>

<h3>How stable is the Heavy Duty Banner Stand compared to standard models?</h3>
<p>The Heavy Duty Banner Stand features enhanced weight and reinforced construction for superior stability compared to standard banner stands. This makes it ideal for high-traffic trade show environments and areas with air circulation that might affect lighter display systems.</p>

<h3>Can I use my own artwork, or do I need to use your templates?</h3>
<p>You can upload your own professional artwork files or choose from our extensive template library. We accept PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and high-resolution JPEG files. Our design team can also create custom artwork based on your specifications.</p>

<h3>What's included with my Heavy Duty Banner Stand order?</h3>
<p>Your complete order includes the heavy duty banner stand hardware, one 24" x 72" full-color printed banner, dowels, mounting hardware, and setup instructions. Additional banners for double-sided display can be added to your order.</p>

<h3>How does the double-sided display capability work?</h3>
<p>The banner stand's horizontal support bars can hold two separate banners, creating a double-sided display. This allows you to maximize your messaging with different content on each side or duplicate your design for 360-degree visibility.</p>

<h3>What are the setup and breakdown requirements?</h3>
<p>The Heavy Duty Banner Stand sets up quickly without tools and breaks down into a compact, flat configuration for easy shipping and storage. Setup typically takes less than 5 minutes, making it perfect for trade show environments with time constraints.</p>

<h3>Can the banner stand accommodate different material thicknesses?</h3>
<p>Yes, the horizontal support bars accommodate pole pocketed banners and can hold rigid substrates up to 6mm thick. This versatility allows you to use various materials depending on your display needs and budget requirements.</p>

<h3>What's the recommended safe zone for text and important graphics?</h3>
<p>Keep all important text and graphics at least 3" from the top and bottom edges of your design. This accounts for the 2" pole pockets and ensures your critical messaging remains visible and professional in the final display.</p>

<h3>Do you offer expedited production for urgent trade show deadlines?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer 2-business-day express production for urgent orders. This service works with our template designs and standard material options. Contact our customer service team to confirm availability for your specific requirements.</p>
