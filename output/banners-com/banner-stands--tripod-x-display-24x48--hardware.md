<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Banner Display Hardware</h2>

<h3>What banner size works with this tripod X display hardware?</h3>
<p>This display hardware is specifically designed for 24" x 48" banners with grommets at all four corners. The fiberglass arms are sized to provide proper tension for this exact banner dimension.</p>

<h3>Are banners included with the display hardware?</h3>
<p>No, banners are sold separately. You can order custom 24" x 48" banners through our banner services, or use existing banners that meet the size and grommet requirements.</p>

<h3>How quickly can the tripod display be set up?</h3>
<p>The tripod X display hardware sets up in under 2 minutes. Simply unfold the tripod base, extend the fiberglass arms, and attach your grommet banner to the connection points.</p>

<h3>Can this display hardware be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>Yes, the tripod display hardware can be used outdoors, though we recommend using appropriate banner materials like mesh or heavy vinyl for wind resistance. The hardware should be weighted or secured in windy conditions.</p>

<h3>What's included with the display hardware order?</h3>
<p>Each order includes the complete tripod base system, fiberglass X-frame arms, connection hardware for banner attachment, and a drawstring storage pouch for transport.</p>

<h3>How much does the display hardware weigh?</h3>
<p>The complete tripod X display hardware weighs 2.2 pounds, making it easy to transport to trade shows, events, and retail locations.</p>

<h3>Can I order multiple display hardware units?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can order between 1 and 1000 units depending on your display needs. Volume pricing may be available for large quantity orders.</p>

<h3>What type of surface can the tripod display be used on?</h3>
<p>The tripod base works on most flat surfaces including concrete, tile, carpet, and outdoor pavement. The folding legs provide stable support on level ground.</p>
