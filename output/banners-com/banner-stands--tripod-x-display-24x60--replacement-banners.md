<h2>Frequently Asked Questions - Replacement Banners</h2>

<h3>What material should I choose for my replacement banner?</h3>
<p>Standard 13oz vinyl works well for most applications. Choose Premium Stay-Flat 14oz vinyl for high-visibility locations where presentation quality is critical, or Banner Soft Fabric for indoor applications requiring a premium appearance.</p>

<h3>How long will my replacement banner last?</h3>
<p>With proper care, vinyl replacement banners typically last 1-3 years for outdoor use and longer for indoor applications. Fabric banners are designed primarily for indoor use and repeated setup/takedown.</p>

<h3>Can I use the same mounting hardware with my replacement banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, all replacement banners are sized and finished to work with your existing Tripod X Display 24x60 hardware system.</p>

<h3>What's the fastest I can receive my replacement banner?</h3>
<p>Combine 1 Day Priority production with overnight shipping for delivery in 2 business days from artwork approval.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design assistance for replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer template customization tools and professional design services. Our design team can create custom artwork based on your specifications and reference materials.</p>

<h3>What file format should I use for uploaded artwork?</h3>
<p>PDF files work best, but we also accept AI, EPS, PSD, high-resolution JPG, and PNG files. Visit our artwork specifications page for detailed requirements.</p>
