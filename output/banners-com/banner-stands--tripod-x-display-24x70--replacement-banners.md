<h2>Tripod X Display Replacement Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Ensure optimal print quality for your replacement banner by following our artwork specifications and file preparation guidelines. Proper artwork preparation results in vibrant colors, sharp text, and professional presentation quality.</p>

<h3>File Specifications</h3>

<h4>Recommended File Formats</h4>
<ul>
<li>PDF (preferred for text and vector graphics)</li>
<li>High-resolution JPEG or PNG for photographic content</li>
<li>AI or EPS files from Adobe Illustrator</li>
<li>PSD files from Adobe Photoshop</li>
</ul>

<h4>Resolution Requirements</h4>
<p>For optimal print quality on your 24" x 70" replacement banner:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 100 DPI at actual print size</li>
<li>Recommended 150 DPI for best results</li>
<li>300 DPI for photographic content and detailed graphics</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Considerations</h3>

<h4>Safe Area Guidelines</h4>
<p>Keep important text and graphics at least 2 inches from all banner edges to account for grommets and finishing. This ensures critical information remains visible and properly positioned on your tripod X display frame.</p>

<h4>Color Management</h4>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB colors may shift during printing, so convert all RGB elements to CMYK before final file submission.</p>

<h4>Text Readability</h4>
<p>Ensure text remains readable from typical viewing distances:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 24-point font size for body text</li>
<li>48-point or larger for headlines and important information</li>
<li>High contrast between text and background colors</li>
<li>Sans-serif fonts for better readability at distance</li>
</ul>

<h3>File Submission Options</h3>
<p>Upload your print-ready artwork files during the ordering process, or work with our design team to create custom artwork for your replacement banner. Our designers can incorporate your existing brand elements, logos, and messaging into effective display graphics.</p>
