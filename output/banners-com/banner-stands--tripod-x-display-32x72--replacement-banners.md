<h2>Artwork Requirements for Tripod X Display Banner Printing</h2>

<p>Professional printing results require proper artwork preparation and file specifications for optimal replacement banner quality.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Accept high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, and PSD files with embedded fonts and linked images. JPEG and PNG files acceptable for simple designs.</p>

<h3>Resolution Specifications</h3>
<p>Minimum 150 DPI at full size recommended for sharp printing results. Higher resolution files produce superior print quality for detailed graphics.</p>

<h3>Design Area Guidelines</h3>
<p>Include 0.5" bleed area beyond finished banner dimensions to ensure complete coverage. Keep important text and graphics 1" from banner edges.</p>

<h3>Color Profile Standards</h3>
<p>CMYK color mode recommended for accurate color reproduction. RGB files converted during processing but may result in color variations.</p>

<h3>Design Services Available</h3>
<p>Professional design team available for custom banner creation, template customization, and artwork optimization for printing requirements.</p>
