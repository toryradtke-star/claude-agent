<h2>Replacement Banner Artwork Requirements for Quality Printing</h2>

<p>Ensure your replacement banner prints with sharp, vibrant results by following our artwork specifications.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit artwork in PDF, AI, EPS, or high-resolution JPEG format. Vector files (AI, EPS) provide the best results for text and logos on replacement banners.</p>

<h3>Resolution and Size Specifications</h3>
<p>For 36x72 replacement banners, provide artwork at 150 DPI minimum. Larger files up to 300 DPI will produce even sharper results for detailed graphics.</p>

<h3>Color and Design Guidelines</h3>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. Allow for a 0.25" bleed area around all edges to ensure proper finishing of your replacement banner.</p>

<h3>Text and Font Requirements</h3>
<p>Keep important text at least 0.5" from edges and use fonts larger than 12pt for optimal readability on your replacement banner display.</p>

<p>Need help with artwork? Our design team can assist with file preparation or create a completely custom replacement banner design for your specific needs.</p>
