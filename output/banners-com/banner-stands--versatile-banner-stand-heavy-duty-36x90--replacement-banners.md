<h2>Replacement Banner Artwork Requirements for Printing</h2>

<p>Proper artwork preparation ensures optimal print quality and professional appearance for your replacement banner. Follow these specifications to achieve the best possible results for your indoor display graphics.</p>

<p><strong>File Requirements:</strong> Submit artwork in PDF, AI, EPS, or high-resolution TIFF format at actual print size. Minimum resolution of 150 DPI at full size ensures sharp text and clear graphics reproduction.</p>

<p><strong>Color Setup:</strong> Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files may result in color shifts during printing. Include Pantone color references for critical brand color matching requirements.</p>

<p><strong>Design Area:</strong> Keep important text and graphics at least 2 inches from all edges to account for finishing and mounting areas. This ensures critical information remains visible after installation on the banner stand frame.</p>

<p><strong>Font Guidelines:</strong> Convert all text to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution issues. Minimum recommended font size is 12 points for body text and 24 points for headlines to ensure readability from typical viewing distances.</p>

<p>Need design assistance? Our professional design team can create custom artwork or modify existing graphics to meet your replacement banner specifications and display requirements.</p>
