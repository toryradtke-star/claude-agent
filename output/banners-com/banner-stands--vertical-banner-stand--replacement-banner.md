<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>Will the replacement banner fit my existing banner stand?</h3>
<p>Yes, our replacement banners are precision-cut to standard banner stand dimensions. If you have a non-standard size, contact customer service at 320-391-5300 for custom sizing options.</p>

<h3>What material should I choose for my replacement banner?</h3>
<p>Choose 13oz vinyl for standard indoor displays and office environments. Select 18oz heavy-duty vinyl for high-traffic areas, retail locations, or applications requiring enhanced durability.</p>

<h3>How long does production take for replacement banners?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 1-5 business days depending on your selected option. Priority production (1 business day) is available for urgent replacement needs.</p>

<h3>Can I see a proof before my replacement banner is printed?</h3>
<p>Yes, digital proofs are provided for all replacement banner orders. Production begins only after you approve your proof, ensuring accuracy and satisfaction.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept PDF, JPG, PNG, and AI file formats. Files should be high-resolution (150 DPI minimum) and sized to your banner dimensions for best print quality.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services if I don't have artwork?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer both customizable templates and professional design services. Our graphic designers can create custom artwork based on your specifications and requirements.</p>

<h3>How will my replacement banner be shipped?</h3>
<p>Replacement banners ship rolled in protective tubes via ground shipping. Multiple delivery speeds available from economy (5-10 days) to priority (1-3 days) options.</p>

<h3>What is your minimum order requirement?</h3>
<p>Our minimum order is $35.00 in product. Most replacement banners meet this requirement, but contact customer service if you have questions about minimum orders.</p>
