<h2>Sign Topper Artwork Requirements for Full-Color Printing</h2>

<h3>Preferred File Formats</h3>

<p>For optimal print quality on your Sign Topper Replacement, submit artwork as vector-based .eps files converted to outlines. We also accept high-resolution .pdf, .tif, and .jpg formats that meet our resolution specifications.</p>

<h3>Resolution Requirements</h3>

<ul>
<li>Minimum 150 DPI at actual print size for acceptable quality</li>
<li>300 DPI at actual print size recommended for best results</li>
<li>Vector files (.eps, .ai) preferred for scalability and sharpness</li>
<li>Avoid low-resolution web images or screenshots</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Specifications</h3>

<p>Include a 1/8" bleed area beyond your finished sign topper dimensions to ensure full coverage during the cutting process. Keep all critical text and graphics at least 1/4" from the finished edge to prevent trimming.</p>

<h3>Professional Design Services</h3>

<p>If you don't have access to high-resolution artwork or need assistance with design creation, our professional graphic designers can help develop your Sign Topper Replacement graphics from concept to completion.</p>

<h2>Sign Topper Replacement FAQ</h2>

<h3>What material are Sign Topper Replacements made from?</h3>
<p>Sign Topper Replacements are printed on 4mm corrugated plastic (Coroplast), a lightweight, waterproof material with hollow flutes that provide excellent durability for both indoor and outdoor applications.</p>

<h3>How long will my sign topper last outdoors?</h3>
<p>When used outdoors, Sign Topper Replacements typically last up to 2 years under normal weather conditions. The corrugated plastic material resists fading, cracking, and moisture damage while maintaining graphic clarity.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for sign topper replacements?</h3>
<p>Sign toppers are available in multiple height and width combinations from 12" to 24" to match your existing vertical banner stand specifications. Custom sizes may be available upon request.</p>

<h3>Can I get a proof before my sign topper goes into production?</h3>
<p>Yes, digital proofs are available for review before production begins. For color-critical projects, we also offer our Color Match Proof System to ensure precise color accuracy.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for artwork?</h3>
<p>We prefer vector-based .eps files converted to outlines for best quality. We also accept high-resolution .pdf, .tif, and .jpg formats. Minimum 150 DPI required, 300 DPI recommended.</p>

<h3>How quickly can I get my Sign Topper Replacement?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3-5 business days after artwork approval, plus shipping time. Rush production options may be available for urgent needs.</p>

<h3>Are Sign Topper Replacements printed on both sides?</h3>
<p>No, sign toppers are printed single-sided with your graphics on the front display surface only. This keeps costs efficient while providing maximum visual impact.</p>

<h3>Can you help design my sign topper if I don't have artwork?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional graphic designers can create custom artwork for your Sign Topper Replacement, or you can choose from our collection of professionally designed templates.</p>

<h3>Will my sign topper fit any vertical banner stand?</h3>
<p>Sign toppers are designed specifically for vertical banner stand systems. Choose the size that matches your stand's specifications for proper fit and professional appearance.</p>

<h3>What shipping options are available?</h3>
<p>Standard UPS Ground shipping is available for most orders. Expedited options including 2-Day and Next Day Air are available for urgent delivery needs.</p>
