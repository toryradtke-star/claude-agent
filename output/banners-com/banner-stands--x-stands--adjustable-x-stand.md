<h2>X Banner Stand Installation & Setup</h2>

<h3>How do I set up the Adjustable X Banner Stand?</h3>
<p>The X banner stand sets up without tools by extending the telescoping arms and attaching the banner to the provided connection points. Adjust arm length to achieve proper banner tension and display size.</p>

<h3>What banner sizes work with the Adjustable X Banner Stand?</h3>
<p>The stand accommodates banners from 20" to 35" wide and 45" to 72" high. Standard production size is 72" x 35" for maximum display visibility.</p>

<h3>Can I use the X banner stand outdoors?</h3>
<p>Yes, when ordered with weather-resistant vinyl material. The 13 oz vinyl and 14 oz no-curl vinyl options are rated for outdoor use. Indoor fabric materials should be used for indoor applications only.</p>

<h3>How compact is the stand for transport?</h3>
<p>The telescoping arms collapse for compact storage and transport. The complete system fits easily in most vehicles for transport between trade show venues and business locations.</p>

<h3>Can I order replacement banners for my existing stand?</h3>
<p>Yes, replacement banners are available for existing X banner stand hardware. Contact customer service with your stand specifications to ensure proper sizing and compatibility.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for custom printing?</h3>
<p>We accept .eps, .pdf, .jpg, and other high-resolution file formats. Vector files with outlined fonts provide the best print quality for large format X banner stand displays.</p>
