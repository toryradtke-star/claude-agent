## About Replacement Material:

Vinyl Replacements are printed on high quality 13oz or 15oz scrim material. The scrim vinyl is a woven fiber inside the material that gives the banner extra strength.

Fabric Replacement - Our Banner Soft material is our fabric option for banner stands. The material offers a matte finish with vibrant print colors. The edges of the fabric will be cut with a hot knife to prevent fraying when rolled in and out of the retractor. We recommend you be very careful when retracting the banner as some fraying may still happen.

[**Click here to request a sample packet of materials**](https://www.banners.com/sample-packet-request)

#### Vinyl Printing:

Vinyl replacements are digitally printed in full color. You can select one of our banner templates to customize in the online designer with our backgrounds, clip-art, and text. You can also upload your own images. 

#### Vinyl Sizes:

Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed.  Vinyl is a temperature sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Please note our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (ex. A 5 ft banner could finish with a 2.5 inch variance).   If your banner size needs to be precise, please ask your Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.

If you need a specific size print that is not listed as an option in the drop-down menu, please [request a quote](https://www.banners.com/vinyl-banner-quote) or contact a customer service representative. 1-866-419-SIGN or [\[email protected\]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#57243225213e343217353639393225247934383a)

## Shipping Information

This product will be produced in our Minnesota or Las Vegas facilities, whichever is more beneficial to the customer's experience based on customer location and product specifications.

Please specify which shipping method you would like when you place your order. We offer a 1-3 day ground shipment to 90% of the United States. [Visit our shipping information page](https://www.banners.com/shipping-info) for more information about our standard and oversized vinyl banner shipping times. If you have any questions about shipping, please contact customer service prior to ordering. Phone: 320-391-5300 

We offer the following shipping methods:

*   Economy (5-10 Business Days)
*   Standard (3-5 Business Days)
*   Three Day (3 Business Days)
*   Two Day (2 Business Days)
*   Overnight (1 Business Day)
*   Freight

Shipping prices are determined by the weight of the package and the destination. Additional shipping charges apply to large orders and over-sized packages. Please note, due to UPS shipping restrictions, banners that exceed 8' in both directions will generally be folded for shipping (billboards, large backdrops etc.)

If you are located outside of the Continental United States, please contact us for a shipping quote.

**You will receive a tracking number once your package has been shipped.**

After products have shipped, all shipping company terms and conditions apply for lost or delayed packages. We will not refund the banner cost if any shipping company has a delay in your package. We recommend ordering your banners early.

Delays because of Acts of God or anything beyond our control will not result in shipping refunds. If the product is damaged in shipping, damaged goods must be returned, and then a replacement product will be sent.

Lost packages will be replaced after the shipping company confirms loss. This generally takes 7-10 business days after initial scheduled delivery.

Please order your banners early as we cannot control delivery after your product leaves our production facilities.

**International shipping Notes:**

For international shipments, customers must provide both shipping labels and customs documentation. Currently, we only accept international shipping labels from USPS and UPS. For more details, please contact our customer service team.

**Economy Shipping**

If choosing economy shipping please add in additional postal information such as P.O. Box in the "Address Two" field on the checkout page to help ensure delivery.

Using economy shipping can also add an additional 5 - 10 days, depending on where the package is being shipped to.
