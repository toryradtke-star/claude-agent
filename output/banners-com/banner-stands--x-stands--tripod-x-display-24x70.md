<p>Our premium 13 oz vinyl banner material serves as the standard choice for professional banner stand applications. This durable material works excellently for both indoor and outdoor display environments, delivering vibrant colors and sharp graphics that attract attention.</p>

<p>Vinyl material responds to temperature changes during printing and finishing processes. <h2>Artwork Requirements for Tripod X Banner Stand Printing</h2>

<h3>Professional Artwork Guidelines for Banner Stand Graphics</h3>
<p>Vector-based .eps files with fonts converted to outlines provide the best printing results for banner stands. We also accept high-resolution .pdf or .jpg files that meet our quality specifications.</p>

<p>Our professional graphic designers can evaluate your artwork files and recommend improvements if needed to ensure optimal print quality for your banner stand display.</p>

<h3>Order Proof Options for Custom Banner Stand Artwork</h3>
<p>Request an order proof to verify all information appears correctly before production begins. Order proofs allow you to confirm colors, text, sizing, and overall design layout match your expectations.</p>

<p>Digital proofs are available for most orders and help prevent costly reprints due to artwork or information errors.</p>

<h3>Color Match Proof System for Accurate Banner Printing</h3>
<p>Our color matching system ensures your banner colors reproduce accurately from screen to final printed banner. This is particularly important for corporate branding and logos that require precise color representation.</p>

<h3>File Preparation Tips</h3>
<ul>
<li>Design dimensions: 24" width x 70" height</li>
<li>Resolution: 150 DPI minimum for quality printing</li>
<li>Color mode: CMYK for accurate color reproduction</li>
<li>Bleed area: Include 0.125" bleed on all sides</li>
<li>Text safety: Keep important text 0.25" from edges</li>
</ul>

<p>Need design assistance? Our professional design team can create custom artwork for your banner stand project.</p>

<p>While the 13 oz vinyl material is rated for outdoor use, the Tripod X Display is designed primarily for indoor applications. The tripod base may not provide adequate stability in windy outdoor conditions.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for custom artwork?</h3>
<p>We prefer vector-based .eps files with outlined fonts, but also accept high-resolution .pdf and .jpg files. Our design team can review your files and recommend improvements if needed.</p>

<h3>How accurate are the banner dimensions?</h3>
<p>Vinyl banners may vary up to 1/2 inch per linear foot due to temperature-sensitive material properties. For applications requiring precise dimensions, please discuss your requirements with our customer service team.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for banner stand graphics?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional design team can create custom artwork for your banner stand, or you can choose from our extensive template gallery and customize with your information.</p>

<h3>What shipping options are available?</h3>
<p>We offer ground shipping, expedited delivery, and overnight shipping when available. Most banner stands ship via ground service unless faster delivery is requested.</p>

<h3>Can I order multiple banner stands with different graphics?</h3>
