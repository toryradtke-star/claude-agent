<h3>What material is best for outdoor X banner stand use?</h3>
<p>13 oz vinyl material is recommended for outdoor applications due to its weather resistance and durability. This material maintains color stability and structural integrity in various weather conditions.</p>

<h3>How portable are X banner stands for event use?</h3>
<p>X banner stands are designed for portability with compact folding hardware and lightweight construction. The complete system can be transported in a standard carrying case for easy event transportation.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse my X banner stand with different graphics?</h3>
<p>Yes, X banner stands are designed for repeated use with different banner graphics. Simply order replacement banners when you need to update your display messaging or graphics.</p>

<h3>What artwork resolution is required for 75" x 40" banners?</h3>
<p>300 DPI resolution at actual print size provides optimal print quality. Lower resolution files may be acceptable depending on viewing distance and graphic content.</p>

<h3>Are setup instructions included with X banner stands?</h3>
