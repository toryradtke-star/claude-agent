<li>Vector files preferred for religious symbols and text elements</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Color Specifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>CMYK color mode for accurate baptism banner printing</li>
<li>RGB files acceptable but may experience slight color shifts</li>

<h2>Baptism Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What size baptism banner is best for church sanctuary display?</h3>
<p>For most church sanctuaries, banners between 3'x8' to 4'x10' provide excellent visibility without overwhelming the sacred space. Consider your church's architectural scale and viewing distances when selecting your baptism banner size.</p>

<h3>Can you print religious symbols and biblical text on baptism banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can print any religious imagery, biblical verses, and sacred symbols on your baptism banner. Our high-resolution printing process accurately reproduces religious artwork and ensures text clarity for church displays.</p>

<h3>What material works best for outdoor baptism ceremonies?</h3>
<p>For outdoor baptisms, we recommend 18oz heavy-duty vinyl for durability or mesh vinyl for windy conditions. Both materials withstand weather exposure while maintaining print quality throughout your outdoor church celebration.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order my baptism banner?</h3>
<p>We recommend placing your baptism banner order at least 2-3 weeks before your church celebration to allow for design, production, and shipping time. Rush options are available for last-minute baptism announcements.</p>

<h3>Can I include multiple names on one baptism banner?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! We can design baptism banners featuring multiple candidates' names for group baptisms or baptism Sunday celebrations. Our design team can help arrange text and imagery to accommodate multiple names beautifully.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for baptism banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our experienced design team can create custom baptism banner artwork incorporating your church's branding, religious imagery, and celebration details. Simply provide your requirements and reference materials.</p>

<h3>What hanging options work best in church sanctuaries?</h3>
<p>Grommets with rope or chain work well for most church installations. Pole pockets are ideal for permanent mounting systems. We can recommend the best finishing option based on your church's specific installation requirements.</p>

<h3>Can baptism banners be reused for multiple celebrations?</h3>
<p>Yes, banners with general baptism messaging (without specific names or dates) can be reused for multiple church celebrations. Consider timeless religious designs that complement various baptism ceremonies throughout the year.</p>
