<h3>Resolution Specifications</h3>
<p>Minimum 150 DPI at final print size for crisp text and graphics. Higher resolution recommended for detailed photographs and complex artwork elements.</p>

<h3>Color Profile Guidelines</h3>
<p>CMYK color mode preferred for accurate color reproduction. RGB files accepted but may experience slight color variations in final print.</p>

<h3>Design Safety Margins</h3>
<p>Keep important text and graphics at least 2 inches from banner edges. Allows for finishing processes and prevents important elements from being obscured by hems or grommets.</p>

<h3>Typography Recommendations</h3>
<p>Bold fonts work best for outdoor birthday banners. Minimum recommended text size is 1 inch tall for standard viewing distances.</p>

<h3>Proof Process</h3>
<p>Digital proof provided before production begins. Color match proofs available for critical color accuracy requirements.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What are your custom birthday banners made from, and are they outdoor-safe?</h3>
<p>Our birthday banners are made from premium vinyl materials including 13 oz matte vinyl, 13 oz gloss vinyl, and 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl. All vinyl materials are outdoor-safe and weather-resistant, designed to withstand rain, wind, and UV exposure for extended outdoor display.</p>

<h3>What birthday banner material options do you offer?</h3>
<p>We offer multiple material options: 13 oz matte vinyl (most popular), 13 oz gloss vinyl (high-shine finish), 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl (maximum durability), mesh vinyl (wind-resistant), fabric banners (premium indoor appearance), and adhesive vinyl (self-stick applications).</p>

<h3>What finishing options are available (grommets, pole pockets, reinforcement)?</h3>
<p>Professional finishing options include heat-welded hems with brass grommets, pole pockets (top, bottom, or sides), reinforced corners for stress points, and reinforced hems for added durability. All finishing work uses commercial-grade equipment for long-lasting results.</p>

<h3>Do birthday banners come with grommets by default?</h3>
<p>Grommets are available as a finishing option but not included by default. You can select grommets during ordering for rope or bungee cord installation, or choose alternative finishing like pole pockets for hardware-free mounting.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available, can I order a custom size, and is there a minimum order?</h3>
<p>We offer standard banner sizes from 2x3 feet to large format options, plus custom sizes to meet specific requirements. No minimum order required - we accommodate single birthday banners or large quantity orders with equal attention to quality.</p>

<h3>How precise will the finished banner size be?</h3>
<p>Vinyl is temperature-sensitive, so size variance up to 1/2 inch per linear foot is normal due to heat application during printing and finishing. If precise sizing is critical, please discuss your specific application with our customer service team.</p>

<h3>How do I design my custom birthday banner, and what files work best?</h3>
<p>Three design options available: use our online designer with free templates, upload your own artwork, or request professional design services. Best file formats are PDF, EPS, AI, or high-resolution JPG/PNG. Minimum 150 DPI resolution recommended.</p>

<h3>Can I request a proof before printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, digital proofs are provided before production begins. Color match proofs available for critical color accuracy requirements. Production starts only after you approve the proof.</p>

<h3>How do I clean and store my birthday banner so I can reuse it?</h3>
<p>Clean with mild soap and water, then allow to dry completely. Store rolled (not folded) in a cool, dry location. Proper care allows vinyl birthday banners to be reused for multiple celebrations while maintaining appearance and durability.</p>
