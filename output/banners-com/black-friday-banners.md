<h2>Black Friday Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Professional artwork specifications to ensure your Black Friday banner prints with maximum visual impact and promotional effectiveness.</p>

<p><strong>File Resolution Requirements</strong></p>
<p>Submit artwork at 150 DPI at actual print size for optimal Black Friday banner quality. High-resolution images ensure crisp text and graphics that grab customer attention from a distance.</p>

<p><strong>Accepted File Formats</strong></p>
<p>PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, JPG, and PNG files accepted. Vector formats (AI, EPS, PDF) preferred for text and logos to maintain sharpness at large Black Friday banner sizes.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode Specifications</strong></p>
<p>Submit artwork in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be automatically converted, which may result in slight color shifts in your Black Friday promotional colors.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safety Margins</strong></p>
<p>Include 2-inch bleed on all sides and keep important text/graphics 4 inches from finished edges. This ensures professional appearance and prevents content from being cut off during finishing.</p>

<p><strong>Font and Text Guidelines</strong></p>
<p>Minimum text size of 2 inches tall for viewing distances beyond 20 feet. Bold, high-contrast fonts recommended for maximum Black Friday promotional impact and readability.</p>

<p><strong>Design Assistance Available</strong></p>
<p>Our graphic design team can create custom Black Friday banner designs or modify existing artwork to meet print specifications. Design services available for additional fee.</p>

<p><strong>Artwork Review Process</strong></p>
