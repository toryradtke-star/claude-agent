<h2>Career Fair Banner Questions & Support</h2>

<h3>Are your career fair banners printed in full color?</h3>
<p>Yes, all career fair banners are printed in vibrant full color using professional large-format printers. This ensures your company branding, logos, and recruiting messages display clearly and professionally at job fair events.</p>

<h3>Do you offer career fair banners with wind slits?</h3>
<p>Yes, wind slits can be added to career fair banners for outdoor recruiting events or venues with significant air circulation. This finishing option helps prevent banner movement and potential damage while maintaining professional appearance.</p>

<h3>How do I clean my career fair display?</h3>
<p>Career fair banners can be cleaned with a damp cloth and mild soap solution. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that might damage the vinyl surface or printed graphics. Allow the banner to dry completely before storing.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to store my career fair banner?</h3>
<p>Store your career fair banner by rolling it loosely around a tube or laying it flat in a clean, dry location. Avoid folding when possible to prevent crease lines. Keep stored banners away from extreme temperatures and direct sunlight.</p>

<h3>What are some common sizes for career fair banners?</h3>
<p>Popular career fair banner sizes include 3'x6', 4'x8', 3'x10', and 4'x10' for booth displays. Larger formats like 6'x10' or 8'x12' work well for prominent job fair positioning. We can create custom sizes to fit your specific recruiting display needs.</p>
