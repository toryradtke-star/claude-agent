<h2>Celebrate Recovery Banner Artwork Requirements for Printing</h2>

<p>Proper artwork preparation ensures your Celebrate Recovery banners print with crisp text, vibrant colors, and professional appearance. Follow these guidelines to achieve the best results for your recovery program banners.</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong></p>
<p>Submit Celebrate Recovery banner artwork as high-resolution PDF, AI, or EPS files. These vector formats maintain image quality at any size and ensure text remains sharp when printed on large format banners.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Specifications</strong></p>
<p>Raster images (photos, logos) within your Celebrate Recovery banner design should be at least 150 DPI at the final print size. Higher resolution images produce better results for detailed graphics and photographs.</p>

<p><strong>Color Profile Guidelines</strong></p>
<p>Design Celebrate Recovery banners using CMYK color mode rather than RGB to ensure accurate color reproduction during printing. Bright colors work especially well for recovery program visibility and engagement.</p>

<p><strong>Text Legibility Standards</strong></p>
<p>Use fonts that are easy to read from typical viewing distances for your Celebrate Recovery banner installation. Minimum text height recommendations vary based on viewing distance and banner size.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safety Areas</strong></p>
<p>Include 0.125" bleed area around your Celebrate Recovery banner design and keep important text and graphics at least 0.25" from the edges to account for finishing and trimming variations.</p>

<p><strong>Design Review Process</strong></p>
<p>Our graphic design team reviews all Celebrate Recovery banner artwork for print quality and technical requirements. We'll contact you if any adjustments are needed before production begins.</p>

<p>If you need design assistance for your Celebrate Recovery banner, our in-house design team can help create professional layouts that effectively communicate your program messaging.</p>
