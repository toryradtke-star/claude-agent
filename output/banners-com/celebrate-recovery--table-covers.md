# Celebrate Recovery Table Covers

### Promote your Celebrate  Recovery Chapter with a Table Cover from Banners.com!

Table Covers will make your Celebrate Recovery booth like stand out! We have full color table covers, blank table throws, and table runners. All of our table covers are printed on fabric materials using a direct dye sublimation printing process to create an eye popping display.

### Full Color Table Colors

As the name implies, there are no limits to the number of colors that you can have on your full color table cover. You can add your Celebrate Recovery Chapter's logo, images, graphics and text to customize the table cover.

The full color table covers are printed on a poplin fabric material that is fire rated and machine washable. We currently offer four types sizes of full color table covers:

*   6 foot : 3 Sided
*   6 foot : 4 Sided
*   8 foot : 3 Sided
*   8 foot : 4 Sided

To order full color table covers, contact a customer service representative 320-965-9300 or email [\[email protected\]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#ee9d8b9c98878d8bae8c8f80808b9c9dc08d8183)

### [Blank Throws](https://www.banners.com/table-covers/blank-throws)

Blank throw table covers are a quick and easy way to make your Celebrate Recovery booth stand out. They work great with our table runners. The blank throws are printed on a Polyester Twill Fabric.

Blank throws come in a variety of colors including: White, Ivory, Yellow, Red, Burgundy, Purple, Kelly Green, Blueberry, Royal Blue, Hunter Green, Gray, Black, Charcoal Gray, Beige, Orange, Lime and Magenta.

### [Table  Runners](https://www.banners.com/table-covers/table-runners)

Table runners are a great addition to the blank throws. They are smaller than the full color table cover and sit on top of the blank throw. The advantage of the table runners are that they can easily be traded out for different events without hurting your wallet.

Table runners are printed on the poplin fabric material. You can customize the table runners with your Celebrate Recovery logo and chapter information, as well as unlimited colors, graphics and text.

We have two table runner sizes to choose from. You can get started on your table runner order below by selecting your options:
