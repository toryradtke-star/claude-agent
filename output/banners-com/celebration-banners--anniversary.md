<h2>Anniversary Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Design specifications to ensure your anniversary banner prints with professional quality:</p>

<p><strong>File Formats</strong> - Acceptable formats include PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, JPG, and PNG. Vector formats (AI, EPS) provide the best quality for anniversary banner printing.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Requirements</strong> - Minimum 150 DPI at actual print size. Higher resolution files ensure crisp text and images on your anniversary banner.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode</strong> - CMYK color mode preferred for accurate color reproduction. RGB files are acceptable but may result in color variations.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safety Areas</strong> - Include 2-inch bleed area around your anniversary banner design. Keep important text and graphics 4 inches from all edges.</p>

<p><strong>Text Considerations</strong> - Minimum 1-inch text height for outdoor viewing. Sans-serif fonts generally provide better readability on anniversary banners.</p>

<p><strong>Design Services</strong> - Our in-house graphic designers can create or modify artwork for your anniversary celebration. Custom design services available for unique anniversary banner requirements.</p>

<p>Our design team will review your artwork and contact you if any adjustments are needed for optimal printing results on your anniversary banner.</p>
