<h2>Artwork Requirements for Church Banner Printing</h2>

<p>To ensure your church banner prints with exceptional quality, please follow these artwork guidelines:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li>High-resolution PDF files (300 DPI minimum)</li>
<li>Adobe Illustrator files (.AI)</li>
<li>High-resolution JPG or PNG images</li>
<li>Vector graphics preferred for text and logos</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Specifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>Include 2-inch bleed area around all edges</li>
<li>Keep important text and images 3 inches from edges</li>
<li>Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction</li>
<li>Embed all fonts or convert text to outlines</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Assistance Available</h3>
<p>Our in-house graphic design team can help create or modify your church banner artwork. Upload reference images, logos, and provide specific instructions for custom design services.</p>

<h2>Church Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What is another name for a church banner?</h3>
<p>Church banners are also commonly referred to as worship banners, sanctuary banners, religious banners, praise banners, or liturgical banners. These terms all describe custom printed displays used to enhance worship services and religious messaging in church settings.</p>

<h3>What is the largest size banner I can order?</h3>
<p>We can produce church banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet using our standard online ordering system. For larger church banners or unique dimensions, contact our team for a custom quote. We regularly produce oversized banners for large sanctuaries and outdoor church installations.</p>

<h3>Can I get banner design help?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our in-house graphic design team specializes in creating compelling church banner designs. You can upload reference images, logos, and text, then provide instructions for our designers to create your custom church banner artwork. We understand religious messaging and can help create visually impactful designs for your worship community.</p>

<h3>Can I get a double-sided banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer double-sided church banners for installations where both sides will be visible. Double-sided banners are perfect for hanging in sanctuary centers, outdoor church displays, or anywhere your message needs to be seen from multiple directions. Contact us to discuss double-sided printing options and pricing for your church banner project.</p>
