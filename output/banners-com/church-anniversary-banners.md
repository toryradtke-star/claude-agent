<p>Professional artwork specifications ensure your church anniversary banner prints with exceptional quality and clarity:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit church anniversary banner artwork in high-resolution formats: PDF, AI, EPS, or high-resolution JPG/PNG files. Vector formats preferred for text and logos to ensure crisp anniversary messaging.</p>

<h3>Resolution Specifications</h3>
<p>Church anniversary banner images should be minimum 150 DPI at full print size. For large celebration banners, 100 DPI acceptable for photographic elements while maintaining 300 DPI for text.</p>

<h3>Color Guidelines</h3>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate church anniversary banner color reproduction. RGB files accepted but colors may shift during printing. Provide Pantone color references for specific church brand colors.</p>

<h3>Text and Font Considerations</h3>
<p>Ensure anniversary dates, church names, and celebration text are easily readable from intended viewing distance. Minimum text size recommendations vary based on banner size and display location.</p>

<h2>Church Anniversary Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What size church anniversary banner should I order?</h3>
<p>Banner size depends on your display location and viewing distance. For sanctuary displays, 3'x8' to 4'x10' banners work well. For outdoor church property or large fellowship halls, consider 5'x15' or larger. Our team can help you determine the ideal size for your anniversary celebration space.</p>

<h3>How long will my church anniversary banner last?</h3>
<p>Indoor church anniversary banners can last several years with proper care. Outdoor banners typically last 1-3 years depending on weather exposure and material choice. Premium materials like 18oz vinyl offer extended durability for long-term anniversary displays.</p>

<h3>Can you help design our church anniversary banner?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our experienced graphic design team specializes in church anniversary banners and can help create a design that reflects your milestone celebration. We offer both design assistance for existing artwork and complete custom design services.</p>

<h3>What's the best material for outdoor church anniversary displays?</h3>
<p>For outdoor church anniversary banners, we recommend 13oz or 18oz vinyl with UV-resistant inks. Mesh material works well for windy locations. All outdoor materials include weather-resistant properties to maintain your celebration message throughout your event.</p>

<h3>Do you offer discounts for churches?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer tax-exempt pricing for qualifying religious organizations and volume discounts for multiple banner orders. Churches can also establish accounts for simplified ordering and net 30 payment terms.</p>

<h3>How quickly can you produce a church anniversary banner?</h3>
<p>Production times range from same-day rush service to 4-5 business days for economy pricing. Most church anniversary banners can be produced within 1-2 business days. Design work may require additional time for approval.</p>

<h3>What information should be included on a church anniversary banner?</h3>
<p>Common elements include church name, founding date, anniversary year (e.g., "Celebrating 50 Years"), celebration theme or verse, and event details. Our design team can help you organize information for maximum visual impact.</p>

<h3>Can you ship directly to our church?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! We ship nationwide directly to churches and religious organizations. Tracking information is provided for all orders, and we offer multiple shipping speeds to meet your anniversary celebration timeline.</p>
