<p>Submit print-ready artwork or work with our design team to create custom class reunion banners. Proper file preparation ensures optimal print quality and color accuracy for your reunion event.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements:</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Preferred Formats:</strong> PDF, AI, EPS, PSD (high-resolution)</li>
<li><strong>Accepted Formats:</strong> JPG, PNG, TIFF (300 DPI minimum)</li>
<li><strong>Vector Files:</strong> Scalable to any size without quality loss</li>
<li><strong>Avoid:</strong> Low-resolution web images, Word documents, PowerPoint files</li>
</ul>

<h3>Resolution & Size Specifications:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 150 DPI at actual print size</li>
<li>300 DPI recommended for optimal quality</li>
<li>Include 2-inch bleed area around design edges</li>
<li>Keep important text 3 inches from banner edges</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Guidelines:</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Color Mode:</strong> CMYK for print accuracy (avoid RGB)</li>
<li><strong>Text Size:</strong> Minimum 1-inch height for readability at distance</li>
<li><strong>Font Requirements:</strong> Outline fonts or include font files</li>
<li><strong>Image Quality:</strong> High-resolution photos prevent pixelation</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Services Available:</h3>
<p>Our graphic design team can create custom reunion banners from your concept, school logos, and text requirements. Design services include layout creation, color matching, and final file preparation.</p>

<h2>Class Reunion Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What size banner works best for class reunion events?</h3>
<p>Popular sizes for reunion banners include 3'x8' for entrance displays, 4'x6' for photo backdrops, and 2'x6' for table decorations. Consider your venue size and viewing distance when selecting banner dimensions.</p>

<h3>Can I use the same banner for indoor and outdoor reunion events?</h3>
<p>Yes, our 13oz vinyl material works well for both indoor and outdoor applications. For outdoor use, we recommend adding wind slits and using secure mounting methods to prevent weather damage.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order my reunion banner?</h3>
<p>We recommend ordering 2-3 weeks before your event to allow time for design review, production, and shipping. Rush orders are available with 1-day production for urgent needs.</p>

<h3>What information should I include on my class reunion banner?</h3>
<p>Typical reunion banners include school name, graduation year, reunion year (e.g., "50th Reunion"), dates, and welcome messages. School logos, colors, and mascots add visual appeal.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for reunion banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house graphic design team can create custom reunion banners from your specifications. Provide school information, preferred colors, and any existing logos for design development.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to hang a reunion banner outdoors?</h3>
<p>For outdoor installation, use grommet finishing with rope or bungee cords for secure mounting. Wind slits reduce wind stress, and proper anchoring prevents banner damage in weather conditions.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse my reunion banner for future events?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Our durable vinyl construction allows banners to be stored and reused for multiple reunion events, school functions, or anniversary celebrations with proper care.</p>

<h3>What file format should I use for my reunion banner artwork?</h3>
<p>PDF files work best for reunion banners. High-resolution JPG or PNG files (300 DPI) are also acceptable. Avoid using low-resolution web images or document files like Word or PowerPoint.</p>
