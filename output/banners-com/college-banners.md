<h2>College Banner Artwork Requirements for Print Production</h2>

<p>Follow these guidelines to ensure optimal print quality for your college banner:</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong><br>
Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats. JPEG and PNG files acceptable for photographic college banner designs.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Specifications</strong><br>
Minimum 150 DPI at actual college banner size. Higher resolution recommended for detailed graphics, text, and school logos.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode Settings</strong><br>
Submit artwork in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted and may result in color shifts.</p>

<p><strong>Design Area Guidelines:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Include 2-inch bleed area around college banner edges</li>
<li>Keep important text and logos 3 inches from banner edges</li>
<li>Account for finishing options in your design layout</li>
<li>Provide vector versions of school logos when possible</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Typography Considerations</strong><br>
Use bold, high-contrast fonts for outdoor college banner viewing. Minimum 3-inch letter height recommended for text visibility from 50+ feet.</p>

<p><strong>Design Services Available</strong><br>
Our graphic design team can create custom college banner artwork from your concepts, existing materials, or brand guidelines. Professional design ensures optimal print results for your campus display.</p>

<h2>College Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What materials work best for outdoor college banner installations?</h3>
<p>For outdoor campus use, we recommend 13oz or 18oz vinyl materials with UV-resistant properties. Mesh banner material is ideal for windy campus locations due to its wind-resistant construction.</p>

<h3>How long do college banners last outdoors on campus?</h3>
<p>With proper installation and quality materials, college banners can last 2-3 years outdoors. Indoor college banners have significantly longer lifespans when protected from weather elements.</p>

<h3>Can you match our school colors exactly on college banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can color-match your school's specific brand colors. Provide Pantone color codes or physical color samples for the most accurate college banner color reproduction.</p>

<h3>What's the largest size college banner you can produce?</h3>
<p>We can produce college banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet. Larger custom sizes may be available through special arrangement and custom freight shipping.</p>

<h3>Do you offer educational discounts for college banner orders?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide educational pricing for schools and universities. Bulk order discounts are also available for multiple college banner purchases.</p>

<h3>How should I prepare artwork for my college banner?</h3>
<p>Submit high-resolution files (minimum 150 DPI) in CMYK color mode. Include school logos in vector format when possible, and maintain 3-inch margins from banner edges for important text and graphics.</p>

<h3>Can college banners be installed on campus fences?</h3>
<p>Yes, college banners with hem and grommet finishing work well for fence installations. Consider mesh material for fence-mounted banners in windy campus locations.</p>

<h3>What's the fastest production time for rush college banner orders?</h3>
<p>Rush production is available in 1 business day for most college banner sizes and standard materials. Complex finishing options may require additional time.</p>
