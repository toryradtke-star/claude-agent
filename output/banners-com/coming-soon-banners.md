<p><strong>Resolution Standards:</strong> Raster images should be 150 DPI at final print size. For a 4'x8' Coming Soon banner, submit images at 720x1440 pixels minimum to ensure sharp printing quality.</p>

<p><strong>Safety Margins:</strong> Keep all important text, logos, and design elements at least 2 inches from the edges to account for finishing processes like hemming and grommets.</p>

<p><strong>Font Requirements:</strong> Convert all text to outlines/curves or embed fonts in your file. This prevents font substitution and ensures your Coming Soon message displays exactly as intended.</p>

<h2>Coming Soon Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>How can I ensure that my vinyl banner lasts as long as possible with outdoor weather conditions?</h3>
<p>To maximize your Coming Soon banner's lifespan, choose our 18oz heavy-duty vinyl material for extended installations. Ensure proper tensioning during installation to prevent flapping, and inspect grommets regularly for signs of wear. Store the banner indoors during severe weather when possible.</p>

<h3>How long do vinyl banners typically last?</h3>
<p>Coming Soon banners made with our premium vinyl materials typically last 6-12 months outdoors with proper installation and care. Indoor installations or covered outdoor locations can extend banner life to 2+ years. UV-resistant inks help prevent fading throughout the installation period.</p>

<h3>How can I choose the right size banner for the space that I have?</h3>
<p>Consider your viewing distance and mounting location when selecting Coming Soon banner size. For roadside visibility, we recommend minimum 3'x8' size with large, bold text. Storefront installations can use smaller sizes like 2'x6' or 3'x6' depending on available mounting space.</p>

<h3>What is the proper way to clean my banner?</h3>
<p>Clean your Coming Soon banner with mild soap and water using a soft cloth or sponge. Avoid abrasive cleaners or pressure washing which can damage the vinyl surface. Rinse thoroughly and allow to air dry completely before storage.</p>

<h3>What is the proper way to store my banner when not in use?</h3>
<p>Roll your Coming Soon banner loosely with the printed side facing outward to prevent ink transfer. Store in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight. Avoid folding when possible, as repeated folding can create permanent creases in the vinyl material.</p>

<h3>Will the colors on my screen match the banner colors exactly?</h3>
<p>Monitor displays can vary significantly from printed colors. We use calibrated printing equipment and CMYK color standards to ensure consistent results. For critical color matching on Coming Soon banners, we recommend requesting a color proof before final production.</p>

<h3>Do these banners come with the grommets needed for installation, or do I need to purchase those separately?</h3>
<p>All Coming Soon banners include reinforced grommets as standard finishing at no additional cost. Grommets are placed approximately every 2 feet around the perimeter for secure mounting with rope, zip ties, or bungee cords. Installation hardware is not included.</p>
