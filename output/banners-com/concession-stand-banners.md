<h2>Concession Stand Banner Finishing & Mounting Options</h2>

<p><strong>Professional Finishing Options</strong></p>

<p><strong>Grommets (Recommended)</strong><br>
Metal grommets placed every 2-3 feet along the edges provide secure mounting points for concession stand banner installation. Ideal for hanging from booth frames, tents, or permanent structures.</p>

<p><strong>Pole Pockets</strong><br>
Sewn pockets along the top and/or bottom edges allow for easy installation on banner stands or pole systems. Perfect for portable concession stand setups.</p>

<p><strong>Hemmed Edges</strong><br>
Clean, finished edges provide a professional appearance and prevent fraying. Reinforces banner durability for long-term concession stand use.</p>

<p><strong>Wind Slits</strong><br>
Strategic cuts in the banner material reduce wind resistance for outdoor concession stands. Helps prevent banner damage in windy conditions while maintaining visibility.</p>

<p><strong>Installation Considerations:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Consider your concession stand setup when choosing finishing options</li>
<li>Grommets work best for frame-mounted installations</li>
<li>Pole pockets are ideal for banner stand systems</li>
<li>Wind slits recommended for permanent outdoor installations</li>
</ul>
