<h2>Construction Fence Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>What material is best for construction fence banners?</h3>
<p>8oz PVC polyester mesh is ideal for construction fence applications. The mesh material provides 37% airflow to reduce wind resistance while maintaining durability for outdoor job site conditions.</p>

<h3>How do construction fence banners attach to fencing?</h3>
<p>Most construction fence banners use grommets every 2 feet for zip-tie attachment to chain link fencing. Pole pockets are available for banners that slide onto fence rails or mounting poles.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for construction fence banners?</h3>
<p>We offer sizes from 1'x1' up to 8'x50' to accommodate most construction fence applications. Custom sizes are available through our quote system for unique installations.</p>

<h3>How long do construction fence banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper installation, mesh construction banners typically last 6-12 months in outdoor conditions. Lifespan depends on weather exposure, wind conditions, and installation quality.</p>

<h3>Can construction fence banners be double-sided?</h3>
<p>Yes, double-sided construction banners are available. These feature two separate mesh banners sewn together with a blackout liner for opacity when viewed from either side.</p>

<h3>What's the minimum text size for construction fence banners?</h3>
<p>For optimal readability from typical viewing distances, text should be at least 1" tall. Consider your specific viewing distance when designing text and logo elements.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation services for construction fence banners?</h3>
<p>We provide the banners and finishing hardware. Installation is typically handled by your construction team or local sign installers familiar with your specific fence configuration.</p>

<h3>Can I get a rush order for urgent construction projects?</h3>
<p>Yes, 1-business day priority production is available for urgent construction site needs. Contact our customer service team to confirm availability for your specific order requirements.</p>
