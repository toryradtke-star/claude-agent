<p><strong>Resolution & Quality Standards</strong></p>
<p>Minimum 150 DPI resolution at actual banner size ensures sharp text and clear imagery. Higher resolution artwork produces superior results for detailed contractor logos and professional photography.</p>

<p><strong>Color Profile Specifications</strong></p>
<p>CMYK color mode provides accurate color reproduction for contractor banner printing. RGB files may experience color shifts during printing - convert to CMYK for consistent results matching your brand colors.</p>

<p><strong>Text & Logo Readability</strong></p>
<p>Minimum 1-inch text height recommended for standard viewing distances. Contractor business names, phone numbers, and key services should be clearly readable from typical job site viewing distances.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed & Safety Margins</strong></p>
<p>Include 0.125-inch bleed area around all edges for proper trimming. Keep critical text and logos at least 0.5 inches from banner edges to prevent trimming issues during contractor banner production.</p>

<p><strong>Design Review Process</strong></p>
<p>Our graphic design team reviews all contractor banner artwork for technical compliance and print optimization. We contact you immediately if adjustments are needed to ensure professional results for your contractor marketing.</p>

<h2>Contractor Banner Questions & Ordering Support</h2>

<h3>What materials work best for outdoor contractor banners?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl material with reinforced hemming provides the best durability for contractor banners in outdoor job site conditions. This material resists wind, rain, and UV exposure while maintaining professional appearance throughout extended display periods.</p>

<h3>How long do contractor banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Properly installed contractor banners typically maintain excellent appearance for 6-12 months in normal outdoor conditions. Factors affecting banner life include weather exposure, wind conditions, and installation method. Indoor contractor banners can last several years with proper care.</p>

<h3>Can I get custom sizing for my contractor banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer contractor banners in custom sizes from 1'x1' up to 8'x50' to fit any marketing application or job site requirement. Contact us for pricing on specialty dimensions or oversized contractor banners requiring freight shipping.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation hardware for contractor banners?</h3>
<p>Installation accessories including bungee cords, zip ties, and mounting hardware are available separately. Our customer service team can recommend the best installation method based on your contractor banner size and display location.</p>

<h3>Can I use my own contractor logo and branding?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! We encourage contractors to submit their existing logos, brand colors, and marketing materials. Our design team will incorporate your branding while optimizing the layout for maximum impact and readability on your contractor banner.</p>

<h3>What's the minimum order for contractor banners?</h3>
<p>No minimum order required - you can purchase a single contractor banner or multiple banners for different job sites. Volume pricing available for 10 or more identical contractor banners.</p>
