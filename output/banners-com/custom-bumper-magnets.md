<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>Will a bumper magnet stick to my vehicle?</h3>
<p>Bumper magnets stick only to steel vehicle surfaces. Many modern vehicles use aluminum or plastic body panels where magnets won't adhere. Test your vehicle's surface with a small magnet before ordering. Magnetic signs work best on older vehicles and commercial trucks with steel body panels.</p>

<h3>What material are your bumper magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Our bumper magnets use 30 mil magnetic material with protective lamination. This heavyweight magnetic stock provides strong adhesion to steel vehicle surfaces while maintaining flexibility for curved bumper installation. The laminated surface protects against UV damage, weather, and scratching during vehicle use.</p>

<h3>Can I upload my own artwork for a bumper magnet?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can upload vector files (.eps), high-resolution PDFs, or JPEG images for custom printing. Vector files with fonts converted to outlines produce the best results. If you need design assistance, our professional graphic design team can create vehicle advertising layouts using your logo and content.</p>

<h3>Can I choose different cut shapes for my bumper magnets?</h3>
<p>We offer three finishing options: rounded corners (recommended for highway driving), square cut for clean edges, and contour cut following your design shape. Rounded corners provide better aerodynamics and secure mounting for vehicles driven at highway speeds.</p>

<h3>Is there a minimum order quantity for bumper magnets?</h3>
<p>No minimum order quantity required. You can order single bumper magnets for personal use or large quantities for business fleet vehicles and promotional campaigns. Bulk pricing is available for larger vehicle advertising orders.</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk pricing?</h3>
<p>Yes, bulk pricing is available for large quantity orders. Contact our customer service team at (320) 391-5300 for volume pricing on fleet vehicle magnets, political campaign orders, or business promotional quantities.</p>

<h3>How long do bumper magnets last?</h3>
<p>With proper care and installation on compatible vehicle surfaces, bumper magnets can last several years. The laminated material protects against UV fading and weather damage. Longevity depends on driving conditions, vehicle maintenance, and proper installation on steel surfaces.</p>

<h3>Are bumper magnets safe for painted surfaces?</h3>
<p>Yes, magnetic vehicle signs are safe for painted steel surfaces when properly maintained. Remove magnets periodically for cleaning to prevent dirt buildup that could cause scratching. Clean both the magnet and vehicle surface before reapplication to maintain paint protection.</p>

<h3>Are bumper magnets okay to go through a car wash?</h3>
<p>Remove bumper magnets before automatic car washes to prevent damage or loss. High-pressure water and brushes can dislodge magnetic signs. Hand washing around magnetic signs is acceptable, but removal during car wash visits protects both the magnet and your vehicle's finish.</p>
