<h2>Business Magnet FAQ</h2>

<h3>What material are your business magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Our business magnets are made from 30 mil magnetic material with protective lamination. This thickness provides excellent holding power while remaining flexible enough to conform to vehicle surfaces and curved applications.</p>

<h3>Can I upload my own artwork for custom business magnets?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can upload your own artwork files. We accept vector files (.EPS, .AI), high-resolution PDFs, and image files (.JPG, .PNG) at 300 DPI. Our prepress team reviews all artwork to ensure optimal print quality before production.</p>

<h3>What cut options are available for custom business magnets?</h3>
<p>We offer three cut options: rounded corners (recommended for vehicles), square cut (standard applications), and custom contour cut (shaped to your design). Rounded corners help prevent wind lift during vehicle use.</p>

<h3>Can my business magnet be cut to the shape of my logo or design?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer custom contour cutting to match your logo or design shape. This creates a unique appearance and enhanced visual impact. Contact our customer service team to discuss your specific contour cutting requirements.</p>

<h3>What surfaces do business magnets stick to best?</h3>
<p>Business magnets adhere to ferrous (magnetic) metal surfaces including steel vehicle doors, refrigerators, filing cabinets, and steel building surfaces. They will not stick to aluminum, plastic, fiberglass, or non-magnetic materials.</p>

<h3>What are business magnets commonly used for?</h3>
<p>Business magnets are popular for vehicle advertising, trade show displays, refrigerator marketing, office signage, and promotional giveaways. They're ideal for contractors, real estate agents, delivery services, and any business needing removable advertising.</p>

<h3>Can I write on a business magnet?</h3>
<p>Our business magnets have a laminated surface that is not designed for writing. For the best appearance and durability, we recommend including all text and graphics in your printed design rather than adding handwritten information.</p>

<h3>How long do custom business magnets last?</h3>
<p>With proper care and application, business magnets can last several years outdoors. The laminated surface protects against UV rays and moisture. Lifespan depends on environmental conditions, surface preparation, and usage frequency.</p>
