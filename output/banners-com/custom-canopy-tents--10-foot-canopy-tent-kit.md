### Features

_Canopy Features:_

*   Constructed of durable, weather-resistant 600 denier polyester
*   Artwork is full-color dye sublimated on white canopy fabric
*   Crank-up canopy creates a taut, clean peak

_Frame Features:_

*   Constructed of 1.2 mm anondized aluminum
*   Features ABS glass-filled nylon joints
*   Hexagon shaped-legs at 1.75" in diameter
*   Legs feature plastic levers for pinch-free height adjustment

_Additional Features:_

*   Easily collapses to fit inside the included soft case with wheels
*   Can be secured to the ground with the stake kit (must be secured to prevent wind damage — failure to secure tent in high winds will void warranty)
*   Includes a rope and stake kit for easy securing to the ground on windy days

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### Can I use multiple colors in the canopy design?

Yes! We print on the fabric using a process called full color dye-sublimation, which saturates the fabric rather than printing onto the fabric surface. This allows for brighter coloring and more crisp detailing. You can use as many colors and photos as you like in your design.

#### What are my shipping options for this canopy tent?

Because this is an oversized item, we only offer UPS Ground Shipping on this product.

#### Is this tent travel-friendly?

Yes! This tent is meant for traveling and outdoor use, which makes it perfect for businesses looking to promote their products and for individuals looking for a unique travel solution to avoid rain and harmful sun rays.

#### What does the full kit include?

The full kit includes the [](https://www.banners.com/custom-canopy-tents/10-foot-aluminum-canopy-tent-frame)[hardware (frame)](https://www.banners.com/custom-canopy-tents/10-foot-aluminum-canopy-tent-frame), [the canopy](https://www.banners.com/custom-canopy-tents/10-foot-fabric-canopy-tent-covers), a rope and stake kit, and the case. Other accessories (like sandbags) are are available for purchase.

#### Can this tent be secured to the ground?

Yes! This canopy tent can be secured to the ground in the case of windy weather or strong winds. Your product comes with a handy dandy rope and stake kit that you can use to secure it to the ground on windy days. If you are in a place (like a cement pad or other location) where you are unable to secure your tent with stakes, we recommend our [sandbag](https://www.banners.com/custom-canopy-tents/sandbags-for-canopy-tents) products.

#### What is the size of this tent when collapsed?

The collapsed and storage dimensions of this custom tent is approximately 9.12" W x 62.75" H x 9.12" D when stored correctly.

#### What is the fabric material on this canopy made from?

This canopy is made from 600 Denier Polyester.

#### What type of metal is the frame for this product made from?

This is made from ABS Nylon and steel.

#### Does this customized tent meet FR Rating Requirements?

Yes, this custom tent meets the FR Rating Requirements.

“FR” refers to the fire rating standards that our products must meet in order to ensure that they are safe for our customers to use if ever in a situation that a fire may occur. 

Our 10’ Canopy Tent products have met these requirements, and are fire-resistant — as well as durable — in order to ensure that it will protect you from the harmful sun rays, falling rain, and outdoor elements.

#### How do I clean and maintain a canopy tent?

Regular maintenance involves cleaning the canopy fabric with mild soap, water, and a soft brush, followed by thorough drying before storage. It's essential to inspect the tent for any damage or wear and tear, such as bent frames or tears, and address them promptly to ensure the longevity of the tent.

#### How long does it take to set up a canopy tent?

The time required for setting up a canopy tent depends on factors such as size, complexity, and the number of people assisting.

Generally, a 10' x 10' canopy tent can be set up by two people within 10 to 20 minutes.
