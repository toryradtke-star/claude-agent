<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What surfaces do car magnets stick to?</h3>
<p>Car magnets adhere to ferrous (magnetic-receptive) metal surfaces such as steel vehicle doors, hoods, and trunk areas. They will not stick to aluminum body panels, plastic parts, or non-ferrous metals. Most vehicle doors provide excellent magnetic adhesion.</p>

<h3>Do car magnets work on all vehicles?</h3>
<p>Car magnets work on vehicles with steel body panels. Many modern vehicles use aluminum or plastic body components where magnets won't adhere. Test a small magnet on your intended surface before ordering to ensure compatibility.</p>

<h3>What materials are your car magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Our car magnets use 30 mil thick magnetic material with protective lamination included. This thickness provides strong adhesion while remaining flexible for easy application and removal. The lamination protects against UV damage and weather exposure.</p>

<h3>Are car magnets weather-resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes, our car magnets include protective lamination that provides excellent weather resistance, UV protection, and durability for long-term outdoor use. They perform well in rain, snow, and extreme temperatures.</p>

<h3>Can I upload my own artwork for a car magnet?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! You can upload your own print-ready artwork during the ordering process. We accept vector files (.eps, .ai) and high-resolution raster files (.pdf, .jpg, .png). Our prepress team reviews all files to ensure optimal print quality.</p>

<h3>Is there a minimum order quantity for car magnets?</h3>
<p>No minimum order quantity is required. You can order a single car magnet or hundreds depending on your needs. Pricing may vary based on quantity ordered.</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk discounts?</h3>
<p>Yes, quantity discounts are available for larger orders. Contact our customer service team at (320) 391-5300 to discuss bulk pricing options for your specific project requirements.</p>

<h3>How long do car magnets last?</h3>
<p>With proper care and installation, our car magnets can last several years. The protective lamination helps prevent fading and wear. Lifespan depends on usage conditions, storage, and maintenance practices.</p>

<h3>Can I leave my car magnet on in the car wash?</h3>
<p>We recommend removing car magnets before entering automatic car washes. High-pressure water and brushes can cause magnets to shift or come off. Hand washing around magnets is acceptable, or remove them during washing.</p>

<h3>Can I write on a car magnet?</h3>
<p>The laminated surface is not designed for writing. For variable information, consider ordering multiple magnets with different text, or look into dry-erase magnetic products specifically designed for writing applications.</p>
