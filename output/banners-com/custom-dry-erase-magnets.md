<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What are your custom dry erase magnets made of?</h3>
<p>Our dry erase magnets are made with 30 mil magnetic material and include protective lamination. This combination provides strong magnetic adhesion and a durable writing surface that works with standard dry erase markers and erases cleanly.</p>

<h3>Do you offer custom sizes and custom shapes for dry erase magnets?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can create dry erase magnets in custom sizes up to 24" x 24". We also offer contour cutting for custom shapes. Square cut and rounded corner options are available, with rounded corners recommended for better durability.</p>

<h3>Can I upload my own artwork for a custom dry erase magnet?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! You can upload your own print-ready files in .eps, .pdf, or high-resolution .jpg formats. Vector files with fonts converted to outlines provide the best print quality. Our design team can also help optimize your artwork.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between standard and reflective custom dry erase magnets?</h3>
<p>Our standard dry erase magnets feature full-color printing on magnetic material with protective lamination. We focus on providing the highest quality standard dry erase magnets rather than specialty reflective options.</p>

<h3>What are common uses for dry erase fridge magnets?</h3>
<p>Popular uses include meal planning, family chore charts, shopping lists, weekly schedules, goal tracking, and any reusable organizational system. They're perfect for kitchens, offices, classrooms, and anywhere you need flexible planning tools.</p>

<h3>What surfaces do dry erase magnets stick to best?</h3>
<p>Dry erase magnets work best on steel surfaces like refrigerators, filing cabinets, whiteboards, and magnetic boards. They will not stick to aluminum, plastic, or non-ferrous materials. Test with a regular magnet if you're unsure about surface compatibility.</p>

<h3>Is there a minimum order quantity for custom dry erase magnets?</h3>
<p>We can accommodate single custom dry erase magnets as well as large quantity orders. Contact customer service for pricing on bulk orders or special quantity requirements.</p>

<h3>Can I use custom dry erase magnets on a vehicle or outdoors?</h3>
<p>While our dry erase magnets are durable, they're designed primarily for indoor applications like refrigerators and office environments. For outdoor or vehicle use, consider our dedicated magnetic signs which are specifically engineered for those conditions.</p>

<h3>Are dry erase fridge magnets waterproof?</h3>
<p>Our dry erase magnets include protective lamination that provides moisture resistance for typical indoor use. They can handle kitchen humidity and light cleaning, but they're not designed for submersion or heavy water exposure.</p>

<h3>What markers can I use on a custom dry erase magnet (and how do I clean it)?</h3>
<p>Use standard dry erase markers for best results. Clean with a dry cloth or standard whiteboard eraser for regular maintenance. For deeper cleaning, use whiteboard cleaner or mild soap with a soft cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could damage the laminated surface.</p>
