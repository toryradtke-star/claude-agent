<p>Yes, the Heavy Duty Cross Foot Base works on various surfaces including concrete, asphalt, grass, and indoor flooring. The cross foot design provides stable contact points on different surface types.</p>

<p>While the base is weather-resistant and suitable for outdoor use, it's designed as a portable flag base system. For permanent installations, consider additional securing methods based on your specific location and weather conditions.</p>
