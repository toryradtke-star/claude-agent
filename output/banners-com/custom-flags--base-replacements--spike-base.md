<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Spike Bases</h2>

<h3>What ground types work with the spike base?</h3>
<p>The steel spike penetrates grass, soil, sand, and packed dirt effectively. Avoid rocky or concrete surfaces where the spike cannot penetrate properly.</p>

<h3>How tall of a flag can the spike base support?</h3>
<p>This 2-pound spike base supports flags up to 12 feet in height under normal wind conditions. Larger flags may require additional stabilization.</p>

<h3>Is the spike base weather resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes, the spike base features a weather-resistant metal coating designed for extended outdoor use in various weather conditions.</p>

<h3>Can I use multiple spike bases for a large flag?</h3>
<p>For oversized flags or high-wind areas, multiple spike bases can provide additional stability. Contact our team for recommendations on your specific installation.</p>

<h3>How do I remove the spike base from the ground?</h3>
<p>Gently rock the flag pole and spike base back and forth while pulling upward. The pointed design allows for relatively easy removal from most ground types.</p>

<h3>What flag pole diameters are compatible?</h3>
<p>The spike base accommodates standard flag pole diameters. Contact us if you have specific pole size requirements for your flag installation.</p>
