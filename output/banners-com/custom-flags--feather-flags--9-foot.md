#### [Material](#materialCollapse)

Feather flags are printed using a direct dye-sublimation printing process on our flag material. Direct dye-sublimation printing means that the ink goes through the material instead of just sitting on top of the material.

Our fabric material is temperature sensitive and due to the printing process it can expand and contract due to heat or cold. Because of this we cannot guarantee the exact size of any fabric product due to temperature fluctuations. We are not responsible for any product received that is too small or big for your purposes. If the size needs to be precise, such as if it is being installed into an existing frame or hardware, please express this to your CSR to discuss your specific application.

## How to Install the 9 foot Feather Flag

We print your custom feather flags using a method called direct dye sublimation. This process cures the ink into the fabric.The result is an eye-popping display. The flag poles are constructed of a strong but flexible Carbon Composite material that is superior to aluminum based poles. This pole hardware is covered by a one year warranty when given proper installation and care.

Use custom feather flags indoors or outdoors. You can invite neighbors to your BBQ, or promote upcoming sales events. Eye-catching flags work well on frontage roads, or in other hard-to-find-your-storefront locations. They can be printed either single-reverse or double-sided.

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### What is a feather flag?

These elegant flags mimic the shape of a feather. Common vernacular also refers to them as “razor flags”. In the world of displays, they are the modest flag choice for driving attention to your messaging, without being obnoxious.

#### What is the shortest flag I can order?

The 9 ft. [feather flag](https://www.banners.com/custom-flags/feather-flags) is one of our medium sizes for this flag type. The shortest available height is 7 feet.

#### Can my feather flag be double-sided?

Yes, it can be either single or double-sided. Note that due to the dye sublimation printing process, the single-sided option will mirror what has been printed on the one side. If you want your messaging to be readable on both sides of your flag, then choose the double-sided option.

![Single-reverse or double-sided flags](https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/22-4-26-BAN-Feather Flag-reversed-riot.png)

#### How do I assemble this flag?

Fear not, setting up your flag is fairly easy! Take the separate metal rods in ascending order and insert them from smallest to largest. Be firm but gentle while doing this. Next, take the metal stake and pound it into the ground. Finally, take the flag and put it onto the holding stake. Voilà, you’re done!

#### How do I find the right template for my flag?

Locate and select the Template tab on the designer box and inside will be the appropriate template for your flag size. Download the file and begin customizing!
