#### [Material](#materialCollapse)

Tear Drop Flags are printed using a direct dye-sublimation printing process on our flag material. Direct dye-sublimation printing means that the ink goes through the material instead of just sitting on top of the material.

Our fabric material is temperature sensitive and due to the printing process it can expand and contract due to heat or cold. Because of this we cannot guarantee the exact size of any fabric product due to temperature fluctuations. We are not responsible for any product received that is too small or big for your purposes. If the size needs to be precise, such as if it is being installed into an existing frame or hardware, please express this to your CSR to discuss your specific application.

**What is the lifespan of your 11.5 foot Teardrop flags?**

With proper care and installation, our custom made 11.5 foot Teardrop flags have an outdoor life span of 3 to 6 months. We do however recommend that you take your flag down and store indoors during inclement weather to help increase its lifespan.

**Do your 11.5 foot Teardrop flags come with a warranty?**

Yes, we offer a warranty on a prorated monthly basis for our 11.5 foot Teardrop flags.

For example, a prorated warranty on a 11.5 foot Teardrop flag that fails 2 months after proper installment will be replaced and we will cover 33% of the replacement cost for a new 11.5 foot Teardrop flag.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are 11.5 ft teardrop flags (teardrop banners)?

11.5 ft teardrop flags are aerodynamic, teardrop-shaped banner flags designed for high-visibility promotions indoors or outdoors.

### What are 11.5 ft teardrop flags used for?

They’re commonly used for grand openings, sales, open houses, trade shows, and storefront promotions.

### What are the printed dimensions of the 11.5 ft teardrop flag?

The 11.5 ft size prints at **38.5" W × 106.2" H**.

### Does “11.5 ft” include the base?

Yes—the listed **11.5 ft display height includes the flag plus the base**, so the printed flag itself is smaller than the overall display height.

### Are 11.5 ft teardrop flags single-sided or double-sided?

Choose **single-sided (single-reverse)** or **double-sided** printing, using the matching template for each option.

### How are 11.5 ft teardrop flags printed and what are they made of?

They’re made from **durable polyester** and printed with **direct dye sublimation**, so ink penetrates the fabric for vivid color.

### What base options are available for the 11.5 ft teardrop flag?

Choose a **spike base** for outdoor soft ground or a **heavy-duty cross base** for indoor or outdoor use (add weight for extra stability).

### Will I receive a proof before production?

Yes—we check your artwork and send a **proof for approval** before production.

### What are 11.5 ft teardrop flag poles made of, and is the hardware covered by a warranty?

Poles are made from **strong, flexible carbon composite**, and the pole hardware is covered by a **lifetime warranty**.
