<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Refrigerator Magnets</h2>

<h3>What surfaces do refrigerator magnets stick to?</h3>
<p>Custom refrigerator magnets stick to ferrous metal surfaces including refrigerators, freezers, filing cabinets, magnetic whiteboards, and steel surfaces. They will not adhere to aluminum, plastic, or non-magnetic metal surfaces. Test the surface with a small magnet before ordering if you're unsure.</p>

<h3>What materials are custom refrigerator magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Our refrigerator magnets are made from durable 30 mil magnetic material with professional lamination. This construction provides strong magnetic hold while protecting the printed surface from moisture, fading, and wear through regular use.</p>

<h3>Are custom refrigerator magnets weather-resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes, the lamination process makes our refrigerator magnets resistant to moisture, temperature changes, and normal kitchen environments. While designed for indoor use, they can withstand typical household conditions including humidity and temperature variations.</p>

<h3>What cut shapes are available for refrigerator magnets?</h3>
<p>We offer three cut options: rounded corners (recommended for better durability), square cut for clean edges, and custom contour cutting to match your design shape. Rounded corners help prevent lifting and provide better airflow around the magnet edges.</p>

<h3>Can I upload my own artwork for refrigerator magnets?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can upload your own artwork files. We recommend vector-based files like .EPS with outlined fonts, or high-resolution .PDF and .JPG files at 300 DPI minimum. Our design team can review your files and provide recommendations for optimal print quality.</p>

<h3>Is there a minimum order quantity for refrigerator magnets?</h3>
<p>We accommodate both small and large quantity orders for custom refrigerator magnets. Contact customer service for specific minimum quantities and to discuss your project requirements.</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk pricing for refrigerator magnets?</h3>
<p>Yes, volume discounts are available for larger refrigerator magnet orders. The more you order, the better your per-unit pricing. Contact our sales team to discuss bulk pricing for your specific quantity requirements.</p>

<h3>How long do custom refrigerator magnets last?</h3>
<p>With proper care, custom refrigerator magnets made from our 30 mil material with lamination can last for years. The magnetic strength remains consistent, and the laminated surface protects against fading, moisture, and normal wear from handling.</p>

<h3>Can I write on refrigerator magnets with markers?</h3>
<p>The laminated surface is not designed for writing with standard markers or pens. If you need writable refrigerator magnets, consider our dry erase magnet options or contact customer service to discuss specialized surface treatments for your application.</p>
