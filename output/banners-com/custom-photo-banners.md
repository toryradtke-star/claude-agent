## Finishing Options for Custom Photo Banner Installation

Choose from professional finishing options designed to make installation and display of your custom photo banner simple and secure. Each finishing option is tailored for different hanging methods and display environments.

**Grommet Installation:**
- Rust-resistant grommets placed every 2-3 feet along top edge
- Reinforced grommet holes prevent tearing during installation
- Compatible with rope, bungee cords, or zip ties
- Ideal for outdoor events and temporary installations
- Standard grommet placement with custom spacing available

**Hemmed Edge Finishing:**
- Heat-welded hem along all four edges
- Creates clean, professional appearance
- Prevents fraying and extends banner lifespan
- Reinforces banner structure for better hanging
- Available with or without grommets

**Pole Pocket Options:**
- 2" or 3" pole pocket sewn along top edge
- Allows banner to slide onto banner poles or mounting hardware
- Creates smooth, professional display without visible hardware
- Ideal for indoor events and permanent installations
- Custom pole pocket sizes available upon request

**Corner Reinforcement:**
All photo banners include reinforced corners to prevent tearing at stress points. This additional reinforcement ensures your custom photo banner maintains its appearance throughout your event or display period.

**Wind Slits:**
For outdoor installations in windy conditions, we can add wind slits to reduce wind resistance and prevent banner damage. Wind slits are strategically placed to maintain photo integrity while improving outdoor performance.

## Photo Banner Artwork Requirements for High-Quality Printing

Ensure your custom photo banner achieves the best possible print quality by following our photo submission guidelines and artwork requirements for optimal results.

**Photo Resolution Requirements:**
- Minimum 150 DPI at final print size
- Higher resolution images (300 DPI) recommended for best quality
- Large file sizes acceptable for better detail reproduction
- RAW or high-quality JPEG formats preferred
- Avoid heavily compressed or low-resolution images

**Photo File Format Guidelines:**
- **Preferred Formats:** JPEG, PNG, TIFF, PDF
- **High-Quality Sources:** Digital camera originals, professional scans
- **Avoid:** Screenshots, web images, heavily edited photos
- **Color Mode:** RGB color mode for digital printing
- **File Size:** Larger files generally produce better print results

**Design Layout Considerations:**
- Allow 1-2 inches of safe area from banner edges
- Important photo elements should avoid grommet placement areas
- Consider banner viewing distance when selecting photo detail level
- Horizontal photos work best for standard banner orientations
- Multiple photo layouts available for collage-style banners

**Photo Upload Process:**
1. Select your highest quality photo files
2. Upload through our secure online system
3. Our design team reviews image quality
4. Receive design proof for approval
5. Request adjustments if needed before production

**Design Assistance Available:**
Our experienced graphic design team can help optimize your photos for banner printing. We offer photo enhancement services including color correction, brightness adjustment, and layout optimization to ensure your custom photo banner exceeds expectations.

**Photo Enhancement Services:**
- Color correction and brightness optimization
- Background removal or replacement
- Multiple photo layout and arrangement
- Text overlay and caption addition
- Border and frame design options

**Quality Guarantee:**
If your submitted photos don't meet our quality standards for professional printing, our design team will contact you with recommendations for improvement or alternative solutions before production begins.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Photo Banners

### Can I order double-sided photo banners?

Yes, we offer double-sided photo banner printing options. Double-sided banners allow different photos or designs on each side, perfect for displays that will be viewed from multiple angles. Double-sided printing requires additional production time and includes supplementary costs for the extra printing and materials required.

### How do I get help with my photo banner design?

Our experienced graphic design team is available to assist with your photo banner layout and optimization. After uploading your photos, we'll review the image quality and create a design proof for your approval. If you need additional design assistance, color correction, or layout modifications, our designers can provide professional support to ensure your banner meets your expectations.

### What hanging options are available for photo banners?

We offer several finishing options for easy photo banner installation. Standard grommets allow hanging with rope, bungee cords, or zip ties. Pole pockets enable sliding the banner onto banner poles or mounting hardware. Hemmed edges provide a clean, professional appearance. All options include reinforced corners to prevent tearing during installation and use.

### What's the maximum size for custom photo banner printing?

Our large-format printing capabilities allow photo banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet in size. For standard photo applications, popular sizes range from 2'x6' up to 8'x20'. Larger custom dimensions are available through special order. Keep in mind that photo resolution requirements become more critical with larger banner sizes to maintain print quality.

### How long does photo banner production take?

Standard production time is 5-7 business days after design approval. Rush production options include 3-4 business day production for most sizes, and 1-2 business day rush production for urgent needs. Same-day production is available for select smaller sizes with morning approval. Production time begins after you approve the design proof of your photo banner.

### What photo resolution do I need for good print quality?

For optimal print quality, submit photos with minimum 150 DPI resolution at the final banner size. Higher resolution images (300 DPI) will produce the best results. Our design team will review your uploaded photos and contact you if the resolution is insufficient for quality printing at your requested banner size.
