<h2>Custom T-Shirt Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What is the minimum order quantity for custom t-shirts?</h3>
<p>Minimum order quantities vary by printing method. Direct-to-garment printing has no minimum, while screen printing typically requires 12 pieces per design/color combination.</p>

<h3>How long do custom t-shirts take to produce?</h3>
<p>Production times range from 2-14 business days depending on printing method and quantity. Rush production is available for most orders.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for custom t-shirt artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) and high-resolution raster files (PNG, JPG) at 300 DPI minimum. Free artwork consultation is available.</p>

<h3>Do custom t-shirts shrink after washing?</h3>
<p>All t-shirt materials are pre-shrunk to minimize shrinkage. We recommend washing in cold water and air drying for best results.</p>

<h3>Can I get a sample before placing a large custom t-shirt order?</h3>
<p>Yes, sample t-shirts are available for most materials and printing methods. Contact customer service for sample pricing and availability.</p>

<h3>What printing method works best for detailed custom t-shirt designs?</h3>
<p>Direct-to-garment printing provides the best results for detailed artwork, photographs, and designs with multiple colors or gradients.</p>
