<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What surfaces can custom wallpaper be applied to?</h3>
<p>Our self-adhesive wallpaper works best on smooth, clean painted walls, including latex and acrylic paint finishes. It also adheres well to smooth plaster, primed drywall, and other non-textured interior wall surfaces. The surface should be clean, dry, and free from dust or grease for optimal adhesion.</p>

<h3>Will custom wallpaper stick to all painted walls?</h3>
<p>The wallpaper adheres best to walls painted with quality latex or acrylic paints that have fully cured. Freshly painted walls should cure for at least 30 days before wallpaper application. Flat or matte paint finishes may not provide optimal adhesion compared to satin or semi-gloss finishes.</p>

<h3>What material is custom wallpaper made from?</h3>
<p>Our custom wallpaper is constructed from durable vinyl material with a self-adhesive backing. The vinyl provides excellent print quality, moisture resistance, and durability for interior applications. The adhesive is designed to be removable without damaging properly painted wall surfaces.</p>

<h3>Do I need wallpaper paste for custom wallpaper installation?</h3>
<p>No wallpaper paste is required. Our custom wallpaper features a self-adhesive backing with a protective release liner. Simply peel away the liner and apply the wallpaper directly to your prepared wall surface. This eliminates the mess and complexity of traditional wallpaper paste application.</p>

<h3>Is custom self-adhesive wallpaper removable?</h3>
<p>Yes, our wallpaper is designed to be removable from most properly painted wall surfaces. The adhesive bonds securely during use but can be peeled away when removal is desired. Some adhesive residue may remain on certain paint types and can typically be cleaned with mild adhesive remover.</p>

<h3>What are the printing size limits for custom wallpaper?</h3>
<p>Custom wallpaper can be produced in widths up to 54 inches and virtually unlimited length for large wall coverage. Multiple panels can be manufactured for wider installations with pattern matching available to ensure seamless appearance across panel joints.</p>
