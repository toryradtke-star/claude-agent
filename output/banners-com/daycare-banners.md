<h2>Day Care Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>How long will my day care banner last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl day care banners are designed to last 1-3 years outdoors depending on weather conditions and UV exposure. Indoor banners can last much longer with proper care.</p>

<h3>What's the largest day care banner size you can print?</h3>
<p>We can print day care banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet. Larger sizes may be available through custom quoting for special applications.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for day care banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional design team can create custom artwork for your day care banner if you don't have existing designs. Design services are available for an additional fee.</p>

<h3>Can I get my day care banner printed on both sides?</h3>
<p>Double-sided printing is available using our blockout vinyl material, which prevents show-through between the front and back designs.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between grommets and pole pockets?</h3>
<p>Grommets are metal eyelets that allow you to hang your day care banner with rope or bungee cords. Pole pockets are sewn sleeves that slide over poles for a cleaner appearance.</p>

<h3>Do you ship day care banners folded or rolled?</h3>
<p>Banners under 8 feet in both directions typically ship rolled. Larger day care banners are folded for shipping unless freight service is requested.</p>
