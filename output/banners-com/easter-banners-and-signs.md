<h2>Easter Banner Artwork Requirements for Custom Printing</h2>

<p>To ensure your Easter banner prints with optimal quality and color accuracy, please follow these artwork specifications:</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements:</strong> Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, or EPS format for best results. JPEG and PNG files are acceptable but may require resolution verification.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Guidelines:</strong> Artwork should be created at actual size with minimum 150 DPI resolution. For large Easter banners, scaled artwork may be acceptable with proportional resolution adjustment.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications:</strong> Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files may result in color shifts during the printing process.</p>

<p><strong>Text Considerations:</strong> Ensure all text is readable from intended viewing distance. Religious text and scripture should be clearly legible for maximum impact during Easter celebrations.</p>

<p>Our design team reviews all Easter banner artwork before production and will contact you if any adjustments are needed to ensure optimal printing results.</p>
