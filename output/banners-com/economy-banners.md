#### [Material Options](#material-optionsCollapse)

#### Banner Materials

Our economy banners are crafted from durable yet affordable materials, ensuring they look great and last for the duration of your event or promotion. All banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.

Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Please note our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (ex. A 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please ask your Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.

**13 oz Matte** **Vinyl Banners** 

Our high-quality 13oz matte vinyl banner material is our most popular banner material. It has a low shine and rich color gamut.

**13 oz Gloss Vinyl Banners**

Our high-quality 13oz gloss vinyl banner material has a high shine to it that produces vibrant, crisp colors and graphics to give it a high end look.

**18 oz Vinyl Banners**

Our extra heavy duty matte 18oz vinyl banner material is our toughest material. It has a low luster, blockout coating for extra durability and print quality.

A thick weblike material (similar to what is used in seatbelts) is heat welded to the perimeter of the banner to provide additional reinforcement.

A triangular piece of vinyl is applied to the corners of the banner and secured with grommets to improve both the strength and durability in the corners of the banner.

A pocket or sleeve added to the banner to allow for a dowel or pole to run through it for support. When selecting pole pockets there will be no increase in the finished size of the banner.

#### Heat Welded Hems & Grommets

Hems consist of one inch of the vinyl banner material folded over to the backside of the banner and heated together to form a clean and strong one-inch hem. We recommend having hems for all banners to increase the strength and durability of the banner.

You will have the following options when selecting hems and grommets for your banners:

**Top and Bottom Only Hems** - This is our standard finishing option for vinyl banners. The top and bottom of every banner will be heat welded and grommeted.

**All Four Sides Hems** - This is another common finishing option for vinyl banners. All 4 sides of the banner will be heat welded and grommeted.

![Top and Bottom Grommets Only Example](https://web2printdata.blob.core.windows.net/w2p-cms-1/24-09-04-BANS-Top-and-bottom-hems-and-grommets-only-2.png)

![All 4 Sides Hems & Grommets Example](https://web2printdata.blob.core.windows.net/w2p-cms-1/24-09-04-BANS-Top-and-bottom-hems-and-grommets-all-sides.png)

#### Reinforced Corners

Reinforced corners provide extra strength for the banner. The corners have a reinforced hem and are grommeted to each corner of the banner. You can not have reinforced corners with pole pockets.

#### Reinforced Hems

Reinforced hems are a 1 3/16" PVC coated webbing which increases the strength of the banner. The vinyl banner is cut to size and the reinforcements are heat welded to the backside of the banner.

We recommend having reinforced hems and reinforced corners on banners over 32 square feet that will be used outdoors or if the banner will be used in a high-wind environment.

\*\*You can not have reinforced hems with pole pockets.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I order an economy vinyl banner online?

You can order an economy vinyl banner by using the online designer, uploading a print-ready file, or requesting help from a professional designer.

### What is the smallest economy banner size I can order?

The smallest economy vinyl banner size is 1' × 2'. Economy banners are offered in 19 standard sizes and are not available in custom sizes.

### Is the finish glossy or matte?

Economy vinyl banners are printed on 13 oz matte scrim vinyl, so they have a matte (non-glossy) finish.

### Are economy banners printed in full color?

Yes. Economy vinyl banners print text-only, and the text can be printed in full CMYK color.

### Can I add a logo or graphics to an economy banner?

No. Economy vinyl banners are text-only on white vinyl, so logos and graphics are not included in this product option.

### What should I include on an economy banner for the best readability?

For best readability, use a short message in large, high-contrast text and include only essential details, such as a date, location, phone number, or website.

### How do I choose the right banner size for my viewing distance?

Choose a larger banner (and larger text) for longer viewing distances, and select the viewing distance option during ordering to help match size and legibility.

### What finishing options are available for economy vinyl banners?

Economy vinyl banners are available with grommets and hems, hems only, or cut to edge.

### Can I get pole pockets on an economy banner?

No. If you need pole pockets, choose a custom [vinyl banner](https://www.banners.com/vinyl-banners) option instead.

### How should I store my vinyl banner?

Store your vinyl banner by rolling it (not folding) and keeping it in a cool, dry place with the printed side facing outward.

### How do economy banners compare in price to other banners?

Economy vinyl banners are typically less expensive than full-color printed banners because they are text-only and use standard options. Example price comparisons include: 2' × 4' ($27.94 vs $18.47), 3' × 6' ($57.54 vs $41.56), 4' × 8' ($100.41 vs $69.72), and 4' × 10' ($125.51 vs $87.15).

### Are economy vinyl banners good for outdoor use, and how long do they last?

Yes. Economy vinyl banners are suitable for outdoor use and, with proper installation and care, can last one year or longer outdoors on 13 oz matte scrim vinyl.

### When do economy banners provide the best value?

Economy vinyl banners provide the best value when you need a clear message at a low cost, such as for simple promotions (“Now Open,” “Sale”), temporary events, directional signs (“Entrance,” “Exit”), and basic notices.

### How quickly can I receive my economy banner?

Timing varies by options and destination, but expedited production and shipping may be available and can be as fast as two business days in some cases. For specifics, visit the [production times](https://www.banners.com/production-times) page.
