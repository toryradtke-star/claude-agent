<h2>Electric Company Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>High-resolution files are required for professional-quality electric company banner printing. Submit artwork at 150 DPI at actual size, or 300 DPI at half size for optimal print quality.</p>

<p>Accepted file formats include PDF, AI, EPS, and high-resolution JPG or PNG files. Vector formats (PDF, AI, EPS) are preferred for electric company logos and text elements to ensure crisp, professional appearance.</p>

<p>Color specifications should be provided in CMYK format for accurate color matching. Pantone color references are helpful for matching existing electric company branding colors.</p>

<p>Text elements should be converted to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution issues. Include all fonts with your artwork files if text is not outlined.</p>

<p>Bleed areas of 2 inches on all sides should be included for banners with graphics or colors extending to the edge. This ensures professional finishing without white borders.</p>

<p>Our design team can work with your existing electric company logos, photos, and branding elements to create professional banner artwork. We can also enhance low-resolution logos or create new designs based on your specifications.</p>

<p>Proof approval is provided via email before printing begins. You'll see exactly how your electric company banner will look and can request any necessary revisions.</p>

<h2>Electric Company Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>How long do electric company banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl electric company banners are designed for 2-3 years of outdoor use under normal weather conditions. The UV-resistant inks and durable 13oz vinyl material maintain professional appearance even with constant sun exposure and weather exposure typical of job site and vehicle installations.</p>

<h3>Can electric company banners be installed on service vehicles?</h3>
<p>Yes, our banners are perfect for service vehicle installation. The flexible vinyl material conforms to curved surfaces, and grommets provide secure attachment points. Many electric companies use our banners on truck sides, tailgates, and trailer installations.</p>

<h3>What information should be included on electric company banners?</h3>
<p>Most effective electric company banners include your business name, phone number, website, and key services such as "24/7 Emergency Service," "Licensed & Insured," or "Residential & Commercial." Keep text large and readable from a distance.</p>

<h3>Are wind slits necessary for electric company banners?</h3>
<p>Wind slits are recommended for banners installed in high-wind areas such as elevated job sites, highway locations, or coastal regions. They reduce wind stress on the banner and attachment points while maintaining visibility of your electric company branding.</p>

<h3>Can you match our existing electric company colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match existing brand colors using Pantone color references or color samples you provide. Our printing process accurately reproduces corporate colors to maintain consistent branding across all your marketing materials.</p>

<h3>What size banner works best for service trucks?</h3>
<p>Popular sizes for electric company service vehicles include 3'x6', 4'x8', and 2'x8' depending on available space. Measure your vehicle's flat surfaces and consider visibility from the side and rear when choosing banner dimensions.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation hardware for electric company banners?</h3>
<p>Grommets are included with every banner for basic installation. Additional hardware such as bungee cords, zip ties, and banner poles are available through our accessories section or local hardware stores.</p>

<h3>Can electric company banners include photos of completed projects?</h3>
<p>Yes, high-resolution photos of your electrical work can be included in banner designs. Before/after photos, completed installations, and team photos help build credibility and showcase your electric company's capabilities.</p>
