<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Fabric Church Banners</h2>

<h3>What material works best for indoor church banners?</h3>
<p>We recommend our Banner Soft fabric, which is specifically designed for indoor use. It's NFPS-701 fire rated for church safety requirements, has a low shine finish that reduces glare during services, and provides excellent color reproduction for religious artwork and text.</p>

<h3>How long do fabric church banners last?</h3>
<p>With proper care and indoor use, fabric church banners can last several years. The durability depends on frequency of use, storage conditions, and handling. Our Banner Soft material is designed to withstand repeated hanging and storage typical of church environments.</p>

<h3>Can I get custom sizes for church banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer custom sizing for church banners. However, fabric material is temperature sensitive and may expand or contract. For installations requiring precise sizing, please discuss your specific requirements with our customer service team when placing your order.</p>

<h3>What finishing options work best for hanging church banners?</h3>
<p>The best finishing depends on your hanging method. Folded sewn hems with grommets work well for rope or hook systems. Pole pockets are ideal for pole mounting systems. We offer 2", 3", and 4" pole pockets to accommodate different pole diameters.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order church banners?</h3>
<p>We recommend ordering 1-2 weeks before you need the banners. We offer production times from 2-5 business days, plus shipping time. For seasonal events or special occasions, ordering earlier ensures availability and reduces costs.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for church banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional graphic designers can help create or enhance your church banner artwork. We can work with your concepts, optimize existing artwork, or create completely custom designs that reflect your church's message and aesthetic.</p>

<h3>What file format should I provide for church banner artwork?</h3>
<p>The best format is .EPS files converted to outline. We also accept high-resolution PDF or JPG files (minimum 300 DPI). If you're unsure about your file quality, our design team can review and optimize your artwork for printing.</p>

<h3>Are fabric church banners fire safe?</h3>
<p>Yes, our Banner Soft fabric is NFPS-701 certified fire rated, making it safe for indoor church use. This certification is important for church insurance and safety requirements.</p>
