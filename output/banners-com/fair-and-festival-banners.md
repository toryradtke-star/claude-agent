<h2>Fair & Festival Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>How long do vinyl fair banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our festival banners are designed to withstand outdoor conditions for 2-3 years with proper care. The UV-resistant inks and weather-resistant vinyl material maintain vibrant colors and structural integrity throughout multiple festival seasons when used for temporary installations.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for festival banners?</h3>
<p>We offer custom fair and festival banners in sizes ranging from 1'x1' up to 8'x50'. Popular festival sizes include 3'x6', 4'x8', 6'x10', and 8'x20'. Custom sizes are available to fit specific vendor booth dimensions or festival display requirements.</p>

<h3>Can I get custom colors for my fair banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, our full-color digital printing process can reproduce virtually any color combination for your festival banner design. We use CMYK printing with UV-resistant inks to ensure accurate color reproduction that maintains vibrancy in outdoor festival conditions.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for last-minute festival needs?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! We offer same-day rush production for festival banners ordered before 10 AM CST, next-day production for urgent fair requirements, and expedited shipping options to meet tight event deadlines.</p>

<h3>What finishing options work best for outdoor festivals?</h3>
<p>For outdoor festival installations, we recommend reinforced hemming with brass grommets every 18-24 inches, plus corner reinforcements. Wind slits can be added for high-wind festival locations, and rope hem finishing provides maximum durability for extended outdoor use.</p>

<h3>Can you ship festival banners directly to my event venue?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can ship your fair and festival banners directly to your event location or venue. We offer various shipping options including ground, expedited, and overnight delivery, plus Saturday delivery for weekend festival setup requirements.</p>

<h3>Do you provide design services for festival banners?</h3>
<p>Our in-house graphic design team offers professional design assistance for festival banners. You can also use our online designer tool to create custom fair banners, or upload your own print-ready artwork files.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between 13oz and 15oz vinyl for fair banners?</h3>
<p>13oz vinyl is our standard weight material, suitable for most indoor fair displays and short-term outdoor festivals. 15oz vinyl offers increased durability and wind resistance, making it ideal for multi-day outdoor festivals and locations with challenging weather conditions.</p>
