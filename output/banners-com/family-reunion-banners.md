<p>To ensure your family reunion banner prints with the highest quality, please follow these artwork guidelines:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Preferred formats:</strong> PDF, AI, EPS, PSD</li>
<li><strong>Acceptable formats:</strong> JPG, PNG, TIFF (high resolution)</li>
<li><strong>Minimum resolution:</strong> 150 DPI at actual family reunion banner size</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Specifications</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Color mode:</strong> CMYK for accurate family reunion banner color reproduction</li>
<li><strong>Bleed area:</strong> Include 2" bleed on all sides for family reunion banners with edge-to-edge graphics</li>
<li><strong>Safe area:</strong> Keep important text and graphics 4" from edges to avoid finishing interference</li>
</ul>

<h3>Family Photo Guidelines</h3>
<ul>
<li>Family photos should be high resolution (minimum 300 DPI)</li>
<li>Avoid low-resolution social media images for family reunion banners</li>
<li>Provide original photo files when possible for best reproduction quality</li>
</ul>

<h3>Text Considerations</h3>
<ul>
<li>Use fonts sized appropriately for viewing distance at your family reunion venue</li>
<li>Embed or outline all fonts in your family reunion banner artwork</li>
<li>Ensure adequate contrast between text and background colors</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Services</h3>

<h2>Family Reunion Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>Can I get my custom family reunion banner double sided?</h3>
<p>Yes! We offer double-sided printing for family reunion banners using our 18 oz. blockout banner material. This prevents show-through and ensures both sides of your family reunion banner look professional. Double-sided family reunion banners are perfect for displaying in the center of venues where both sides will be visible to family members.</p>

<h3>Is there a minimum amount I have to order?</h3>
<p>No minimum order required! You can order just one family reunion banner for intimate gatherings, or multiple banners for large extended family reunions. We accommodate orders of all sizes, from single custom family reunion banners to bulk orders for multi-family events.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to keep my banner clean?</h3>
<p>Family reunion banners can be cleaned with mild soap and water. For outdoor family reunion events, simply rinse off dirt and debris after use. Allow your family reunion banner to dry completely before storing to prevent mildew. For stubborn stains, use a soft brush with gentle cleaning solution.</p>

<h3>What if I don't have anything to hang my banner?</h3>
<p>We offer banner hanging accessories perfect for family reunion displays! Our selection includes banner stands, hanging hardware, bungee cords, and zip ties. Many family reunions use simple rope or bungee cords to hang banners between trees, tent poles, or building fixtures. Contact us for hanging recommendations specific to your family reunion venue.</p>

<h3>Do I have to use a template for my family reunion banner?</h3>
<p>Not at all! While we offer family reunion banner templates for quick ordering, you can also upload your own custom design or work with our graphic designers to create a completely unique family reunion banner. Many families prefer custom designs that incorporate family photos, heritage elements, or specific reunion themes that reflect their family's personality and history.</p>
