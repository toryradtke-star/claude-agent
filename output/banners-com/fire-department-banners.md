Vector logos preferred for crisp reproduction at large sizes. Provide logos in original format when possible for best print quality.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Guidelines</strong><br>
Minimum 150 DPI at final fire department banner size. Higher resolution recommended for detailed logos or photographic elements.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications</strong><br>
CMYK color mode preferred for accurate fire department color matching. PMS color matching available for official department colors.</p>

<p><strong>Text Readability Standards</strong><br>
Minimum text size recommendations: 2 inches for viewing distances up to 50 feet, 4 inches for distances up to 100 feet.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safety Areas</strong><br>
Include 2-inch bleed area around fire department banner edges. Keep important text and logos 3 inches from finished edges.</p>

<p><strong>Design Assistance Available</strong><br>
Our graphic design team can help optimize fire department logos and create custom layouts. Design services available for additional fees.</p>

<p><strong>Proof Review Process</strong><br>
Digital proofs provided for all fire department banner orders. Approval required before production begins.</p>

<h2>Fire Department Banner FAQs</h2>

<h3>What size fire department banner should I order for my station?</h3>
<p>Station identification banners typically work best at 3'x8' or 4'x10' for good visibility. For parade banners, 3'x6' provides easy handling while maintaining readability. Consider viewing distance when selecting size - larger banners work better for roadside visibility.</p>

<h3>Can you match our official fire department colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer PMS color matching to ensure your fire department banner matches your official colors exactly. Provide PMS color numbers or physical color samples for the most accurate matching.</p>

<h3>How long will my fire department banner last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our premium vinyl fire department banners typically last 2-3 years in outdoor conditions with proper installation. UV-resistant inks and weather-resistant materials help maintain color vibrancy and structural integrity.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation services for fire department banners?</h3>
<p>We don't provide installation services, but we include detailed hanging instructions with every fire department banner order. Our finishing options like grommets and D-rings make installation straightforward for your maintenance staff.</p>

<h3>Can you create a banner design using our fire department logo?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Our graphic design team specializes in working with fire department logos and can create professional banner layouts. Provide your logo in the highest resolution available for best results.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between 13oz and 18oz vinyl for fire department banners?</h3>
<p>13oz vinyl works well for indoor displays and temporary outdoor use. 18oz vinyl provides extra durability for permanent outdoor fire department installations and high-wind areas. Both materials use the same high-quality inks and finishing.</p>

<h3>Do you offer quantity discounts for multiple fire department banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide quantity discounts for orders of 5 or more banners. Contact our sales team for volume pricing on large fire department banner orders or multi-station purchases.</p>

<h3>Can fire department banners be double-sided?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer double-sided printing using blockout material to prevent image bleed-through. This option works well for fire department banners that need visibility from both directions.</p>
