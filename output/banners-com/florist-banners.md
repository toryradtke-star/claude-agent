<h2>Florist Banner Artwork Requirements for Print Production</h2>

<p>Professional artwork specifications ensure optimal print quality for your florist banner:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats. JPEG and PNG files are acceptable if created at 300 DPI or higher resolution for your final banner size.</p>

<h3>Resolution & Image Quality</h3>
<p>Minimum 150 DPI at final printed size for sharp text and clear floral imagery. Higher resolution (300 DPI) recommended for banners with detailed flower photography or intricate design elements.</p>

<h3>Color Mode & Profiles</h3>
<p>Design files should use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted during production, which may result in slight color variations from your original design.</p>

<h3>Bleed & Safety Areas</h3>
<p>Include 2-inch bleed area beyond finished banner size. Keep important text and design elements at least 4 inches from banner edges to account for hemming and finishing processes.</p>

<h3>Design Services Available</h3>
<p>Professional design services available if you need assistance creating your florist banner artwork. Our graphic design team specializes in effective flower shop marketing materials and can work from your concepts, existing logos, and promotional messaging.</p>

<p>Artwork is reviewed before production begins, and our design team will contact you if any adjustments are needed to ensure optimal print results.</p>

<h2>Florist Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material works best for outdoor florist banners?</h3>
<p>For outdoor applications, we recommend 13oz or 18oz vinyl materials which provide excellent weather resistance and UV protection. Mesh vinyl is ideal for windy locations as it allows air flow while maintaining visibility.</p>

<h3>How long do florist banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper installation and care, vinyl florist banners typically last 1-3 years outdoors depending on weather exposure. Indoor banners can last much longer with minimal fading or wear.</p>

<h3>Can you match my flower shop's brand colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, our printing process can accurately reproduce most brand colors. Provide us with Pantone color numbers or color swatches for the best color matching results on your florist banner.</p>

<h3>What's the largest florist banner size you can produce?</h3>
<p>We can produce florist banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet. For larger sizes or special applications, contact us for a custom quote and production consultation.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for florist banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional graphic design team can create custom florist banner designs from your concepts, logos, and promotional messaging. Design services typically add 1-2 business days to production time.</p>

<h3>How should I clean and maintain my florist banner?</h3>
<p>Clean vinyl banners with mild soap and water using a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaning materials. Proper storage in a dry location extends banner life significantly.</p>

<h3>Can I use my florist banner for multiple events?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Vinyl florist banners are designed for repeated use and easy installation. Consider general messaging rather than date-specific content if you plan to reuse your banner for multiple promotional events.</p>
