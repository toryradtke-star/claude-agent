<h2>Flu Shot Banner Artwork Requirements for Medical Graphics</h2>

<p>Professional artwork ensures your flu shot banner delivers clear medical messaging with graphics that meet healthcare advertising standards.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>High-resolution PDF, AI, or EPS files preferred for flu shot banner printing. JPEG and PNG files acceptable for simple medical graphics and text-based designs.</p>

<h3>Resolution Standards</h3>
<p>Minimum 150 DPI at actual print size for flu shot banner artwork. Higher resolution recommended for detailed medical graphics, logos, and small text elements.</p>

<h3>Color Specifications</h3>
<p>CMYK color mode required for accurate flu shot banner printing. RGB files will be converted, which may result in color variations from screen display.</p>

<h3>Text & Font Guidelines</h3>
<p>Minimum 1-inch text height recommended for flu shot banner readability at typical viewing distances. Sans-serif fonts provide better clarity for medical information.</p>

<h3>Safety Areas & Bleed</h3>
<p>0.25-inch bleed required around all flu shot banner edges. Keep important medical text and graphics 1 inch from banner edges to account for finishing processes.</p>

<h3>Professional Design Service</h3>
<p>Our graphic design team can create custom flu shot banner artwork using your medical practice branding, healthcare messaging, and contact information.</p>
