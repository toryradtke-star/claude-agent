<h2>For Lease Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>What size For Lease banner works best for my property?</h3>
<p>Banner size depends on your property type and viewing distance. For storefronts, 3x8 or 4x8 feet provides excellent visibility. Large commercial buildings benefit from 5x15 or 6x20 foot banners. Consider that text should be at least 3" tall for every 100 feet of viewing distance.</p>

<h3>How long will my For Lease banner last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our premium vinyl For Lease banners are designed for 2-3 years of outdoor use under normal weather conditions. The UV-resistant inks and weather-resistant vinyl construction ensure your banner maintains professional appearance throughout extended lease marketing periods.</p>

<h3>Can you create a design for my For Lease banner?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our professional design team can create custom For Lease banner layouts that include your contact information, property details, and branding. We offer both template-based designs and completely custom layouts to match your property marketing needs.</p>

<h3>What information should I include on my For Lease banner?</h3>
<p>Essential information includes "For Lease" messaging, property type or square footage, your contact phone number, and website if applicable. Keep text large and easy to read from a distance. Avoid overcrowding the design with too much information.</p>

<h3>How quickly can I get my For Lease banner?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3-5 business days, with rush options available for 1-2 day turnaround. Same-day production is possible for simple designs and standard sizes. Add shipping time based on your location and selected delivery method.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to install my For Lease banner?</h3>
<p>Installation method depends on your location and banner finishing. Grommeted banners work well with zip ties, rope, or bungee cords on fences or building facades. Pole pocket banners are ideal for banner stands during open houses or indoor showings.</p>

<h3>Can I use the same banner design for multiple properties?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Many property managers order multiple banners with the same design and contact information for their rental portfolio. We offer quantity discounts and can accommodate bulk orders with consistent branding across all your properties.</p>

<h3>Do you offer quantity discounts for property management companies?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide volume pricing for property management companies and real estate professionals who order multiple banners. Contact us for custom quotes on bulk orders and to discuss business account options with net payment terms.</p>
