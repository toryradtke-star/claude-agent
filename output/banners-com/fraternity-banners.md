<h2>Fraternity Banner Artwork Requirements for Greek Letter Printing</h2>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong></p>
<p>Submit fraternity banner artwork in high-resolution formats:</p>
<ul>
<li>PDF (preferred for Greek letter designs)</li>
<li>AI (Adobe Illustrator with outlined fonts)</li>
<li>EPS (Encapsulated PostScript)</li>
<li>High-resolution JPG or PNG (300 DPI minimum)</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Greek Letter Design Guidelines</strong></p>
<p>Ensure your Greek letters are clearly readable and properly sized for your banner dimensions. Minimum letter height should be 4 inches for banners viewed from 20+ feet away.</p>

<p><strong>Color Requirements</strong></p>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate fraternity color reproduction. Provide PMS color codes for official Greek organization colors when available.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution & Size Specifications</strong></p>
<p>Artwork should be created at actual banner size or proportional scale with 150 DPI minimum resolution. Larger Greek letter banners require higher resolution for crisp printing.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed & Safe Area Guidelines</strong></p>
<p>Include 0.25" bleed area around your fraternity banner design and keep important Greek letters and text 0.5" from finished edges.</p>

<p><strong>Font & Text Considerations</strong></p>
<p>Convert all text to outlines or provide font files with your fraternity banner artwork. Sans-serif fonts work best for Greek letters on outdoor banners.</p>

<p><strong>Design Assistance Available</strong></p>
<p>Our team can help optimize your Greek letter artwork for banner printing or create custom fraternity designs based on your specifications and organizational branding.</p>

<h2>Fraternity Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What's the best material for outdoor fraternity banners?</h3>
<p>13oz vinyl is our most popular choice for fraternity banners that need to withstand outdoor weather. For windy locations, mesh material allows air flow while maintaining your Greek letter visibility.</p>

<h3>Can you match our official fraternity colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match official Greek organization colors using PMS color codes or color samples. Our printing process ensures accurate reproduction of traditional fraternity colors.</p>

<h3>How long do fraternity banners typically last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper care, vinyl fraternity banners can last 2-3 years outdoors. UV-resistant inks help prevent fading of Greek letters and organizational colors over time.</p>

<h3>What's the largest size available for fraternity banners?</h3>
<p>We can print fraternity banners up to 8 feet tall by 50 feet wide. Larger sizes may be available through custom quotes for special Greek organization projects.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush delivery for urgent fraternity events?</h3>
<p>Yes, rush production (1-2 business days) and expedited shipping options are available for fraternity banners needed for time-sensitive rush events and activities.</p>

<h3>Can you help design our fraternity banner if we don't have artwork?</h3>
<p>Our design team can create custom fraternity banner layouts incorporating your Greek letters, organizational logos, colors, and messaging. Design services are available for an additional fee.</p>

<h3>What mounting options work best for fraternity house installations?</h3>
<p>Grommets work well for temporary displays during rush events. Pole pockets provide a cleaner look for permanent fraternity house banners displayed on poles or mounting systems.</p>

<h3>Do you offer discounts for multiple fraternity banner orders?</h3>
<p>Greek organizations can establish accounts for volume pricing on multiple banner orders throughout the year. Contact us to discuss fraternity organization pricing.</p>
