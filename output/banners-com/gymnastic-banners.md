<h2>Artwork Requirements for Custom Gymnastic Banner Printing</h2>

<p>To ensure your gymnastic banner prints with the highest quality, please follow these artwork guidelines and specifications.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Preferred Formats:</strong> PDF, AI, EPS, PSD</li>
<li><strong>Acceptable Formats:</strong> JPG, PNG, TIFF (high resolution)</li>
<li><strong>Vector Files:</strong> Recommended for logos and text elements</li>
<li><strong>Avoid:</strong> Word documents, PowerPoint files, low-resolution web images</li>
</ul>

<h3>Resolution & Size Specifications</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Minimum Resolution:</strong> 150 DPI at full size</li>
<li><strong>Recommended Resolution:</strong> 300 DPI for optimal print quality</li>
<li><strong>Large Format:</strong> 72-150 DPI acceptable for banners over 10 feet</li>
<li><strong>Bleed Area:</strong> Include 1-2 inches of bleed around all edges</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Best Practices for Gymnastic Banners</h3>
<ul>
<li>Use high contrast colors for gymnasium visibility</li>
<li>Keep text large enough to read from competition distance</li>
<li>Ensure team logos are vector format when possible</li>
<li>Consider viewing angles from bleachers and floor level</li>
<li>Plan for grommet or pole pocket placement in design</li>
</ul>

<h3>Color Guidelines</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Color Mode:</strong> CMYK for print accuracy</li>
<li><strong>Pantone Colors:</strong> Specify exact team color matches</li>
<li><strong>RGB Files:</strong> Will be converted to CMYK (colors may shift slightly)</li>
<li><strong>Color Proofing:</strong> Digital proofs available upon request</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Services Available</h3>
<p>Don't have artwork ready? Our professional design team can create custom gymnastic banner designs:</p>
<ul>
<li>Team logo integration and enhancement</li>
<li>Typography and layout optimization</li>
<li>Color matching to team uniforms</li>
<li>Custom graphics and gymnastics-themed elements</li>
</ul>

<h3>File Upload Process</h3>
<p>Upload your artwork files during the ordering process, or email high-resolution files to our production team. We'll review all artwork and contact you within 24 hours if any adjustments are needed before printing begins.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Gymnastic Banners</h2>

<h3>What sizes are available for gymnastic banners?</h3>
<p>We offer gymnastic banners in sizes ranging from 1'x1' up to 8'x50'. Popular sizes for gymnastics teams include 3'x8', 4'x10', and 6'x12' banners. Custom sizes are available to fit your specific gymnasium or display requirements.</p>

<h3>How long do gymnastic banners last?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl gymnastic banners are designed to last multiple seasons with proper care. Indoor banners typically last 3-5 years, while outdoor banners last 1-3 years depending on weather exposure. All materials are UV-resistant and built for durability.</p>

<h3>Can you match our team colors exactly?</h3>
<p>Yes! We can match Pantone colors and work with you to ensure your banner colors match your team uniforms and branding. Provide us with color specifications or samples, and our design team will ensure accurate color reproduction.</p>

<h3>What's the best material for gymnasium installation?</h3>
<p>For indoor gymnasium use, our standard 13oz vinyl material provides excellent durability and vibrant color reproduction. The material is easy to clean and maintain, making it ideal for high-traffic gymnastics facilities.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for teams without artwork?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our professional design team can create custom gymnastic banner designs incorporating your team logo, colors, and messaging. We specialize in creating eye-catching designs that represent your team's spirit and achievements.</p>

<h3>How are large gymnastic banners shipped?</h3>
<p>Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be folded for standard shipping. If you prefer your banner not be folded, we can arrange freight shipping for an additional cost. Contact us for freight shipping quotes on oversized banners.</p>

<h3>Can I get a proof before my banner is printed?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide digital proofs for all custom gymnastic banners. Our design team will email you a proof within 24 hours of receiving your artwork. Production begins only after you approve the final proof.</p>

<h3>What payment options do you accept for team orders?</h3>
<p>We accept credit cards, PayPal, and purchase orders from qualified schools and organizations. Net payment terms are available for educational institutions and established gymnastics programs.</p>

<h3>Do you offer discounts for multiple banner orders?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide volume discounts for teams ordering multiple banners or schools purchasing banners for multiple sports programs. Contact us for custom pricing on bulk orders.</p>

<h3>How quickly can I get my gymnastic banner?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3-5 business days after artwork approval. Rush production options include 2-3 day, 24-48 hour, and same-day production for urgent orders (additional fees apply for rush services).</p>
