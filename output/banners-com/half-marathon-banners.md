<ul>

<li>Large format banners - 100 DPI acceptable for viewing distances over 10 feet</li>

</ul>

<p><strong>Color Specifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>CMYK color mode preferred for accurate sporting event banner printing</li>

<h2>Half Marathon Race Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material works best for outdoor half marathon race banners?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl is the most popular choice for outdoor sporting events, offering excellent durability and vibrant color reproduction. For windy race venues, mesh vinyl provides wind resistance while maintaining visibility.</p>

<h3>How long will my half marathon race banner last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper care, our vinyl banners typically last 2-3 years in outdoor sporting event conditions. UV-resistant inks and materials help maintain color vibrancy throughout multiple race seasons.</p>

<h3>Can you print sponsor logos on half marathon race banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can incorporate multiple sponsor logos, race branding, and custom messaging on your half marathon banners. Our design team will ensure proper logo placement and sizing for maximum visibility.</p>

<h3>What's the largest size available for half marathon race banners?</h3>
<p>We can produce half marathon banners up to 8 feet tall by 50 feet long. Larger sizes require freight shipping and special handling but are available for major sporting events.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for last-minute race events?</h3>
<p>Yes, same-day and next-day production options are available for rush sporting event orders. Contact us before 11 AM CST for same-day production on standard sizes with approved artwork.</p>

<h3>How should I install my half marathon race banner at the venue?</h3>
<p>Installation depends on your finishing choice. Grommets work well with rope or zip ties, pole pockets slide over mounting hardware, and reinforced edges provide extra strength for permanent installations at race venues.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse my half marathon race banner for multiple events?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Our durable materials are designed for multiple uses. Consider removable mounting options like grommets or velcro strips for easy installation and storage between race events.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for half marathon race banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house graphic design team can create custom designs from your concepts, logos, and text. Design services include unlimited revisions and are available for all half marathon race banner orders.</p>
