<h2>Halloween Sign Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What materials work best for outdoor Halloween displays?</h3>
<p>13oz vinyl banners provide maximum durability for outdoor Halloween installations. This weather-resistant material withstands rain, wind, and UV exposure throughout the Halloween season while maintaining vibrant colors and structural integrity.</p>

<h3>How long do Halloween signs last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl Halloween banners typically last 2-3 years outdoors with proper installation. Corrugated plastic yard signs are designed for seasonal use and will last through multiple Halloween seasons when stored properly between uses.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse my Halloween signs each year?</h3>
<p>Yes, our durable Halloween signs are designed for multiple seasons of use. Vinyl banners and fabric flags can be rolled for compact storage, while corrugated plastic yard signs store flat for easy seasonal reuse.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for Halloween banners?</h3>
<p>Custom sizing available up to 16' x 50' seamless for large Halloween displays. Popular sizes include 3'x6', 4'x8', and 6'x10' banners. Oversized Halloween banners available for large venues and commercial installations.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for Halloween orders?</h3>
<p>Yes, expedited 1-2 day production available for urgent Halloween events. Standard production time is 3 business days, with rush options available during peak Halloween season for time-sensitive orders.</p>

<h3>How should I install Halloween yard signs?</h3>
<p>Use H-stakes or Beefy stakes for secure ground installation of corrugated plastic Halloween yard signs. Stakes should penetrate 8-12 inches into ground for wind resistance and stability during outdoor display.</p>
