<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What material options do you offer for these banners?</h3>
<p>We offer six premium vinyl materials for Hanukkah banners: 8oz vinyl (lightweight), 13oz vinyl (standard), 18oz vinyl (heavy-duty), mesh vinyl (wind-resistant), fabric material (elegant finish), and canvas material (textured). Each material is suitable for different installation environments and celebration requirements.</p>

<h3>What is the star of Hanukkah named?</h3>
<p>The Star of David (Magen David in Hebrew) is the six-pointed star symbol commonly associated with Judaism and often featured on Hanukkah banners. Our design team can incorporate authentic Star of David designs and other traditional Hanukkah symbols into your custom banner.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer double-sided printing for Hanukkah banners that will be viewed from both sides, such as suspended installations or parade banners. Double-sided banners use a blockout liner between prints to prevent show-through and ensure vibrant colors on both sides.</p>

<h3>What is the largest banner I can order?</h3>
<p>Our standard production accommodates Hanukkah banners up to 8'x50'. For larger sizes or custom dimensions, we can provide specialized printing through our custom quote service. Contact our team to discuss oversized banner requirements for large venues or outdoor installations.</p>

<h3>What is the better finish for indoor use, glossy or matte?</h3>
<p>For indoor Hanukkah displays, matte finish is typically preferred as it reduces glare and provides better readability under artificial lighting. Glossy finish works well for outdoor applications where weather resistance is important. Our team can recommend the best finish based on your specific installation environment.</p>
