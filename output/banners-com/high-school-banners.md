<p>Your high school banner is crafted with premium vinyl materials designed to withstand both indoor gymnasium conditions and outdoor weather exposure. We offer six specialized banner materials, each optimized for different school applications and display environments.</p>

<h3>Outdoor Vinyl for Athletic Fields & School Grounds</h3>
<p>Heavy-duty 18oz vinyl material delivers superior durability for outdoor high school banner installations. This weather-resistant option withstands UV exposure, rain, and wind conditions common at athletic fields and school entrance displays.</p>

<h2>High School Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Professional artwork preparation ensures your high school banner prints with sharp details, vibrant colors, and clear text readable from appropriate viewing distances. Our design team provides comprehensive support to optimize your artwork for large-format printing.</p>

<h3>Resolution Requirements for Large Banners</h3>
<p>High school banner artwork should be provided at 150 DPI at actual size, or 300 DPI at half size minimum. This resolution ensures sharp text and clear school logos when printed at full banner dimensions.</p>

<h3>File Format Specifications</h3>
<p>Preferred file formats include AI, EPS, PDF, and high-resolution JPG or PNG files. Vector formats (AI, EPS) provide the best results for school logos, text, and graphic elements that need to scale cleanly.</p>

<h3>Color Profile & School Brand Guidelines</h3>
<p>CMYK color profiles produce the most accurate color reproduction for high school banners. If you have specific school color requirements, provide Pantone color codes or brand guidelines to ensure color accuracy.</p>

<h3>Text Legibility Guidelines</h3>
<p>Text on high school banners should be sized appropriately for viewing distance. For banners viewed from 20+ feet, minimum text height should be 3-4 inches. Our design team can advise on optimal text sizing for your specific application.</p>

<h3>Design Assistance Available</h3>
<p>Our in-house graphic designers can help optimize your high school banner artwork, adjust layouts for banner proportions, and ensure all elements print clearly. Design assistance is available at no additional charge for standard modifications.</p>

<p>If you need complete design services, our team can create custom high school banner designs incorporating your school logos, colors, and messaging for a professional, cohesive appearance.</p>

<h2>High School Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material works best for outdoor high school banners?</h3>
<p>For outdoor applications like athletic fields and school entrances, we recommend 18oz vinyl or mesh material. These options provide excellent durability against weather conditions while maintaining vibrant colors throughout extended outdoor display periods.</p>

<h3>How long does it take to produce a custom high school banner?</h3>
<p>Standard production time is 3-5 business days after artwork approval. Rush production options are available for urgent school events, with 1-2 day turnaround possible depending on banner specifications and current production schedule.</p>

<h3>Can you match our specific school colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match school colors using Pantone color codes or brand guidelines. Our printing process uses fade-resistant inks to ensure your school colors remain vibrant throughout the banner's display life.</p>

<h3>What's the largest high school banner size available?</h3>
<p>We can produce high school banners up to 8 feet x 50 feet for large-scale applications like gymnasium displays or outdoor stadium banners. Larger sizes may require custom freight shipping for delivery.</p>

<h3>Do you accept school purchase orders?</h3>
<p>Yes, we accept official purchase orders from educational institutions and can work with school district payment schedules. Established educational accounts may also qualify for net payment terms.</p>

<h3>How should we hang our high school banner?</h3>
<p>Hanging methods depend on your finishing choice and location. Grommets work well with rope or zip ties, while pole pockets create professional displays. Our team can recommend the best finishing option for your specific installation needs.</p>

<h3>Can you help design our high school banner?</h3>
<p>Our in-house graphic design team provides complimentary design assistance for standard modifications and can create complete custom designs for an additional fee. We work with your school logos, colors, and messaging to create professional results.</p>

<h3>Will my banner arrive folded?</h3>
<p>Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions are folded for standard shipping due to carrier restrictions. Schools needing unfolded delivery should request custom freight quotes, though folding typically doesn't affect banner appearance once installed.</p>
