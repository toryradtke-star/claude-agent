<h2>Easter Banner Finishing Options for Indoor & Outdoor Installation</h2>

<p>Standard finishing for Easter banners includes heat-welded hems and rust-resistant grommets spaced every 2-4 feet around the perimeter. This finishing provides secure mounting points and reinforced edges for long-lasting performance.</p>

<h3>Additional Finishing Options</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>2" Pole Pockets:</strong> Fits poles up to 3/4" diameter for pole mounting</li>
<li><strong>3" Pole Pockets:</strong> Accommodates poles up to 1 1/4" diameter</li>
<li><strong>4" Pole Pockets:</strong> Designed for poles up to 1 1/2" diameter</li>
<li><strong>Reinforced Corners:</strong> Extra material and stitching at corners for high-wind areas</li>
<li><strong>Reinforced Hems:</strong> Additional reinforcement along all edges</li>
</ul>

<p>When selecting pole pockets, avoid placing text or important imagery within 3 inches of the banner edge. Pole pockets maintain the finished banner size and provide a clean, professional appearance for pole-mounted installations.</p>

<h3>Two Sided Easter Banner Finishing</h3>
<p>Double-sided Easter banners available up to 150 square feet with pole pocket or hem and grommet finishing. Perfect for installations where the banner will be viewed from both sides, such as hanging displays in sanctuaries or outdoor locations with foot traffic on multiple sides.</p>

<p>All finishing includes extra material beyond your artwork dimensions, so no bleeds are required in your design files.</p>

<h2>Easter Banner Artwork Requirements for Printing</h2>

<p>Proper artwork preparation ensures your Easter banner prints with sharp text, vibrant colors, and professional appearance. Follow these guidelines for optimal results.</p>

<h3>Artwork Guidelines</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Resolution:</strong> 150 DPI minimum for crisp text and imagery at final banner size</li>
<li><strong>File Formats:</strong> PDF, AI, EPS, or high-resolution JPG/PNG files preferred</li>
<li><strong>Color Mode:</strong> CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction</li>
<li><strong>Fonts:</strong> Convert text to outlines or include font files with artwork</li>
</ul>

<h3>Easter Banner Design Considerations</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Text Size:</strong> Minimum 2-inch letter height for outdoor viewing distances</li>
<li><strong>Color Contrast:</strong> High contrast between text and background for readability</li>
<li><strong>Image Quality:</strong> High-resolution photos and graphics prevent pixelation</li>
<li><strong>Safe Zone:</strong> Keep important elements 3+ inches from edges when using pole pockets</li>
</ul>

<h3>Order Proofs</h3>
<p>Every Easter banner includes a digital proof showing exactly how your banner will print. Review your proof carefully for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Text accuracy and spelling</li>
<li>Color appearance and vibrancy</li>
<li>Layout and element positioning</li>
<li>Overall design composition</li>
</ul>

<h3>Color Match Proof System</h3>
<p>Our color management system ensures consistent, accurate color reproduction from proof to final printed Easter banner. Colors displayed in your digital proof represent the final printed result.</p>

<h2>Easter Banner Printing Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>How long do Easter banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our Easter banners are made with weather-resistant 13oz vinyl and UV-resistant inks designed to withstand outdoor conditions for several years. For short-term Easter season display (4-8 weeks), you can expect excellent color retention and durability.</p>

<h3>Can I get my Easter banner in time for Easter Sunday?</h3>
<p>Yes, with our rush production options. Choose 1-day or 2-day production and expedited shipping to receive your Easter banner quickly. Contact customer service for specific timeline requirements based on your location.</p>

<h3>What size Easter banner do I need for my church?</h3>
<p>Banner size depends on viewing distance and installation location. For outdoor church signs viewed from the street, 4'x8' or larger works well. For indoor sanctuary displays, 3'x6' provides good visibility. Our team can help determine the ideal size for your specific location.</p>

<h3>Do you offer Easter banner templates?</h3>
<p>Yes, we have Easter banner templates that you can customize with your church information, service times, and personal touches. You can also upload your own artwork or use our professional design service.</p>

<h3>Can Easter banners be double-sided?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can create double-sided Easter banners up to 150 square feet. Double-sided banners are perfect for hanging displays or installations where the banner will be viewed from both sides.</p>

<h3>How do I hang my Easter banner?</h3>
<p>Standard Easter banners include grommets every 2-4 feet for easy hanging with rope, bungee cords, or zip ties. Pole pocket finishing is available for pole mounting. We also offer banner stands and mounting hardware.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for Easter banner artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept PDF, AI, EPS, and high-resolution JPG or PNG files. For best results, provide artwork in CMYK color mode at 150 DPI resolution. Need help with artwork? Our design team can create custom Easter banner designs.</p>
