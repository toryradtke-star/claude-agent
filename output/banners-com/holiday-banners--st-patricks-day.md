<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About St. Patrick's Day Banners</h2>

<h3>What sizes are available for St. Patrick's Day banners?</h3>
<p>We offer custom St. Patrick's Day banners in sizes ranging from 1'x1' up to 8'x50'. Popular sizes include 3'x6', 4'x8', and 6'x10' for most business and event applications. Custom dimensions are available to fit your specific display requirements.</p>

<h3>How long do St. Patrick's Day banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl St. Patrick's Day banners are designed for outdoor durability and typically maintain their appearance for 2-3 years with proper installation. The UV-resistant inks and weather-resistant vinyl construction help preserve colors and prevent fading during outdoor display.</p>

<h3>Can I use the same banner for multiple years?</h3>
<p>Yes, vinyl St. Patrick's Day banners can be reused for multiple holiday seasons. After your event, clean the banner with mild soap and water, allow it to dry completely, and store it rolled or folded in a cool, dry location.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between rolled and folded shipping?</h3>
<p>Banners under 8 feet in both dimensions ship rolled in a protective tube to prevent creasing. Larger banners must be folded due to shipping carrier size restrictions. Fold lines typically relax after hanging, but freight shipping is available if folding must be avoided.</p>

<h3>Do you provide design services for custom St. Patrick's Day banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house graphic design team can create custom St. Patrick's Day banner designs using your logos, text, and specifications. Design services include layout creation, color optimization, and artwork preparation for optimal print quality.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order my St. Patrick's Day banner?</h3>
<p>We recommend ordering at least 7-10 business days before your event, especially during peak St. Patrick's Day season in late February and early March. Rush production options are available for urgent orders, with same-day completion possible for orders placed before 11 AM CST.</p>

<h3>What payment methods do you accept for banner orders?</h3>
<p>We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, business checks (with approved credit), wire transfers, and purchase orders from approved business accounts. Net 30 payment terms are available for qualified business customers.</p>

<h3>Can I get a proof before my St. Patrick's Day banner is printed?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide digital proofs for all custom St. Patrick's Day banner orders. Proofs are typically ready within 2-4 business hours and must be approved before production begins. This ensures your banner meets your expectations before printing.</p>
