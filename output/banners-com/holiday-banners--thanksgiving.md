<h2>Thanksgiving Banner Artwork Requirements for Holiday Printing</h2>

<p>Professional artwork guidelines to ensure your custom Thanksgiving banner prints with optimal quality and vibrant holiday colors.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit Thanksgiving banner artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats. PNG and JPEG files accepted for simple designs. Vector formats preferred for text and graphic elements that scale cleanly.</p>

<h3>Resolution & Size Specifications</h3>
<p>Provide artwork at actual banner size with minimum 150 DPI resolution. For large Thanksgiving banners, artwork can be submitted at 25% or 50% scale with proportionally higher resolution (300-600 DPI).</p>

<h3>Color Profile for Holiday Printing</h3>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate Thanksgiving banner color reproduction. RGB files are converted during processing, which may result in slight color variations from screen display.</p>

<h3>Text & Typography Guidelines</h3>
<p>Ensure all fonts are outlined or embedded in Thanksgiving banner files. Maintain minimum 1-inch text height for outdoor viewing distances. Bold, high-contrast fonts work best for holiday banner readability.</p>

<h3>Bleed & Safety Areas</h3>
<p>Include 2-inch bleed area around all edges for proper finishing. Keep important text and graphics at least 4 inches from banner edges to account for grommets and finishing options.</p>

<p>Our design team reviews all Thanksgiving banner artwork and provides feedback if modifications are needed for optimal printing results. Complimentary design assistance available for complex holiday graphics.</p>
