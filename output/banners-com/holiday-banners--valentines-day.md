<p>Professional artwork guidelines ensuring optimal print quality for your Valentine banner:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Accept high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and PNG files. Vector formats preferred for Valentine's Day banner graphics requiring scaling and text elements.</p>

<h3>Resolution Standards</h3>
<p>Minimum 150 DPI at actual Valentine banner size for optimal print quality. Higher resolution files ensure crisp text and detailed graphics.</p>

<h3>Color Specifications</h3>
<p>CMYK color mode recommended for accurate Valentine banner color reproduction. RGB files accepted but may experience slight color variation in printing.</p>

<h3>Design Guidelines</h3>
<ul>
<li>Include 1-2 inch bleed area around banner edges</li>
<li>Keep important text 2-3 inches from edges</li>
<li>Use fonts larger than 24pt for readability</li>
<li>Embed or outline all fonts in final files</li>
<li>Avoid small detailed elements that may not reproduce clearly</li>
</ul>

<h3>Template Library</h3>
<p>Professional Valentine's Day banner templates available through our online designer. Fully customizable with your text, colors, and branding elements.</p>

<h2>Valentine's Day Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material is best for outdoor Valentine's Day banners?</h3>
<p>For outdoor Valentine displays, we recommend 13oz or 18oz vinyl with grommets for secure installation. These materials resist weather, UV rays, and maintain color vibrancy throughout the Valentine's season.</p>

<h3>How long do Valentine's Day banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl Valentine banners typically last 2-3 years outdoors with proper installation and care. UV-resistant inks and weather-resistant materials ensure your investment provides long-term value.</p>

<h3>Can I use my own Valentine's Day artwork?</h3>
<p>Yes, we accept custom artwork in PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and PNG formats. Our design team reviews all files and will contact you if any adjustments are needed for optimal printing results.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for Valentine banners?</h3>
<p>Standard sizes range from 1'x1' to 8'x50'. Custom sizes available through special quote requests. Large banners may require freight shipping to avoid folding.</p>

<h3>How quickly can Valentine's Day banners be produced?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3-5 business days. Rush service available for 1-2 day production. Same-day service possible for select sizes - contact us to verify availability.</p>

<h3>Do you offer Valentine's Day banner templates?</h3>
<p>Yes, our online designer includes professional Valentine's Day templates that you can customize with your text, colors, and branding elements.</p>

<h3>What finishing options work best for Valentine banners?</h3>
<p>Grommets are most popular for outdoor installations using rope or bungee cords. Pole pockets work well for indoor displays using rods or mounting systems. We can recommend the best option for your specific installation.</p>

<h3>Can Valentine banners be reused for future years?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! With proper storage, our Valentine's Day banners maintain their quality for multiple seasons. Roll loosely and store in a cool, dry location to prevent creasing.</p>

<h3>Do you ship Valentine banners internationally?</h3>
<p>We ship to Canada and select international destinations. International customers are responsible for customs documentation and any applicable duties or fees.</p>

<h3>What if my Valentine banner arrives damaged?</h3>
