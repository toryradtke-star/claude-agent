<h2>Homecoming Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material works best for outdoor homecoming banners?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl material is ideal for outdoor homecoming displays. It's weather-resistant, fade-resistant, and durable enough to withstand wind and rain during football games and outdoor celebrations.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order my homecoming banner?</h3>
<p>We recommend ordering 2-3 weeks before your event to allow time for design, production, and shipping. Rush production is available for urgent orders, but advance planning ensures the best pricing and design options.</p>

<h3>Can you match our official school colors exactly?</h3>
<p>Yes! Provide us with Pantone color codes or physical color samples, and our printing process will match your official school colors accurately. We understand how important precise color matching is for school branding.</p>

<h3>What's the largest homecoming banner size you can produce?</h3>
<p>We can produce banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet. For gymnasium displays and large stadium banners, we offer seamless printing that creates impressive displays perfect for major school celebrations.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for schools without artwork?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our graphic design team specializes in school banners and can create custom homecoming designs using your school colors, mascot, and celebration theme. Design services are available for an additional fee.</p>

<h3>How do large homecoming banners ship?</h3>
<p>Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be folded for standard shipping. For crease-free delivery of large gymnasium banners, we offer freight shipping options. Contact us for freight quotes on oversized orders.</p>

<h3>Can homecoming banners be reused for multiple years?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our vinyl banners are designed for durability and can be stored and reused for multiple homecoming celebrations. Avoid designs with specific years if you plan to reuse your banner annually.</p>

<h3>What finishing option works best for gymnasium hanging?</h3>
<p>Pole pockets provide the cleanest look for indoor gymnasium displays. They slide onto banner poles or mounting hardware without visible grommets, creating a professional appearance for formal homecoming presentations.</p>
