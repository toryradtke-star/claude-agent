<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Hotel Banners</h2>

<h3>What sizes are available for hotel banners?</h3>
<p>We offer custom hotel banners in sizes ranging from 1'x1' up to 8'x50', with custom sizing available for unique hotel installations. Popular hotel banner sizes include 3'x6', 4'x8', and 6'x10' for building facades and promotional displays.</p>

<h3>How long do hotel banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our premium vinyl hotel banners are designed for outdoor durability, typically lasting 2-3 years in outdoor conditions with UV-resistant inks and weather-resistant materials. Indoor hotel banners can last 5+ years with proper care.</p>

<h3>Can you match our hotel's brand colors exactly?</h3>
<p>Yes, we specialize in matching hotel brand colors using Pantone color specifications and can work with your brand guidelines to ensure accurate color reproduction that meets corporate standards.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for hotel marketing banners?</h3>
<p>Our in-house graphic design team provides professional design services specifically for hospitality marketing, including custom hotel banner design, brand compliance review, and artwork optimization for large format printing.</p>

<h3>What's the fastest turnaround time for urgent hotel banner needs?</h3>
<p>Rush production is available with next-day turnaround for urgent hotel marketing needs, plus expedited shipping options for critical deadlines like grand openings or special events.</p>

<h3>Can large hotel banners be shipped without folding?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer custom freight shipping for large hotel banners to avoid folding. Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions are folded for standard shipping, but freight delivery keeps banners rolled for premium presentation.</p>

<h3>Do you work with hotel chains and management companies?</h3>
<p>We provide comprehensive services for hotel chains including corporate account setup, multi-location shipping, volume pricing, and brand compliance support for franchise requirements.</p>

<h3>What artwork do I need to provide for hotel banner printing?</h3>
<p>High-resolution artwork files (150 DPI minimum) in vector formats (AI, EPS, PDF) or high-quality raster files (TIFF, PNG) work best. Our design team provides free artwork review and can optimize files for professional hotel banner printing.</p>
