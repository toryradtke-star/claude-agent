<h2>Indoor Banner Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What material is used for indoor banners?</h3>
<p>Our indoor banners are made from premium 13 oz vinyl material specifically designed for indoor use. This material provides excellent print quality, durability, and a professional appearance perfect for trade shows and events.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for custom indoor banners?</h3>
<p>We offer custom indoor banners in sizes ranging from 1'x1' up to 8'x50'. Custom sizes are available to meet your specific display requirements.</p>

<h3>How long does it take to produce an indoor banner?</h3>
<p>Production times vary based on your needs: rush production (1-2 business days), standard production (3-5 business days), or economy production (7-10 business days). Production begins after artwork approval.</p>

<h3>What finishing options are available?</h3>
<p>We offer hemmed edges, grommets, pole pockets, and velcro strips. Custom finishing options are available based on your installation requirements.</p>

<h3>Can you help with banner design?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our professional design team can create custom artwork, enhance existing logos, or modify your designs for optimal print quality.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for artwork?</h3>
<p>We prefer PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD files at 150 DPI minimum resolution. We also accept JPG, PNG, and TIFF formats. CMYK color mode is recommended for best color accuracy.</p>

<h3>How will my banner be shipped?</h3>
<p>Smaller banners are rolled and shipped in protective tubes. Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions are folded for standard shipping. Freight shipping is available if you prefer your large banner not to be folded.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for urgent events?</h3>
<p>Yes, rush production is available for 1-2 business day turnaround, subject to availability and may require additional fees.</p>
