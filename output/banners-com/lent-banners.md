<h2>Lent Banner Artwork Requirements for Church Printing</h2>

<p>Professional artwork specifications ensure your Lent banner prints with optimal quality and visual impact. Our design team provides technical support to help churches achieve the best results for their seasonal messaging.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats for best print quality. JPEG and PNG files acceptable for simple designs but may limit color accuracy in complex Lent banner artwork.</p>

<h3>Resolution and Size Guidelines</h3>
<p>Provide artwork at actual banner size with minimum 150 DPI resolution. For large Lent banners, artwork can be created at 50% size with 300 DPI resolution while maintaining print quality.</p>

<h3>Color Requirements for Church Banners</h3>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate print reproduction. Traditional Lenten colors include deep purple, burgundy, and gold. RGB colors may shift during printing - CMYK ensures color consistency.</p>

<h3>Text and Font Considerations</h3>
<p>Choose readable fonts for scripture verses and church messaging. Minimum text size of 2 inches for optimal readability from typical church viewing distances. Outline all fonts to prevent substitution issues.</p>

<h3>Bleed and Safety Areas</h3>
<p>Include 2-inch bleed area around all artwork edges. Keep important text and design elements at least 4 inches from banner edges to account for finishing and installation hardware.</p>

<h3>Design Services for Churches</h3>
<p>Professional design assistance available for churches without graphic design capabilities. Our team creates custom Lent banner artwork based on your seasonal themes, scripture selections, and church branding requirements.</p>

<h3>Artwork Approval Process</h3>
<p>Digital proofs provided within 24 hours showing your Lent banner design at actual size and colors. Unlimited revisions included until your church approves the final artwork for production.</p>

<h3>Copyright and Religious Imagery</h3>
<p>Churches are responsible for copyright permissions on submitted artwork. We provide royalty-free religious clip art and stock imagery suitable for Lenten themes when design services are requested.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Lent Banners</h2>

<h3>What size Lent banner is best for our church sanctuary?</h3>
<p>For most church sanctuaries, banners between 3'x6' and 4'x8' provide excellent visibility without overwhelming the worship space. Consider your ceiling height, altar area, and viewing distance when selecting size. Our team can provide size recommendations based on your specific sanctuary dimensions.</p>

<h3>How long will our Lent banner last for seasonal use?</h3>
<p>With proper storage between seasons, your vinyl Lent banner should last 5-10 years of annual use. Indoor banners typically last longer than outdoor installations. Store rolled (not folded) in a dry location to maximize banner lifespan.</p>

<h3>Can you match our church's specific purple color for Lenten themes?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match specific Pantone colors or color samples you provide. Traditional Lenten colors reproduce well on vinyl materials. Submit color references with your order or work with our design team to achieve your desired color scheme.</p>

<h3>What's the best material for outdoor Lent banner display?</h3>
<p>For outdoor church installations, we recommend 13oz or 18oz vinyl with wind slits and reinforced grommets. Mesh material works well in high-wind areas. All outdoor materials are UV-resistant and weather-resistant for spring conditions during Lent.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for churches without artwork?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Our professional design team creates custom Lent banner artwork based on your themes, scripture verses, and church branding. Simply provide your text, preferred colors, and any specific imagery requests when placing your order.</p>

<h3>How do we hang large Lent banners in our sanctuary?</h3>
<p>Installation depends on your banner size and sanctuary architecture. Options include pole pockets for banner poles, grommets for rope or cable hanging, or Velcro strips for permanent mounting systems. Contact us for installation guidance specific to your church.</p>

<h3>Can we order multiple Lent banners for different church locations?</h3>
<p>Yes, we frequently work with multi-campus churches and denominational orders. Volume pricing available for multiple banner orders. We can coordinate production and shipping to ensure all locations receive their banners simultaneously.</p>

<h3>What if our Lent banner arrives damaged?</h3>
<p>All banners are carefully packaged and insured during shipping. If damage occurs during transit, contact us immediately for replacement. We stand behind our quality and will resolve any shipping damage issues quickly to meet your church timeline.</p>
