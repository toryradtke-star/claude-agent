<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Magnetic Signs</h2>

<h3>What are custom magnetic signs used for?</h3>
<p>Custom magnetic signs are primarily used for vehicle advertising, business promotions, and temporary signage. They're perfect for contractors, real estate agents, delivery services, and any business needing removable advertising on vehicles or magnetic surfaces like refrigerators and filing cabinets.</p>

<h3>What surfaces do magnetic signs stick to?</h3>
<p>Magnetic signs adhere to ferrous metal surfaces including steel vehicle panels, refrigerators, filing cabinets, and metal doors. They will not stick to aluminum, plastic, fiberglass, or non-magnetic surfaces commonly found on some newer vehicles.</p>

<h3>Are your custom magnetic signs compatible with vehicles?</h3>
<p>Yes, our magnetic signs are specifically designed for vehicle use with durable 30 mil magnetic material and protective lamination. However, they only work on steel vehicle panels. Many newer vehicles have aluminum or plastic body panels where magnets won't adhere.</p>

<h3>What cut options do you offer for custom magnetic signs?</h3>
<p>We offer rounded corners (recommended for vehicles), square cut, and contour cut options. Rounded corners provide better aerodynamics and reduce wind lift, making them ideal for vehicle applications.</p>

<h3>Are your magnetic signs printed in full color?</h3>
<p>Yes, all our custom magnetic signs are printed in full color using digital printing technology. We can reproduce vibrant graphics, photographs, and detailed designs with excellent color accuracy.</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk or quantity discounts on magnetic signs?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer competitive pricing for bulk orders. Contact our customer service team at (320) 391-5300 to discuss quantity pricing for your specific magnetic sign needs.</p>

<h3>Are magnetic signs weather-resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes, our magnetic signs include protective lamination that makes them weather-resistant and suitable for outdoor vehicle use. The lamination protects against UV rays, moisture, and general weather exposure.</p>

<h3>How long do magnetic signs last?</h3>
<p>With proper care and storage, our laminated magnetic signs can last several years. Lifespan depends on usage conditions, but they typically maintain their appearance and magnetic strength for 3-5 years with regular vehicle use.</p>

<h3>Can I write on a magnetic sign?</h3>
<p>The laminated surface of our magnetic signs is not designed for writing. If you need writable signage, consider our dry erase magnetic products or contact customer service to discuss custom solutions.</p>

<h3>Will magnetic signs damage my vehicle paint?</h3>
<p>When properly applied and regularly cleaned, magnetic signs should not damage vehicle paint. We recommend removing signs periodically for cleaning both the sign and vehicle surface, especially in harsh weather conditions.</p>
