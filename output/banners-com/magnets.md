<h2>Custom Magnet Material Specifications & Surface Compatibility</h2>

<p>Our custom magnets are manufactured using premium materials designed for durability and professional presentation across various applications.</p>

<p><strong>Standard 30 Mil Magnetic Material:</strong> Our primary magnetic substrate provides strong adhesion to ferromagnetic surfaces including steel, iron, nickel, and cobalt. This material offers reliable holding power for vehicle applications and indoor magnetic surfaces.</p>

<p><strong>Reflective 30 Mil Magnetic Material:</strong> Enhanced visibility option featuring reflective properties for improved safety and visibility during low-light conditions, particularly beneficial for vehicle advertising applications.</p>

<p><strong>Laminated Surface Protection:</strong> Both material options include water-resistant and scratch-resistant laminated surfaces that protect your custom graphics from outdoor elements and daily handling.</p>

<p><strong>Surface Compatibility:</strong> Magnets adhere to ferromagnetic metals only. They will not stick to aluminum, fiberglass, carbon fiber, plastic, or most non-magnetic stainless steel surfaces. We recommend testing a small area before full application on vehicle surfaces.</p>

<h2>Custom Magnet Artwork Requirements & Design Guidelines</h2>

<p>Proper artwork preparation ensures optimal print quality and professional results for your custom magnet project.</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements:</strong> Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, or EPS formats with all fonts outlined and images embedded. JPEG and PNG files acceptable for simple designs with 300 DPI minimum resolution.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications:</strong> Design files should use CMYK color mode for accurate print reproduction. RGB colors will be converted to CMYK during production, which may result in slight color variations.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safety Areas:</strong> Include 0.125" bleed area around all edges for trimmed designs. Keep important text and graphics at least 0.25" from finished edges to prevent trimming during production.</p>

<p><strong>Design Assistance:</strong> Our in-house design team can assist with artwork creation, file preparation, or design modifications. Simple text-based layouts and basic graphic adjustments available at no additional charge.</p>

<p><strong>Proof Approval:</strong> Digital proofs provided for all custom magnet orders before production begins. Review colors, text, and layout carefully as production proceeds after proof approval.</p>

<h2>Custom Magnet Production & Delivery Information</h2>

<h3>Magnetic Material Compatibility with Vehicle Surfaces</h3>
<p>Magnets only stick to magnetic (ferromagnetic) metal, such as steel, iron, nickel, and cobalt. They won't stick to aluminum, fiberglass, carbon fiber, plastic, or most non-magnetic stainless steel.</p>

<h3>Custom Magnet Construction & Material Specifications</h3>
<p>Our magnets are made with standard 30 mil magnetic material or reflective 30 mil magnetic material. Both options are finished with a water- and scratch-resistant laminated surface for added durability.</p>

<h3>Weather Resistance for Outdoor Custom Magnet Applications</h3>
<p>They're water-resistant, not fully waterproof. Light rain and normal outdoor use are fine, but we recommend removing magnets before car washes and avoiding trapped moisture underneath.</p>

<h3>Custom Magnet Durability & Lifespan Expectations</h3>
<p>With proper use and care, our magnets can last up to 5 years.</p>

<h3>Minimum Order Requirements for Custom Magnet Orders</h3>
<p>No. There's no minimum order—you can order one magnet or buy in bulk.</p>

<h3>Bulk Pricing Options for Large Custom Magnet Orders</h3>
<p>Yes. Pricing is quantity-based—ordering more lowers the per-unit cost (you'll see the discount update as you increase quantity).</p>

<h3>Full Color Printing Capabilities for Custom Magnets</h3>
<p>Yes. All magnets are printed in full CMYK color.</p>

<h3>Writing & Cleaning Instructions for Custom Magnets</h3>
<p>Yes. Use Sharpie for permanent text and dry-erase markers for temporary notes. To remove marker, clean gently with mild soap/water or a light cleaning solution (test a small area first).</p>

<h3>Common Business Applications for Custom Magnets</h3>
<p>Popular uses include car/truck magnets for advertising, business card magnets for handouts or mailers, and refrigerator magnets for calendars, reminders, lists, and photos.</p>
