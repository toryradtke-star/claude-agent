**Six Customizable Banner Materials**

Your banner is made with the finest materials available and [printed using state-of-the-art, large-format printers](https://www.banners.com/banner-printing) for vibrant colors that pop. All banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.

Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature-sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Please note our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (ex., a 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please ask your customer service representative to discuss your specific application.

**13 oz Matte Vinyl Banners**

Our high-quality 13 oz matte vinyl banner material is our most popular banner material. It has a low shine and rich color gamut.

**13 oz Gloss Vinyl Banners**

Our high-quality 13 oz gloss vinyl banner material has a high shine to it that produces vibrant, crisp colors and graphics to give it a high-end look.

**18 oz Vinyl Banners**

Our extra heavy-duty matte 18 oz vinyl banner material is our toughest material. It has a low luster, blockout coating for extra durability and print quality.

**Mesh Banners**

Made with 8 oz PVC polyester mesh material that has a criss-crossed breathable scrim that allows a 37% airflow. Mesh banners are ideal for outdoor and windy conditions.

**Fabric Banners**

Made with a premium nylon/polyester blend material called Banner Soft. This fabric is ideal for indoor displays and has a low shine that produces a high-end look and feel.

**Adhesive Banners**

Standard Tack Vinyl: Standard tack vinyl can be used both indoors and outdoors, with an outdoor lifespan of 3 to 5 years when properly installed and maintained.

Extreme Tack Vinyl: Extreme Tack vinyl is white calendered vinyl material with a high tack adhesive that is specifically designed to adhere to low energy surfaces.

**Heat Welded Hems & Grommets**

Hems consist of one inch of the vinyl banner material folded over and heated together to form a strong hem. We recommend hems for all banners to increase durability.

You will have the following options when selecting hems and grommets:

**Top and Bottom Only Hems** – Standard finishing option for vinyl banners.

**All Four Sides Hems** – All 4 sides of the banner will be heat welded and grommeted.

![Top and Bottom Grommets Only Example](https://web2printdata.blob.core.windows.net/w2p-cms-1/24-09-04-BANS-Top-and-bottom-hems-and-grommets-only-2.png)

![All 4 Sides Hems & Grommets Example](https://web2printdata.blob.core.windows.net/w2p-cms-1/24-09-04-BANS-Top-and-bottom-hems-and-grommets-all-sides.png)

**Pole Pockets**

Pole pockets consist of 2, 3, or 4 inches of material wrapped around the sides, top, or bottom of the banner.

*   **2" Pole Pockets** – fits a pole 3/4" in diameter
*   **3" Pole Pockets** – fits a pole 1 1/4" in diameter
*   **4" Pole Pockets** – fits a pole 1 1/2" in diameter

By selecting pole pockets, there will be no increase in the finished size of your banner.

_Pole pockets are not available with reinforced hems or corners._

**Reinforced Corners**

Reinforced corners provide extra strength and are grommeted to each corner of the banner.

**Reinforced Hems**

Reinforced hems are a PVC-coated webbing which increases the strength of the banner.

_You cannot have reinforced hems with pole pockets._
