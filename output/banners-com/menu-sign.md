<h2>Menu Banner Questions & Installation Support</h2>

<h3>What size menu banner works best for restaurants?</h3>
<p>Popular sizes include 3'x6' for storefront displays, 2'x4' for drive-thru menus, and 4'x8' for building-mounted installations. The optimal size depends on viewing distance and available space.</p>

<h3>How long do vinyl menu banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl menu banners typically last 3-5 years outdoors with proper installation and care. Indoor banners can last much longer when protected from direct sunlight and weather.</p>

<h3>Can I update my menu banner design for seasonal specials?</h3>
<p>Yes, we save all customer designs for easy reordering. You can modify text, images, and pricing for new seasonal menus while keeping the same layout and branding.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to install a menu banner outdoors?</h3>
<p>Use the provided grommets with bungee cords or banner rope. Ensure adequate support points every 2-3 feet to prevent wind damage. Consider wind slits for high-wind locations.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for menu banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house graphic designers can create custom menu layouts, incorporate your branding, and optimize the design for maximum readability and customer appeal.</p>

<h3>Can menu banners be washed or cleaned?</h3>
<p>Yes, vinyl menu banners can be cleaned with mild soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals or pressure washing that might damage the printed surface or finishing.</p>

<h3>What file format should I use for menu artwork?</h3>
<p>PDF files work best for menu designs with text and pricing. Ensure your file is at least 150 DPI resolution and includes a 2-inch bleed area around all edges.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for menu banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer same-day and next-day production options for urgent restaurant promotions. Contact us before 10 AM CST for same-day production availability.</p>
