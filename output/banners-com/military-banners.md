<p><strong>Resolution Standards</strong> - Military banners require minimum 150 DPI at actual print size to ensure crisp text, clear military insignia, and professional presentation quality.</p>

<p><strong>Color Profile Setup</strong> - CMYK color mode recommended for military banners to ensure accurate color reproduction of military colors, flags, and official insignia.</p>

<p><strong>Military Insignia Guidelines</strong> - Official military logos, unit crests, and branch insignia must be provided as vector files or high-resolution images for proper reproduction.</p>

<p><strong>Text Legibility Standards</strong> - Military banner text should be minimum 1 inch tall for outdoor viewing distances, with bold fonts recommended for maximum readability.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed Area Requirements</strong> - Include 1/4 inch bleed area around military banner designs to account for finishing and trimming during production process.</p>

<p><strong>Design Review Process</strong> - Professional design team reviews all military banner artwork for print quality, military regulation compliance, and visual impact before production.</p>

<h2>Military Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What materials work best for outdoor military banners?</h3>
<p>13oz and 18oz vinyl materials provide the best durability for outdoor military events and long-term displays. These materials resist weather, UV exposure, and maintain vibrant colors for extended outdoor use.</p>

<h3>Can you print official military insignia on banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can print official military logos and insignia when you provide high-resolution artwork files. We recommend vector files or images at 300 DPI for best reproduction quality.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for military banners?</h3>
<p>Military banners are available from 1'x1' up to 8'x50' in standard production. Custom sizes can be quoted for special requirements or unique display needs.</p>

<h3>How long do military banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper care, vinyl military banners typically last 2-5 years outdoors depending on weather exposure and UV conditions. Indoor banners can last significantly longer.</p>

<h3>Do you offer military discounts?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide special pricing for active duty military, veterans, and military family organizations. Contact our customer service team for discount verification and application.</p>

<h3>Can military banners be double-sided?</h3>
<p>Double-sided printing is available on select banner materials for military applications requiring visibility from both directions during parades and events.</p>

<h3>What's the fastest turnaround time for military banners?</h3>
<p>Rush production options are available including same-day service for simple designs ordered before 10 AM CST. Standard production is 3-5 business days after artwork approval.</p>

<h3>How are large military banners shipped?</h3>
<p>Military banners larger than 8'x8' are professionally folded for shipping unless custom freight service is requested for unfolded delivery.</p>
