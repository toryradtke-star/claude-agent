<h2>Mini Golf Banner Finishing & Mounting Options</h2>

<p>Professional finishing options ensure your mini golf banners install easily and maintain their appearance throughout their lifespan.</p>

<h3>Grommet Finishing</h3>
<p>Reinforced metal grommets placed every 2 feet around the perimeter provide secure mounting points for rope, bungee cords, or zip ties. Ideal for fence mounting and temporary installations.</p>

<h3>Pole Pocket Finishing</h3>
<p>Sewn pockets along the top, bottom, or sides allow for clean pole mounting without visible hardware. Perfect for professional displays and permanent installations.</p>

<h3>Hemmed Edges</h3>
<p>Double-folded and sewn edges provide added strength and prevent fraying. Recommended for long-term outdoor use and high-traffic areas.</p>

<h3>Wind Slits</h3>
<p>Strategic cuts in the banner material reduce wind resistance and prevent tearing in outdoor installations. Particularly useful for large mini golf course entrance banners.</p>

<p><strong>Installation Considerations:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Select finishing options based on your mounting method</li>
<li>Consider wind exposure for outdoor installations</li>
<li>Hemmed edges extend banner lifespan in outdoor conditions</li>
<li>Multiple finishing options can be combined on single banners</li>
</ul>

<h2>Mini Golf Banner Questions & Support</h2>

<h3>What material works best for outdoor mini golf course banners?</h3>
<p>For outdoor installations, we recommend 13oz or 18oz vinyl banner material with hemmed edges and appropriate wind resistance features. Mesh vinyl is ideal for windy locations as it allows air flow while maintaining visibility.</p>

<h3>Can you create custom hole marker banners with numbers and themes?</h3>
<p>Yes, our design team can create custom hole marker banners incorporating your course theme, hole numbers, par information, and any special graphics or branding elements you require.</p>

<h3>What's the largest mini golf banner size you can produce?</h3>
<p>We can produce banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet. Larger custom sizes may be available through special quotes. Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be folded for standard shipping unless freight shipping is selected.</p>

<h3>How long do mini golf banners typically last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper installation and care, our vinyl banners can last 2-3 years outdoors with UV-resistant inks and quality materials. Indoor applications typically last much longer due to reduced weather exposure.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation services for mini golf course banners?</h3>
<p>We focus on manufacturing and shipping banners. For installation, we recommend working with local sign installers or following our provided installation guidelines for proper mounting.</p>

<h3>Can I get a proof before my mini golf banners are printed?</h3>
<p>Digital proofs are provided for custom design work. If you're uploading print-ready artwork, our team will review files and contact you if any technical issues need to be addressed before production.</p>

<h3>What payment methods do you accept for mini golf banner orders?</h3>
<p>We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, business checks for approved accounts, and purchase orders for qualifying organizations.</p>

<h3>Can you match specific colors for our mini golf course branding?</h3>
<p>We can work with Pantone color references and existing brand guidelines to achieve the closest possible color match using our CMYK printing process. Exact color matching may vary due to material and printing limitations.</p>
