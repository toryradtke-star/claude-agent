<h2>Museum Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>What materials work best for indoor museum exhibitions?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl banner material is ideal for indoor museum and gallery environments. For upscale cultural venues, consider our fabric banner options for a premium appearance. Both materials provide excellent color reproduction for cultural and educational content.</p>

<h3>Can you accommodate tight exhibition deadlines?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer rush production options including same-day and next-day turnaround for urgent museum banner needs. Rush options are available for most sizes and designs to meet critical exhibition deadlines.</p>

<h3>Do you work with cultural institutions and museums?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. We regularly serve museums, galleries, universities, and cultural organizations. We offer institutional billing, purchase order processing, and tax exemptions for qualifying educational and cultural institutions.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to hang museum banners in exhibition spaces?</h3>
<p>Hem and grommets provide secure mounting for most exhibition environments. For rotating displays, consider Velcro attachment options. Pole pockets work well for clean installations with display hardware. Our team can recommend the best finishing for your specific cultural venue.</p>

<h3>Can you help with design for cultural content?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house design team has experience creating banners for cultural institutions and educational organizations. We can ensure your museum banner meets institutional brand guidelines and cultural presentation standards.</p>

<h3>What resolution do you need for museum banner artwork?</h3>
<p>We require minimum 150 DPI at full size for exhibition-quality printing. Files should be in CMYK color mode with all fonts outlined. Our design team will review your artwork to ensure it meets professional exhibition standards.</p>

<h3>Do you ship to cultural institutions internationally?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide international shipping to cultural institutions and museums worldwide. Additional customs documentation may be required for educational materials. Contact us for international shipping options to cultural venues.</p>

<h3>What size limitations exist for museum banners?</h3>
<p>We can produce museum banners up to 8'x50' in size. Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be folded for standard shipping. Freight shipping is available if you prefer your large museum banners shipped unfolded for exhibition use.</p>
