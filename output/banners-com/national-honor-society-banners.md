<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About National Honor Society Banners</h2>

<h3>What size NHS banner should I order for our school ceremony?</h3>
<p>For indoor ceremonies and hallway displays, 3'x8' or 4'x8' banners provide excellent visibility. For outdoor graduation ceremonies or large auditoriums, consider 4'x10' or larger sizes to ensure readability from distance.</p>

<h3>Can you match our school's official colors on the NHS banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match official school colors using Pantone color specifications. Provide your school's Pantone color codes or color samples, and our printing process will accurately reproduce them on your NHS banner.</p>

<h3>How long does production take for National Honor Society banners?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3-5 business days after artwork approval. Rush production (1-2 days) is available for urgent chapter events. Production time begins after final design approval and payment confirmation.</p>

<h3>Do you accept school purchase orders for NHS banner orders?</h3>
<p>Yes, we accept purchase orders from qualified educational institutions with NET 30 terms. We also honor tax-exempt status for schools and offer educational pricing for large orders.</p>

<h3>What's included with NHS banner finishing options?</h3>
<p>Standard finishing includes hemmed edges and rust-resistant grommets every 2-3 feet. We also offer pole pockets, wind slits, and custom grommeting placement for specific school installation needs.</p>

<h3>Can you design our NHS banner if we don't have artwork?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our professional graphic designers can create custom NHS banner layouts incorporating your school colors, logos, and National Honor Society elements. Design services are available for an additional fee.</p>

<h3>Will my NHS banner work for both indoor and outdoor school events?</h3>
<p>Yes, our vinyl banner material is suitable for both indoor displays and outdoor ceremonies. The weather-resistant, fade-resistant material maintains its professional appearance in various school environments.</p>

<h3>How should I care for and store my NHS banner between events?</h3>
<p>Clean with mild soap and water, allow to dry completely, then roll (don't fold) for storage. Store in a cool, dry place away from sharp objects to preserve your banner for multiple chapter uses.</p>
