<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Non-Profit Banners</h2>

<h3>What material works best for outdoor fundraising events?</h3>
<p>Our 13 oz. vinyl banner material provides excellent durability for most outdoor fundraising events, while 18 oz. heavy-duty vinyl is recommended for extended campaigns or harsh weather conditions. Mesh material works well for windy locations.</p>

<h3>Do you offer discounts for non-profit organizations?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide special pricing considerations for qualified 501(c)(3) organizations, particularly on bulk orders and recurring campaigns. Contact our customer service team to discuss non-profit pricing options.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order banners for my fundraising event?</h3>
<p>We recommend ordering at least one week before your event to allow for design consultation, production, and shipping. Rush options are available for urgent needs, including same-day production for orders placed before 11 AM CST.</p>

<h3>Can you help design banners that include sponsor logos?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our graphic design team specializes in creating professional layouts that incorporate multiple sponsor logos while maintaining clear campaign messaging and visual hierarchy.</p>

<h3>What's the best banner size for volunteer recruitment displays?</h3>
<p>For volunteer recruitment, 3'x8' or 4'x8' banners provide excellent visibility while being manageable for transport between locations. Larger sizes like 4'x12' work well for high-traffic permanent installations.</p>

<h3>How do I ensure my banner text is readable from a distance?</h3>
<p>We recommend minimum 2-inch text height for banners viewed from 10+ feet. Bold, sans-serif fonts in high contrast colors (dark text on light backgrounds) provide maximum readability for campaign messages.</p>

<h3>Can banners be shipped directly to my event location?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can coordinate delivery directly to your fundraising venue or campaign location with advance scheduling. This is particularly helpful for multi-location campaigns and events.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for banner artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, JPG, and PNG files, with PDF preferred for best reproduction quality. Our design team can work with most file types and optimize them for banner printing.</p>
