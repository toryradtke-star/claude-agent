<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Now Open Banners</h2>

<h3>How do I make a now open banner?</h3>
<p>Creating your custom now open banner is simple with our online design tools. Choose your banner size, upload your artwork or select from our templates, and our design team will prepare a digital proof for your approval. We handle all printing, finishing, and quality control to ensure you receive a professional now open banner ready for installation.</p>

<h3>Are these banners printed in RGB or CMYK color?</h3>
<p>All now open banners are printed using CMYK color profiles for accurate, consistent color reproduction. If you're designing your artwork, please use CMYK color mode to ensure the colors you see on screen match your printed banner. Our design team can convert RGB files to CMYK during the proofing process.</p>

<h3>What is the largest size banner I can order?</h3>
<p>We can produce now open banners up to 8 feet tall by 50 feet wide. For banners larger than 8 feet in both height and width, the banner will be folded for shipping. If you need larger sizes or prefer flat shipping, we can arrange freight delivery for an additional cost.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer double-sided now open banners for installations where both sides will be visible, such as hanging displays or pole-mounted applications. Double-sided banners feature full-color printing on both sides with a blockout liner to prevent color bleed-through. Contact our sales team for double-sided pricing and specifications.</p>
