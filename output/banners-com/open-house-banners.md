<h2>Real Estate Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material is best for outdoor real estate banners?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl material is specifically chosen for real estate applications because it provides excellent durability against weather conditions while maintaining professional appearance. This heavyweight vinyl resists tearing, fading, and water damage, making it ideal for extended outdoor display at property locations.</p>

<h3>How long do real estate banners typically last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper installation and care, our 13oz vinyl real estate banners can maintain their appearance for 1-3 years of continuous outdoor exposure. The actual lifespan depends on local weather conditions, UV exposure, and installation method. For temporary open house events, these banners will maintain excellent appearance throughout your marketing campaign.</p>

<h3>What sizes work best for real estate banner visibility?</h3>
<p>Popular sizes for real estate marketing include 3'x6', 4'x8', and 2'x8' banners. These dimensions provide excellent visibility from street view while remaining manageable for installation. Larger sizes like 4'x10' or 5'x10' work well for commercial properties or high-traffic locations where maximum visibility is needed.</p>

<h3>Can you match my real estate company's brand colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, our printing process can accurately reproduce your brand colors using CMYK color matching. If you have specific Pantone colors or brand guidelines, our graphic design team can ensure color accuracy during the design process. We recommend providing brand guidelines or color specifications with your artwork.</p>

<h3>How are large real estate banners shipped?</h3>
<p>Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be carefully folded for standard shipping. If you prefer your banner to arrive without fold lines, we can arrange custom freight shipping for an additional cost. Contact our customer service team for freight quote options and delivery arrangements.</p>

<h3>What's the minimum text size for readability?</h3>
<p>For optimal readability from typical viewing distances, we recommend minimum text size of 1 inch height. Contact information should be prominently displayed with high contrast colors. Our design team can advise on text sizing based on your banner dimensions and intended viewing distance.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for last-minute open houses?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide rush production options including 2-3 business day turnaround and emergency same-day services when available. Rush pricing applies, and availability depends on banner size and current production schedule. Contact customer service to confirm rush availability for your timeline.</p>

<h3>Can I reorder the same real estate banner design?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! We maintain your design files for easy reordering. Simply reference your previous order number when placing new orders, or contact customer service for assistance with reorders and design modifications.</p>
