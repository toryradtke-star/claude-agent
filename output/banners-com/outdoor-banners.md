#### Four Customizable Banner Materials

Our outdoor banners are made from heavy-duty materials like vinyl and mesh, designed to withstand harsh weather conditions, including rain, wind, and UV exposure. All banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.

Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Please note our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (ex. A 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please ask your Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.

13 oz Matte Vinyl Banners

Our high-quality 13oz matte vinyl banner material is our most popular banner material. It has a low shine and rich color gamut.

13 oz Gloss Vinyl Banners

Our high-quality 13oz gloss vinyl banner material has a high shine to it that produces vibrant, crisp colors and graphics to give it a high end look.

18 oz Vinyl Banners

Our extra heavy duty matte 18oz vinyl banner material is our toughest material. It has a low luster, blockout coating for extra durability and print quality. 

Mesh Banners

Made with 8oz  PVC polyester mesh material that has a criss crossed breathable scrim that allows a 37% airflow. Mesh Banners are ideal for outdoor and windy conditions.

##### Pole Pocket Finishing Options

*   2" Pole Pockets - fits a pole 3/4" in diameter
*   3" Pole Pockets - fits a pole 1 1/4" in diameter
*   4" Pole Pockets - fits a pole 1 1/2" in diameter

By selecting pole pockets, there will be no increase in the finished size of your banner. Pole pockets can either be 2", 3" or 4". We don't recommend having any text or images within 3 inches of the edge of the banner when you select pole pockets.

\*\*Pole Pockets are not available with reinforced hems or corners. Reinforced corners are not available on banners under 2 feet in height.

##### 2 Sided Banner Finishing

We can finish 2 sided banners up to 150 square feet. For anything larger, please contact us. Double sided banners can only have pole pockets or hems and grommet finishing.

We do not recommend wind slits in banners, therefore we do not offer them. For more information as to why, visit our [wind slit banners](https://www.banners.com/wind-slits) information page.

## Frequently Asked Questions [Return to Top](#top)

### What is the best banner material for outdoor use?

For outdoor use, we recommend either a mesh banner (best for wind) or an 18 oz matte vinyl banner (our toughest vinyl option for durability).

### Which banner material is best for windy locations?

Mesh banners are best for windy areas because the 8 oz PVC polyester mesh allows 37% airflow, helping reduce wind load on the banner.

### What materials are outdoor banners made from?

Our outdoor banners are made from heavy-duty vinyl and mesh materials designed to handle rain, wind, and UV exposure.

### Are outdoor banners manufactured and printed in the USA?

Yes—our outdoor banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.

### What’s the difference between 13 oz matte vinyl and 13 oz gloss vinyl?

13 oz matte vinyl is low-shine with a rich color gamut, while 13 oz gloss vinyl has a high-shine finish for vibrant, crisp graphics.

### What is the most durable vinyl banner option?

Our most durable vinyl option is 18 oz matte vinyl, which includes a blockout coating for added durability and print quality.

### Will my vinyl banner be an exact finished size?

Exact sizes can’t be guaranteed because vinyl is temperature sensitive, and size fluctuation can occur during printing, finishing, and outside climate conditions.

### What is the acceptable size variance for vinyl banners?

Acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (for example, a 5' banner could finish with up to a 2.5" variance).

### What should I do if I need a precisely finished banner size?

If your banner needs to be precise, please contact a Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.

### What is the maximum size outdoor banner I can order?

Outdoor banners are available in sizes ranging from 1' × 1' up to 8' H × 50' W. If you need a larger size, visit our [oversized banner page](https://www.banners.com/oversized-banners).

### Will my outdoor vinyl banner be folded for shipping?

Outdoor vinyl banners that exceed 8 feet in both directions will be folded for shipping; contact us for a custom freight quote if you don’t want folding.

### What finishing options are available for outdoor banners?

Finishing options include hems & grommets, reinforced hems, reinforced corners, and pole pockets to support secure installation and longevity.

### How are hems and grommets finished, and how often are grommets added?

Hems and grommets use a 1-inch fold with a heat-welded hem, and grommets are typically added every 2–4 feet.

### Can I combine pole pockets with reinforced hems or reinforced corners?

No—pole pockets are not available with reinforced hems or reinforced corners.

### Are reinforced corners available on all banner sizes?

No—reinforced corners are not available on banners under 2 feet in height.

### What finishing options are available for double-sided banners?

We can finish double-sided banners up to 150 square feet, and they can only have pole pockets or hems and grommets.
