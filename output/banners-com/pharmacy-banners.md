<p>Professional pharmacy banner printing requires properly prepared artwork that reproduces clearly and maintains brand standards. Our artwork specifications ensure your pharmacy banners meet healthcare marketing standards and produce vibrant, professional results.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>We accept high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, and PSD files for pharmacy banner printing. Vector-based formats work best for text and logos, while high-resolution images should be at least 100 DPI at full print size.</p>

<h3>Design Guidelines</h3>
<p>Pharmacy banner designs should include adequate white space around text and important elements. Text should be large enough for easy reading at viewing distance, with high contrast between text and background colors for maximum legibility.</p>

<h3>Brand Standards Compliance</h3>
<p>Healthcare marketing materials often require compliance with brand guidelines and regulatory standards. Our design team can review artwork for professional appearance and suggest improvements for healthcare marketing effectiveness.</p>

<h3>Color Considerations</h3>
<p>CMYK color mode produces the most accurate printing results for pharmacy banners. RGB files are converted during printing, which may cause slight color variations. Pantone color matching is available for brand-critical applications.</p>

<h3>Design Services Available</h3>
<p>Our in-house design team can create custom pharmacy banner artwork or modify existing designs for optimal printing results. Design services include layout optimization, color correction, and healthcare industry-appropriate styling.</p>
