#### Pole Banner Material

Your banner is made with the finest materials available and printed using state-of-the-art, large format printers for vibrant colors that pop. All banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.

Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Please note our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (ex. A 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please ask your Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.

13 oz Matte Vinyl Banners

Our high-quality 13oz matte vinyl banner material is our most popular banner material. It has a low shine and rich color gamut.

18 oz Vinyl Banners

Our extra heavy duty matte 18oz vinyl banner material is our toughest material. It has a low luster, blockout coating for extra durability and print quality. 

#### Finishing Options

• The max print size is 72" height x 36" width

• Pole banners are printed double sided with pole pockets at the top and bottom

• Two hardware options are available: [Standard Bracket System](https://www.banners.com/accessories/standard-hardware) (We recommend only up to 24" as any larger size should use our Wind Deflecting Hardware) and [Wind Deflecting Bracket System](https://www.banners.com/accessories/wind-deflecting-hardware). 

Pole banners are intended to be used with adjustable hardware. Most commercial banner hardware is adjustable and we do not recommend our product to be used with any non-adjustable/stationary hardware and we will not be held liable if your product doesn't fit. During the printing process, the banners are heated and cooled which may cause the material to expand or shrink, thus we cannot guarantee an exact fit.

Pole banners are printed on both sides and have pockets on top and bottom. They are usually shown on light poles with a bracket system.

Line the streets of your city with vibrant pole banners. They’re printed on either 13-oz. matte vinyl or 18-oz. heavy duty matte vinyl material.

## Frequently Asked Questions [Return to Top](#top)

### What are pole banners (light pole banners)?

Pole banners are vertical banners designed to mount on light poles using banner brackets/hardware.

### Where are your pole banners made?

All pole banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.

### What materials are available for pole banners?

We offer 13 oz matte vinyl and 18 oz vinyl pole banner materials.

### What’s the difference between 13 oz matte vinyl and 18 oz vinyl?

13 oz matte vinyl is our most popular option with low shine and a rich color gamut. 18 oz vinyl is extra heavy-duty with a blockout coating for added durability and print quality.

### What is the maximum size you can print for a pole banner?

The maximum print size is 72" high × 36" wide.

### Are pole banners printed single-sided or double-sided?

Pole banners are printed double-sided.

### Do pole banners include pole pockets?

Yes. Pole banners are finished with pole pockets at the top and bottom.

### Can you guarantee the exact finished size of my pole banner?

Exact sizes can’t be guaranteed because vinyl is temperature sensitive and can fluctuate during printing/finishing and with outdoor climate.

### What is your acceptable size variance for pole banners?

Acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (for example, a 5' banner could finish with up to a 2.5" variance).

### Will your pole banner fit my existing hardware?

Pole banners are intended for adjustable hardware. We don’t recommend using non-adjustable/stationary hardware, and fit isn’t guaranteed.

### Which hardware should I use: Standard Bracket System or Wind Deflecting Bracket System?

We recommend the Standard Bracket System only up to 24"; larger sizes should use the Wind Deflecting Bracket System.

### What if I need a very precise fit?

If your banner needs to be precise, contact Customer Service to discuss your specific application.
