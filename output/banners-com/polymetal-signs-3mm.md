<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What are polymetal signs used for?</h3>
<p>Polymetal signs are ideal for professional business signage, including storefront identification, building directories, office signage, and commercial property displays. The weather-resistant construction makes them perfect for long-term outdoor installations.</p>

<h3>What material are polymetal signs made from?</h3>
<p>Our polymetal signs are constructed from 3mm aluminum composite material with a solid plastic core. This construction provides superior weather resistance and professional appearance while allowing for back-routing installation options.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for custom polymetal signs?</h3>
<p>We offer standard sizes from 12" to 48" in various height and width combinations. Custom sizes are available through our custom quote service - contact customer service for pricing on non-standard dimensions.</p>

<h3>Can polymetal signs be cut to custom shapes?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer contour cutting services to create custom shapes that match your logo or design specifications. This allows for unique signage that stands out while maintaining professional appearance and structural integrity.</p>

<h3>Are polymetal signs available with double-sided printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can print polymetal signs on both sides for applications like pole-mounted signs or projecting business signs where visibility from multiple directions is important.</p>

<h3>Do polymetal signs print in full color?</h3>
<p>Yes, we use full-color printing with UV-resistant inks to ensure vibrant graphics and long-lasting color performance in outdoor environments. Full bleed printing is available for maximum visual impact.</p>

<h3>How weather-resistant are polymetal signs?</h3>
<p>Polymetal signs are designed for long-term outdoor exposure with excellent weather resistance. The aluminum composite construction and solid core prevent water infiltration, while UV-resistant inks maintain color vibrancy over time.</p>

<h3>How should polymetal signs be stored before installation?</h3>
<p>Store polymetal signs in a clean, dry environment away from direct sunlight. Keep signs flat or properly supported to prevent bending, and protect the printed surface from scratches during storage and transport.</p>

<h3>Can I review a proof before my polymetal signs are printed?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer order proofs that include a preview of your artwork, complete order details, and billing information. Proof approval is required before production begins, ensuring your signs meet your exact specifications.</p>

<h3>What are your production times and when does turnaround begin?</h3>
<p>We offer 3 business day standard production and 5 business day saver options. Production begins when file upload is complete and artwork is approved. If proof approval is requested, production starts after you approve the proof.</p>
