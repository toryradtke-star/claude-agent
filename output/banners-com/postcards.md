<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Postcards</h2>

<h3>What size are your custom printed postcards?</h3>
<p>Our custom postcards measure 4.00" x 5.625", which is the standard postcard size. This size qualifies for USPS postcard rates, making it cost-effective for direct mail marketing campaigns and personal communications.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between matte and gloss postcard finishes?</h3>
<p>Gloss finish provides vibrant colors with a shiny, reflective surface that's ideal for photo-heavy designs and marketing materials. Matte finish offers a non-reflective, sophisticated appearance that's easy to write on and reduces glare, making it perfect for text-heavy designs and elegant presentations.</p>

<h3>How thick are your custom postcards?</h3>
<p>Our postcards are printed on premium 12.4 pt, 120 lb. coverstock paper. This thickness provides a professional feel, durability during mailing, and meets USPS requirements for automated processing while maintaining standard postcard postage rates.</p>

<h3>How do I design a custom postcard?</h3>
<p>You can upload your own print-ready artwork in .eps, .ai, .pdf, or high-resolution .jpg formats, or utilize our professional design services for $60 per hour. Our designers can create custom layouts from your photos, text, and concepts to produce eye-catching postcards.</p>

<h3>What information should I include on a custom postcard?</h3>
<p>For business postcards, include your logo, contact information, call-to-action, and key message or offer. For personal postcards, include recipient address space, return address, and your message or announcement. Remember to follow USPS guidelines for address placement and postage area.</p>

<h3>What production times are available for custom postcards?</h3>
<p>We offer production times from 1 to 5 business days. Options include 1-day priority, 2-day express, 3-day standard (most popular), and 5-day saver. Production begins after artwork approval and payment processing.</p>

<h3>Do you offer color matching for brand-specific colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer a Color Match Proof System for color-critical projects. This service ensures accurate reproduction of brand-specific colors and is recommended for professional marketing materials where color accuracy is essential.</p>

<h3>What file types do you accept for custom postcard printing?</h3>
<p>We prefer vector-based files (.eps, .ai) with fonts converted to outlines for best results. We also accept high-resolution .pdf and .jpg files at 300 DPI minimum. All files should be in CMYK color mode with appropriate bleed areas.</p>
