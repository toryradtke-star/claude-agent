<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>Can I use zip ties to hang my printed banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, zip ties work well for temporary installations through grommets. For permanent or long-term outdoor display, we recommend bungee cords or rope which allow for wind movement and reduce stress on the banner material.</p>

<h3>How do I get design help for my printed banner?</h3>
<p>Our in-house design team can create custom artwork or modify existing designs to meet your specifications. Simply select our design services during the ordering process and provide your requirements and any reference materials.</p>

<h3>Can I add photos to my printed banners?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. High-resolution photos reproduce beautifully on our vinyl materials. Ensure photos are at least 150 DPI at final print size for optimal quality. Our design team can help optimize photo placement and resolution if needed.</p>
