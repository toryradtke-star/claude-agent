<h2>Ball Bungee FAQ</h2>

<h3>What size grommets work with these ball bungees?</h3>
<p>Our 9-inch ball bungees work with standard banner grommets ranging from 1/2 inch to 3/4 inch diameter, which are the most common grommet sizes used in professional banner production.</p>

<h3>How many ball bungees do I need for my banner?</h3>
<p>We recommend one ball bungee per grommet for optimal banner tension and security. Most banners include grommets every 2-3 feet along the edges.</p>

<h3>Can ball bungees be used for outdoor banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our ball bungees are designed for outdoor use with weather-resistant materials that withstand UV exposure, rain, and temperature fluctuations.</p>

<h3>How long do ball bungees last in outdoor conditions?</h3>
<p>With proper installation, ball bungees typically maintain their elasticity and strength for 6-12 months in continuous outdoor exposure, depending on weather conditions.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse ball bungees for different banner installations?</h3>
<p>Yes, ball bungees are designed for multiple uses and can be easily removed and reinstalled for different banner displays as needed.</p>
