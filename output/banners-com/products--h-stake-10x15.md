<h2>H-Stake FAQ</h2>

<h3>What yard sign sizes work with 10x15 H-stakes?</h3>
<p>Our 10x15 H-stakes are compatible with most standard yard signs up to 18" x 24". The H-frame design accommodates signs with bottom channel slots and positions them approximately 1 inch above ground level.</p>

<h3>How deep do H-stakes penetrate into the ground?</h3>
<p>H-stakes typically penetrate 8-10 inches into soft ground, providing stable anchoring while leaving the H-frame above ground to support your yard sign securely.</p>

<h3>Can H-stakes be reused for multiple sign installations?</h3>
<p>Yes, our galvanized H-stakes are designed for multiple uses. The durable metal construction and corrosion-resistant coating allow for repeated installation and removal without degradation.</p>

<h3>What soil types work best with H-stake installation?</h3>
<p>H-stakes work best in soft to medium-firm soil conditions. Extremely hard or rocky ground may require pre-drilling pilot holes. Clay soil provides excellent holding power once stakes are properly seated.</p>

<h3>Are H-stakes suitable for permanent yard sign installation?</h3>
<p>Yes, the galvanized coating provides long-term corrosion resistance for permanent installations. Many real estate professionals use our H-stakes for property signs that remain in place for months or years.</p>
