<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How deep do H-Stakes penetrate into the ground?</h3>
<p>H-Stakes are designed to be pushed into soft ground to a depth that provides stable mounting. The 30" length allows for adequate ground penetration while positioning your sign 15 inches above ground level when using 10" x 30" stakes.</p>

<h3>What type of ground conditions work best with H-Stakes?</h3>
<p>H-Stakes work best in soft soil, grass, and dirt conditions. They are not recommended for hard-packed earth, concrete, asphalt, or rocky terrain where penetration would be difficult.</p>

<h3>How many H-Stakes do I need per yard sign?</h3>
<p>Most standard yard signs require 2 H-Stakes for proper support and stability. Larger signs or high-wind areas may benefit from additional stakes.</p>

<h3>Are H-Stakes reusable for multiple sign installations?</h3>
<p>Yes, the heavy-gauge galvanized construction allows H-Stakes to be reused multiple times. Simply remove them carefully from the ground and store properly between uses.</p>

<h3>What's the maximum sign size these H-Stakes can support?</h3>
<p>10" x 30" H-Stakes are suitable for most standard yard signs. For very large signs or high-wind installations, contact our team to discuss appropriate stake sizing and quantity recommendations.</p>

<h3>Do you offer expedited shipping for H-Stakes?</h3>
<p>Yes, expedited shipping options are available at checkout. Standard ground shipping provides 1-3 day delivery to 90% of the United States, with faster options available for urgent installations.</p>
