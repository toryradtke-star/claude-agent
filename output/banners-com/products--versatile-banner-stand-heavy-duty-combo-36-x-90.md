<h2>Banner Stand Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<h3>File Format Specifications</h3>
<p>Vector files in .eps format provide optimal print quality for banner stand graphics. High-resolution PDF files with embedded fonts are also acceptable for most designs.</p>

<h3>Resolution Requirements</h3>
<p>Raster images should be supplied at minimum 150 DPI at actual print size. Higher resolution files ensure crisp graphics and text reproduction at large display sizes.</p>

<h3>Color Profile Guidelines</h3>
<p>CMYK color profiles produce the most accurate print results. RGB files can be processed but may experience color shifts during printing conversion.</p>

<h3>Safe Zone Compliance</h3>
<p>Position all critical text and graphics at least 3" from top and bottom edges to account for pole pocket construction. This ensures your important content remains fully visible when mounted.</p>

<h3>Proofing Options</h3>
<p>Digital proofs are provided for approval before production begins. Color match proofs are available for projects requiring precise color accuracy.</p>

<h3>Design Services</h3>
<p>Professional design services are available if you need custom graphics created for your banner stand. Our design team can work with your brand guidelines and messaging requirements.</p>
