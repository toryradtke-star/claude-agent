<h2>Prom Banner Artwork Requirements for High-Quality Printing</h2>

<p>Ensure your custom prom banner prints with professional quality by following our artwork guidelines and specifications.</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong><br>
Submit artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats. Vector-based files (AI/EPS) preferred for text and logo elements to ensure crisp printing at any size.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Standards</strong><br>
Raster images should be minimum 150 DPI at actual print size. High-resolution photos and graphics ensure sharp detail reproduction on your prom banner.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications</strong><br>
Design files in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files acceptable but may result in color shifts during printing process.</p>

<p><strong>Text and Font Guidelines</strong><br>
Minimum text size of 1 inch for optimal readability from typical viewing distances. All fonts should be outlined or embedded in design files.</p>

<p><strong>Design Safety Areas</strong><br>
Keep important text and graphics at least 2 inches from banner edges to avoid interference with finishing options like grommets and pole pockets.</p>

<p><strong>School Logo Usage</strong><br>
Provide high-resolution school logos and mascot graphics for incorporation into prom banner designs. Vector formats ensure clean reproduction at large sizes.</p>

<p><strong>Professional Design Service</strong><br>
Our graphic design team can create custom prom banners from your concepts and school branding materials. Design service includes unlimited revisions until approval.</p>

<p>Free design proofs provided before production to ensure your prom banner meets expectations. Our design team will contact you if artwork requires modifications for optimal printing results.</p>

<h2>Prom Banner Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What materials work best for indoor prom banners?</h3>
<p>For indoor gymnasium and event hall displays, our 13oz vinyl banner material provides excellent durability and color reproduction. Fabric banner materials offer a more elegant appearance for formal prom backdrops and photography areas.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should schools order prom banners?</h3>
<p>We recommend placing prom banner orders at least 2-3 weeks before your event date. This allows time for design development, proof approval, production, and shipping while avoiding rush charges.</p>

<h3>Can you incorporate our school logo and colors into prom banner designs?</h3>
<p>Yes, our design team specializes in incorporating school branding, logos, mascots, and colors into custom prom banner layouts. Provide us with your school's brand guidelines and we'll ensure consistent representation.</p>

<h3>What sizes work best for prom entrance banners?</h3>
<p>Popular prom entrance banner sizes range from 3'x8' to 4'x12' depending on your installation location. Our team can recommend optimal sizing based on your school's entrance dimensions and viewing distances.</p>

<h3>Do prom banners work for outdoor school entrance displays?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Our vinyl banner materials are weatherproof and suitable for outdoor installations. For windy locations, we recommend mesh vinyl materials or reinforced finishing options for added durability.</p>

<h3>What's included with prom banner design service?</h3>
<p>Our design service includes initial concept development, unlimited revisions, incorporation of your school's branding, and a final digital proof for approval before production begins.</p>

<h3>Can banners be reused for future prom events?</h3>
<p>With proper storage, vinyl prom banners can be reused for multiple years. Consider designing with timeless elements rather than specific dates to extend usability across multiple prom celebrations.</p>

<h3>What payment options are available for school orders?</h3>
<p>Schools can pay via credit card, PayPal, or submit purchase orders for Net 30 payment terms. Educational discounts and tax-exempt processing available for qualifying institutions.</p>
