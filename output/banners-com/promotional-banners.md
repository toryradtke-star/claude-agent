<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Promotional Banners</h2>

<h3>What should I put on my promotional banner?</h3>
<p>Your promotional banner should include your key message, business name or event title, important dates or details, and contact information. Keep text large and readable from a distance, and include eye-catching colors or graphics that align with your brand.</p>

<h3>What is the largest size banner I can order?</h3>
<p>We offer promotional banners up to 8'x50' in size. For larger sizes or special dimensions, contact us for a custom quote. Large banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be folded for shipping unless freight shipping is arranged.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, double-sided promotional banners are available. This option is ideal for banners that will be viewed from both sides, such as those hanging in the center of spaces or mounted perpendicular to walls.</p>

<h3>What are my material options?</h3>
<p>We offer six different banner materials, each selected for specific applications and durability requirements. All materials are printed with UV-resistant inks and are suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. Material selection depends on your specific installation environment and longevity needs.</p>
