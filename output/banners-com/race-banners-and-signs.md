# Fun Run and Race Banners & Signs - Easy Online Ordering!

Banners.com is your source for custom signage for running events! Whether you are organizing a fun run 5K, fundraising 10K, competitive marathon, or an awareness walk, get promotional banners, signs, and flags for any type of distance running event. Use custom signage to direct attendees to various areas, post routes and schedules, mark start and finish lines, recognize sponsors, or provide any other information you want people to know. We have lots of different products and materials available, so no matter what your signage needs are we can help you out! If you need something that you don't see listed, feel free to contact our customer service professionals! [**Click here for a free sample packet of materials**](https://www.banners.com/sample-packet-request)

### [Race Event Yard Signs](https://www.banners.com/running-race-yard-signs)

Yard signs are also referred to as lawn signs, or corrugated plastic signs. They are durable, lightweight, and waterproof making them great for outdoor advertising!

*   Digitally printed in full color
*   Single Sided or Double Sided options
*   Printed on 4 mm corrugated plastic material
*   Max print size: 4' x 8'
*   Large selection of sizes to choose from
*   Material has hollow "flutes" 
*   Easily displayed using Beefy Stakes or H Stakes
*   Yard Signs are designed for outdoor use
*   [Click here to get started on your race event yard signs](https://www.banners.com/running-race-yard-signs)
