<h2>Real Estate Banner Artwork Requirements for Printing</h2>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Preferred: PDF, AI, EPS with embedded fonts and images</li>
<li>Accepted: High-resolution JPG, PNG, TIFF files</li>
<li>Vector formats recommended for text and logos</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Resolution Specifications</strong></p>
<p>Minimum 150 DPI at full size for optimal real estate banner print quality. Images should be sized to final dimensions or larger to prevent pixelation.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode</strong></p>
<p>CMYK color mode preferred for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted during production and may result in slight color shifts.</p>

<p><strong>Design Guidelines</strong></p>
<p>Include 0.25" bleed area around entire design. Keep important text and contact information at least 0.5" from banner edges for proper finishing.</p>

<p><strong>Font Requirements</strong></p>
<p>Fonts must be outlined or embedded in artwork files. Standard system fonts may be substituted if not properly embedded.</p>

<p><strong>Design Assistance</strong></p>
<p>Professional design services available for real estate banners. Upload reference images and provide detailed instructions for custom design creation.</p>
