<h2>Realtor Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What size realtor banner works best for property listings?</h3>
<p>For most property listings, we recommend 3'x6' or 4'x8' realtor banners. These sizes provide excellent visibility while remaining easy to install and transport. Larger sizes like 5'x10' or 6'x12' work well for high-traffic locations or when you need to display more detailed information about the property.</p>

<h3>How long will my realtor banner last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl realtor banners are designed for 3-5 years of outdoor use. The actual lifespan depends on weather conditions, UV exposure, and installation location. Banners installed in covered areas or used seasonally will last longer than those in constant direct sunlight.</p>

<h3>Can you print my existing realtor logo and branding?</h3>
<p>Yes! We can work with your existing realtor logos, brand colors, and design elements. Provide us with high-resolution logo files, and our design team will ensure your branding is reproduced accurately on your banner. We can also help digitize and optimize low-resolution logos for better print quality.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for urgent property listings?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. We offer same-day and next-day rush production for urgent real estate marketing needs. Same-day service is available for orders placed by 10 AM CST, and next-day service is available for most banner sizes and finishing options.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to install a realtor banner on property fencing?</h3>
<p>For fence installation, we recommend choosing grommets with zip ties or bungee cords for secure attachment. The grommets should be spaced every 2-3 feet for proper support. For chain-link fences, zip ties work particularly well. For wooden fences, you can use screws through the grommets.</p>

<h3>Can I get multiple realtor banners with the same design?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer volume discounts for multiple banner orders. This is perfect for real estate teams, agencies with multiple locations, or agents who want consistent branding across multiple properties. Contact us for volume pricing on identical or similar banner designs.</p>

<h3>Do you provide design services for realtor banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house design team can create custom realtor banner designs from scratch or modify existing layouts. We offer full design services including layout creation, photo editing, and brand integration. Design services are available for an additional fee, and we'll work with you through multiple revision rounds until you're satisfied.</p>

<h3>What payment options do you accept for realtor banner orders?</h3>
<p>We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, business checks, and offer Net 30 payment terms for qualified business accounts. Real estate agencies can apply for business accounts to take advantage of volume discounts and flexible payment options.</p>

<h3>How do you ship large realtor banners?</h3>
<p>Banners larger than 8 feet in both directions are folded for standard shipping to meet carrier regulations. If you prefer your large banner to arrive without fold lines, we can arrange custom freight shipping for an additional fee. Smaller banners can be shipped rolled in protective tubes.</p>

<h3>Can realtor banners be used for both indoor and outdoor applications?</h3>
<p>Yes, our vinyl realtor banners are versatile enough for both indoor and outdoor use. They work great for outdoor property signs, indoor office displays, trade show booths, and open house events. The durable vinyl material and UV-resistant inks ensure professional appearance in any setting.</p>
