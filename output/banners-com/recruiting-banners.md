Submit recruiting banner artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, or PSD formats at actual size or proportional scale. Vector-based team logos and graphics ensure optimal print quality for professional athletic displays.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Specifications</strong><br>
Provide artwork at minimum 150 DPI at actual size for crisp text and graphics. Team photos and player images should be high-resolution to ensure professional appearance on large format recruiting banners.</p>

<p><strong>Color Profile Standards</strong><br>

<p><strong>Typography Guidelines</strong><br>
