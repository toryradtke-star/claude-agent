<p>Proper artwork preparation ensures your retirement banner prints with sharp text, vibrant colors, and professional appearance. Follow these guidelines for optimal printing results.</p>

<h3>File Format and Resolution Requirements</h3>

<p>Retirement banner artwork should be submitted in high-resolution formats suitable for large-format printing. Vector files produce the sharpest results for text and logos on retirement banners.</p>
