<h2>Artwork Requirements for Race Banner Printing</h2>

<p>Professional artwork specifications to ensure optimal race banner print quality for your running event:</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong><br>
Submit race banner artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats. JPEG and PNG files acceptable for simple race banner designs.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Standards</strong><br>
Race banner artwork should be created at 150 DPI at actual size for optimal print quality and sharp text visibility during events.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode</strong><br>
Design race banners using CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted and may result in slight color variations.</p>

<p><strong>Text & Font Guidelines</strong><br>
Use minimum 1-inch text height for race banner readability from typical viewing distances. Outline all fonts or include font files with artwork submissions.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed & Safety Areas</strong><br>
Include 2-inch bleed around all race banner edges and keep important text and logos at least 3 inches from edges to avoid trimming issues.</p>

<p>Our design team reviews all race banner artwork for print compatibility and will contact you if adjustments are needed before production begins.</p>

<p>Free design services available if you need assistance creating professional race banner artwork for your running event.</p>

<h2>Race Banner Questions & Support</h2>

<h3>What is the best way to hang a race banner?</h3>
<p>For race banners, we recommend grommets with rope or bungee cords for quick setup during race day preparation. Pole pockets work well for finish line banners spanning wide areas. Consider your race venue's mounting options and wind conditions when selecting hanging methods for your running event.</p>

<h3>What is the largest size race banner I can order?</h3>
<p>Our race banners can be produced up to 8 feet by 50 feet, perfect for large finish line displays and major marathon events. For oversized race banners exceeding these dimensions, contact our team for custom production options and freight shipping arrangements.</p>

<h3>Can I add photos to my race banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, photos can be incorporated into your race banner design. High-resolution images work best for race banner printing. Our design team can help optimize photos for outdoor display and ensure they reproduce clearly on your race day signage.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order race banners?</h3>
<p>We recommend ordering race banners at least 2 weeks before your event to allow time for design review, production, and shipping. Rush production is available for last-minute race banner needs, with same-day options for simple designs.</p>

<h3>Are race banners weather resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes, our race banners are printed on weather-resistant vinyl materials designed for outdoor use during running events. UV-resistant inks maintain color vibrancy, and the materials can withstand typical outdoor conditions during race day.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse race banners for multiple events?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Our durable race banner materials are designed for multiple uses. Proper storage between events will extend the life of your race banners, making them a cost-effective investment for recurring running events and annual marathons.</p>
