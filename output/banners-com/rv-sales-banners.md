<h2>RV Sales Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What banner material works best for outdoor RV dealerships?</h3>
<p>We recommend 13oz vinyl for most RV dealership applications, offering excellent durability for outdoor conditions. For high-wind locations, consider 18oz heavy-duty vinyl or mesh material for wind resistance.</p>

<h3>How long do RV dealership banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Quality vinyl RV banners typically last 2-5 years in outdoor dealership conditions, depending on sun exposure, weather, and material choice. UV-resistant inks help maintain color vibrancy throughout the banner's life.</p>

<h3>Can you match our RV dealership's brand colors exactly?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match Pantone colors and existing brand standards. Provide your dealership's brand guidelines or color specifications, and our design team will ensure accurate color reproduction.</p>

<h3>What's the largest RV banner size you can produce?</h3>
<p>We can manufacture RV banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet for most applications. Larger sizes are available through custom quotes with freight shipping options.</p>

<h3>Do you offer installation services for RV dealership banners?</h3>
<p>While we don't provide installation, we can recommend local sign installers in your area and provide detailed mounting specifications for professional installation at your RV dealership.</p>

<h3>Can I get multiple banners for different dealership locations?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. We can ship different banner designs to multiple RV dealership locations from a single order, with volume pricing available for multi-location dealership groups.</p>
