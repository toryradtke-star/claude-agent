#### Pole Banner Material

Your banner is made with the finest materials available and printed using state-of-the-art, large format printers for vibrant colors that pop. All banners are manufactured and printed in the USA.

Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Please note our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (ex. A 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please ask your Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.

13 oz Matte Vinyl Banners

Our high-quality 13oz matte vinyl banner material is our most popular banner material. It has a low shine and rich color gamut.

18 oz Vinyl Banners

Our extra heavy duty matte 18oz vinyl banner material is our toughest material. It has a low luster, blockout coating for extra durability and print quality. 

#### Finishing Options

• The max print size is 72" height x 36" width

• Pole banners are printed double sided with pole pockets at the top and bottom

• Two hardware options are available: [Standard Bracket System](https://www.banners.com/accessories/standard-hardware) (We recommend only up to 24" as any larger size should use our Wind Deflecting Hardware) and [Wind Deflecting Bracket System](https://www.banners.com/accessories/wind-deflecting-hardware). 

Pole banners are intended to be used with adjustable hardware. Most commercial banner hardware is adjustable and we do not recommend our product to be used with any non-adjustable/stationary hardware and we will not be held liable if your product doesn't fit. During the printing process, the banners are heated and cooled which may cause the material to expand or shrink, thus we cannot guarantee an exact fit.

#### Artwork Guidelines

Vector-based file types will print best, such as .eps— with fonts converted to outlines. We can also print high resolution .pdf or .jpg files. If you are unsure of the quality of your files, our professional graphic designers will be more than happy to help you.

For pole banner artwork, we recommend placing any imagery or wording at least 1" in from where the pole packets will be placed.

## More About School Pole Banners 

Pole banners can be used to decorate your school grounds, parking lots, and adjacent streets. You can get pole banners with the name of your school and a welcome message. You can also get pole banners for school events, sports, student organizations, or anything else you want to promote.

Double sided pole banners have printing on both sides of the material. There is a pole pocket at the top of the banner and at the bottom of the banner. The pole banners also have a grommet in all four corners.

The pole banners are typically attached to light poles using a bracket system. If you are interested in a bracket system, please contact a customer service representative. 

We have several templates available for you to customize in the online designer. You can choose from our wide selection of backgrounds and clip art images. You can also add text and upload your own images in the online designer. You can also upload your own print ready files, or have one of our graphic designers create your artwork for you.
