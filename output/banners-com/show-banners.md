<h2>Show & Event Banner Questions</h2>

<h3>What material are show and event banners made from?</h3>
<p>Our show and event banners are made from premium 13oz vinyl material that provides excellent durability for both indoor and outdoor use. This material is weather-resistant, tear-resistant, and provides a smooth surface for vibrant graphic reproduction.</p>

<h3>How long do show banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>When properly installed and maintained, our vinyl show banners can last 3-5 years outdoors. The UV-resistant inks and weather-resistant vinyl material are designed to withstand sun, rain, and temperature changes without significant fading or deterioration.</p>

<h3>Can I get rush production for a last-minute event?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer 1-2 business day rush production for urgent event needs. Rush options are available during checkout, and our team prioritizes these orders to meet tight deadlines for trade shows and special events.</p>

<h3>What's the largest size banner you can produce?</h3>
<p>We can produce show banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet. Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be folded for standard shipping. Contact us for freight shipping options if you prefer your large banner to be rolled instead of folded.</p>

<h3>Do you provide design services for show banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house graphic design team can create custom layouts, recreate logos, and ensure your banner meets professional standards. Design services are available for customers who need assistance with artwork creation or enhancement.</p>

<h3>How are show banners typically installed at events?</h3>
<p>Show banners include grommets for easy hanging with rope, bungee cords, or zip ties. They can be mounted on banner stands, suspended from ceiling points, attached to trade show booth frames, or hung on walls and fences.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for banner artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept high-resolution PDF, Adobe Illustrator (.ai), Photoshop (.psd), JPEG, and PNG files. For best results, provide artwork at 150-300 DPI resolution in CMYK color mode with appropriate bleed areas.</p>

<h3>Can I reorder the same banner design later?</h3>
<p>Yes, we maintain your artwork files and order history, making it easy to reorder identical banners or request size variations for future events. Contact our customer service team with your previous order number for quick reordering.</p>
