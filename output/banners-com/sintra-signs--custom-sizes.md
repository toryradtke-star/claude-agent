<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Sintra Signs</h2>

<h3>What material are Sintra signs made from?</h3>
<p>Our Sintra signs are made from expanded PVC (polyvinyl chloride) board, commonly known as Sintra board. This material is lightweight, durable, and provides an excellent smooth surface for high-quality digital printing. The 3mm thickness offers good rigidity while remaining easy to handle and mount.</p>

<h3>How long do Sintra signs last indoors?</h3>
<p>When used indoors, Sintra signs can last many years without significant degradation. The PVC material is resistant to moisture, chemicals, and normal wear. The digital printing we use is fade-resistant and designed for long-term indoor display applications.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for custom Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>We offer custom sizes from 6" to 48" in both height and width dimensions. You can create any rectangular size within these parameters. For larger sizes or special requirements, contact us for a custom quote to discuss your specific needs.</p>

<h3>Can you drill holes for mounting in Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can drill mounting holes in your Sintra signs. Options include corner holes for wall mounting, center holes for specific mounting systems, or custom hole patterns based on your requirements. Specify your hole preferences when placing your order.</p>

<h3>What is the difference between Sintra and other sign materials?</h3>
<p>Sintra (expanded PVC) is lighter than solid materials like aluminum or wood, yet more durable than paper-based materials. It's easy to cut, drill, and mount, making it ideal for indoor applications where you need professional appearance with practical installation options.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer professional design services if you need help creating artwork for your Sintra signs. Our design team can work with your ideas, logos, and content to create professional layouts that work well for your intended application.</p>
