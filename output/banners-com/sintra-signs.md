<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Sintra Signs</h2>

<h3>What are Sintra signs made of?</h3>
<p>Sintra signs are made from rigid PVC foam core material available in 3mm or 6mm thickness. This lightweight yet durable plastic material provides excellent print quality and can be router-cut into custom shapes for unique design applications.</p>

<h3>Can I order custom-shaped Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer custom shape cutting for Sintra signs using router-cutting technology. Contact our customer service team to discuss your specific shape requirements and receive a personalized quote for custom-cut Sintra signage.</p>

<h3>Can Sintra signs be printed double-sided?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! We offer both single-sided and double-sided printing options for Sintra signs. Double-sided printing provides maximum visibility and is perfect for signs that will be viewed from multiple directions.</p>

<h3>What colors are used to print Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>We use full-color CMYK digital printing to reproduce vibrant, accurate colors on Sintra signs. Our printing process can handle complex designs, photographs, gradients, and any color combination your design requires.</p>

<h3>Are Sintra signs suitable for outdoor use?</h3>
<p>Yes, Sintra signs are excellent for both indoor and outdoor applications. The material is UV-resistant and weather-durable, making it perfect for outdoor advertising, construction signs, real estate displays, and long-term promotional signage.</p>

<h3>How should I store Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>Store Sintra signs in a flat position in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight when not in use. Avoid placing heavy objects on top of stored signs to prevent bending or surface damage.</p>

<h3>What are common uses for custom Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>Popular applications include business advertising, trade show displays, restaurant menus, real estate signs, construction site signage, promotional cutouts, office displays, and retail point-of-purchase advertising.</p>

<h3>Can I request a proof before printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer order proofs that include a low-resolution preview of your artwork along with complete order details. For color-critical projects, we also provide a Color Match Proof System to ensure precise color reproduction.</p>

<h3>What production times do you offer for Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>We offer flexible production timeframes: 2 business days (Express), 3 business days (Standard), and 5 business days (Saver). Production begins once your artwork is approved and uploaded. Emergency same-day rushes may be available in select cases.</p>

<h3>What's the maximum size for Sintra signs?</h3>
<p>Our maximum print size is 4' x 8'. Signs over 32" x 48" or where height plus width exceeds 81 inches require freight shipping. Contact our team for oversized sign requirements and shipping information.</p>
