<h2>Sorority Banner Artwork Requirements for Custom Printing</h2>

<p>Professional printing results depend on artwork that meets our technical specifications and quality standards. Our artwork requirements ensure your sorority letters, colors, and branding reproduce accurately on your custom banner with sharp detail and vibrant colors that represent your chapter professionally.</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements:</strong> We accept artwork files in PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and high-resolution JPG formats. Vector-based files (AI, EPS) provide the best results for sorority letters and logos, ensuring crisp edges and scalability for any banner size.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Standards:</strong> Raster images must be at least 150 DPI at full print size. For a 3'x8' sorority banner, your artwork should be 3600x1350 pixels minimum. Higher resolution artwork produces better print quality, especially for detailed sorority crests or photographic elements.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications:</strong> Submit artwork in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. If your sorority has specific Pantone colors for house branding, please include color specifications or reference numbers to ensure proper color matching during printing.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed & Safety Areas:</strong> Include 0.125" bleed on all edges for proper trimming. Keep important text, sorority letters, and logos at least 0.25" from banner edges to prevent cutting during finishing processes.</p>

<p><strong>Font & Text Guidelines:</strong> Embed all fonts in your artwork files or convert text to outlines/curves. Use fonts that are clearly readable at distance for sorority banners displayed during recruitment events and campus activities.</p>

<p><strong>Design Review Process:</strong> Our graphic design team reviews all sorority banner artwork for technical compliance and print quality. If improvements are needed, we'll contact you with specific recommendations to optimize your artwork before production begins.</p>

<p><strong>Design Assistance Available:</strong> If you need help creating artwork for your sorority banner, our design team can assist with layout, color correction, and technical preparation to ensure optimal printing results that properly represent your chapter's brand.</p>
