<h2>Spelling Bee Banner Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What size spelling bee banner works best for school gymnasiums?</h3>
<p>For gymnasium installations, we recommend banners between 4'x8' to 6'x12' depending on your ceiling height and audience seating distance. These sizes provide excellent visibility without overwhelming the space or creating installation difficulties.</p>

<h3>How long do spelling bee banners last for repeated school use?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl spelling bee banners typically last 3-5 years with proper indoor storage between events. The UV-resistant inks and reinforced construction make them suitable for multiple spelling bee competitions throughout their lifespan.</p>

<h3>Can you include our school logo and spelling bee sponsors on the same banner?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our design team specializes in creating layouts that incorporate school branding, spelling bee competition details, and sponsor recognition in a balanced, professional design that serves all your event needs.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between indoor and outdoor spelling bee banners?</h3>
<p>All our spelling bee banners use the same weather-resistant 13oz vinyl material. For outdoor events, we recommend adding wind slits if the banner will be exposed to breezy conditions, but the base material performs excellently in both environments.</p>

<h3>How do I hang a spelling bee banner in our school auditorium?</h3>
<p>Our standard grommets work with most auditorium hanging systems. For permanent installation points, use S-hooks or carabiners. For temporary displays, banner stands or rope threading through grommets provides secure mounting options.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse the same spelling bee banner for multiple years?</h3>
<p>Yes! Design your banner with timeless elements like your school name and "Spelling Bee Competition" rather than specific dates or grade levels. This allows reuse across multiple school years, making it a cost-effective investment.</p>

<h3>What payment options do you accept for school purchase orders?</h3>
<p>We accept school purchase orders with Net 30 terms for established educational institutions. Credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers are also available. First-time school customers may need to complete a brief credit application.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order my spelling bee banner?</h3>
<p>For standard production, place your order 2-3 weeks before your spelling bee event. This allows time for design approval, production, and shipping. Rush production is available for urgent school events with shorter timelines.</p>
