<h2>Golf Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What materials are golf banners made from?</h3>
<p>Golf banners are constructed using premium vinyl materials specifically chosen for outdoor tournament conditions. Our most popular option is 13oz vinyl banner material, which provides excellent durability and vibrant color reproduction for golf course display. We also offer mesh banner material for windy locations and fabric material for indoor clubhouse use.</p>

<h3>What finishing options are available for golf banners?</h3>
<p>Golf banners include several finishing options optimized for tournament installation. Grommets provide reinforced attachment points for rope or bungee installation, while pole pockets allow for easy mounting on display poles. Velcro strips work well for indoor clubhouse displays, and reinforced edges add strength for large outdoor banners.</p>

<h3>Do you offer single or double sided golf banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, both single and double-sided printing options are available for golf banners. Double-sided banners provide maximum visibility from all angles around the golf course and are particularly effective for tournament entrance displays and sponsor recognition areas where viewers approach from multiple directions.</p>

<h3>Are golf banners weather resistant for outdoor tournaments?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Golf banners are designed specifically for outdoor tournament use with UV-resistant inks that prevent fading and weather-resistant vinyl materials that withstand rain, wind, and sun exposure. The reinforced construction and professional finishing ensure reliable performance throughout your golf event.</p>

<h3>Can I get a proof before my golf banner goes into production?</h3>
<p>Yes, digital proofs are provided within 2-4 hours of artwork submission for your approval before production begins. This allows you to verify colors, text accuracy, and overall design before your golf banner enters the printing process. Unlimited revisions are included at no additional charge.</p>

<h3>How should I store my custom golf banner?</h3>
<p>Store your golf banner in a clean, dry location rolled around a tube or loosely folded to prevent permanent creasing. Avoid storing in extreme temperatures or direct sunlight when not in use. Proper storage extends banner life and maintains print quality for multiple tournament seasons.</p>

<h3>How do I remove wrinkles from my golf banner?</h3>
<p>Minor wrinkles typically disappear when the banner is hung and exposed to sunlight and gentle stretching. For stubborn creases, use a hair dryer on low heat while gently stretching the vinyl material. Avoid high heat which can damage the banner material or cause shrinkage.</p>
