<h2>Martial Arts Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material is best for outdoor martial arts events?</h3>
<p>For outdoor competitions and demonstrations, we recommend our 18oz heavy-duty vinyl or mesh vinyl material. Both options provide excellent durability against wind and weather while maintaining vibrant colors for your tournament displays.</p>

<h3>How long does production take for martial arts banners?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3-5 business days after artwork approval. Rush production (1-2 business days) and same-day options are available for urgent tournament deadlines. Contact us to discuss your specific event timing.</p>

<h3>What size martial arts banner should I order?</h3>
<p>Banner size depends on your intended use. For dojo displays, 3'x6' or 4'x8' work well. For tournament backdrops, consider 6'x12' or larger. Our team can help you determine the ideal size for your martial arts facility or event space.</p>

<h3>Can you match my martial arts academy's specific colors?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match specific colors for your dojo or academy branding. Provide us with Pantone color codes, existing materials to match, or detailed color specifications, and our design team will ensure accurate color reproduction.</p>

<h3>Do you offer discounts for multiple martial arts banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer bulk pricing for martial arts academies ordering multiple banners, tournament organizers, and organizations with recurring banner needs. Contact us for custom pricing on your specific quantity requirements.</p>

<h3>What artwork format works best for martial arts banners?</h3>
<p>High-resolution PDF or vector files (AI, EPS) work best for crisp, professional results. If you only have JPG or PNG files, ensure they're at least 300 DPI at the actual banner size. Our design team can help optimize your artwork if needed.</p>
