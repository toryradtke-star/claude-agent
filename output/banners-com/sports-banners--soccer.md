<h2>Soccer Banner Artwork Requirements for Team Graphics</h2>

<p>Guidelines for creating high-quality soccer banner artwork that showcases your team professionally:</p>

<p><strong>File Formats:</strong> We accept AI, EPS, PDF, PSD, and high-resolution JPG files. Vector formats (AI, EPS) are preferred for soccer team logos and text elements.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Requirements:</strong> Raster images should be at least 100 DPI at full size. For a 4'x8' soccer banner, provide artwork sized at 48"x96" at 100 DPI minimum.</p>

<p><strong>Team Logo Guidelines:</strong> Soccer team logos should be provided in vector format when possible to ensure crisp reproduction at large banner sizes.</p>

<p><strong>Player Photo Requirements:</strong> Individual player photos should be high-resolution (300 DPI minimum) with good lighting and contrast for optimal printing results.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications:</strong> Provide team colors in CMYK format for accurate color matching. We can match specific soccer team color standards when provided.</p>

<p><strong>Text and Typography:</strong> Avoid fonts smaller than 1 inch tall for optimal readability from typical soccer field viewing distances.</p>

<p><strong>Design Services Available:</strong> Our in-house design team can create custom soccer banners using your team information, player details, and provided images.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed Requirements:</strong> Include 1-2 inches of extra artwork beyond the finished soccer banner size to account for trimming during production.</p>

<p>If you need assistance preparing artwork for your soccer banner, our design team can provide guidance or create the banner design for you using your team's information and preferences.</p>

<h3>How do I remove wrinkles from my soccer banner?</h3>
<p>Small wrinkles from shipping can be removed by hanging your soccer banner in direct sunlight for a few hours or using a hair dryer on low heat while gently stretching the banner. For persistent wrinkles, you can iron the banner on low heat with a cloth barrier between the iron and banner surface.</p>
