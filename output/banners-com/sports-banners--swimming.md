<h2>Swimming Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>What materials work best for swimming pool environments?</h3>
<p>We recommend 18oz vinyl for outdoor swimming facilities and 13oz vinyl for indoor pools. Both materials resist moisture and maintain color vibrancy in pool environments. Mesh vinyl is ideal for outdoor installations where wind resistance is important.</p>

<h3>How long do swimming banners last in pool facilities?</h3>
<p>With proper care, vinyl swimming banners typically last 3-5 years indoors and 2-3 years outdoors. Pool chemicals and UV exposure can affect longevity, but our premium materials are designed to withstand these conditions.</p>

<h3>Can you print team photos on swimming banners?</h3>
<p>Yes! We can print high-quality team photos, action shots, and individual swimmer images. Photos need to be at least 300 DPI at the size they'll appear on your banner for best results.</p>

<h3>What's the largest swimming banner size you can produce?</h3>
<p>We can print swimming banners up to 8 feet tall by 50 feet wide. Larger sizes require custom quotes and may need special shipping arrangements.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for swimming meets?</h3>
<p>Yes! Rush production is available in 24-48 hours for most swimming banner orders. Contact us as soon as possible to confirm availability for your event timeline.</p>

<h3>How should swimming banners be hung in pool facilities?</h3>
<p>We recommend grommets every 2-3 feet for wall mounting, or pole pockets for banner stands. Our team can advise on the best finishing options based on your pool facility's installation requirements.</p>

<h3>Can swimming banners be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our vinyl materials are UV-resistant and weatherproof. For outdoor swimming complexes, we recommend 18oz vinyl or mesh vinyl for durability and wind resistance.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for swimming team graphics?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our in-house graphic designers specialize in swimming team banners. We can create custom designs, incorporate team logos, and ensure your banner looks professional and represents your team with pride.</p>
