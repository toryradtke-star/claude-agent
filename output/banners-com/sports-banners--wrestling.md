<h2>Wrestling Banner Artwork Requirements for Team Graphics</h2>

<p>Professional artwork specifications to ensure your wrestling team banner prints with optimal quality and vibrant colors:</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong> - Wrestling banner artwork should be submitted in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD format for best printing results.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Standards</strong> - Team logos and wrestling graphics should be at least 150 DPI at actual banner size for sharp, professional appearance.</p>

<p><strong>Team Logo Preparation</strong> - Wrestling team logos work best in vector format to ensure crisp edges and clean lines at any banner size.</p>

<p><strong>Color Specifications</strong> - Wrestling banner colors should be designed in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction in printing.</p>

<p><strong>Text Readability</strong> - Wrestling banner text should be at least 2 inches tall for optimal readability from gymnasium distances.</p>

<p><strong>Design Templates</strong> - Pre-sized wrestling banner templates are available to help with proper layout and design proportions.</p>

<p><strong>Photo Requirements</strong> - Wrestling team photos should be high-resolution (at least 300 DPI) and professionally shot for best banner reproduction.</p>

<p><strong>Bleed and Safety Areas</strong> - Wrestling banner designs should include appropriate bleed areas and keep important text away from edges to ensure proper finishing.</p>

<p>Our design team reviews all wrestling banner artwork and will contact you if any adjustments are needed to ensure optimal printing quality for your team graphics.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>Can I order a wrestling banner background?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can create custom wrestling banner backgrounds featuring team colors, wrestling-specific graphics, or tournament themes. Our design team can work with your existing team branding or create new background designs specifically for your wrestling banner application.</p>

<h3>What is the best way to hang my vinyl wrestling banner?</h3>
<p>The best hanging method depends on your installation location. For gymnasium installations, we recommend grommets with rope or bungee cords for flexibility during other sports. For permanent wrestling facility displays, pole pockets work well with ceiling-mounted hardware. Our team can recommend the best finishing option based on your specific wrestling banner application.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided wrestling banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer double-sided wrestling banners perfect for center-hung gymnasium displays or tournament applications where both sides will be visible. Double-sided wrestling banners feature full-color printing on both sides with a blockout layer between to prevent show-through, ensuring your team graphics look professional from every angle.</p>
