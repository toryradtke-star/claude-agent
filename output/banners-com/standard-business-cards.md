<h2>Business Card Printing FAQ</h2>

<h3>What is the standard business card size in the US?</h3>
<p>Standard US business cards measure 3.5" x 2" and are printed on premium 12.4 pt, 120 lb. coverstock paper with your choice of glossy or matte coating finish.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between matte and gloss business cards?</h3>
<p>Glossy business cards provide vibrant color reproduction and a premium, polished appearance ideal for colorful designs and photography. Matte business cards offer an elegant, non-reflective finish that's easy to write on and provides a sophisticated, professional look.</p>

<h3>How thick are your business cards?</h3>
<p>Our business cards are printed on 12.4 pt, 120 lb. coverstock paper, providing substantial thickness and professional feel for networking and business applications while maintaining durability for frequent handling.</p>

<h3>How do I design a business card?</h3>
<p>You can upload your own print-ready design files or take advantage of our professional design services. We recommend vector-based files (.eps with outlined fonts) or high-resolution PDFs. Our graphic designers are available at $60 per hour for custom business card creation and optimization.</p>

<h3>What information should I include on a business card?</h3>
<p>Essential business card information includes your name, job title, company name, phone number, email address, and website. Consider adding your company logo, physical address (if relevant), and social media handles. Keep the design clean and readable for maximum networking effectiveness.</p>
