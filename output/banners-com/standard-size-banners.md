<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What are standard size banners?</h3>
<p>Standard size banners are professionally printed vinyl, mesh, or fabric banners available in common dimensions for business displays, events, and promotional installations. They feature durable materials and professional finishing options.</p>

<h3>What standard vinyl banner sizes do you offer?</h3>
<p>We offer standard sizes from 2x4 feet up to 4x12 feet, with custom sizing available for specific installation requirements. Popular sizes include 3x6, 4x8, and 2x6 feet for most business applications.</p>

<h3>What size banner should I choose for my viewing distance?</h3>
<p>For viewing distances of 10-20 feet, choose 2x4 or 3x6 banners. For 20-50 feet, select 4x8 or larger. Consider text height of 1 inch per 10 feet of viewing distance for optimal readability.</p>

<h3>What material are they made of?</h3>
<p>Standard size banners are made from 13oz or 18oz vinyl, 8oz mesh, fabric, or adhesive vinyl materials. Each material offers different durability and installation characteristics for specific applications.</p>

<h3>What's the best material for outdoor standard-size banners?</h3>
<p>18oz vinyl provides maximum durability for harsh outdoor conditions, while mesh banners reduce wind load in high-wind areas. 13oz vinyl works well for moderate outdoor use and indoor displays.</p>

<h3>Matte vs. gloss vinyl: which finish should I pick?</h3>
<p>Matte finish reduces glare and works well for outdoor viewing and photography, while gloss finish creates vibrant colors ideal for indoor retail displays and close-up viewing.</p>

<h3>What is the thickness of your banners?</h3>
<p>Our banner materials range from 8oz (mesh) to 18oz (heavy-duty vinyl). 13oz vinyl is our standard weight, providing good durability while remaining cost-effective for most applications.</p>

<h3>Do these banners come double-sided?</h3>
<p>Double-sided banners are available with printing on both faces, ideal for hanging installations and street-facing displays where visibility from both directions is important.</p>

<h3>What finishing should I choose: hems and grommets or pole pockets?</h3>
<p>Hems and grommets work best for mounting with ropes, bungees, or zip ties to fences and structures. Pole pockets are ideal for sliding over poles or rods for clean installations.</p>

<h3>How do I install my banner?</h3>
<p>Installation depends on finishing type. Grommet banners use zip ties, bungees, or rope through mounting holes. Pole pocket banners slide over poles or rods. Consider wind load and mounting surface when planning installation.</p>

<h3>Do you have recommended file types and resolution for printing?</h3>
<p>We accept PDF, AI, EPS, JPG, PNG, and TIFF files at 300 DPI minimum resolution. CMYK color mode ensures accurate color reproduction for professional results.</p>

<h3>Should I include bleed and a safe area in my design?</h3>
<p>Yes, include 0.125" bleed around all edges and keep important text/graphics 0.25" from edges (safe area) to ensure proper trimming and prevent content loss during finishing.</p>
