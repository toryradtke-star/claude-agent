<li>Photoshop files (.PSD) with flattened layers</li>

<ul>
<li>Main banners: 24" wide × 72" high (plus 0.125" bleed)</li>
<li>Sign toppers: 24" wide × 18" high (plus 0.125" bleed)</li>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Vertical Banner Stands</h2>

<h3>What's included with the vertical banner stand?</h3>
<p>Each kit includes the steel banner stand hardware, two 24" × 72" custom printed banners, two 24" × 18" sign toppers, assembly instructions, and all mounting hardware needed for setup.</p>

<h3>How stable is the banner stand hardware?</h3>
<p>Our vertical banner stands feature heavy steel base plates designed for stability in high-traffic areas. The hardware supports dual banners plus sign toppers without tipping or movement during normal use.</p>

<h3>Can I use different artwork on each banner?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can use different designs on each banner and sign topper. When placing your order, include a note about multiple artwork files, and our team will contact you to coordinate the artwork submission process.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between 13 oz and 18 oz vinyl?</h3>
<p>The 13 oz vinyl is our standard material suitable for most indoor displays. The 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl offers enhanced durability with a matte finish and blockout coating, ideal for frequent use and long-term displays.</p>

<h3>How long does production take?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3 business days, with an economy 5-day option available. Production begins after artwork approval, and orders received after 5PM CST process the following business day.</p>

<h3>Is assembly required for the banner stand?</h3>
<p>Yes, some assembly is required, but the process is designed to be tool-free and straightforward. Complete instructions are included, and assembly typically takes 15-30 minutes per stand.</p>

<h3>Can the banner stand be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>This banner stand is designed primarily for indoor use. While the vinyl materials can withstand some outdoor conditions, the hardware is optimized for indoor trade shows, events, and retail environments.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept PDF, high-resolution JPEG/PNG (300 DPI), Adobe Illustrator (.AI), and Photoshop (.PSD) files. Files should be set up at full size with 0.125" bleed and CMYK color mode.</p>

<h3>Do you offer volume discounts for multiple banner stands?</h3>
<p>Yes, volume pricing is available for larger quantities. Contact our sales team at 866.419.7446 to discuss pricing for multiple banner stand orders and business account options.</p>

<h3>How do I submit artwork for multiple components?</h3>
<p>When your order includes multiple artwork files (main banners and sign toppers), include a note in the order comments. Our team will reach out with instructions for submitting and organizing your artwork files.</p>
