<h2>Photo Tile Printing FAQ</h2>

<h3>Can I reposition photo tiles after installation?</h3>
<p>Yes, photo tiles with repositionable adhesive can be moved and repositioned multiple times without damaging wall surfaces or leaving residue. This makes them perfect for rental properties and changing design preferences.</p>

<h3>What wall surfaces work with photo tile mounting?</h3>
<p>Photo tiles adhere to most wall surfaces. Repositionable adhesive works best on smooth surfaces like drywall and painted walls. Permanent adhesive is recommended for textured surfaces like brick, concrete, or wood paneling. Picture hangers work with any surface that supports traditional hanging hardware.</p>

<h3>How large can I print my photo tiles?</h3>
<p>Custom sizing is available to accommodate various project requirements. Contact customer service for large format printing options and pricing for oversized photo tile installations.</p>

<h3>Will photo tiles damage my walls?</h3>
<p>Repositionable adhesive mounting is designed to remove cleanly without wall damage or residue. Permanent adhesive creates a stronger bond and may not remove as cleanly. Picture hanger mounting uses existing wall hardware without additional wall contact.</p>

<h3>What image resolution do I need for quality printing?</h3>
<p>Minimum 800 x 800 pixels for standard sizes, with 300 DPI at final print size recommended for optimal quality. Higher resolution images produce sharper, more detailed results. Our design team can evaluate your images if you're unsure about quality.</p>

<h3>How long do photo tiles last?</h3>
<p>Premium polystyrene construction with scratch, warp, and moisture resistance ensures long-term durability. High-resolution printing maintains color quality over time when displayed indoors away from direct sunlight.</p>

<h3>Can I order photo tiles in different shapes?</h3>
<p>Standard rectangular and square formats are available through online ordering. Contact customer service for custom shape options and specialty cutting requirements.</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk pricing for multiple photo tiles?</h3>
<p>Quantity discounts are available for large orders. Upload multiple images during checkout or contact customer service for bulk pricing on commercial projects and large installations.</p>
