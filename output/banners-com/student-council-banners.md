<h2>Student Council Banner Artwork Requirements for Custom Printing</h2>

<p>To ensure your student council banner prints with sharp text and vibrant school colors, please follow our artwork specifications for optimal results.</p>

<p><strong>File Format:</strong> High-resolution PDF, AI, or EPS files work best for student council banner printing. JPEG and PNG files are acceptable but may have limitations for text clarity at large sizes.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution:</strong> Minimum 150 DPI at actual banner size ensures crisp text and clear graphics when printed at full student council banner dimensions.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode:</strong> CMYK color mode provides the most accurate color reproduction for school colors and student council branding elements.</p>

<p><strong>Text Requirements:</strong> Use fonts larger than 1/2 inch for optimal readability from typical viewing distances in school hallways and outdoor campus areas.</p>

<h2>Student Council Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>What size student council banner works best for school hallways?</h3>
<p>For indoor school hallway displays, banners between 2'x6' and 3'x8' provide excellent visibility without overwhelming the space. Consider ceiling height and foot traffic patterns when selecting your student council banner dimensions.</p>

<h3>Can you match our school colors exactly?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can match specific school colors using Pantone color codes or color samples. Provide your school's brand guidelines or color specifications during the design process for accurate student council banner color reproduction.</p>

<h3>How long will my student council banner last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper installation and finishing, vinyl student council banners typically last 1-2 years outdoors. Indoor display extends banner life significantly, making them suitable for multiple school events and academic years.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to hang a student council banner in a gymnasium?</h3>
<p>Pole pocket finishing works best for gymnasium installations, allowing the banner to slide onto poles or mounting systems. Grommets provide an alternative for temporary gymnasium displays during specific student council events.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for student council banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our in-house design team can create custom artwork for your student council banner. We work with your school's branding guidelines, logos, and messaging to develop professional designs that represent your student government effectively.</p>
