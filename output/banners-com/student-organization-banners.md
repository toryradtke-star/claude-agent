<h2>Student Organization Banner Artwork Requirements & File Specs</h2>

<p>Professional artwork specifications for optimal printing results:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Upload high-resolution files in PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD format. JPEG and PNG files accepted at 300 DPI minimum resolution for best print quality.</p>

<h3>Design Dimensions</h3>
<p>Create artwork at actual banner size or proportional scale. Include 0.25" bleed area around all edges for professional trimming and finishing.</p>

<h3>Color Specifications</h3>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted and may result in slight color variations from screen display.</p>

<h3>Text & Logo Guidelines</h3>
<p>Minimum text size of 1 inch for optimal readability from viewing distance. Embed or outline all fonts to prevent font substitution issues.</p>

<h3>Design Templates</h3>
<p>Download free design templates for your banner size from our template library. Templates include proper dimensions, bleed areas, and safe zones.</p>

<h2>Student Organization Banner Questions & Support</h2>

<h3>What size student organization banner should I order?</h3>
<p>Banner size depends on your display location and viewing distance. For activities fairs and close-up viewing, 3'x6' to 4'x8' works well. For outdoor campus displays and long-distance viewing, consider 4'x10' or larger.</p>

<h3>Can I include our organization logo and school colors?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Upload your organization logo and specify school colors in your design. Our design team will ensure proper color matching and logo placement for professional results.</p>

<h3>How long will my student organization banner last outdoors?</h3>
<p>With proper care, vinyl banners typically last 2-3 years outdoors. Indoor banners can last much longer. Mesh material and reinforced finishing options extend outdoor lifespan.</p>

<h3>Do you offer discounts for student organizations?</h3>
<p>Contact us for information about educational pricing and volume discounts for multiple banner orders or recurring annual orders.</p>

<h3>Can I reorder the same banner design for future events?</h3>
<p>Yes! We keep your approved artwork on file for easy reordering. Simply reference your previous order number when placing a new order.</p>

<h3>What if my banner is damaged during shipping?</h3>
<p>We guarantee your banner will arrive in perfect condition. If shipping damage occurs, contact us immediately for a free replacement at no charge.</p>
