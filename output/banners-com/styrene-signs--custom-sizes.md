<h2>Styrene Sign Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>To ensure your styrene signs print with professional quality and accurate colors, please follow our artwork guidelines. Proper file preparation ensures the best possible results for your business signage.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>

<h4>Preferred File Types</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>PDF:</strong> Vector-based preferred, high-resolution acceptable</li>
<li><strong>AI (Adobe Illustrator):</strong> Native vector files with fonts outlined</li>
<li><strong>EPS:</strong> Encapsulated PostScript with embedded fonts</li>
<li><strong>High-Resolution JPEG/PNG:</strong> Minimum 300 DPI at actual size</li>
</ul>

<h4>Resolution Standards</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vector Graphics:</strong> Preferred for text and logos</li>
<li><strong>Raster Images:</strong> 300 DPI minimum at actual print size</li>
<li><strong>Large Format:</strong> 150 DPI acceptable for signs over 24"</li>
<li><strong>Text Elements:</strong> Vector format recommended for crisp edges</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Specifications</h3>

<h4>Bleed and Safety Areas</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Bleed Area:</strong> Extend background colors 0.125" beyond trim</li>
<li><strong>Safety Zone:</strong> Keep important text 0.25" from edges</li>
<li><strong>Critical Text:</strong> Maintain 0.5" margin for essential information</li>
<li><strong>Logo Placement:</strong> Ensure adequate white space around branding</li>
</ul>

<h4>Color Considerations</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Color Mode:</strong> CMYK for accurate print reproduction</li>
<li><strong>Color Matching:</strong> PMS color matching available upon request</li>
<li><strong>Black Text:</strong> Use 100% black (K) for solid black text</li>
<li><strong>Color Profiles:</strong> Include embedded color profiles when possible</li>
</ul>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Styrene Signs</h2>

<h3>What is styrene and why is it good for indoor signs?</h3>
<p>Styrene is a lightweight, durable plastic material that's ideal for indoor business signage. It provides excellent print quality, professional appearance, and easy installation while maintaining durability for long-term use in commercial environments.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between 0.03" and 0.06" thickness?</h3>
<p>The 0.03" thickness is more flexible and cost-effective, perfect for temporary displays and lightweight mounting. The 0.06" thickness offers greater rigidity and professional appearance, recommended for permanent installations and high-traffic areas.</p>

<h3>Can styrene signs be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>Styrene signs are specifically designed for indoor use. For outdoor applications, we recommend our weather-resistant materials like aluminum, PVC, or outdoor vinyl options that can withstand environmental conditions.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for styrene signs?</h3>
<p>We offer custom sizing starting from 10" x 10" up to 32" x 48". You can choose any height and width combination within these parameters. For larger sizes, please contact us for a custom quote.</p>

<h3>Do you offer double-sided printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer both single-sided and double-sided printing options. Double-sided printing is perfect for hanging displays, trade show applications, or any situation where your sign will be viewed from multiple angles.</p>

<h3>How do I mount styrene signs?</h3>
<p>Styrene signs can be mounted using various methods including double-sided tape, adhesive backing, screws, or display hardware. The lightweight material makes installation simple and flexible for different mounting situations.</p>

<h3>What file formats do you accept for artwork?</h3>
<p>We accept PDF, AI, EPS, and high-resolution JPEG or PNG files. Vector formats are preferred for text and logos, while raster images should be at least 300 DPI at actual print size for best quality.</p>

<h3>Can you help with design if I don't have artwork?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our professional design team can create custom artwork for your styrene signs, including logo recreation, layout design, and typography optimization. Design services are available for an additional fee.</p>

<h3>How long does production take?</h3>
<p>We offer production times from 1-5 business days depending on your needs. Production begins after final proof approval, and we provide tracking information once your signs ship.</p>

<h3>Do you provide proofs before printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide a digital proof for every order that shows exactly how your sign will look when printed. You must approve this proof before production begins, and we include revision rounds to ensure your complete satisfaction.</p>
