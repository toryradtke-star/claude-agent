<ul>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What are styrene signs?</h3>
<p>Styrene signs are printed on lightweight PVC plastic sheets available in 0.03" flexible or 0.06" rigid thicknesses. These durable signs feature UV-resistant inks and are suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications, providing 3-5 years of outdoor durability when properly installed.</p>

<h3>Can I order custom-shaped styrene signs?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer custom die-cutting services for unique shapes and designs. Custom-shaped styrene signs are perfect for logos, branded displays, and creative applications that require more than standard rectangular formats.</p>

<h3>Can styrene signs be printed double-sided?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Double-sided printing is available for styrene signs that will be viewed from multiple angles. This option is ideal for window displays, freestanding installations, and applications where both sides of the sign will be visible.</p>

<h3>What colors are used to print styrene signs?</h3>
<p>We use full-color CMYK printing with UV-resistant inks that maintain vibrant colors and sharp detail. Our printing process can reproduce virtually any color and supports full-color photographic images, gradients, and complex graphics.</p>

<h3>Can styrene signs be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>Yes, styrene signs can be used outdoors. While we recommend indoor use for maximum longevity, our UV-resistant inks allow outdoor installation with 3-5 years of color retention when properly installed and maintained.</p>

<h3>How should I store styrene signs?</h3>
<p>Store styrene signs in a clean, dry environment away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Flexible 0.03" signs can be rolled for compact storage, while rigid 0.06" signs should be stored flat to prevent warping or damage.</p>

<h3>What are common uses for styrene signs?</h3>
<p>Styrene signs are versatile for retail displays, office signage, trade show graphics, restaurant menus, real estate marketing, construction site signage, event displays, and educational materials. They're ideal for both temporary and permanent installations.</p>

<h3>Can I request a proof before printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer digital proofs that include a low-resolution image preview and complete order summary. For color-critical projects, we also provide physical Color Match Proofs to ensure accurate color reproduction before final production.</p>

<h3>What production times do you offer, and when does turnaround start?</h3>
<p>We offer 1, 2, 3, and 5+ business day production options. Turnaround begins when your artwork upload is complete and approved. Orders requiring prepress review or proof approval begin production upon final approval confirmation.</p>
