<h2>Sunday School Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Ensure your Sunday School banner prints with sharp text and vibrant images by following our artwork specifications designed for large-format religious displays.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit Sunday School banner designs as high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD files. JPEG and PNG files are acceptable for simple designs but may limit editing capabilities.</p>

<h3>Resolution Standards for Quality Printing</h3>
<p>Provide artwork at 150 DPI minimum for Sunday School banners up to 10 feet wide. Larger banners can use 100 DPI while maintaining professional print quality.</p>

<h3>Color Mode and Profile Settings</h3>
<p>Use CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction on Sunday School banners. RGB files will be converted but may result in slight color variations from your screen display.</p>

<h3>Text Readability Guidelines</h3>
<p>Ensure Bible verses and Sunday School information remain readable by using fonts at least 1 inch tall for every 10 feet of viewing distance. Sans-serif fonts typically provide better readability on banners.</p>

<h3>Religious Imagery and Copyright</h3>
<p>Use copyright-free religious artwork or provide proper licensing documentation. Our design team can suggest appropriate Christian imagery that complements your Sunday School message.</p>

<h3>Professional Design Assistance</h3>
<p>Churches without design experience can utilize our professional design services. Provide your Sunday School text, preferred colors, and theme concepts, and our designers will create compelling banner artwork.</p>

<h3>Artwork Review and Approval</h3>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Sunday School Banners</h2>

<h3>What material is best for indoor Sunday School classroom use?</h3>
<p>For indoor classroom environments, our 13oz vinyl material provides excellent durability and vibrant color reproduction. Fabric banners are also popular for sanctuary displays due to their elegant matte finish and reduced glare.</p>

<h3>Can Sunday School banners be used outdoors for church events?</h3>
<p>Yes, our 18oz vinyl and mesh banner materials are specifically designed for outdoor use. These weather-resistant materials maintain their appearance through sun exposure, rain, and temperature changes, making them perfect for church festivals and outdoor ministry events.</p>

<h3>How do I ensure Bible verses and text are readable on large banners?</h3>
<p>Use fonts at least 1 inch tall for every 10 feet of viewing distance. For example, if your Sunday School banner will be viewed from 20 feet away, use text that's at least 2 inches tall. Our design team reviews all orders to ensure optimal readability.</p>

<p>Standard shipping requires folding for banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions. Churches preferring rolled delivery to prevent creases can request custom freight shipping. All banners are carefully packaged for protection during transit.</p>
