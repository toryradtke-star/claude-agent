<h2>Table Cover FAQ</h2>

<h3>What are custom table covers?</h3>
<p>Custom table covers are fabric tablecloths designed to cover event and display tables for a clean, branded look. They feature full-color printing of your logos, graphics, and messaging to create professional displays for trade shows, corporate events, and special occasions.</p>

<h3>What material are your table covers made from?</h3>
<p>Our table covers are made from banner soft fabric, a premium polyester blend specifically designed for professional event displays. This material is durable, machine washable, and optimized for vibrant color reproduction through our direct dye sublimation printing process.</p>

<h3>Are table covers machine washable?</h3>
<p>Yes—our table covers are machine washable on a gentle/light cycle in cold water, similar to caring for a standard tablecloth. Air dry first, then tumble dry on low heat if needed to remove wrinkles. This makes them perfect for repeated use at multiple events.</p>

<h3>Are table covers printed in full color?</h3>
<p>Yes—table covers are digitally printed in full CMYK color using direct dye sublimation technology. This process ensures vibrant, permanent colors that penetrate the fabric fibers rather than sitting on the surface, resulting in superior color retention and durability.</p>

<h3>What are table covers commonly used for?</h3>
<p>Table covers are most commonly used for trade show booths, corporate events, job fairs, and conferences. They're also popular for weddings, graduations, anniversary parties, craft fairs, and event gift tables. They provide professional branding while protecting tables from minor scuffs during displays.</p>

<h3>What is the minimum order quantity for table covers?</h3>
<p>There's no minimum order requirement—you can order as few as one table cover or as many as 1,000 or more. This flexibility makes custom table covers accessible for small businesses and large corporations alike.</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk pricing for table covers?</h3>
<p>Yes—bulk discounts are automatically applied as your quantity increases. The per-cover price decreases significantly for larger orders, making them cost-effective for companies that participate in multiple events throughout the year or need covers for multiple booth locations.</p>
