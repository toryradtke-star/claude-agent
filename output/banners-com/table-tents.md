<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Table Tent Printing</h2>

<h3>What sizes are available for custom table tents?</h3>
<p>We offer three standard table tent sizes to fit different table settings and display needs: 4.25" x 6.00" (compact vertical format), 5.00" x 5.00" (square format), and 7.00" x 5.00" (horizontal landscape format). All sizes are printed on 13.4 pt coverstock and include pre-scoring for easy setup.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between matte and gloss table tent finishes?</h3>
<p>Matte finish provides an elegant, non-reflective surface that reduces glare under restaurant lighting and offers easy readability from multiple angles. Gloss finish delivers vibrant color reproduction with enhanced image sharpness and an eye-catching reflective surface that's easy to clean. Choose based on your lighting environment and brand aesthetic preferences.</p>

<h3>How thick are your table tent cards?</h3>
<p>Our table tent cards are printed on premium 13.4 pt coverstock paper, providing excellent stability and durability for restaurant and business environments. This weight ensures your table tents maintain their shape and professional appearance even with frequent customer interaction and table service activities.</p>

<h3>How do I design professional table tent cards?</h3>
<p>For best results, provide vector-based files (.eps or .ai) with fonts converted to outlines, or high-resolution .pdf/.jpg files at 300 DPI. Include 0.125" bleed on all sides and keep important text 0.25" from fold lines. Our professional graphic design team is available to help optimize your artwork or create designs from scratch.</p>

<h3>How are table tents typically used in restaurants and businesses?</h3>
<p>Table tents serve as powerful point-of-service marketing tools for promoting daily specials, drink promotions, dessert menus, and catering services in restaurants. Businesses use them for service promotions, product information, event announcements, and contact details. They're also effective for trade show displays and special event marketing.</p>
