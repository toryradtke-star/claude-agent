# Text - Only Banners

### White Banners with Text

![Apartment For Rent - Text only banner | Banners.com](https://lag.azureedge.net/w2p-cms-1/sliderImages/363d99d3308a4dd3b584d5657bf430b9.jpg)

#### [Material](#materialCollapse)

Text only vinyl banners are printed on high-quality 13oz scrim vinyl. The text only banners have a white background with unlimited text. The text can be multiple colors, but there can be no images, graphic or logos on the banner. If you require these things, please visit our [full color banners page](https://www.banners.com/full-color-banners).

13oz scrim vinyl is rated for both indoor and outdoor use. Outdoor banners can last several years with proper handling, installation and storage.

Exact sizes of banners cannot be guaranteed. Vinyl is a temperature sensitive material, and some fluctuation can occur due to heat application in printing, finishing, and outside climate. Please note our acceptable size variance is up to 1/2 inch per linear foot (ex. A 5' banner could finish with a 2.5" variance). If your banner needs to be precise, please ask your Customer Service Representative to discuss your specific application.

Text only vinyl banners are digitally printed up to 16ft by 60ft without seams. Exact sizes cannot be guaranteed as banners can fluctuate up to 1/2 inch per linear foot.

[Click here to request a sample packet of materials.](https://www.banners.com/sample-packet-request)

#### [Finishing Options](#finishing-optionsCollapse)

The standard finishing option for vinyl banners is heat welded hems and grommets every 2-4 feet around the banner perimeter. You will not need to add any bleeds on your artwork. We will add the extra material when we print your banner.

Additional Finishing Options:

*   2" Pole Pockets - fits a pole 3/4" in diameter
*   3" Pole Pockets - fits a pole 1 1/4" in diameter
*   4" Pole Pockets - fits a pole 1 1/2" in diameter
*   Reinforced Corners
*   Reinforced Hems

By selecting pole pockets, there will be no increase in the finished size of your banner. Pole pockets can either be 2", 3" or 4". We don't recommend having any text or images within 3 inches of the edge of the banner when you select pole pockets.

Pole pockets are not available with reinforced hems or corners.

Reinforced corners are not available on banners under 2 feet in height.

Our durable vinyl material can be used indoors and outdoors. We offer premium finishing options like heat-welded hems and grommets, pole pockets and more. These options can boost the lifespan of your banner especially when using it outdoors.

## Frequently Asked Questions [Return to Top](#top)

### What are some common uses for text-only banners?

Text-only banners are popular for quick announcements like garage sales (dates/times), promotions, and short business messages such as “Help Wanted,” “New Management,” “For Sale,” or “Going Out of Business.”

### What is the smallest text-only banner size I can order?

The smallest size available is 1 ft × 1 ft.

### Do text-only banners have a glossy or matte finish?

Text-only banners are available in premium 13 oz matte vinyl only.

### Are text-only banners printed in full color?

Yes—text-only banners are printed in full CMYK color.

### How should I store my vinyl banner when not in use?

Roll it up (don’t fold it) and store it in a cool, dry place with the printed side facing out to help protect the ink.

### How do I clean a text-only vinyl banner?

Wipe gently with lukewarm water and mild soap using a soft cloth, then let it air-dry completely. Avoid abrasive brushes, harsh cleaners, and power washing.

### How do I remove wrinkles from my vinyl banner?

Unroll it and let it relax flat or hang it, or place it in warm sun for a short time to help it smooth out. You can also use a clothing steamer carefully (no direct high heat on the print).
