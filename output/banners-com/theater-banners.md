<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Theater Banners</h2>

<h3>What size theater banner should I order for my venue?</h3>
<p>Theater banner size depends on your venue and viewing distance. For outdoor theaters, larger banners (6'x3' or bigger) work best. Indoor lobbies typically use 4'x2' to 8'x4' banners. Our team can help you determine the ideal size for maximum impact.</p>

<h3>Can theater banners withstand outdoor weather?</h3>
<p>Yes, our vinyl theater banners are designed for outdoor use and can withstand rain, wind, and UV exposure. For extended outdoor display, we recommend our heavy-duty 18oz vinyl material.</p>

<h3>How long do theater banners take to produce?</h3>
<p>Standard production time is 3-5 business days. Rush options of 24-48 hours are available for urgent theater banner needs with additional fees.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for theater banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our professional graphic designers can create custom theater banner artwork from your text, images, and concepts. Design services are available for an additional fee.</p>

<h3>What's the best way to hang a theater banner?</h3>
<p>This depends on your venue. Grommets work well for outdoor installations, while pole pockets are ideal for indoor hanging systems. We can add custom finishing options to match your specific mounting requirements.</p>

<h3>Can I reuse my theater banner for multiple productions?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! High-quality vinyl theater banners are durable enough for multiple uses. Many theater companies create seasonal banners or reusable company branding banners.</p>

<h3>What if my theater banner arrives damaged?</h3>
<p>We guarantee your satisfaction. If your theater banner arrives damaged or doesn't meet our quality standards, we'll reprint it at no charge.</p>
