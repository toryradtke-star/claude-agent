<p>Professional artwork guidelines to ensure optimal print quality for your trade show banner:</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li>PDF (preferred for professional printing)</li>
<li>High-resolution JPG or PNG files</li>
<li>AI or EPS files with embedded fonts</li>
<li>Minimum 150 DPI at actual print size</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Specifications</h3>
<p>Include 2-inch bleed area around your design to ensure professional edge finishing. Text should be at least 1 inch from banner edges for optimal readability in exhibition environments.</p>

<h3>Color Profile</h3>
<p>Submit artwork in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted, which may result in slight color variations.</p>

<h3>Professional Design Services</h3>
<p>Our experienced graphic designers can create custom trade show banner designs that maximize impact in exhibition environments. Design services include layout optimization, color correction, and trade show-specific formatting.</p>

<h2>Trade Show Banner Questions & Answers</h2>

<h3>What material is best for indoor trade show banners?</h3>
<p>Our 13oz vinyl material is ideal for indoor trade show displays. It provides excellent print quality, professional appearance, and sufficient durability for multi-day exhibitions while remaining cost-effective.</p>

<h3>Can you ship directly to my trade show venue?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer direct-to-show shipping with proper convention center labeling and protocols. This service ensures your banner arrives at the exhibition venue according to their receiving requirements.</p>

<h3>What's the largest trade show banner size available?</h3>
<p>We can produce trade show banners up to 8 feet by 50 feet. Larger custom sizes may be available through special order - contact our team for custom sizing options.</p>

<h3>Do you offer rush production for urgent trade show deadlines?</h3>
<p>Yes, rush production is available in 1-2 business days for most trade show banner orders. Contact us immediately to confirm availability for your specific exhibition date.</p>

<h3>Will my large trade show banner be folded for shipping?</h3>
<p>Banners exceeding 8 feet in both directions will be folded for standard shipping due to carrier regulations. If you prefer your banner rolled, we can arrange freight shipping - contact us for a custom quote.</p>

<h3>Can you help design my trade show banner?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Our experienced graphic designers specialize in trade show banner design and can create artwork optimized for exhibition environments and maximum visual impact.</p>

<h3>What finishing options work best for trade show displays?</h3>
<p>Hemmed edges with grommets every 24 inches provide the most versatile hanging options for trade show booths. Pole pockets are ideal if you're using banner stands or display systems.</p>

<h3>How far in advance should I order my trade show banner?</h3>
<p>We recommend placing orders at least one week before your trade show date to allow for production, shipping, and any unforeseen delays. Rush production is available for shorter timeframes.</p>
