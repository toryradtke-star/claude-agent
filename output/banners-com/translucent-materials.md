<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What material choices are available for translucent (backlit) prints?</h3>
<p>We offer two translucent materials: Translucent Non-Adhesive Backlit Film and Adhesive-Backed Translucent Vinyl. The non-adhesive film is 5.5 mil and scratch and water-resistant, and the adhesive-backed vinyl is 3 mil with a semi-gloss finish and up to 7-year durability with the long-term lamination option.</p>

<h3>What are the size limits for translucent backlit film and translucent vinyl?</h3>
<p>Both material options can be printed up to 144" × 58".</p>

<h3>What colors/ink system do you use to print translucent materials?</h3>
<p>We digitally print using CMYK (cyan, magenta, yellow, and black).</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk pricing for translucent material products?</h3>
<p>Yes—bulk discounts are available, and pricing decreases as quantity increases.</p>

<h3>Can translucent materials be used outdoors?</h3>
<p>Yes, both our adhesive vinyl and backlit film are designed for outdoor use. The adhesive vinyl offers up to 7 years of outdoor durability when laminated, while the backlit film features scratch and water-resistant properties for long-term outdoor installations.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between adhesive and non-adhesive translucent materials?</h3>
<p>Adhesive-backed vinyl applies directly to smooth surfaces like light box faces, while non-adhesive backlit film is designed for mounting systems, tensioning frames, or mechanical fastening methods. Choose based on your specific installation requirements.</p>
