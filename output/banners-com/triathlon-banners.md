<h2>Triathlon Banner Artwork Requirements for Custom Printing</h2>

<p>Professional artwork ensures your triathlon banners print with sharp sponsor logos, clear race information, and vibrant colors that represent your event professionally.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<p>Submit artwork in these preferred formats for best printing results:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vector Files:</strong> AI, EPS, PDF (preferred for logos and text)</li>
<li><strong>High-Resolution Images:</strong> PSD, TIFF, PNG, JPG (300 DPI minimum)</li>
<li><strong>Design Files:</strong> InDesign files with linked images and fonts</li>
</ul>

<h3>Resolution and Size Guidelines</h3>
<p><strong>Print Resolution:</strong> 150 DPI at actual banner size for optimal quality</p>
<ul>
<li>Large format banners: 100-150 DPI acceptable for viewing distance</li>
<li>Small banners with detailed text: 300 DPI recommended</li>
<li>Sponsor logos: Vector format preferred for scalability</li>
</ul>

<h3>Color Profile Specifications</h3>
<p><strong>CMYK Color Mode:</strong> Required for accurate print color matching</p>
<ul>
<li>Convert RGB images to CMYK before submission</li>
<li>Rich black: C=30, M=30, Y=30, K=100 for deep blacks</li>
<li>Pantone color matching available for sponsor brand colors</li>
</ul>

<h3>Text and Logo Legibility</h3>
<p>Ensure race information and sponsor recognition remains readable:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Minimum Text Size:</strong> 1 inch tall for viewing from 10 feet</li>
<li><strong>Font Selection:</strong> Bold, sans-serif fonts for outdoor readability</li>
<li><strong>Contrast:</strong> High contrast between text and background colors</li>
<li><strong>Sponsor Logos:</strong> Minimum 6 inches wide for fence-mounted banners</li>
</ul>

<h3>Bleed and Safe Areas</h3>
<p>Proper margins ensure complete printing without content cutoff:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Bleed Area:</strong> 2 inches beyond finished banner size</li>
<li><strong>Safe Area:</strong> Keep important text 3 inches from banner edges</li>
<li><strong>Grommet Placement:</strong> Avoid placing text near grommet locations</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Services Available</h3>
<p>Our graphic design team can create professional triathlon banners using your race information and sponsor logos. Services include layout design, sponsor integration, and brand compliance review.</p>

<h2>Triathlon Banner Questions & Support</h2>

<h3>What banner material works best for outdoor triathlon courses?</h3>
<p>13oz scrim vinyl provides the best durability for outdoor race courses. It resists tearing, handles wind well, and maintains vibrant sponsor logo colors throughout the race weekend. For very windy locations, mesh vinyl reduces wind load while keeping sponsor graphics visible.</p>

<h3>How large should sponsor logos be for fence-mounted banners?</h3>
<p>Sponsor logos should be minimum 6 inches wide on fence-mounted banners for clear visibility from participant and spectator viewing distances. Title sponsors can be featured larger (12+ inches) for maximum brand impact along the race route.</p>

<h3>Can banners be reused for multiple race events?</h3>
<p>Yes, our vinyl banners are designed for multiple uses. Proper storage between events and careful installation/removal will allow banners to serve several race seasons. Hemmed edges and reinforced grommets extend banner lifespan significantly.</p>

<h3>What's the best finishing option for temporary fence installation?</h3>
<p>Grommets every 2 feet provide secure attachment points for zip-tie mounting to temporary race fencing. This allows quick installation by race volunteers while ensuring banners stay secure during the event.</p>

<h3>How early should I order banners before race day?</h3>
<p>Order 2-3 weeks before race day for standard production, allowing time for design approval and any needed revisions. Rush production (3-4 days) is available for late sponsor additions or urgent replacement needs.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for triathlon banners?</h3>
<p>Yes, our graphic design team can create professional race banners using your event logo, sponsor graphics, and race information. We ensure proper sizing, legibility, and brand compliance for all sponsor materials.</p>

<h3>What happens if banners are damaged during the race?</h3>
<p>While our vinyl material is very durable, we can provide replacement banners with rush production if damage occurs. Many race organizers order a spare banner for critical applications like finish line displays.</p>

<h3>Can you print international sponsor logos and text?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can accommodate international sponsor requirements including foreign language text and global brand logos. Provide vector logo files when possible for best reproduction quality.</p>
