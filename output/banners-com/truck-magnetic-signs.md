<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Truck Magnets</h2>

<h3>Will a magnetic truck sign damage my paint?</h3>
<p>No, properly maintained magnetic truck signs will not damage your vehicle's paint. Remove magnets periodically for cleaning both the magnet and vehicle surface. Dirt or debris trapped between the magnet and paint could potentially cause scratching, so regular cleaning prevents any issues.</p>

<h3>What metals do magnetic signs stick to?</h3>
<p>Magnetic truck signs adhere to ferrous (steel) surfaces only. They will stick to most truck doors, tailgates, and steel body panels. Magnets will not stick to aluminum, plastic, fiberglass, or stainless steel surfaces commonly found on some modern vehicles.</p>

<h3>Can I use magnetic truck signs on other vehicles?</h3>
<p>Yes, truck magnets work on any vehicle with steel body panels, including cars, vans, trailers, and commercial vehicles. Always test a small area first to ensure magnetic compatibility before placing your full order.</p>

<h3>What size truck magnet should I order?</h3>
<p>Consider your vehicle's available flat surface area and viewing distance for optimal readability. Larger magnets (18" x 24") provide maximum visibility for highway driving, while smaller sizes work well for local driving and detailed information display.</p>

<h3>How durable are magnetic truck signs?</h3>
<p>Our truck magnets are built for long-term outdoor use with 30 mil magnetic material and protective lamination. With proper care and periodic cleaning, they can last several years while maintaining strong magnetic hold and vibrant colors.</p>

<h3>Are magnets better than stickers/decals for truck advertising?</h3>
<p>Magnetic signs offer flexibility that permanent decals cannot match. Remove them for personal vehicle use, transfer between vehicles, or update designs without residue or paint damage. Choose magnets for versatility or decals for permanent, lower-cost solutions.</p>
