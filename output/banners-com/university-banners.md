<h2>University Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Ensure your university banner prints with crisp detail and vibrant school colors by following our professional artwork guidelines.</p>

<h3>File Format Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Preferred:</strong> PDF, AI, EPS with embedded fonts and images</li>
<li><strong>Accepted:</strong> PSD, TIFF, PNG, JPG (high resolution)</li>
<li><strong>Avoid:</strong> Word documents, PowerPoint files, low-resolution web images</li>
</ul>

<h3>Resolution & Quality Standards</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Minimum Resolution:</strong> 150 DPI at final university banner size</li>
<li><strong>Recommended:</strong> 300 DPI for optimal print quality</li>
<li><strong>Color Mode:</strong> CMYK for accurate color reproduction</li>
<li><strong>Bleed Area:</strong> Include 2-inch bleed around all edges</li>
</ul>

<h3>University Logo & Branding</h3>
<ul>
<li>Use official university logos in vector format when possible</li>
<li>Maintain proper logo clearance and sizing according to brand guidelines</li>
<li>Ensure university colors match official brand specifications</li>
<li>Include trademark symbols where required by university policy</li>
</ul>

<h3>Design Best Practices</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Text Legibility:</strong> Use fonts 2 inches or larger for viewing distances over 10 feet</li>
<li><strong>Color Contrast:</strong> Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background</li>
<li><strong>Image Placement:</strong> Position key elements away from grommet and hem areas</li>
<li><strong>Viewing Distance:</strong> Design elements should be visible from intended campus viewing distances</li>
</ul>

<h3>File Submission Process</h3>
<p>Upload your university banner artwork during the ordering process, or email files to our design team. Our graphic designers will review all artwork for print quality and contact you if any adjustments are needed before production begins.</p>

<h2>University Banner Questions & Support</h2>

<h3>What material is best for outdoor university banners?</h3>
<p>Our 13 oz vinyl material is specifically designed for outdoor campus installations. It provides excellent weather resistance, UV protection, and durability for extended outdoor display periods while maintaining vibrant school colors.</p>

<h3>How long do university banners last outdoors?</h3>
<p>University banners made with our premium vinyl material typically last 2-3 years in outdoor campus environments, depending on weather conditions and display location. Indoor campus banners can last significantly longer.</p>

<h3>What finishing options work best for university banners?</h3>
<p>For most campus installations, we recommend hemmed edges with grommets placed every 2-3 feet. This provides secure mounting points for campus buildings, fences, and display structures. Pole pockets work well for campus walkway installations.</p>

<h3>How should I submit artwork for university banner printing?</h3>
<p>Upload high-resolution files (minimum 150 DPI) in PDF, AI, or EPS format during ordering. Include official university logos in vector format when possible, and ensure designs follow your institution's brand guidelines.</p>

<h3>What are typical production times for university banners?</h3>
<p>Standard production is 3-5 business days, with rush options available in 1-2 business days or same-day production for urgent campus needs. Production begins after artwork approval and payment confirmation.</p>

<h3>Do you offer university account terms?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide Net 30 payment terms for qualified university accounts, accept purchase orders, and process university procurement cards. Contact our team to set up institutional billing.</p>

<h3>Can university banners be shipped to multiple campus locations?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. We can coordinate delivery to multiple campus buildings, departments, or university locations within a single order. Provide complete campus addresses including building names and department information.</p>

<h3>What if I need design help for my university banner?</h3>
<p>Our in-house graphic design team can create custom university banners using your logos, colors, and messaging. We're familiar with university brand requirements and can ensure your banner maintains a professional appearance.</p>
