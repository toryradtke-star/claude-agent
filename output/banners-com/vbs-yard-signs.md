#### Full Color Custom Yard Signs

These signs are digitally printed in full color on a 4 mm white corrugated plastic material. This material has hollow flutes in it similar to cardboard, which run horizontally on a 4’ x 8’ sheet (the 96 in direction.)

The 4 mm corrugated plastic material is durable, lightweight, waterproof, and can be printed single sided or double sided, making this product a great option for outdoor advertising.

\*Please note, all coroplast yard signs will be cut 1/8th of an inch on each side to allow for finishing. (ex. a 18" x 24" yard sign will actually ship out to you as a 17.75" x 23.75" yard sign.) If your yard sign needs to be precise, please reach out to one of our customer service reps to discuss your specific application.\*

#### Scoring

All signs over 32" x 48" or any sign where the H + W is 81 inches or greater are automatically scored and folded. To score your coroplast signs, we cut through the top layer of the yard sign without cutting through the entire piece. The sign is then folded into an optimal fit to meet shipping guidelines. 

If you don't want your yard signs to be scored and folded please visit our [oversized flat shipping page](https://www.banners.com/oversized-signs-flat-shipping) or contact one of our customer service representatives at: 320-391-5300.

#### Artwork Guidelines

**You will receive a tracking number once your package has been shipped.**

Shipping prices are determined by the weight and destination of the package. Additional shipping charges apply to large orders.

After products have been shipped, all shipping company terms and conditions apply for lost or delayed packages. We will not refund the banner cost if UPS or any other shipping company has a delay in your package.

Delays due to natural disasters or anything beyond our control will not result in shipping refunds. If the product is damaged in shipping, the damaged goods must be returned, and then a replacement product will be sent.

Lost packages will be replaced after the shipping company confirms loss. This generally takes 7–10 business days after the initial scheduled delivery.

**International shipping Notes:**

For international shipments, customers must provide both shipping labels and customs documentation. Currently, we only accept international shipping labels from USPS and UPS. For more details, please contact our customer service team.
