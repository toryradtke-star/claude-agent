<h2>Banner Finishing Options for Mounting & Display Requirements</h2>

<h3>Heat Welded Hems & Grommets for Banner Durability</h3>
<p>Standard finishing includes one-inch heat-welded hems around the perimeter and brass grommets placed every 2-4 feet. This combination prevents tearing and provides secure mounting points for ropes, bungees, or hanging clips.</p>

<h3>Pole Pockets for Banner Mounting Systems</h3>
<p>Available in 2-inch or 3-inch depths for top, bottom, or side mounting. Heat-welded construction creates smooth, professional seams that accommodate poles or rods for clean display installation.</p>

<h3>Reinforced Corners for Heavy-Duty Banner Applications</h3>
<p>Additional corner reinforcement with extra hem material and grommets at each corner. Ideal for banners requiring secure four-point mounting systems.</p>

<h3>Reinforced Hems for Wind-Resistant Banner Installation</h3>
<p>PVC coated webbing reinforcement increases banner strength for outdoor applications. Recommended for banners over 32 square feet or high-wind environments.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Banner Finishing</h2>

<h3>What finishing option is best for outdoor banners?</h3>
<p>Heat-welded hems with brass grommets provide excellent durability for most outdoor applications. For banners over 32 square feet or high-wind areas, add reinforced corners and reinforced hems for maximum strength.</p>

<h3>Can I combine different finishing options?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can combine hems, grommets, and reinforced options. However, pole pockets cannot be combined with reinforced corners or reinforced hems due to construction limitations.</p>

<h3>How are grommets spaced on my banner?</h3>
<p>Brass grommets are placed every 2-4 feet around the banner perimeter, with grommets always placed at each corner. Exact spacing depends on banner dimensions for optimal weight distribution.</p>

<h3>What size poles fit in pole pockets?</h3>
<p>2-inch pole pockets accommodate poles up to 1.5 inches in diameter. 3-inch pole pockets accommodate poles up to 2.5 inches in diameter. Contact us for custom pole pocket dimensions.</p>

<h3>Do finishing options affect banner size?</h3>
<p>Standard hems and grommets are included in your ordered banner dimensions. Pole pockets are also finished within your specified dimensions unless you request them added in addition to the finished size.</p>

<h3>How long does specialty finishing take?</h3>
<p>Standard hems and grommets are available in 1, 2, 3, or 5 day production times. Reinforced finishing may require additional time depending on banner size and complexity.</p>
