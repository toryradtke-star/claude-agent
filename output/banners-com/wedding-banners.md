<h2>Wedding Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Achieve exceptional print quality for your wedding banners by following these artwork specifications:</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>PDF (preferred for professional printing)</li>
<li>AI (Adobe Illustrator files)</li>
<li>PSD (Photoshop files with flattened layers)</li>
<li>High-resolution JPG or PNG (minimum 150 DPI)</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Resolution Specifications:</strong><br>
Submit artwork at 150 DPI at full size for optimal wedding banner print quality. Higher resolution files ensure crisp text and clear image reproduction.</p>

<p><strong>Color Settings:</strong><br>
Design files should be created in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted but may result in slight color variations.</p>

<p><strong>Design Guidelines:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Include 0.5" bleed area around all edges</li>
<li>Keep important text and graphics 1" from banner edges</li>
<li>Use high-contrast colors for outdoor ceremony visibility</li>
<li>Consider viewing distance when selecting text sizes</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Professional Design Assistance:</strong><br>
Our graphic design team can assist with wedding banner creation, layout optimization, and color matching to ensure your vision is perfectly executed.</p>

<p>Artwork proofs are provided for approval before production begins, allowing you to review and approve your wedding banner design.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>Are wedding banners waterproof for outdoor ceremonies?</h3>
<p>Yes, our wedding banners are made from waterproof 13oz vinyl material that performs excellently in outdoor ceremony conditions. The material resists water, maintains color vibrancy in sunlight, and withstands typical outdoor weather conditions during your celebration.</p>

<h3>How do I hang my wedding banner?</h3>
<p>Wedding banners include your choice of finishing options for easy installation. Grommets allow for rope or bungee cord mounting, pole pockets create elegant draping effects, and we provide all necessary hardware. Our team can recommend the best hanging method based on your venue requirements.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided wedding banner?</h3>
<p>Double-sided wedding banners are available and perfect for ceremonies where the banner will be viewed from multiple angles. This option ensures your wedding banner looks professional from all viewing positions at your venue.</p>
