<h2>Proposal Banner FAQ</h2>

<h3>How durable are proposal banners for outdoor use?</h3>
<p>Our proposal banners are made with 13oz scrim vinyl material that's specifically designed for outdoor use. They're weather-resistant, waterproof, and UV-protected to withstand rain, wind, and sun exposure during your outdoor proposal or special event.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for proposal banners?</h3>
<p>We offer proposal banners in sizes ranging from 1'x1' up to 8'x50'. Common popular sizes for proposals include 3'x6', 4'x8', and 2'x10'. Custom sizes are also available to fit your specific proposal location requirements.</p>

<h3>Can I customize the design for my proposal banner?</h3>
<p>Yes! You can completely customize your proposal banner with your own text, photos, colors, and graphics. Use our online design tool or upload your own artwork. Our professional design team is also available to create a custom design for your special moment.</p>

<h3>How long does it take to produce a proposal banner?</h3>
<p>Production times vary based on your selected option: Same-day rush production (orders by 12 PM CST), 1-business day fast production, or 2-3 business days standard production. All timeframes begin after artwork approval.</p>

<h3>Will my proposal banner look good in photos and videos?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our high-resolution printing and vibrant colors are designed to look excellent in photos and videos. We use professional-grade inks and materials that photograph beautifully for capturing your special proposal moment.</p>

<h3>How do I hang my proposal banner?</h3>
<p>We offer several finishing options including grommets for rope or bungee cord installation, pole pockets for banner poles, and reinforced hems for added durability. We also sell installation hardware like bungee cords and banner stands.</p>

<h3>Can I get my proposal banner shipped overnight?</h3>
<p>Yes! We offer overnight shipping for rush orders. If you place your order with same-day production before 12 PM CST, we can ship it overnight for next-day delivery (business days only).</p>

<h3>What if I need to make changes to my design?</h3>
<p>We provide unlimited revisions to your design proof before production begins. Once you approve the final design, we'll begin production. If you need changes after approval, contact our customer service team to discuss options.</p>

<h3>Are there any design restrictions for proposal banners?</h3>
<p>We can print most designs, text, and photos. We recommend keeping important text at least 1 inch from banner edges and using fonts that are readable from your intended viewing distance. Our design team will review your artwork and suggest improvements if needed.</p>

<h3>Do you offer design services for proposal banners?</h3>
<p>Yes! Our professional graphic design team can create a custom design for your proposal banner. We'll work with you to incorporate your ideas, photos, and message into a beautiful, professional design that's perfect for your special moment.</p>
