<h2>Yacht Banner Artwork Requirements for Professional Printing</h2>

<p>Ensure your yacht banner prints with professional quality by following our artwork specifications and design guidelines.</p>

<p><strong>File Format Requirements</strong><br>
Submit yacht banner artwork in high-resolution PDF, AI, EPS, or PSD formats. JPEG and PNG files accepted for simple yacht club logos and designs.</p>

<p><strong>Resolution Specifications</strong><br>
Yacht banner artwork should be created at 150 DPI at final print size, or 75 DPI minimum for large yacht banners over 10 feet in any direction.</p>

<p><strong>Color Mode</strong><br>
Design yacht banners in CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction. RGB files will be converted and colors may shift during printing.</p>

<p><strong>Yacht Club Logo Guidelines</strong><br>
Vector yacht club logos (AI, EPS formats) provide the best print quality and scalability for large yacht banner installations.</p>

<p><strong>Design Considerations for Marine Use:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Bold fonts and high contrast colors improve yacht banner readability from distances</li>
<li>Account for viewing angles from both water and land approaches</li>
<li>Consider wind and weather impact on yacht banner placement and design orientation</li>
<li>Include bleed area (1 inch on all sides) for yacht banners requiring edge finishing</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Professional Design Services</strong><br>
Our graphic design team can create custom yacht banners incorporating your club logo, colors, and messaging. Design services include unlimited revisions until your yacht banner meets your specifications.</p>

<p>Yes, our yacht banners are printed with fade-resistant inks and constructed from materials designed to withstand UV exposure, wind, and moisture common in marine environments.</p>
