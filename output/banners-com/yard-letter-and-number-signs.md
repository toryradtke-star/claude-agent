<h2>Yard Letter Material Options for Durable Outdoor Display</h2>

<p>Our yard letters and numbers are manufactured using 4mm corrugated plastic (coroplast), specifically selected for outdoor yard display applications.</p>

<h3>Coroplast Material Benefits</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Weather-Resistant:</strong> Waterproof construction suitable for outdoor display in various weather conditions</li>
<li><strong>Lightweight Design:</strong> Easy to handle and install while maintaining structural integrity</li>
<li><strong>Hollow Flute Construction:</strong> Built-in channels designed for metal stake insertion and secure ground mounting</li>
<li><strong>UV-Resistant Printing:</strong> High-quality digital printing that resists fading from sun exposure</li>
<li><strong>Flexible Durability:</strong> Resistant to cracking and breaking during normal handling and installation</li>
</ul>

<p>The 4mm thickness provides the optimal balance between lightweight portability and structural stability for yard display applications. This material is commonly used for political campaign signs, real estate signs, and outdoor event signage due to its proven outdoor performance.</p>

<p><strong>Care Recommendation:</strong> While waterproof and durable, we recommend bringing signs indoors during severe weather conditions including high winds to prevent damage and ensure longevity.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What material are yard letters and numbers made from?</h3>
<p>Our yard letters and numbers are printed on 4mm corrugated plastic (coroplast)—a lightweight, durable material with hollow flutes for inserting metal stakes.</p>

<h3>Are yard letter and number signs waterproof?</h3>
<p>Yes—coroplast is waterproof and suitable for outdoor display. Because the signs are lightweight, we recommend bringing them inside during high winds.</p>

<h3>What font styles are available for yard letters and numbers?</h3>
<p>Choose from Capucine Basic Italic, Helvetica Neue Lt 85 Heavy, or Urbane Rounded Heavy.</p>

<h3>Do you sell pre-made yard letter signs like "Happy Birthday"?</h3>
<p>Yes—we offer pre-assembled yard letter sets (including "Happy Birthday") in the same three font styles, with multiple colors and designs.</p>

<h3>What size are the yard letters and numbers?</h3>
<p>Each letter and number is 18 inches tall. Width varies by character and font style.</p>

<h3>How do I order individual yard letters and numbers?</h3>
<p>Go to the yard letters or yard numbers product page, choose your font and options, and add the characters you need to your order.</p>

<h3>What are yard letters and numbers commonly used for?</h3>
<p>They're popular for birthdays, graduations, anniversaries, and holidays, plus business promotions, open houses, school spirit, and sports events.</p>
