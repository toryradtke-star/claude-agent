### Alphabet Yard Letters (26 pc.)

Use our alphabet yard letters to form custom messages for any occasion. These letters are great for welcoming home or congratulating loved ones, retirement parties, anniversaries, proposals, and so much more!

Our alphabet yard letters are printed on a UV and weather-proof 4mm coroplast material. The signs are cut to a height of 18" with the width of each sign varying depending on the letter. Choose the background template and font style for your letter sign display to get started today.

#### [Material](#materialCollapse)

#### Custom Yard Letters Material

These signs are digitally printed in full color on a 4 mm white corrugated plastic material. This material has hollow flutes in it similar to cardboard.

The 4 mm corrugated plastic material is durable, lightweight, waterproof, and can be printed single sided or double sided, making this product a great option for outdoor use.

## Frequently Asked Questions [Return to Top](#top)

#### What material are yard letters printed on?

They’re printed on a  4 millimeter coroplast plastic. This material is durable and lightweight. The plastic material has also been designed with hollow chutes in it for inserting metal stakes.

#### Are yard letter signs waterproof?  

Coroplast is a dream material. It is both waterproof as well as maintenance free! So rest assured you can display your message rain or shine. However, the letters are lightweight, so take them inside during super windy weather.

#### What font styles can I choose from?

You can choose from these three fun font styles:

*   Capucine Basic Italic
    
*   Helvetica Neue Lt 85 Heavy
    
*   Urbane Rounded Heavy
    

#### Are they printed in color?

Yes, our yard letters are all printed in full CMYK color.

#### I don’t want to fuss with individual letters. Do you make a yard letter sign that already says Happy Birthday — or something else?

Why yes, we do! We offer pre-assembled happy birthday messaging in the same three font options listed above. They’re also available in many print and color styles.

#### How tall and wide are these letters?

The letters are 18” high, an average height for lawn signs in general. The letter width will vary depending on your font choice.
