### Numbers Yard Letters (10 pc.)

Use our number yard letters to support your child's jersey number before a big game or use them to take photos with at a family member's graduation party. 

Our number yard letters are printed on a UV and weather-proof 4mm coroplast material. The signs are cut to a height of 18" with the width of each sign varying depending on the letter. Choose the background template and font style for your letter sign display to get started today.

#### [Material](#materialCollapse)

#### Custom Yard Letters Material

These signs are digitally printed in full color on a 4 mm white corrugated plastic material. This material has hollow flutes in it similar to cardboard.

The 4 mm corrugated plastic material is durable, lightweight, waterproof, and can be printed single sided or double sided, making this product a great option for outdoor use.

## Frequently Asked Questions [Return to Top](#top)

#### What material are number yard signs printed on?

These signs are printed on a 4 millimeter coroplast plastic. It is a durable and lightweight plastic material that has also been designed with hollow chutes in it for inserting metal stakes. These stakes hold up the numbers.

#### Are these signs waterproof?  

Coroplast is a waterproof and maintenance free sign material! The yard numbers are lightweight though, so take them inside during super windy weather.

#### Are they printed in full color?

Yes, our yard letters are all printed in full CMYK color.

#### What font styles can I choose from?

You can choose from these three fun font styles:

*   Capucine Basic Italic
    
*   Helvetica Neue Lt 85 Heavy
    
*   Urbane Rounded Heavy
    

#### How do I order individual yard letters?

You can visit our letter yard sign page to get your yard lettering.

#### How big and wide are these number signs?

The letters are 18” high, and the width varies depending on your font choice.
