<h2>Material Options for Custom Shaped Yard Sign Durability</h2>

<p>These signs are digitally printed in full color on a 4 mm white corrugated plastic material. This material has hollow flutes in it similar to cardboard, which run horizontally on a 4' x 8' sheet (the 96 in direction.) The 4 mm corrugated plastic material is durable, lightweight, waterproof, and can be printed single sided or double sided, making this product a great option for outdoor advertising.</p>

<p><em>*Please note, all coroplast yard signs will be cut 1/8th of an inch on each side to allow for finishing. (ex. a 18" x 24" yard sign will actually ship out to you as a 17.75" x 23.75" yard sign.) If your yard sign needs to be precise, please reach out to one of our customer service reps to discuss your specific application.*</em></p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What material are die-cut yard signs made from?</h3>
<p>Die-cut yard signs are made from 4mm white corrugated plastic (coroplast) material. This durable, lightweight, and waterproof material is perfect for outdoor use and can withstand various weather conditions while maintaining its shape and print quality.</p>

<h3>Are die-cut yard signs waterproof and weather-resistant?</h3>
<p>Yes, custom shaped yard signs are fully waterproof and weather-resistant. The corrugated plastic material and UV-resistant inks ensure your signs maintain their appearance and durability through rain, sun, and other outdoor conditions.</p>

<h3>What ground stake options are available?</h3>
<p>We offer several ground stake accessories including H Stakes in sizes 10x15, 10x24, and 10x30, as well as Beefy Stakes for enhanced stability. We also provide zip ties in 12-inch black and 36-inch white options for secure installation.</p>

<h3>Can you help with my die-cut yard sign design?</h3>
<p>Absolutely! Our professional graphic design team can help create your custom shaped yard sign design. We accept various file formats and offer design services to ensure your sign looks professional and achieves maximum impact.</p>

<h3>What are shaped (die-cut) yard signs commonly used for?</h3>
<p>Custom shaped yard signs are popular for business promotions, political campaigns, real estate listings, construction sites, special events like birthdays and graduations, garage sales, and non-profit organization campaigns. The unique shapes help these signs stand out from traditional rectangular signage.</p>

<h3>Do you offer custom sizes for die-cut yard signs?</h3>
<p>Yes, we can create custom shaped yard signs in virtually any size you need. Please note that signs larger than 32" x 48" are automatically scored and folded for shipping. Contact our customer service team to discuss specific size requirements for your project.</p>

<h3>Can I request a proof before printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer order proofs that include a low-resolution image of your artwork, order summary with pricing, and billing/shipping information. Please note that requesting a proof will pause production until you provide approval.</p>

<h3>What production times do you offer, and when does turnaround start?</h3>
<p>We offer production times ranging from 1 to 5 business days: 1-day Priority, 2-day Express, 3-day Standard, and 5-day Saver options. Turnaround time starts from when your file upload is complete, or from approval time if a proof is requested.</p>
