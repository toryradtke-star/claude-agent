<h2>Corrugated Plastic Material for Weather-Resistant Yard Sign Printing</h2>

<p>These signs are digitally printed in full color on a 4 mm white corrugated plastic material. This material has hollow flutes in it similar to cardboard, which run horizontally on a 4' x 8' sheet (the 96 in direction.)</p>

<p>The 4 mm corrugated plastic material is durable, lightweight, waterproof, and can be printed single sided or double sided, making this product a great option for outdoor advertising.</p>

<p><strong>Please note, all corrugated plastic yard signs will be cut 1/8th of an inch on each side to allow for finishing.</strong> (ex. a 18" x 24" yard sign will actually ship out to you as a 17.75" x 23.75" yard sign.) If your yard sign needs to be precise, please reach out to one of our customer service reps to discuss your specific application.</p>

<p>The corrugated plastic material provides excellent durability for outdoor installations, resisting fading, cracking, and weather damage. This lightweight yet sturdy construction makes yard signs easy to transport and install while maintaining professional appearance throughout extended outdoor display periods.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What material are yard signs made out of?</h3>
<p>Our custom yard signs are made from 4mm corrugated plastic (also known as corrugated polypropylene or Coroplast). This durable, lightweight, waterproof material features hollow flutes similar to cardboard construction, providing excellent weather resistance for outdoor installations while maintaining vibrant full-color printing quality.</p>

<h3>Can I order a double-sided yard sign?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer both single-sided and double-sided yard sign printing options. Double-sided signs are ideal for locations with traffic approaching from multiple directions, maximizing visibility for political campaigns, real estate listings, and business advertising applications.</p>

<h3>What is the minimum order quantity for yard signs?</h3>
<p>You can order as few as one custom yard sign. There is no minimum order quantity requirement, making our service ideal for both individual customers and large campaigns requiring hundreds of signs.</p>

<h3>What are yard signs commonly used for?</h3>
<p>Custom yard signs are popular for political campaigns, real estate marketing, business promotions, construction site identification, garage sales, event announcements, directional signage, and community advertising. Their lightweight, weatherproof construction makes them perfect for temporary outdoor installations.</p>

<h3>What are the standard yard sign sizes?</h3>
<p>Common yard sign sizes include 12" x 18", 18" x 24", 24" x 18", and 24" x 36". However, we specialize in custom sizing, allowing any combination from 12" x 12" minimum up to 48" x 96" maximum to fit your specific advertising needs.</p>

<h3>What ground stake options are available for yard signs?</h3>
<p>We offer H Stakes in 10x15, 10x24, and 10x30 sizes, plus heavy-duty Beefy Stakes for windy conditions. Installation accessories include 12-inch black zip ties and 36-inch white zip ties for secure mounting to existing posts or structures.</p>

<h3>Can I request a proof before printing?</h3>
<p>Yes, order proofs are available and include a low-resolution image of your artwork, complete order summary with pricing, and billing/shipping information verification. When you request a proof, production will not begin until you provide approval.</p>

<h3>What production times do you offer, and when does turnaround start?</h3>
<p>We offer 1-day Priority, 2-day Express, 3-day Standard (most popular), and 5-day Saver production options. Turnaround begins when file upload is complete. If artwork requires prepress review or proof approval, timing starts from final approval confirmation.</p>
