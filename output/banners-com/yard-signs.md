<h2>Weather-Resistant Yard Sign Material for Outdoor Installations</h2>

<p>Custom yard signs are a versatile promotional tool most commonly displayed in front lawns for business advertising, political promotion, real estate showings, and yard sales. Lawn signs are perfect for so many different events because they're lightweight, easy to install, and inexpensive when compared to other signage options. They're also incredibly effective at sharing a quick message with passing cars and pedestrians.</p>

<p>These signs are digitally printed in full color on durable 4mm white corrugated plastic material. This material has hollow flutes similar to cardboard, running horizontally on a 4' x 8' sheet. The 4mm corrugated plastic material is durable, lightweight, waterproof, and can be printed single sided or double sided, making this product ideal for outdoor advertising applications.</p>

<p>Here at Banners.com, we make designing, ordering, and displaying your yard sign easy with free online templates, an intuitive online designer, and real-time tracking for your order. You can also add wire installation stakes to accommodate your single or double-sided yard sign for secure property display.</p>

<p><strong>Note:</strong> All coroplast yard signs are cut 1/8th of an inch on each side for finishing. A 18" x 24" yard sign will measure 17.75" x 23.75" when shipped. If precise dimensions are required, please contact our customer service team to discuss your specific application.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Yard Signs</h2>

<h3>What are yard signs and how are they used?</h3>
<p>Yard signs are lightweight, weather-resistant corrugated plastic signs designed for outdoor display on lawns, properties, and public spaces. They're commonly used for real estate listings, political campaigns, business advertising, event promotion, and community announcements. Their durability and easy installation make them ideal for temporary and semi-permanent outdoor messaging.</p>

<h3>What material are your custom yard signs printed on?</h3>
<p>Our yard signs are printed on premium 4mm white corrugated plastic (coroplast) material. This waterproof, UV-resistant material features hollow flutes for lightweight strength and is specifically designed for outdoor durability. The smooth white surface provides excellent print quality for vibrant full-color graphics and text.</p>

<h3>What are the most common yard sign sizes available?</h3>
<p>Popular yard sign sizes include 12" x 18", 18" x 24", and 24" x 36" for standard residential and commercial use. We also offer custom sizing for specific applications. Signs larger than 32" x 48" are automatically scored and folded for shipping, while smaller sizes ship flat.</p>

<h3>Can my yard signs be printed with double-sided graphics?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer both single-sided and double-sided printing for all yard sign sizes. Double-sided printing is ideal when your sign will be viewed from multiple directions, such as on corner properties or in high-traffic areas where visibility from both sides increases your message impact.</p>

<h3>Do you offer bulk pricing for large yard sign orders?</h3>
<p>Yes, volume discounts are available for bulk yard sign orders. This makes large orders cost-effective for political campaigns, real estate agencies, business chains, and community organizations. Contact our customer service team at 320-391-5300 for specific bulk pricing information.</p>

<h3>What production times do you offer for yard signs?</h3>
<p>We offer flexible production times ranging from 1 to 5 business days: 1-day Priority for urgent needs, 2-day Express for quick turnaround, 3-day Standard (most popular), and 5-day Saver for cost-effective orders. Turnaround begins when artwork upload is complete and approved.</p>

<h3>Do yard signs include installation stakes?</h3>
<p>Installation stakes are available as add-on accessories during ordering. We offer H-stakes for various sign sizes (10x15, 10x24, 10x30), heavy-duty Beefy stakes for challenging ground conditions, and zip ties for fence mounting. Stakes are ordered separately to accommodate different installation needs.</p>

<h3>Can I display yard signs without ground stakes?</h3>
<p>Yes, yard signs can be mounted using various methods beyond ground stakes. Options include zip-tie attachment to fences, adhesive mounting to smooth surfaces, insertion into existing sign frames, or leaning against structures. The lightweight corrugated material is versatile for multiple display methods.</p>

<h3>Are yard signs weather-resistant for long-term outdoor use?</h3>
<p>Yes, our 4mm corrugated plastic material is waterproof and UV-resistant, designed to withstand outdoor weather conditions including rain, sun exposure, and moderate wind. For optimal longevity, store signs indoors during extreme weather and clean with mild soap and water when needed. Proper care extends their usable life significantly.</p>
