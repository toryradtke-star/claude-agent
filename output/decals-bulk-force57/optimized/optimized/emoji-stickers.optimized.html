Emoji Sticker Cutting Options

Choose between die-cut and kiss-cut options for your emoji stickers. The main difference between these two cut options comes down to the paper backing on each sticker and how you'll apply them to surfaces.

Die-Cut Emoji Stickers

Die-cut means we cut through both the vinyl sticker material and the paper backing. This cut type doesn't leave any material around the sticker. Think of it like a "cookie cutter" - your emoji stickers are individually cut out to the contour of your design, giving you individually cut stickers with no paper material around the design. Perfect for precise application and professional appearance.

Please note: Back Slit (Crack and Peel) is only available on our Standard and Permanent material options.

Kiss-Cut Emoji Stickers

Kiss-cut means we only cut through the vinyl layer (sticker material) and not through the paper backing, leaving a paper material border around your emoji sticker. When you choose kiss-cut stickers, you'll have extra material around your sticker for easy peeling and application. This option works well for detailed emoji designs and provides easier handling.

Emoji Sticker Artwork Requirements

Proper artwork setup ensures your emoji stickers print with crisp, vibrant colors and sharp details. Follow these guidelines to achieve the best results for your custom emoji designs.

Preferred File Types

The maximum file size we accept is 100 MB. Our preferred file types for emoji stickers are Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated Post Script) and PDF (Adobe PDF). When submitting these files, make sure you convert all fonts to outlines or curves to ensure they print correctly and fonts are not substituted.

Additional File Formats

We also accept JPG (High Resolution JPEG), TIFF (High Resolution TIFF), and PNG (High Resolution PNG) for emoji sticker artwork. These raster formats work well for photographic emoji designs or complex illustrations.

File Setup Tips

Set up your emoji artwork proportionate to the exact size product you're ordering. Convert all text to outlines and only allow for bleed if necessary. Never flatten or merge layers in PSD files, and indicate the cut line clearly for all emoji sticker products.

For raster images, follow these resolution guidelines: 100% scale at 100 dpi, 50% scale at 200 dpi or higher, 25% scale at 300 dpi or higher. Vector art is ideal for emoji designs because they can be enlarged to any size while maintaining crisp outlines and details without sacrificing quality.

Emoji Sticker Installation Guide

Proper application ensures your emoji stickers adhere smoothly and last longer on your chosen surfaces. Follow these steps for professional-looking results every time.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly with soap and water, then dry completely. Remove any dust, dirt, or residue that could prevent proper adhesion. For best results, allow the surface to reach room temperature before applying your emoji stickers.

Applying the Emoji Sticker

Peel the backing paper slowly while applying the sticker to avoid air bubbles. Start from one edge and work across the design, pressing firmly as you go. Use a squeegee or credit card to smooth out any air bubbles, working from the center outward.

Final Steps

For transfer-cut emoji stickers, carefully remove the transfer tape at a 45-degree angle after ensuring the sticker has adhered completely. Allow 24 hours for full adhesion before exposing to moisture or extreme temperatures. Your emoji stickers are now ready to showcase your personality or brand message.

Emoji Sticker FAQs

What material are emoji stickers made from?

Emoji stickers are made from high-quality vinyl material that provides excellent durability and color retention. Our vinyl is designed to withstand both indoor and outdoor conditions, making it perfect for laptops, cars, water bottles, and other surfaces where you want to display your emoji designs.

What cut options are available for emoji stickers?

We offer both die-cut and kiss-cut options for emoji stickers. Die-cut emoji stickers are individually cut to your design's exact shape with no background material, while kiss-cut emoji stickers include a paper backing around the design for easier peeling and application.

How long do emoji stickers last outdoors?

Emoji stickers made with our standard vinyl material typically last 3-5 years outdoors when applied to clean surfaces. The actual lifespan depends on weather exposure, surface type, and environmental conditions. For maximum durability, apply emoji stickers to smooth, clean surfaces away from high-wear areas.

What file format should I use for emoji sticker artwork?

For best results, upload emoji artwork as vector files in EPS or PDF format with fonts converted to outlines. We also accept high-resolution JPG, PNG, and TIFF files. Vector files are preferred because they can be scaled to any size while maintaining crisp details and vibrant colors.

Can I create emoji stickers in custom shapes?

Yes, emoji stickers can be cut to any custom shape to match your design. Our die-cutting process precisely follows your artwork outline, creating perfectly shaped emoji stickers whether you want traditional circular emojis or completely unique custom shapes and characters.

What surfaces work best for emoji stickers?

Emoji stickers adhere best to clean, smooth surfaces like laptops, phones, glass, metal, and painted walls. Avoid applying them to textured surfaces, low-energy plastics, or areas that will experience heavy wear. Clean the surface thoroughly before application for optimal adhesion.

How do I remove emoji stickers without damaging surfaces?

To remove emoji stickers, gently heat them with a hair dryer to soften the adhesive, then peel slowly at a 45-degree angle. Any remaining adhesive residue can be cleaned with adhesive remover or rubbing alcohol. This method works for most surfaces without causing damage.
