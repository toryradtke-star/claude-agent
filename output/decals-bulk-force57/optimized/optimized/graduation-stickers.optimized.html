Graduation Sticker Cut Options for Custom Applications

Choose between die-cut and kiss-cut options for your graduation stickers. The main difference between these cutting methods relates to the paper backing and how your finished stickers are presented.

Die-Cut Graduation Stickers

Die-cut graduation stickers are cut completely through both the vinyl material and paper backing, similar to a cookie cutter. This creates individually separated stickers with no excess material around each design. Your graduation stickers arrive as individual pieces, perfect for easy application to graduation caps, scrapbook pages, or party favors. Back slit (crack and peel) backing is available on Standard and Permanent materials for even easier application.

Kiss-Cut Graduation Stickers

Kiss-cut graduation stickers are cut only through the vinyl layer, leaving the paper backing intact. This creates a border of backing material around each sticker, making them easier to peel and handle. Kiss-cut graduation stickers work well when you need to apply multiple stickers quickly or want extra material for easier handling during application.

Consider your graduation application needs when selecting your cut option. Die-cut offers a clean, professional look for individual placement, while kiss-cut provides easier handling for multiple applications.

Graduation Sticker Artwork Requirements & File Specifications

Submitting proper artwork ensures your graduation stickers print with excellent quality and vibrant colors. Follow our file guidelines to achieve the best results for your custom graduation celebration stickers.

Preferred File Types

For optimal graduation sticker printing, submit Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated Post Script) or PDF (Adobe PDF) files. Maximum file size is 100 MB. When using these formats, convert all fonts to outlines or curves to ensure text prints correctly without font substitution.

Additional File Formats

We also accept JPG (High Resolution JPEG), TIFF (High Resolution TIFF), and PNG (High Resolution PNG) files for graduation sticker artwork. These raster formats work well for photographic elements or complex graduation-themed designs.

File Setup Tips

Set up your graduation sticker artwork proportionate to your exact ordered size. Convert all text elements to outlines to prevent font issues. Allow for bleed only when necessary, and never flatten or merge layers in PSD files. Clearly indicate cut lines for all graduation sticker designs.

Vector vs. Raster Guidelines

Vector artwork (created with mathematical lines and curves) is ideal for graduation text, logos, and drawn elements because it maintains crisp quality at any size. Raster images (made of pixels) work well for graduation photos but require proper resolution: 100% scale at 100 DPI minimum, 50% scale at 200+ DPI, or 25% scale at 300+ DPI to avoid pixelation in your finished graduation stickers.

How to Apply Graduation Stickers

Proper application ensures your graduation stickers adhere smoothly and look professional on graduation caps, scrapbook pages, and celebration accessories. Follow these steps for best results.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly before applying graduation stickers. Remove dirt, dust, oils, and residue using rubbing alcohol or a mild cleaning solution. Allow the surface to dry completely. Smooth surfaces like graduation cap tops, picture frames, and laminated materials work best for graduation sticker application.

Applying Die-Cut Graduation Stickers

Peel the graduation sticker from its backing carefully, avoiding contact with the adhesive. Position the sticker on your desired location and press firmly from the center outward, smoothing any air bubbles. For larger graduation stickers, use a squeegee or credit card to ensure complete adhesion.

Applying Kiss-Cut Graduation Stickers

With kiss-cut graduation stickers, peel away the excess backing material around your design first, then lift the sticker using the remaining backing. Position carefully and apply pressure from the center outward for smooth adhesion.

For graduation caps, ensure the fabric surface is taut and smooth before application. Graduation stickers adhere best to flat, non-textured surfaces. Allow 24 hours for full adhesion strength before handling extensively.

Graduation Sticker FAQs

What materials are used for graduation stickers?

Our graduation stickers are made from high-quality vinyl materials available in standard removable, permanent adhesive, and specialty options. The vinyl provides excellent durability and vibrant color reproduction, making it ideal for graduation cap decorations and long-lasting keepsakes that will preserve your graduation memories.

Do graduation stickers have a matte or glossy finish?

Graduation stickers feature a glossy finish that enhances color vibrancy and provides excellent durability. The glossy surface resists moisture and wear, making these stickers perfect for graduation caps that may be worn outdoors during ceremony events.

What surfaces work best for graduation stickers?

Graduation stickers adhere best to smooth, clean surfaces such as graduation cap tops, picture frames, laptops, water bottles, scrapbook pages, and glass. They work well on plastic, metal, and sealed wood surfaces commonly found on graduation gifts and celebration accessories.

How long do graduation stickers last?

When applied to appropriate surfaces and cared for properly, graduation stickers can last several years indoors and 3-5 years outdoors. The high-quality vinyl material resists fading, cracking, and peeling, ensuring your graduation memories remain vibrant over time.

Can I remove graduation stickers without damage?

Graduation stickers made with removable adhesive can be removed from most surfaces without leaving residue when removed carefully. For permanent adhesive options, removal may require heat application and could leave some residue, but they provide stronger long-term adhesion for keepsakes.

What file format should I use for graduation sticker artwork?

For best graduation sticker quality, submit Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF files with fonts converted to outlines. We also accept high-resolution JPG, PNG, and TIFF files. Ensure your artwork is sized proportionally to your ordered sticker dimensions for optimal results.

Do graduation stickers work for outdoor graduation ceremonies?

Yes, our graduation stickers are weather-resistant and suitable for outdoor graduation ceremonies. The vinyl material and adhesive can withstand normal outdoor conditions including light rain and sun exposure during typical graduation events.
