Cut Options for Custom Oval Label Sheets

Oval label sheets are manufactured using precision cutting methods that ensure clean edges and professional appearance. Our cutting process creates individual oval labels that separate easily from the sheet backing while maintaining adhesive integrity.

Kiss Cut Oval Label Sheets

Kiss cutting cuts through the vinyl layer and adhesive while leaving the backing sheet intact. This method allows individual oval labels to be peeled away from the sheet backing easily, making application quick and efficient. The backing sheet remains in one piece, providing convenient handling and storage until labels are needed.

Sheet Perforations

Label sheets include precise perforations between individual oval labels, allowing for clean separation without tools. These perforations are designed to maintain sheet integrity during shipping and storage while enabling easy label removal when needed.

Each oval label maintains consistent adhesive coverage and edge quality regardless of the cutting method used. Our manufacturing process ensures every label on the sheet meets the same professional standards for appearance and performance.

Artwork Requirements for Oval Label Sheet Printing

Professional oval label sheet printing requires properly prepared artwork files that meet our technical specifications. Following these guidelines ensures your labels print with optimal quality and color accuracy.

Preferred File Types

Vector-based files provide the best results for oval label sheet printing. Our preferred formats include Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated Post Script) and PDF (Adobe PDF) files. These formats maintain crisp edges and precise colors at any size. Convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution during printing.

Additional File Formats

Raster image files are accepted for photographic content and complex designs. Submit JPG, TIFF, or PNG files at high resolution to ensure print quality. Maximum file size accepted is 100 MB to accommodate detailed designs and multiple label layouts.

File Setup Tips

Design files should be set up at 100% scale (full size output) at 100 DPI minimum resolution. For smaller scale files, increase resolution proportionally: 50% scale requires 200 DPI, 25% scale needs 300 DPI, and 10% scale should be 600-1200 DPI for optimal results.

Maintain consistent spacing between individual oval labels on your sheet layout. Include bleed areas only when necessary for edge-to-edge printing. Never flatten or merge layers in PSD files, as this limits our prepress team's ability to make adjustments if needed.

Design Services

Professional design assistance is available for oval label sheet projects requiring artwork creation or file optimization. Our design team can recreate logos, adjust layouts, and optimize files for printing. Most logo recreation projects start at $15, with complex designs subject to custom quoting. Contact our sales team for design service pricing and availability.

Applying & Installing Oval Label Sheets

Proper application technique ensures oval labels adhere securely and maintain professional appearance throughout their intended use. Follow these installation steps for best results with your custom label sheets.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly with rubbing alcohol or degreasing cleaner to remove dirt, oil, and residue. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying oval labels. Room temperature surfaces (65-75°F) provide optimal adhesive bonding during installation.

Smooth, non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, and plastic provide the best adhesion for oval labels. Textured or porous surfaces may reduce adhesive contact and long-term durability.

Applying Oval Labels

Peel individual oval labels from the sheet backing starting from one corner. Position the label carefully, as repositioning may reduce adhesive effectiveness. Apply pressure from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete surface contact.

For larger oval labels, use a squeegee or application tool to smooth the label during installation. Work from center to edges to prevent trapped air and ensure uniform adhesion across the entire label surface.

Final Installation Steps

After application, apply firm pressure across the entire oval label surface to activate the adhesive bond. Allow 24-48 hours for full adhesive cure before exposing labels to moisture, extreme temperatures, or mechanical stress.

For outdoor applications, avoid installing oval labels during extreme weather conditions. Ideal installation conditions include moderate temperature (60-80°F) and low humidity for optimal adhesive performance.

Oval Label Sheet FAQs

How many oval labels come on each sheet?

The number of oval labels per sheet depends on the individual label size you select. Smaller oval labels allow more labels per sheet, while larger labels result in fewer labels per sheet. Our configurator displays the exact label count for each size option during the ordering process.

What surfaces work best for oval label sheets?

Oval labels from our sheets adhere best to clean, smooth, non-porous surfaces including glass, metal, plastic, and painted surfaces. These surfaces provide optimal adhesive contact for long-term durability and professional appearance.

What file format should I use for oval label sheet artwork?

Vector-based files like Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF provide the best print quality for oval label sheets. These formats maintain sharp edges and accurate colors at any size. High-resolution JPG, TIFF, or PNG files are acceptable for photographic content.

Can I order different designs on the same oval label sheet?

Each oval label sheet contains the same design repeated across all labels on that sheet. For multiple designs, you would need to order separate sheets for each design variation. This ensures consistent quality and simplifies the printing process.

How long do oval labels last when applied outdoors?

Oval labels made from our premium vinyl material typically last 3-5 years outdoors when applied to suitable surfaces and properly maintained. Indoor applications generally last longer due to reduced exposure to UV rays and weather conditions.

What is the minimum order quantity for oval label sheets?

Our minimum order is typically one sheet, though this may vary based on the specific size and production requirements. Contact our customer service team at (320) 391-5250 for minimum order details on your specific oval label sheet configuration.

Do oval label sheets require special storage conditions?

Store oval label sheets in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Proper storage maintains adhesive quality and prevents premature aging of the label material before application.
