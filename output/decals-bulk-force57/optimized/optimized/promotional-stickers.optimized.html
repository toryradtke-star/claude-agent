Die Cut vs Kiss Cut Promotional Stickers

Choose between die cut and kiss cut options for your promotional stickers based on application needs and distribution methods. Both cutting methods accommodate the same design files and printing specifications while offering different handling characteristics.

Die Cut Promotional Stickers

Die cut promotional stickers are cut completely through both the vinyl material and paper backing, creating individually separated stickers with no excess material around the edges. Each promotional sticker peels away cleanly following your design's exact contour, similar to cookie-cutter shapes.

Die cut promotional stickers work best for hand distribution, mailers, and applications where individual stickers need separation from backing material. The back slit option (crack and peel backing) is available on standard and permanent adhesive materials for easier application of larger promotional stickers.

Kiss Cut Promotional Stickers

Kiss cut promotional stickers are cut only through the vinyl layer while leaving the paper backing intact. This creates a paper border around each promotional sticker, making them easy to peel and apply. The backing sheet keeps multiple promotional stickers organized on one sheet.

Kiss cut promotional stickers excel for sheet distribution, organized handouts, and situations where you want multiple promotional stickers on a single backing sheet. The paper border provides convenient handling for quick application and prevents small promotional stickers from getting lost.

Promotional Sticker Artwork Requirements & File Setup

Submit artwork for your promotional stickers using our preferred file formats for optimal print quality. Proper file preparation ensures your promotional stickers reproduce accurately with sharp text and vibrant colors.

Preferred File Types

Upload promotional sticker artwork in Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF format for best results. These vector-based files maintain crisp edges at any size and provide precise color reproduction. Convert all fonts to outlines or curves before uploading to prevent font substitution issues.

Maximum file size limit is 100 MB for all promotional sticker uploads. Compress large files while maintaining print resolution for faster upload and processing times.

Additional File Formats

High-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG files are also accepted for promotional sticker artwork. These raster formats work well for photographic elements and complex graphics when prepared at appropriate resolution.

Follow resolution guidelines based on your final promotional sticker size: 100% scale requires 100 DPI minimum, 50% scale needs 200 DPI, 25% scale requires 300 DPI, and 10% scale needs 1000 DPI for sharp printing results.

File Setup Tips

Size your promotional sticker artwork proportionate to the final printed dimensions. Convert all text elements to outlines to preserve font appearance. Clearly indicate cut lines for custom shapes, and include bleed areas only when necessary for full-coverage designs.

Avoid flattening or merging layers in PSD files if submitting layered artwork. Maintain separate layers for easy editing during prepress review. Use CMYK color mode for accurate color matching in final promotional sticker production.

Promotional Sticker Installation & Application Guide

Apply your promotional stickers correctly to ensure strong adhesion and professional appearance. Proper installation techniques help promotional stickers adhere securely and maintain their marketing impact throughout their lifespan.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly before applying promotional stickers. Remove dirt, dust, oils, and residue using isopropyl alcohol or mild detergent solution. Allow surfaces to dry completely before promotional sticker application.

Smooth, non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, and plastic provide the best adhesion for promotional stickers. Textured or porous surfaces may reduce adhesive performance and longevity.

Applying Promotional Stickers

Peel promotional stickers slowly from backing material, starting from one corner. Position the sticker carefully before making contact, as repositioning may reduce adhesive strength. Press firmly from center outward to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete contact.

For larger promotional stickers, use the hinge method by securing one edge and gradually applying while smoothing with a squeegee or credit card. Work out air bubbles as you progress across the promotional sticker surface.

Transfer Tape Removal

If your promotional stickers include transfer tape for multi-element designs, remove the transfer tape slowly at a sharp angle after application. If sticker elements lift with the transfer tape, press down firmly and try removing the tape again more slowly.

Remove transfer tape while promotional stickers are at room temperature for best results. Cold conditions may cause transfer tape to stick more aggressively to promotional sticker surfaces.

Promotional Sticker FAQs

What materials are used for promotional stickers?

Promotional stickers are printed on high-quality vinyl material with permanent or removable adhesive options. The vinyl material provides durability and weather resistance, while full-color digital printing ensures vibrant reproduction of your promotional designs. Standard and premium vinyl options are available depending on your application requirements and budget.

Is there a minimum order quantity for promotional stickers?

No minimum order quantity is required for promotional stickers. You can order as few as one promotional sticker or as many as thousands for large campaigns. Our flexible ordering system accommodates both small test runs and bulk promotional sticker orders with the same high-quality production standards.

How long do promotional stickers last outdoors?

Promotional stickers typically last 3-5 years outdoors when applied to clean, smooth surfaces and exposed to normal weather conditions. Indoor applications can last significantly longer since they're protected from UV exposure and temperature extremes. Actual lifespan depends on surface type, environmental exposure, and application quality.

Can I get design help for my promotional stickers?

Yes, design assistance is available for promotional sticker projects. Our design services team can help create professional promotional stickers from your concepts, logos, or existing materials. Contact customer service to discuss design requirements, pricing, and timeline for custom promotional sticker design services.

What surfaces work best for promotional stickers?

Promotional stickers adhere best to clean, smooth, non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, plastic, and painted surfaces. These materials provide optimal adhesion and longevity for promotional applications. Textured, porous, or heavily curved surfaces may reduce adhesive performance and should be tested before large-scale application.

Are promotional stickers waterproof?

Yes, promotional stickers are printed with waterproof inks on water-resistant vinyl material. This construction allows promotional stickers to withstand rain, washing, and moisture exposure without ink bleeding or material degradation. The waterproof properties make promotional stickers suitable for both indoor and outdoor marketing applications.

What cut options are available for promotional stickers?

Promotional stickers are available in die cut and kiss cut options. Die cut promotional stickers are individually cut through both vinyl and backing for separate handling, while kiss cut promotional stickers remain on backing sheets with paper borders for easy organization. Choose based on your distribution method and application preferences.
