<h3>Preferred File Types</h3>
<p>For best printing results, submit artwork in Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated Post Script) or PDF format. These vector formats maintain crisp edges and accurate colors at any size. Convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution issues during production.</p>

<h3>Additional File Formats</h3>
<p>We also accept high-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG files for business magnet printing. Maximum file size is 100 MB. Ensure raster images meet resolution requirements: 100 DPI at full size, 200 DPI at 50% scale, or 300 DPI at 25% scale.</p>

<h2>Business Magnet Installation & Application</h2>

<p>Business magnets are designed for easy application and removal on ferrous metal surfaces. Proper installation ensures maximum magnetic adhesion and professional appearance for your promotional materials.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the metal surface thoroughly with a lint-free cloth to remove dust, grease, or debris. Ensure the surface is completely dry before applying your business magnet. Smooth, flat metal surfaces provide the strongest magnetic hold and best appearance.</p>

<h3>Applying Business Magnets</h3>
<p>Position the magnet carefully on the cleaned surface, starting from one edge and smoothing across to eliminate air bubbles. The flexible magnetic material conforms to slight surface variations while maintaining strong adhesion to ferrous metals like steel filing cabinets, refrigerators, and vehicle surfaces.</p>

<h3>Removing and Repositioning</h3>
<p>Business magnets can be easily removed and repositioned without leaving residue on metal surfaces. Gently lift from one corner and peel away slowly. Store removed magnets flat to maintain their shape and magnetic strength for future applications.</p>

<p>Compatible surfaces include refrigerators, filing cabinets, metal doors, vehicle surfaces, and any ferrous metal in offices, homes, or commercial environments. For optimal performance, avoid applying to painted surfaces that may scratch or surfaces exposed to extreme temperatures.</p>

<h2>Business Magnet FAQs</h2>

<h3>What materials are business magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Business magnets are constructed from flexible magnetic sheeting with a printable vinyl surface and protective laminate coating. This combination provides strong magnetic adhesion, vibrant full-color printing, and durability against normal wear and weather exposure.</p>

<h3>What surfaces do business magnets stick to?</h3>
<p>Business magnets adhere to ferrous metal surfaces including steel refrigerators, filing cabinets, metal doors, vehicle surfaces, and office equipment. They work best on smooth, flat metal surfaces and do not stick to aluminum, brass, copper, or non-metallic surfaces.</p>

<h3>How long do business magnets last?</h3>
<p>Quality business magnets typically last 3-5 years with normal indoor use and 1-2 years with outdoor exposure. The protective laminate coating helps resist fading, moisture, and scratching, while the magnetic backing maintains adhesion strength over time.</p>

<h3>What is the minimum order quantity for business magnets?</h3>
<p>Business magnet orders start at a minimum quantity of one piece, making them suitable for both small business needs and large promotional campaigns. Volume discounts are available for larger quantities.</p>

<h3>Can business magnets be cut to custom shapes?</h3>
<p>Yes, business magnets can be die cut to follow the outline of your design or logo. This creates unique custom shapes that enhance brand recognition and visual impact. Standard square and rounded corner options are also available.</p>

<h3>Are business magnets waterproof?</h3>
<p>Business magnets feature a protective laminate finish that provides water resistance for normal indoor and outdoor use. While they can withstand moisture exposure, they are not designed for submersion or extreme weather conditions.</p>

<h3>What file format should I use for business magnet artwork?</h3>
<p>Vector files in Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF format provide the best printing results for business magnets. High-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG files are also accepted. Ensure all fonts are outlined and files meet resolution requirements for your magnet size.</p>
