<h2>Magnet Installation & Application Guide</h2>

<p>Custom magnets are designed for easy application and removal on compatible metal surfaces. Follow proper installation techniques to ensure optimal adhesion and prevent surface damage.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the metal surface thoroughly with soap and water, removing all dirt, grease, and debris. Dry completely before applying custom magnets. Smooth, flat metal surfaces provide the strongest magnetic hold and best appearance.</p>

<h3>Applying Custom Magnets</h3>
<p>Position your magnet carefully on the prepared surface, starting from one edge and smoothing toward the opposite side to eliminate air bubbles. Press firmly across the entire surface to ensure complete contact with the metal substrate.</p>

<h3>Removing and Storing Magnets</h3>
<p>Remove custom magnets by lifting one corner and peeling away slowly to prevent tearing. Store magnets flat in a cool, dry location away from other magnetic materials. Clean both the magnet and surface periodically to maintain optimal adhesion.</p>

<p>Custom magnets work best on steel surfaces such as vehicle doors, refrigerators, filing cabinets, and steel building surfaces. They are not compatible with aluminum, brass, or non-metallic surfaces.</p>

<h2>Custom Magnet FAQs</h2>

<h3>What materials are custom magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Custom magnets are manufactured using flexible magnetic sheeting with a white printable vinyl surface that accepts high-quality digital printing. All magnets include a protective laminate coating for enhanced durability and weather resistance.</p>

<h3>What metals do custom magnets work on?</h3>
<p>Custom magnets adhere to ferrous metal surfaces including steel car doors, refrigerators, filing cabinets, and steel building panels. They will not stick to aluminum, brass, stainless steel, or non-metallic surfaces.</p>

<h3>How long do custom magnets last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Most custom magnets last 3-5 years outdoors when applied to clean metal surfaces and properly maintained. The protective laminate coating helps resist fading, scratching, and weather damage during extended outdoor use.</p>

<h3>Will custom magnets damage painted surfaces?</h3>
<p>Custom magnets are designed for safe removal from properly cured automotive paint and appliance finishes without causing damage. However, magnets should not remain on vehicles during car washes or extreme weather conditions.</p>

<h3>What is the minimum order quantity for custom magnets?</h3>
<p>Custom magnets can be ordered in quantities starting from a single piece, making them ideal for both small personal projects and large promotional campaigns. Volume pricing is available for larger quantities.</p>

<h3>Can custom magnets follow the shape of my design?</h3>
<p>Yes, die-cut custom magnets can be manufactured to follow the exact contours of your logo or design artwork. This premium finishing option creates unique shapes that maximize visual impact for promotional applications.</p>

<h3>What file format works best for custom magnet artwork?</h3>
<p>Vector files in Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF format provide the best results for custom magnets, especially for text and logo designs. High-resolution JPG, TIFF, or PNG files work well for photographic content with proper resolution settings.</p>
