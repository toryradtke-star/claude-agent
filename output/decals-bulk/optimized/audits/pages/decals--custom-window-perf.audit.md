<h2>Perforated Window Decal Artwork Requirements</h2>

<p>Submitting proper artwork ensures your perforated window decals print with sharp detail and vibrant colors. We accept various file formats with specific requirements for optimal results.</p>

<h3>Preferred File Types</h3>
<p>For the best quality perforated window decals, submit vector-based files: Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated PostScript) or PDF formats. Maximum file size accepted is 100 MB. Convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution issues during printing.</p>

<h3>Additional File Formats</h3>
<p>We also accept high-resolution raster files: JPG, TIFF, and PNG formats. These work well for photographic elements in your perforated window decal designs but require proper resolution for crisp printing.</p>

<h3>File Setup Guidelines</h3>
<p>Set up your artwork proportionate to your ordered perforated window decal size. Convert all text to outlines, allow for bleed only when necessary, and indicate cut lines clearly. For raster images, follow these resolution guidelines: 100% scale at 100 dpi, 50% scale at 200+ dpi, 25% scale at 300+ dpi, or 10% scale at 600-1200 dpi.</p>

<h3>Design Services</h3>
<p>If you need artwork created or improved, our vectorizing service can recreate logos and images starting at $15. More detailed graphics may require custom quoting. This service requires advance payment and we keep your recreated vector artwork on file for future perforated window decal orders.</p>

<h2>Installing Your Perforated Window Decals</h2>

<p>Proper installation ensures your perforated window decals adhere smoothly and last for years on your storefront windows. Follow these steps for professional results that maximize visibility and durability.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean your window thoroughly with glass cleaner and lint-free cloths before applying perforated window decals. Remove all dirt, dust, and residue that could prevent proper adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely before starting installation.</p>

<h3>Applying Perforated Window Decals</h3>
<p>Position your perforated window decals carefully before removing the backing paper. Start from one edge and gradually smooth the decal onto the glass surface, working out air bubbles with a squeegee or application tool. The perforated material allows some air to escape through the tiny holes.</p>

<h3>Finishing Installation</h3>
<p>Press firmly along all edges of your perforated window decals to ensure complete adhesion. Small air bubbles often disappear naturally within 24-48 hours due to the perforated vinyl's structure. For larger bubbles, use a pin to create a small hole and smooth out the trapped air.</p>

<p>Your installed perforated window decals provide excellent visibility from inside while displaying your graphics clearly to exterior viewers. The perforations allow natural light to enter while maintaining your promotional message's impact.</p>

<h2>Perforated Window Decal FAQs</h2>

<h3>What material is used for perforated window decals?</h3>
<p>Perforated window decals are made from specialized vinyl material with tiny holes that allow one-way visibility. The perforated vinyl provides excellent print quality while maintaining see-through properties from the inside, making it ideal for storefront windows and commercial applications.</p>

<h3>Can perforated window decals be repositioned after installation?</h3>
<p>Perforated window decals are designed for permanent installation and cannot be easily repositioned once applied to glass surfaces. The adhesive creates a strong bond with the window for long-term durability, so careful positioning during initial installation is important.</p>

<h3>Are perforated window decals applied inside or outside the window?</h3>
<p>Perforated window decals are typically applied to the exterior surface of windows for best visibility and weather resistance. Exterior application allows the graphics to face outward toward potential customers while the perforations provide clear visibility from inside your business.</p>

<h3>How large can custom perforated window decals be made?</h3>
<p>Custom perforated window decals can be produced in large format sizes to cover entire storefront windows. Maximum dimensions depend on your specific requirements, and our production team can create perforated window decals sized to fit standard commercial windows or custom architectural features.</p>

<h3>What are common uses for perforated window decals?</h3>
<p>Common applications include retail store promotions, restaurant menu displays, office privacy graphics, and commercial building signage. Perforated window decals work well for any business wanting to advertise while maintaining natural light and interior-to-exterior visibility.</p>

<h3>Do perforated window decals damage glass when removed?</h3>
<p>Properly manufactured perforated window decals remove cleanly from glass surfaces without leaving residue or causing damage. The adhesive is formulated to bond securely during use but allow clean removal when replacement is needed, though some gentle heat application may help during removal.</p>

<h3>How long do perforated window decals last outdoors?</h3>
<p>High-quality perforated window decals typically last 3-5 years in outdoor applications when properly installed and maintained. UV-resistant inks and durable vinyl materials help perforated window decals withstand sun exposure, weather conditions, and normal wear in commercial environments.</p>
