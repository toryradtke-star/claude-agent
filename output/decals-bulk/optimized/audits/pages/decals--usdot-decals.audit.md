<h2>Transfer Cut US DOT Decals</h2>

<p>Our US DOT decals use transfer cut technology to create clean, professional lettering and graphics without vinyl backgrounds. This cutting method ensures your decals meet FMCSA visibility requirements while providing easy application.</p>

<h3>Transfer Cut Process</h3>
<p>Transfer cutting involves precisely cutting vinyl decal material to your specified text and graphics, then removing all excess vinyl material through a process called "picking." This leaves only your required lettering and design elements. Transfer tape is then applied over the remaining vinyl pieces to hold everything in position for application.</p>

<h3>Transfer Cut Benefits for US DOT Decals</h3>
<p>Transfer cut US DOT decals provide clean edges and professional appearance required for commercial vehicle compliance. The removal of background vinyl ensures maximum contrast against vehicle surfaces, meeting FMCSA visibility standards. Multiple pieces of text and graphics can be applied together using the transfer tape system.</p>

<h3>Transfer Cut Specifications</h3>
<p>Vector EPS file format is required for transfer cut US DOT decals. Standard pricing includes 8 picks with a maximum of 40 picks per square foot. Additional picks are charged $0.02 to $0.04 per pick depending on complexity. Highly detailed designs may require <a href="https://www.decals.com/custom-quotes">custom quotes</a> based on cutting difficulty.</p>

<h2>US DOT Decal Artwork Requirements</h2>

<p>Proper artwork preparation ensures your US DOT decals meet FMCSA compliance standards and print with optimal quality. Follow our artwork guidelines for best results.</p>

<h3>Preferred File Types</h3>
<p>Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated Post Script) and PDF formats provide the highest quality for US DOT decal printing. Maximum file size accepted is 100 MB. When submitting these files, convert all fonts to outlines or curves to ensure accurate printing and prevent font substitution.</p>

<h3>Additional File Formats</h3>
<p>We also accept JPG, TIFF, and PNG files at high resolution. These raster formats work well for photographic elements but may have size limitations compared to vector artwork. Ensure sufficient resolution for your final US DOT decal size.</p>

<h3>File Setup Tips</h3>
<p>Set up artwork proportionate to your exact US DOT decal order size. Convert all text to outlines to prevent font issues. Allow for bleed only when necessary and never flatten or merge layers in PSD files. Clearly indicate cut lines for all decal elements to ensure proper transfer cutting.</p>

<h3>Transfer Cut Guidelines</h3>
<p>For transfer cut US DOT decals, use simple and open fonts for best results. All text must be at least 1/2" tall to meet FMCSA minimum height requirements. Maintain at least 1/8" spacing between characters and design elements. Main strokes and line weights should be minimum 1/8" wide. We recommend requesting a proof for all transfer cut projects.</p>

<h2>US DOT Decal Installation Guide</h2>

<p>Proper installation of your US DOT decals ensures FMCSA compliance and long-lasting adhesion to commercial vehicles. Follow these steps for professional results.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Vehicle Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the application area thoroughly with soap and water, then dry completely. Remove any wax, grease, or debris that could prevent proper adhesion. The surface temperature should be between 50-90°F for optimal US DOT decal application.</p>

<h3>Applying Transfer Cut US DOT Decals</h3>
<p>Position the entire decal with transfer tape attached before removing the backing paper. Use a squeegee or application tool to smooth the vinyl onto the vehicle surface, working from center outward to eliminate air bubbles. Apply firm, even pressure across all lettering and graphics.</p>

<h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>After securing the US DOT decal to the vehicle surface, slowly peel away the transfer tape at a low angle. If any vinyl letters lift with the tape, press them back down and continue removal. The completed installation should show only your required DOT information adhered directly to the vehicle surface.</p>

<h2>US DOT Decal FAQs</h2>

<h3>How do I know if I need US DOT decals for my commercial vehicle?</h3>
<p>Commercial vehicles weighing more than 10,000 pounds and operating in interstate commerce require US DOT decals according to FMCSA regulations. This includes trucks, buses, and other commercial vehicles transporting passengers or cargo across state lines. Check with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration website for complete regulatory requirements specific to your operation.</p>

<h3>Where should I place my US DOT decals on my vehicle?</h3>
<p>US DOT decals must be displayed on both sides of your commercial vehicle in clearly visible locations. The lettering must contrast with the vehicle surface and be easily readable by law enforcement. Common placement areas include truck doors, side panels, or cab areas where the information remains unobstructed and visible.</p>

<h3>What size requirements apply to US DOT decals?</h3>
<p>FMCSA regulations require US DOT decal lettering to be at least 2 inches tall for proper visibility and compliance. The lettering must be bold and provide sufficient contrast against the vehicle surface. Our transfer cut process ensures clean, professional lettering that meets these regulatory size and visibility standards.</p>

<h3>Can I include multiple colors and custom graphics in my US DOT decal design?</h3>
<p>Yes, our US DOT decals can incorporate multiple colors and custom graphics while maintaining FMCSA compliance requirements. We offer 11 standard color options, and custom designs can include company logos and branding elements alongside required DOT information. Transfer cutting accommodates complex designs with multiple elements.</p>

<h3>How long do US DOT decals last on commercial vehicles?</h3>
<p>Our vinyl US DOT decals are designed for long-term outdoor durability on commercial vehicles. With proper installation and normal vehicle use, these decals typically maintain their appearance and adhesion for 5-7 years. The actual lifespan depends on environmental conditions, vehicle usage, and maintenance practices.</p>

<h3>Are US DOT decals repositionable after application?</h3>
<p>US DOT decals are not repositionable once applied to vehicle surfaces. The permanent adhesive creates a strong bond for long-term durability but does not allow for repositioning. Careful placement during installation is important to ensure proper positioning and regulatory compliance.</p>
