<h2>Square Paper Roll Label Artwork Requirements</h2>
<p>Proper artwork preparation ensures your square paper roll labels print with sharp detail and accurate colors. Following these guidelines helps achieve professional results that represent your brand effectively.</p>

<h3>Preferred File Types</h3>
<p>For optimal print quality, submit Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF files with all fonts converted to outlines. These vector formats maintain crisp edges and accurate colors at any size. Maximum file size accepted is 100 MB to ensure smooth processing through our production system.</p>

<h3>Alternative File Formats</h3>
<p>High-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG files are also accepted when vector formats aren't available. For raster images, maintain 300 DPI resolution at full print size, or scale proportionally: 200 DPI at 150% scale, or 600-1200 DPI at smaller scales for best results.</p>

<h3>File Setup Guidelines</h3>
<p>Set up artwork proportionate to your ordered label size and convert all text to outlines to prevent font substitution. Include bleed only if necessary for your design. For layered files, never flatten or merge layers as this can affect print quality. Clearly indicate cut lines for proper production processing.</p>

<p>If you need assistance with artwork preparation, our design services can recreate or vectorize existing logos and images. Most recreations start at $15, with complex designs quoted individually. Contact our team for details about design assistance and file preparation support.</p>

<h2>How to Apply Square Paper Roll Labels</h2>
<p>Proper application technique ensures your square paper roll labels adhere smoothly and maintain their professional appearance. Following these steps helps achieve consistent results across all your labeling projects.</p>

<h3>Surface Preparation</h3>
<p>Clean the application surface thoroughly with isopropyl alcohol or a lint-free cleaner to remove dust, oils, and residue. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying labels. Smooth, clean surfaces provide the best adhesion and longest-lasting results for your square paper roll labels.</p>

<h3>Label Application Process</h3>
<p>Peel each label carefully from the backing liner, starting at one corner. Position the label on your intended surface and press down firmly from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. The kiss-cut format makes removal from the backing easy while maintaining label integrity.</p>

<h3>Achieving Professional Results</h3>
<p>Work slowly and deliberately to ensure proper alignment, especially when applying multiple labels. The rounded corners help prevent edge lifting, but proper pressure during application ensures long-term adhesion. For high-volume applications, consider using a label applicator tool for consistent placement and speed.</p>

<h3>Application Tips</h3>
<p>Room temperature application works best for optimal adhesive performance. Avoid applying labels in extreme temperatures or high humidity conditions when possible. Once applied, allow 24 hours for the adhesive to reach full strength, especially on textured or challenging surfaces.</p>
