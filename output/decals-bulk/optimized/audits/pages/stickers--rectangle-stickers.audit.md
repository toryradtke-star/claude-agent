<h2>Rectangle Sticker Cut Options</h2>

<p>Choose the cutting method that best suits your rectangle sticker application. Each cut option affects how the stickers separate from their backing material and how they appear when applied to surfaces.</p>

<h3>Die-Cut Rectangle Stickers</h3>
<p>Die-cut rectangle stickers are cut completely through both the vinyl material and paper backing, similar to a cookie cutter effect. This creates individually separated rectangle stickers with no excess material around the design. Die-cut rectangle stickers provide a clean, professional appearance and are ideal for precise logo applications and branding.</p>

<p>Back slit (crack and peel) feature is available on Standard and Permanent material options for easier application of die-cut rectangle stickers.</p>

<h3>Kiss-Cut Rectangle Stickers</h3>
<p>Kiss-cut rectangle stickers are cut only through the vinyl layer, leaving the paper backing intact around each sticker. This creates a border of backing material around your rectangle stickers, making them easy to peel and apply. Kiss-cut rectangle stickers work well for applications where easy handling and application speed are priorities.</p>

<h3>Transfer-Cut Rectangle Stickers</h3>
<p>Transfer-cut rectangle stickers include a protective transfer tape layer that allows for precise positioning during application. This method is particularly useful for detailed rectangle sticker designs or when applying multiple stickers in exact alignment. The transfer tape is removed after application, leaving only the vinyl rectangle sticker on the surface.</p>

<p>For best rectangle sticker printing results, submit vector-based files including Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated PostScript) and PDF (Adobe PDF) formats. Maximum file size accepted is 100 MB. <h3>Additional File Formats</h3>
<p>Raster image files are also accepted for rectangle stickers, including high-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG formats. These files require specific resolution guidelines to ensure quality printing results.</p>

<h3>File Setup Guidelines</h3>
<p>Set up artwork proportionate to your exact rectangle sticker size requirements. Convert all text elements to outlines to prevent font issues. Include bleed only when necessary for your specific design. For PSD files, never flatten or merge layers, and clearly indicate cut lines for all rectangle sticker designs.</p>

<h3>Resolution Requirements</h3>
<p>Raster images for rectangle stickers require adequate resolution based on final output size: 100% scale at 100 DPI minimum, 50% scale at 200 DPI or higher, 25% scale at 300 DPI or higher, and 10% scale at 600-1200 DPI recommended for optimal print quality.</p>

<h2>Rectangle Sticker Installation Guide</h2>

<p>Proper installation ensures your rectangle stickers adhere securely and maintain their appearance for years. Follow these steps for optimal adhesion and professional results on any clean, smooth surface.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the application surface thoroughly with soap and water, removing all dirt, grease, and residue that could interfere with rectangle sticker adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying rectangle stickers. For best results, surface temperature should be between 50°F and 90°F during installation.</p>

<h3>Applying Rectangle Stickers</h3>
<p>For die-cut rectangle stickers, carefully peel the sticker from its backing and position it on the prepared surface. Apply pressure from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. For kiss-cut rectangle stickers, peel individual stickers from the backing sheet and apply using the same center-outward pressure technique.</p>

<h3>Transfer Tape Removal</h3>
<p>When using transfer-cut rectangle stickers, position the entire transfer tape assembly on the surface and smooth firmly. Slowly peel away the transfer tape at a 45-degree angle, leaving only the vinyl rectangle sticker adhered to the surface. If any portion lifts with the transfer tape, press back down and continue removal more slowly.</p>

<p>For detailed rectangle sticker installation videos and troubleshooting tips, visit our <a href="https://www.decals.com/installation-guides">installation guides</a> section for step-by-step visual instructions.</p>

<h2>Rectangle Sticker FAQs</h2>

<h3>What material options are available for rectangle stickers?</h3>
<p>Rectangle stickers are available in Standard, Permanent, and Removable vinyl materials. Standard vinyl provides excellent durability for most indoor and outdoor applications. Permanent vinyl offers enhanced adhesive strength for long-term installations. Removable vinyl allows for clean removal without residue, making it ideal for temporary applications or surfaces where damage must be avoided.</p>

<h3>How long do rectangle stickers last outdoors?</h3>
<p>Most rectangle stickers last 3-5 years outdoors when applied to clean, smooth surfaces like glass or metal. Actual lifespan depends on material choice, surface type, and environmental conditions including UV exposure, temperature fluctuations, and weather patterns. Premium vinyl materials with UV-resistant inks provide the longest outdoor durability for rectangle stickers.</p>

<h3>What surfaces work best for rectangle stickers?</h3>
<p>Rectangle stickers adhere best to clean, smooth, non-porous surfaces such as glass, metal, plastic, and painted surfaces. Avoid applying to textured, dirty, or heavily curved surfaces as this can reduce adhesion and longevity. For optimal results, ensure surfaces are completely clean and dry before installing rectangle stickers.</p>

<h3>Can I get rectangle stickers in custom sizes?</h3>
<p>Yes, custom rectangle sticker sizes are available to meet your specific dimensional requirements. Standard sizes are offered for common applications, while custom dimensions can be specified during the ordering process. Minimum and maximum size limitations apply based on printing and cutting equipment capabilities.</p>

<h3>What file format should I use for rectangle sticker artwork?</h3>
<p>Vector files like Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF provide the best quality for rectangle stickers, especially when text or logos need to remain crisp at any size. High-resolution raster files (JPG, PNG, TIFF) are also accepted when vector files aren't available. Ensure all fonts are converted to outlines in vector files to prevent font substitution issues.</p>

<h3>Are rectangle stickers waterproof?</h3>
<p>Yes, rectangle stickers made from vinyl materials are waterproof and suitable for outdoor use. The vinyl material and adhesive are designed to withstand rain, humidity, and moisture exposure without deteriorating. This makes rectangle stickers excellent for vehicle applications, outdoor signage, and equipment labeling where weather resistance is essential.</p>
