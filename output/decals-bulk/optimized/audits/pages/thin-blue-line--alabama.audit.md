<h2>State of Alabama Decal Installation Guide</h2>
<p>Proper installation ensures your State of Alabama decals create a strong, lasting bond that looks professional and withstands environmental conditions. Following these installation steps will help you achieve the best possible results.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the installation surface thoroughly before applying your State of Alabama decal. Use warm water and soap, rubbing alcohol, or similar cleaning products to remove dirt, debris, wax, or other contaminants. Allow the surface to dry completely before proceeding. Any moisture or residue can prevent proper adhesion and reduce decal longevity.</p>

<h3>Applying the State of Alabama Decal</h3>
<p>Position your State of Alabama decal using masking tape to ensure proper alignment before removing the backing. For larger decals, create a hinge with tape along the top edge, then gradually remove the backing while applying the decal from top to bottom. Use steady pressure with a squeegee or credit card to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete contact.</p>

<h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>Once your State of Alabama decal is firmly applied, carefully remove any transfer tape by peeling slowly at a 45-degree angle. If any portions of the decal lift with the transfer tape, press them back down and continue removal. Work out any remaining air bubbles by pressing toward the nearest edge.</p>
