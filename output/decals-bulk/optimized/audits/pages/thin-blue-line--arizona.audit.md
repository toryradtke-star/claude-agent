<p>Clean the application surface thoroughly before installing your State of Arizona decal. Use warm water and soap, rubbing alcohol, or common household cleaners to remove dirt, debris, and moisture. <h3>Positioning Your State of Arizona Decal</h3>
<p>Before removing the backing, tape your State of Arizona decal in position on the target surface. Measure and adjust placement to ensure the decal is centered and straight. Create a hinge with tape across the top edge to facilitate controlled application.</p>

<h3>Applying the Decal</h3>
<p>Remove the white backing paper while holding the bottom of the State of Arizona decal away from the surface. Apply pressure with a plastic applicator, squeegee, or credit card, starting from the center and working outward. <h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>After the State of Arizona decal is firmly applied, slowly remove the transfer tape at a 45-degree angle. Press down any loose areas and work out remaining air bubbles. Your decal installation is complete and ready to display your support for Arizona law enforcement.</p>
