<h3>Positioning the State of Kansas Decal</h3>

<h3>Applying the Decal</h3>
<p>Remove the white backing paper from your State of Kansas decal. For best results, start at one edge and apply the decal gradually across the surface. Use a plastic applicator, squeegee, or credit card to smooth the decal and remove air bubbles, working from center outward.</p>

<h3>Final Installation Steps</h3>
<p>After the State of Kansas decal is fully applied, firmly press all edges and details to ensure complete adhesion. If transfer tape is present, remove it slowly at a sharp angle. Press down any lifted areas and work out any remaining air bubbles for a professional finish.</p>
