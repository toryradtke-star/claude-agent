<h2>State of Mississippi Installation Guide</h2>
<p>Proper installation ensures your State of Mississippi decals create a strong, long-lasting bond with optimal appearance. Follow these step-by-step instructions for professional results on any smooth, non-porous surface.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the installation surface thoroughly using warm water and soap, rubbing alcohol, or household cleaning products. Remove all dirt, debris, oils, and moisture that could interfere with adhesion. Wipe the surface completely dry with a clean cloth before applying your State of Mississippi decal.</p>

<h3>Positioning the State of Mississippi Decal</h3>
<p>Before removing any backing material, tape your State of Mississippi decal in the desired position. Use masking tape to secure the decal temporarily while you measure and adjust placement. Ensure the state outline is level and centered according to your preferences.</p>

<h3>Applying the State of Mississippi Decal</h3>
<p>Create a hinge by running tape across the top edge of the decal. Slowly peel away the backing paper while applying the decal from top to bottom. Use a <a href="https://www.decals.com/squeegee">plastic applicator squeegee</a> or credit card to smooth the decal, working from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles.</p>

<h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>For transfer-cut State of Mississippi decals, carefully remove the transfer tape after the decal is fully applied. Peel the transfer tape slowly at a low angle to avoid lifting the decal edges. Press down any loose sections and work out remaining air bubbles with the applicator tool.</p>
