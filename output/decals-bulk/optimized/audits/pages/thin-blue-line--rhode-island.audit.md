<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the installation surface thoroughly using warm water and soap, or rubbing alcohol for optimal adhesion. Remove all dirt, debris, wax, and moisture before applying your Rhode Island thin blue line decal. Allow the surface to dry completely and reach room temperature for best results.</p>

<h3>Positioning the Decal</h3>
<p>Use masking tape to position your Rhode Island thin blue line decal accurately before removing the backing. Measure and adjust placement to ensure the state shape appears centered and straight. Create a tape hinge along the top edge to control the application process.</p>

<h3>Applying the Rhode Island Thin Blue Line Decal</h3>
<p>Peel away the backing material slowly while pressing the decal onto the surface using a plastic applicator or credit card. Start from the center and work outward to eliminate air bubbles. Apply firm, consistent pressure to ensure complete adhesion across the entire state shape.</p>

<h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>Once the Rhode Island thin blue line decal is fully applied, carefully remove the transfer tape at a 45-degree angle. Remove the tape slowly to prevent lifting the decal edges. Press down any areas that may have lifted during transfer tape removal.</p>
