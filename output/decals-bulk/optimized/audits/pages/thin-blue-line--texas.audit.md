<h2>State of Texas Decal Installation & Application Instructions</h2>

<p>Proper installation ensures your State of Texas decal creates a strong, long-lasting bond with excellent appearance. Following these application steps will help you achieve professional results on any smooth surface.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the intended surface thoroughly using warm water and soap, rubbing alcohol, or standard household cleaners. Remove all dirt, debris, oils, and moisture that could interfere with adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely before proceeding with State of Texas decal application.</p>

<h3>Applying the State of Texas Decal</h3>
<p>Position your decal on the surface using masking tape to create a hinge along the top edge. For die-cut decals, slowly remove the backing paper while applying pressure with a squeegee or credit card. Work from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete contact.</p>

<h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>If your State of Texas decal includes transfer tape, remove it slowly at a 45-degree angle after the decal is fully applied. Press down any lifting edges and smooth out remaining air bubbles. The transfer tape should separate cleanly, leaving your decal properly adhered to the surface.</p>
