<p>Proper installation ensures your State of Washington thin blue line decals create a strong, lasting bond that maintains appearance and durability. Follow these steps for professional installation results on any smooth surface.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean your intended surface thoroughly with warm soapy water, rubbing alcohol, or appropriate cleaning solution. Remove all dirt, debris, wax, or moisture that could interfere with adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely before installing your State of Washington decal.</p>

<h3>Applying the State of Washington Decal</h3>
<p>Position your decal using masking tape to ensure proper placement before removing the backing. For larger State of Washington decals, create a hinge along the top edge with tape. Remove backing material gradually while applying pressure with a squeegee or credit card, working from center outward to eliminate air bubbles.</p>

<h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>After your State of Washington decal is fully adhered, slowly remove the transfer tape at a low angle. If any portions of the decal lift with the transfer tape, press them back down and continue removal. Work out any remaining air bubbles using firm pressure.</p>
