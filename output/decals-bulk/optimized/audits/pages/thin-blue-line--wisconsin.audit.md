<h2>Installation Guide for State of Wisconsin Decals</h2>
<p>Proper installation ensures your State of Wisconsin decals maintain strong adhesion and professional appearance for years. Follow these steps for best results when applying State of Wisconsin decals to vehicles, equipment, or other smooth surfaces.</p>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the installation surface thoroughly with soap and water or rubbing alcohol. Remove all dirt, wax, and debris that could interfere with adhesion. The surface must be completely dry before applying your State of Wisconsin decal. Avoid installation in direct sunlight or extreme temperatures.</p>

<h3>Applying the State of Wisconsin Decal</h3>
<p>Position your State of Wisconsin decal using the transfer tape, ensuring proper alignment before removing the backing paper. For die-cut state shapes, take extra care to maintain the Wisconsin border details during application. Use a squeegee or credit card to smooth the decal from center outward, eliminating air bubbles.</p>

<h3>Removing Transfer Tape</h3>
<p>Once your State of Wisconsin decal is firmly applied, slowly remove the transfer tape at a low angle. If any part of the Wisconsin state shape lifts with the transfer tape, press it back down and continue removal more slowly. The completed installation should show only the Wisconsin state decal adhered to your surface.</p>
