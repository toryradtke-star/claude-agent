Custom Adhesive Wallpaper Artwork Requirements

Proper artwork preparation ensures your custom adhesive wallpaper prints with optimal color accuracy, sharpness, and professional appearance. Our specifications are designed to produce the highest quality results on premium vinyl material.

Preferred File Types

For best print quality, submit vector-based files in Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF format. These file types maintain crisp edges and solid colors at any size, making them ideal for wallpaper applications. Maximum file size accepted is 100 MB.

When submitting vector files, convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution during printing. This ensures your text appears exactly as intended on the finished adhesive wallpaper.

Additional File Formats

High-resolution raster images are also accepted in JPG, TIFF, or PNG formats. For optimal print quality on adhesive wallpaper, follow these resolution guidelines:

  • 100% scale (full size output) at 100 DPI minimum
  • 50% scale at 200 DPI or higher
  • 25% scale at 300 DPI or higher
  • 10% scale at 600-1200 DPI recommended

File Setup Tips

Set up your artwork file proportionate to your final wallpaper dimensions to ensure accurate scaling and print quality. Include bleed only if your design extends to the edges and you want to ensure complete coverage during installation.

For custom projects requiring design assistance, our vectorizing service can recreate logos or convert images to optimal print formats starting at $15. Complex artwork may require custom quoting based on detail level and time requirements.

How to Install Your Custom Adhesive Wallpaper

Installing adhesive wallpaper is straightforward when you follow proper surface preparation and application techniques. Our self-adhesive material is designed for easy installation without requiring paste or additional adhesives.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the wall surface thoroughly with mild detergent to remove dust, grease, or residue that could prevent proper adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely before beginning installation. Smooth, non-porous surfaces like painted drywall, glass, or metal provide the best adhesion for adhesive wallpaper.

Test adhesion on a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure compatibility with your specific wall surface and paint type.

Applying the Adhesive Wallpaper

Remove a small portion of the backing paper and position the wallpaper carefully, as repositioning becomes difficult once the adhesive contacts the wall. Use a smooth, firm pressure to apply the wallpaper, working from the center outward to prevent air bubbles.

For larger wallpaper panels, work with a helper to ensure proper alignment and smooth application. Remove air bubbles immediately using a smoothing tool or credit card wrapped in soft cloth.

Finishing Installation

Once fully applied, go over the entire surface with firm, consistent pressure to ensure complete adhesion. Pay special attention to edges and corners where lifting is most likely to occur.

Allow the adhesive wallpaper to cure for 24 hours before cleaning or applying any additional treatments. For installation tips on complementary wall decorations, see our guides for dry erase wall decals and other wall graphics.

Adhesive Wallpaper FAQs

What material is adhesive wallpaper made from?

Adhesive wallpaper is made from high-quality vinyl material with a permanent adhesive backing. The vinyl provides durability and fade resistance while maintaining vibrant print colors, making it ideal for long-term interior decorating applications.

Do I need adhesive paste for installation?

No adhesive paste is required for our self-adhesive wallpaper. The wallpaper comes with permanent adhesive backing that bonds directly to properly prepared wall surfaces, eliminating the need for paste, water, or professional installation tools.

What surfaces will adhesive wallpaper stick to?

Adhesive wallpaper adheres best to smooth, clean surfaces including painted drywall, glass, metal, and sealed wood. Textured surfaces, unsealed materials, or walls with special coatings may not provide optimal adhesion for reliable installation.

How long does custom adhesive wallpaper last indoors?

When properly installed on appropriate surfaces, custom adhesive wallpaper maintains its appearance and adhesion for many years in interior applications. The vinyl material and fade-resistant inks provide excellent durability for residential and commercial use.

What file format gives the best print quality for wallpaper?

Vector files in Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF format produce the highest print quality for adhesive wallpaper. These formats maintain crisp edges and solid colors at any size, ensuring professional appearance in your finished wallpaper installation.

Can adhesive wallpaper be removed without damaging walls?

Removal depends on the wall surface and paint type. While the adhesive is designed for permanent installation, careful removal using heat and proper techniques may be possible on some surfaces, though paint damage cannot be guaranteed.

What is the largest size available for custom adhesive wallpaper?

Custom adhesive wallpaper can be printed in various sizes to accommodate different wall dimensions and decorating projects. Contact our customer service team to discuss specific size requirements and determine the best approach for your installation needs.
