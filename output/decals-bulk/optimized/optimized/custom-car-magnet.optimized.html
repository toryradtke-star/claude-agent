Die Cut Car Magnets

Die cutting creates car magnets that follow the exact outline of your design or logo. Square Cut Car Magnets with Rounded Corners

The standard finishing option for most car magnets, square cut with rounded corners provides a classic rectangular shape with softened edges. Car Magnet Artwork Requirements & File Setup

Proper file preparation ensures your car magnets reproduce with sharp detail and accurate colors. Follow these artwork guidelines to achieve the best results for your magnetic signs.

Preferred File Types

For optimal car magnet quality, submit Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated Post Script) or PDF files. These vector formats maintain crisp edges and allow unlimited scaling without quality loss. When submitting these files, convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution issues during production of your magnetic signs. Maximum file size limit is 100 MB.

Additional File Formats

We also accept high-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG files for car magnet production. These raster formats work well for photographic elements or designs created in pixel-based programs. Ensure these files meet our resolution requirements for the best reproduction quality on your magnetic signs.

File Setup Tips

Set up your car magnet artwork at the exact proportional size you're ordering. Convert all text elements to outlines to prevent font problems. Only include bleed if specifically required for your design. Never flatten or merge layers in PSD files. For die cut car magnets, clearly indicate cut lines in your artwork file so our production team can follow your intended shape precisely.

Vector vs Raster Guidelines

Vector art consists of mathematically defined lines and curves that can be enlarged infinitely while maintaining crisp detail - ideal for logos and text on car magnets. Raster images are made of pixels and have resolution limitations. For raster files, follow these specifications: 100% scale at 100 dpi minimum, 50% scale at 200 dpi or higher, 25% scale at 300 dpi or higher, or 10% scale at 600-1200 dpi for best results.

If you don't have print-ready artwork, our design recreation service can vectorize your logo or image starting at $15. This service creates high-quality vector artwork that we keep on file for future car magnet orders.

Car Magnet Installation & Application

Proper installation ensures your car magnets stay securely attached while protecting your vehicle's finish. Follow these steps for best results with your magnetic signs.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the vehicle surface thoroughly before applying car magnets. Remove all dirt, wax, and debris from the area where you'll place the magnetic signs. Use soap and water followed by a clean, dry cloth to ensure the surface is completely clean. Avoid applying car magnets to freshly painted vehicles - wait at least 90 days for paint to fully cure before using magnetic signs.

Applying Car Magnets

Position your car magnet on the clean metal surface and smooth it down from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. Press firmly around all edges to ensure complete contact between the magnet and vehicle surface. The magnetic signs should lay flat without any lifted corners or edges. Properly applied car magnets will stay in place during normal driving conditions and car washes.

Magnetic Sign Maintenance

Remove car magnets regularly to clean both the magnet and vehicle surface underneath. This prevents buildup of dirt and debris that can scratch your vehicle's finish. Store magnetic signs flat when not in use to maintain their shape and magnetic strength. Avoid exposing car magnets to extreme temperatures for extended periods, as this can affect magnetic adhesion.

Car magnets work best on ferrous metal surfaces like steel. They may not adhere properly to aluminum, fiberglass, or plastic vehicle parts. Test magnetic attraction before final application if you're unsure about surface compatibility. For additional vehicle signage options, consider our custom vehicle decals for non-magnetic surfaces.

Car Magnet FAQs

What material are car magnets made from?

Our car magnets are made from flexible magnetic vinyl material with a printed surface layer and protective laminate coating. This construction provides strong magnetic adhesion while allowing full-color printing and weather resistance for outdoor vehicle use.

What vehicle surfaces do car magnets stick to?

Car magnets adhere to ferrous metal surfaces including steel car doors, truck panels, and van sides. They will not stick to aluminum, fiberglass, plastic, or non-magnetic metal surfaces commonly found on some vehicle parts.

Can I leave car magnets on during car washes?

While car magnets can withstand automatic car washes, we recommend removing them before washing to prevent potential damage or loss. Hand washing around magnetic signs is generally safe, but removal during cleaning allows you to clean both the magnet and vehicle surface thoroughly.

How long do car magnets last outdoors?

Properly maintained car magnets typically last 2-3 years with regular outdoor use. The protective laminate coating resists UV fading and weather damage, while the magnetic material maintains its adhesion strength throughout normal vehicle use and storage.

What sizes are available for car magnets?

We offer car magnets in multiple standard sizes and can produce custom dimensions to fit your specific vehicle requirements. Popular sizes include door panel formats, bumper magnet dimensions, and large vehicle side applications for maximum visibility impact.

Can car magnets be die cut to custom shapes?

Yes, our die cutting service can create car magnets that follow your logo outline or custom shape requirements. Die cut magnetic signs eliminate excess material around your design and create professional-looking results that match your artwork exactly.
