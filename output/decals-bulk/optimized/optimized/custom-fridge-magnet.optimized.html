Artwork Requirements for Refrigerator Magnets

Preferred File Types

For optimal printing quality on your refrigerator magnets, submit files in Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated Post Script) or PDF (Adobe PDF) format. These vector-based formats ensure crisp, professional results at any size. Convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution during printing.

Maximum accepted file size is 100 MB for all refrigerator magnet orders.

Additional File Formats

We also accept high-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG files for refrigerator magnet printing. When submitting raster images, ensure adequate resolution for your final magnet size to prevent pixelation or quality loss.

File Setup Tips

Set up your artwork proportionate to the exact refrigerator magnet size you're ordering. Convert all text elements to outlines to ensure accurate font reproduction. Allow for bleed only when necessary for your specific design. Never flatten or merge layers in PSD files, and clearly indicate cut lines for all magnet products.

Vector vs Raster Guidelines

Vector art consists of mathematically defined lines and curves that maintain quality at any size, making it ideal for logos and text on refrigerator magnets. Raster images use pixels and have resolution limitations - submit raster files at 100% scale with 100 DPI minimum, or proportionally higher resolution for smaller scale submissions.

If you need assistance with artwork preparation, our design services can recreate or vectorize your images starting at $15, with complex designs quoted individually.

Installing & Using Your Refrigerator Magnets

Your custom refrigerator magnets arrive ready to use immediately. The magnetic backing provides strong adhesion to any ferromagnetic surface including refrigerators, filing cabinets, whiteboards, and metal doors.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the target surface with a damp cloth to remove dust, grease, or residue that might interfere with magnetic contact. Ensure the surface is completely dry before applying your refrigerator magnet for optimal magnetic strength and longevity.

Applying Your Refrigerator Magnets

Simply position your magnet on the desired location and press gently. The magnetic backing will immediately adhere to the metal surface. No additional adhesives or tools are required for installation.

Refrigerator magnets can be easily repositioned or removed without leaving residue, making them perfect for frequently updated information like menus, contact details, or promotional messages.

Maximizing Magnet Performance

For best results, place refrigerator magnets on flat, smooth metal surfaces. Avoid textured or painted surfaces that may reduce magnetic contact. Store unused magnets flat and avoid stacking multiple magnets together to prevent damage to printed surfaces.

The laminated finish protects your refrigerator magnets from moisture, fading, and everyday wear, ensuring your message remains visible and professional-looking for extended periods.

Custom Refrigerator Magnet FAQs

What materials are refrigerator magnets made from?

Our refrigerator magnets are made from durable vinyl material with a flexible magnetic backing and protective laminate coating. The magnetic base provides strong adhesion to metal surfaces while the laminated finish protects against moisture, fading, and everyday wear for long-lasting promotional visibility.

What surfaces do refrigerator magnets stick to?

Refrigerator magnets adhere to any ferromagnetic metal surface including refrigerators, filing cabinets, whiteboards, metal doors, and appliances. They work best on smooth, flat metal surfaces and will not stick to aluminum, brass, copper, or non-metal surfaces.

How long do custom refrigerator magnets last?

With proper care, refrigerator magnets typically last 2-5 years depending on environmental conditions and usage. The laminated finish protects against fading and moisture damage, while the magnetic backing maintains its strength over extended periods when stored and used properly.

What file formats work best for refrigerator magnet artwork?

Vector files in EPS or PDF format produce the highest quality results for refrigerator magnet printing. These formats maintain crisp edges and text at any size. High-resolution JPG, PNG, and TIFF files are also acceptable when provided at adequate resolution for your final magnet size.

Can refrigerator magnets be cut to custom shapes?

Yes, we offer die cutting services to create refrigerator magnets in custom shapes that match your artwork or logo outline. You can also choose standard square cut or square cut with rounded corners depending on your design preferences and intended use.

What are the most common uses for custom refrigerator magnets?

Businesses commonly use refrigerator magnets for contact information, restaurant takeout menus, service reminders, real estate marketing, and brand promotion. They're also popular for personal use including photo magnets, save-the-date announcements, and promotional giveaways at trade shows and events.
