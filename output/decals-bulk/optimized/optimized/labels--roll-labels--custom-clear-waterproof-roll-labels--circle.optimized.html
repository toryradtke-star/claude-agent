Clear Circle Waterproof Roll Labels Artwork Requirements

Proper artwork preparation ensures the best possible results for your clear circle waterproof roll labels. Follow these guidelines to achieve optimal print quality and color accuracy on transparent BOPP material.

Preferred File Types

Vector artwork is required for clear circle waterproof roll labels: EPS, AI, and PDF formats provide the best results. These file types maintain sharp edges and crisp details at any size. When submitting vector files, convert all fonts to outlines or curves to ensure accurate font reproduction.

Additional File Formats

If vector artwork is not available, high-resolution raster files are acceptable with proper specifications: - 100% scale at 100 dpi minimum - 50% scale at 200 dpi or higher - 25% scale at 300 dpi or higher - 10% scale at 600-1200 dpi recommended

File Setup Guidelines

Set up artwork proportionate to your exact clear circle waterproof roll label size. Clearly indicate cut lines for proper die-cutting. Allow for bleed only if necessary, and never flatten or merge layers in PSD files. Remember that transparent areas in your design will show the underlying product or packaging.

Design Services

If you need artwork creation or vectorization services, our design team can recreate most logos or images starting at $15. More detailed images may require custom quoting. Clear Circle Waterproof Roll Labels Installation Guide

Proper installation of your clear circle waterproof roll labels ensures maximum adhesion, longevity, and professional appearance. Follow these steps for optimal results on various surfaces.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly with isopropyl alcohol or degreasing agent to remove dirt, oils, and residue. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying your clear circle waterproof roll labels. Ensure the surface temperature is between 50-90°F for optimal adhesion.

Applying Clear Circle Waterproof Roll Labels

Peel each label from the roll liner and position carefully on the cleaned surface. Apply firm, even pressure from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. The waterproof adhesive bonds immediately, so position accurately on first application. Use a squeegee or smooth tool for larger labels to ensure complete contact.

Final Installation Steps

After application, allow 24-48 hours for the adhesive to fully cure before exposing your clear circle waterproof roll labels to moisture or cleaning chemicals. This curing period ensures maximum bond strength and waterproof performance.

For best results on curved surfaces like bottles and jars, apply labels at room temperature and allow extra time for the material to conform to the surface contours. The BOPP material will adapt to slight curves while maintaining its waterproof properties.

Clear Circle Waterproof Roll Labels FAQs

What surfaces work best for clear circle waterproof roll labels?

Clear circle waterproof roll labels adhere best to clean, smooth surfaces like glass, plastic, metal, and coated cardboard. The BOPP material and waterproof adhesive perform excellently on bottles, jars, containers, and packaging that may be exposed to moisture or refrigeration.

How long do clear circle waterproof roll labels last outdoors?

Clear circle waterproof roll labels typically last 2-3 years outdoors when applied to clean surfaces. The BOPP material resists UV fading, moisture, and temperature fluctuations, making these labels suitable for products stored in various environmental conditions.

What file format should I use for clear circle waterproof roll labels?

Vector artwork in EPS, AI, or PDF format provides the best results for clear circle waterproof roll labels. These formats ensure crisp edges and accurate colors on transparent material. If using raster images, provide files at 300 dpi or higher at actual size.

Can clear circle waterproof roll labels be removed without residue?

Clear circle waterproof roll labels use permanent adhesive designed for long-term application. While they can be removed with heat and adhesive remover, they are not intended for repositioning. Consider removable adhesive options if temporary application is needed.

Are clear circle waterproof roll labels food safe?

Yes, our clear circle waterproof roll labels meet FDA requirements for indirect food contact applications. The BOPP material and adhesive are safe for food packaging labeling, including refrigerated and frozen products where moisture resistance is critical.

What is the minimum order quantity for clear circle waterproof roll labels?

Our clear circle waterproof roll labels have flexible minimum quantities depending on size and customization requirements. Contact our customer service team at (320) 391-5250 for specific quantity options and pricing for your labeling project.

Do clear circle waterproof roll labels work in freezer conditions?

Yes, clear circle waterproof roll labels maintain adhesion and clarity in freezer temperatures down to -10°F. The BOPP material remains flexible and the waterproof adhesive continues to bond effectively, making these labels ideal for frozen food packaging and cold storage applications.
