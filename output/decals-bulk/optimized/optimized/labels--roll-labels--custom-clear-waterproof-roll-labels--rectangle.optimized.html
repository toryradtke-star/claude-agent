Additional File Formats

High-resolution raster files are acceptable when vector artwork isn't available. Submit raster images at 100% scale with 100 DPI minimum, or provide smaller scale versions with proportionally higher resolution (50% scale at 200 DPI, 25% scale at 300 DPI, etc.).

Installation & Application Guide for Clear Rectangle Waterproof Roll Labels

Proper application ensures your clear rectangle waterproof roll labels adhere securely and maintain their professional appearance throughout their service life. Clean surface preparation and correct application technique maximize adhesion and durability.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly with isopropyl alcohol or degreasing solution to remove oils, dust, and residue. Allow surfaces to dry completely before applying clear rectangle waterproof roll labels. Smooth, non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, and high-quality plastics provide optimal adhesion.

Applying Clear Rectangle Waterproof Roll Labels

Remove labels from the backing liner carefully to avoid stretching or damaging the material. Position the label accurately before making contact with the surface, as repositioning may compromise adhesion. Apply firm, even pressure from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete contact.

For curved surfaces like bottles, start application at one edge and work across the label gradually. This technique prevents wrinkles and ensures the BOPP material conforms properly to the surface curvature without creating stress points.

Final Application Tips

Allow 24-48 hours for full adhesive cure before exposing clear rectangle waterproof roll labels to moisture, temperature extremes, or mechanical stress. The rounded corners design helps prevent edge lifting, but proper surface preparation remains critical for long-term performance.

What cut options are available for clear rectangle waterproof roll labels?

Why choose clear rectangle waterproof roll labels for my products?

Clear rectangle waterproof roll labels offer transparent branding that allows your product colors and textures to show through while providing essential product information. How long do clear rectangle waterproof roll labels last on products?

Clear rectangle waterproof roll labels maintain their adhesion and clarity for 2-3 years when properly applied to clean, smooth surfaces. The BOPP material resists fading, cracking, and moisture damage, ensuring your product labeling remains professional throughout the product's shelf life.

What surfaces work best with clear rectangle waterproof roll labels?

Clear rectangle waterproof roll labels adhere best to smooth, non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, high-quality plastics, and sealed containers. Clear rectangle waterproof roll labels can be removed from most surfaces, though some adhesive residue may remain depending on surface type and application duration. The BOPP material typically removes cleanly from glass and metal surfaces when warmed slightly during removal.
