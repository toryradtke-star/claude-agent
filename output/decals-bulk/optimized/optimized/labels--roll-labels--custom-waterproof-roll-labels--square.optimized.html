Square Waterproof Roll Label Artwork Requirements

Achieving optimal print quality for your square waterproof roll labels requires properly prepared artwork files. Our specifications ensure your designs print clearly and cut accurately for professional results.

Preferred File Types

For best results with square waterproof roll labels, submit Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF files with all fonts converted to outlines. These vector formats provide crisp text and graphics at any size while ensuring accurate color reproduction. Maximum file size accepted is 100 MB.

Additional File Formats

We also accept high-resolution JPG, TIFF, and PNG files for square waterproof roll label artwork. Raster files should be submitted at 100% scale with 100 DPI resolution, or proportionally higher resolution for smaller scale submissions (200 DPI at 50% scale, 300 DPI at 25% scale).

File Setup Guidelines

Set up your square waterproof roll label artwork proportionate to your ordered size. Convert all text to outlines to prevent font substitution issues. Include bleed only if necessary, and clearly indicate cut lines for proper die-cutting. Never flatten layers in PSD files, as this limits our ability to make production adjustments.

Square Waterproof Roll Label Installation & Application

Proper application ensures your square waterproof roll labels provide maximum adhesion and longevity. These waterproof labels work best on clean, smooth surfaces and can be applied to bottles, jars, containers, and packaging materials.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly before applying square waterproof roll labels. Remove any dust, oil, moisture, or residue that could interfere with adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely. Room temperature surfaces (65-75°F) provide optimal bonding conditions for waterproof label adhesive.

Applying Square Waterproof Roll Labels

Peel the label from the roll backing and position it carefully on your target surface. Start from one edge and smooth across to eliminate air bubbles. Apply firm, even pressure across the entire label surface. The rounded corners make application easier and reduce the risk of lifting during handling.

Installation Tips

For curved surfaces like bottles, apply square waterproof roll labels slowly to prevent wrinkles or bubbles. Use a squeegee or credit card to smooth larger labels during application. Once applied, allow 24 hours for full adhesive bonding before exposure to moisture or temperature extremes.

These square waterproof roll labels resist water, oils, and chemicals once properly bonded, making them ideal for food service, cosmetic packaging, and industrial applications where moisture resistance is essential.
