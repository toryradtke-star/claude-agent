Installation Guide for Plastic Squeegees

Proper plastic squeegee technique ensures professional vinyl decal installation results. These application tools work best when used with consistent pressure and overlapping strokes to eliminate air bubbles and ensure strong adhesion.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly before beginning vinyl decal installation. Remove any dust, oils, or debris that could interfere with adhesion. Ensure the surface is completely dry before proceeding.

Using the Plastic Squeegee

Hold the squeegee at a 45-degree angle to the vinyl surface. Apply firm, consistent pressure while moving in overlapping strokes from the center outward. This technique prevents air bubbles and ensures complete vinyl adhesion.

Removing Transfer Tape

After using the plastic squeegee to secure the vinyl decal, carefully remove transfer tape at a low angle. The squeegee application ensures the vinyl remains properly adhered during tape removal.
