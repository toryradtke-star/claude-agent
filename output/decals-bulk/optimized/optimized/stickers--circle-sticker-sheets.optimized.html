Circle Sticker Sheet Cut Options

Circle sticker sheets are produced using kiss-cut technology that allows individual round stickers to be easily removed from the backing sheet while keeping the overall sheet intact for storage and distribution.

Kiss Cut Circle Sticker Sheets

Kiss cutting cuts through the face material and adhesive layer while leaving the backing paper intact. This creates individual circle stickers that can be peeled away from the backing sheet one at a time. The backing sheet remains whole, making it easy to handle multiple stickers and distribute them efficiently.

Kiss cut circle sticker sheets are ideal for applications requiring individual sticker removal while maintaining organized presentation. Each circle can be peeled and applied separately, while unused stickers remain protected on the backing sheet.

Sheet Layout Options

We can arrange multiple circles on each sheet according to your specifications. Common layouts include uniform grids, varied sizes on single sheets, or custom spacing to match your specific distribution needs. The kiss-cut process ensures clean edges on each individual circle while maintaining easy removal from the sheet.

Circle Sticker Sheet Artwork Requirements

Proper artwork preparation ensures your circle sticker sheets print with optimal quality and accurate colors. Follow our specifications to achieve professional results with sharp details and vibrant color reproduction.

Preferred File Types

For best results with circle sticker sheets, provide vector files in Adobe Illustrator EPS or PDF format. These file types maintain crisp edges and allow unlimited scaling without quality loss. Convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution during production.

Additional File Formats

We also accept high-resolution raster files including JPG, TIFF, and PNG formats. For circle sticker sheets, raster files should be provided at 300 DPI at final print size to ensure sharp reproduction of details and text.

File Setup Tips

Design your artwork as a single circle at the exact size you want each individual sticker to be. Our production team will arrange multiple copies on each sheet according to your quantity specifications. Include any necessary bleed area if your design extends to the edge of each circle.

For circle sticker sheets with text, ensure all fonts are outlined and text is large enough to remain legible at the final circle size. Small text can become difficult to read on smaller circle formats.

Circle Sticker Sheet Installation Guide

Installing individual stickers from circle sticker sheets is simple and requires no special tools. Follow these steps for best adhesion and professional appearance.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly with isopropyl alcohol or mild detergent to remove dirt, oils, and residue. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying any circle stickers. Smooth, non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, and painted surfaces provide the best adhesion.

Removing Circle Stickers from Sheets

Carefully peel each circle sticker from the backing sheet starting at one edge. The kiss-cut process allows clean removal while keeping unused stickers protected on the sheet. Avoid touching the adhesive side to maintain bonding strength.

Applying Circle Stickers

Position each circle sticker carefully on the prepared surface and press down from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. Apply firm, even pressure across the entire circle to ensure complete adhesion, paying special attention to edges.

For best results, avoid applying circle stickers in direct sunlight or extreme temperatures. Room temperature application provides optimal adhesive performance and easier positioning.

Final Installation Tips

After application, allow 24-48 hours for the adhesive to fully cure before exposing circle stickers to moisture or extreme conditions. This curing time ensures maximum durability and adhesive strength for long-term performance.

Circle Sticker Sheet FAQs

What material are circle sticker sheets printed on?

Circle sticker sheets are printed on premium vinyl material that provides durability, weather resistance, and excellent adhesion to most surfaces. The vinyl material maintains color vibrancy and resists fading, cracking, and peeling under normal use conditions.

How many circle stickers can fit on one sheet?

The number of circle stickers per sheet depends on the individual circle size and overall sheet dimensions. Smaller circles allow more stickers per sheet, while larger circles require fewer per sheet. Our configurator tool shows exact quantities based on your size selections.

Can circle sticker sheets be used outdoors?

Yes, circle sticker sheets are suitable for outdoor applications. The vinyl material and adhesive are formulated to withstand weather exposure including rain, UV light, and temperature variations. Outdoor circle stickers typically maintain quality for 3-5 years depending on environmental conditions.

What surfaces work best for circle stickers from sheets?

Circle stickers from sheets adhere best to smooth, clean, non-porous surfaces such as glass, metal, painted walls, plastic, and sealed wood. Textured or porous surfaces may reduce adhesion quality and longevity.

Do you offer design services for circle sticker sheets?

Yes, our design team can create or recreate artwork for circle sticker sheets starting at $15 for simple designs. More complex artwork may require custom quotes. We can vectorize existing logos, create new designs, or adapt existing artwork for optimal circle sticker production.

How should artwork be prepared for circle sticker sheets?

Provide artwork as a single circle design at final size in vector format (EPS or PDF) for best results. Include any necessary bleed if your design extends to the circle edge. Our production team arranges multiple copies on each sheet according to your specifications.

What is the difference between kiss-cut and die-cut circle stickers?

Kiss-cut circle stickers cut through the face material while leaving the backing sheet intact, allowing individual removal while keeping unused stickers on the sheet. Die-cut decals cut completely through all materials, creating individual pieces without backing sheets for permanent applications.
