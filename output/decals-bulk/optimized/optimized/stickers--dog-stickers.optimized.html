Custom Dog Sticker Cut Options

Choose between die-cut and kiss-cut options for your custom dog stickers based on how you plan to use and apply them. Each cutting method offers distinct advantages for different applications and personal preferences.

Die-Cut Dog Stickers

Die-cut dog stickers are cut completely through both the vinyl material and paper backing, creating individually separated stickers with no excess material around the edges. This cutting method works like a cookie cutter, following the exact contour of your design for a clean, professional appearance.

Die-cut stickers work well when you want each sticker to stand alone without visible borders. The back slit option (crack and peel) is available for Standard and Permanent material options, making removal from the backing easier during application.

Kiss-Cut Dog Stickers

Kiss-cut dog stickers are cut only through the vinyl layer while leaving the paper backing intact. This creates a border of backing material around each sticker, making them easier to peel and handle during application.

Kiss-cut dog stickers remain on a single sheet with the backing material providing structure and protection. This option works well for sticker sheets, multiple applications, or when you prefer the extra material for easier handling during installation.

Dog Sticker Artwork Requirements

Proper artwork preparation ensures your custom dog stickers print with sharp details and accurate colors. Follow these guidelines to achieve the best results for your pet-themed designs, whether you're uploading photos, illustrations, or logo artwork.

Preferred File Types

For optimal print quality on dog stickers, submit artwork as Adobe Illustrator EPS (Encapsulated PostScript) or PDF files. These vector formats maintain crisp edges and clean details at any size. The maximum file size we accept is 100 MB. When submitting these files, convert all fonts to outlines or curves to prevent font substitution during printing.

Additional File Formats

We also accept high-resolution raster files including JPG, TIFF, and PNG formats. These work well for photographic dog images or detailed artwork that originated as raster graphics. Ensure these files meet our resolution requirements for clear printing results.

File Setup Tips

Set up your dog sticker artwork at a size proportionate to your final product dimensions. Convert all text elements to outlines to prevent font issues. Allow for bleed only when necessary, and clearly indicate cut lines for all sticker products. Never flatten or merge layers in PSD files, as this limits our ability to make adjustments if needed.

Resolution Guidelines

For raster artwork like dog photos, follow these resolution requirements: 100% scale at 100 DPI minimum, 50% scale at 200 DPI or higher, 25% scale at 300 DPI or higher, and 10% scale at 400 DPI or higher. Higher resolution files provide better print quality and more flexibility for sizing adjustments.

How to Apply Dog Stickers

Proper application ensures your custom dog stickers adhere correctly and maintain their appearance over time. Follow these step-by-step instructions for the best results whether applying to vehicles, windows, laptops, or other smooth surfaces.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly with soap and water, then dry completely. Remove any wax, oils, or residue that could prevent proper adhesion. For vehicle applications, ensure the surface temperature is between 50-90°F for optimal adhesive performance.

Applying Dog Stickers

For die-cut dog stickers, peel the backing paper carefully and position the sticker on your desired surface. Press from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. For kiss-cut stickers, peel each sticker from the backing material before applying.

Use firm, even pressure across the entire sticker surface to ensure complete adhesion. A squeegee or credit card can help smooth larger stickers and remove trapped air.

Final Steps

Allow 24 hours for the adhesive to fully cure before exposing dog stickers to moisture or extreme temperatures. This curing time ensures maximum adhesion strength and longevity for your custom pet-themed designs.

Dog Sticker FAQs

What surfaces work best for dog stickers?

Dog stickers adhere best to smooth, clean, non-porous surfaces including glass, metal, painted surfaces, and high-quality plastics. Popular applications include car windows, laptops, water bottles, refrigerators, and storefront windows. Avoid applying to heavily textured, dirty, or waxed surfaces as these can prevent proper adhesion and reduce sticker longevity.

How long do custom dog stickers last outdoors?

High-quality vinyl dog stickers typically last 3-5 years outdoors when applied to appropriate surfaces and properly maintained. Indoor applications can last significantly longer since they're protected from UV exposure, weather, and temperature fluctuations. The actual lifespan depends on environmental conditions, surface preparation, and material quality.

What file format works best for dog photos?

For dog photos, submit high-resolution JPG files at 300 DPI or higher at your intended print size. Vector formats like EPS work better for illustrated designs, logos, or text-based dog stickers. Ensure dog photos have good lighting, sharp focus, and sufficient resolution to maintain quality when printed on vinyl material.

Can I get dog stickers in custom shapes?

Yes, die-cut dog stickers can be produced in virtually any custom shape following the contour of your design. This includes breed silhouettes, paw prints, bone shapes, or complex multi-element designs. Kiss-cut options provide custom shapes while maintaining the rectangular backing material for easier handling.

What's the difference between die-cut and kiss-cut dog stickers?

Die-cut dog stickers are cut completely through both vinyl and backing, creating individual stickers with no excess material around the design edges. Kiss-cut stickers are only cut through the vinyl layer, leaving backing material around each sticker for easier peeling and handling during application.

Do you offer rush production for dog stickers?

Yes, we offer 1-day priority production for urgent dog sticker orders. Orders approved after 5:00 PM CST begin production the following business day. Emergency same-day rush may be available in special circumstances - contact customer service to discuss your specific timeline requirements and availability.
