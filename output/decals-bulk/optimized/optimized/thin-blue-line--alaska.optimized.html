Installing Your State of Alaska Thin Blue Line Decal

Proper installation ensures your State of Alaska decal creates a strong bond and maintains its appearance over time. Follow these steps for professional installation results on any smooth surface.

Surface Preparation Steps

Clean your chosen surface thoroughly before applying your State of Alaska decal. Use warm water and soap or rubbing alcohol to remove dirt, debris, and residue. Allow the surface to dry completely, as moisture can prevent proper adhesion of the decal material.

Use masking tape to position your State of Alaska decal before removing the backing. This allows you to adjust placement and ensure the decal is centered and straight. For die-cut decals, peel away the backing slowly while applying pressure with a squeegee or credit card from the center outward.

Final Installation Steps

After applying your State of Alaska decal, work out any air bubbles by pressing from the center toward the edges. The matte vinyl material allows for minor repositioning during the first few moments of application, but forms a permanent bond once fully adhered.
