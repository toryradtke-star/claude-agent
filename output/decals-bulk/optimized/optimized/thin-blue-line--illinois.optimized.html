Cut Options for State of Illinois Decals

Die Cut State of Illinois Decals

Transfer Cut State of Illinois Decals

Installing Your State of Illinois Decal

Proper installation ensures your State of Illinois thin blue line decal creates a strong bond and maintains its appearance for years. Follow these steps for professional results on vehicles, windows, walls, and other smooth surfaces.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the installation surface thoroughly with warm water and soap, or use rubbing alcohol for best results. Remove all dirt, debris, and moisture that could weaken adhesion. Wipe away cleaning product residue with a dry cloth before applying your State of Illinois decal.

Positioning the State of Illinois Decal

Before removing the backing, tape your decal in position and measure to ensure proper alignment. For State of Illinois decals, consider the natural orientation of the state outline when positioning on vehicles or other surfaces.

Applying Your Decal

Remove the backing paper and apply the State of Illinois decal starting from one edge. Use a plastic applicator or credit card to smooth the decal, working from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. Apply firm, consistent pressure across the entire Illinois outline.

Removing Transfer Tape

If your State of Illinois decal includes transfer tape, remove it slowly at a 45-degree angle. Press down any loose areas and work out remaining air bubbles. Your decal is now ready to display your support for Illinois law enforcement.
