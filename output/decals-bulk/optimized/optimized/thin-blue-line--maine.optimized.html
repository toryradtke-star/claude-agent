State of Maine Decal Installation Guide

Proper installation ensures your State of Maine decal creates a strong, lasting bond with your chosen surface. Following these installation steps will help you achieve professional-looking results that maintain their appearance over time.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the installation surface thoroughly with rubbing alcohol or warm soapy water to remove dirt, oils, and debris. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying your State of Maine decal. Clean surfaces ensure maximum adhesion strength and longevity.

Positioning Your State of Maine Decal

Before removing any backing material, position your State of Maine decal on the surface and secure the top edge with masking tape to create a hinge. This allows you to flip the decal up while you remove the backing and ensures accurate placement.

Applying the Decal

Remove the white backing paper while keeping the transfer tape in place over the decal face. Lower the decal onto your surface and use a squeegee or credit card to smooth it down, working from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles. Apply firm, consistent pressure across the entire State of Maine design.

Removing Transfer Tape

After the decal is fully applied and smoothed, carefully remove the transfer tape at a 45-degree angle. Pull slowly and steadily to ensure the State of Maine decal remains bonded to your surface rather than lifting with the transfer tape.
