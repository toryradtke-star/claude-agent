Clean the application surface thoroughly before installing your State of Nevada decal. Remove dirt, debris, and moisture using warm soapy water or rubbing alcohol. Allow the surface to dry completely before proceeding with your Nevada decal installation.

Applying the State of Nevada Decal

Position your State of Nevada decal on the intended surface and secure with masking tape to check alignment. Create a hinge with tape across the top of the Nevada decal, then peel away the backing paper while applying the decal from top to bottom.

Once your State of Nevada decal is firmly applied, slowly remove the transfer tape at a 45-degree angle. If any portion of the Nevada decal lifts with the transfer tape, press it back down and continue removal more slowly.
