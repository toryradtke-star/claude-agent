How to Apply New Hampshire Thin Blue Line Decals

Proper installation ensures your New Hampshire thin blue line decals create a strong, lasting bond with excellent appearance. Follow these steps for professional results when applying your New Hampshire law enforcement support decals.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly before applying your New Hampshire decal. Remove all dirt, debris, and moisture using warm soapy water, rubbing alcohol, or household cleaners. Dry completely with a clean cloth, ensuring no cleaning residue remains that could affect adhesion.

Positioning Your New Hampshire Decal

Before removing any backing material, tape your New Hampshire thin blue line decal in position on the intended surface. Use a tape measure to ensure proper placement and alignment. This dry-fit process prevents mistakes during actual application.

Applying the New Hampshire Decal

Create a hinge with tape across the top edge of your New Hampshire decal. Slowly peel away the backing paper while smoothing the decal onto the surface using a plastic applicator or credit card. Work from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete adhesion.

Final Installation Steps

After your New Hampshire thin blue line decal is fully applied, carefully remove the transfer tape if present. Press down any loose edges and smooth out remaining air bubbles. Your New Hampshire law enforcement support decal is now ready to display proudly.
