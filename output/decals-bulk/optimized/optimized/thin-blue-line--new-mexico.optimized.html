Installing State of New Mexico Thin Blue Line Decals

Proper installation of your State of New Mexico thin blue line decal ensures strong adhesion and professional appearance. Follow these step-by-step instructions for successful application on vehicles, laptops, water bottles, and other smooth surfaces.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the intended surface thoroughly using warm water and soap or rubbing alcohol to remove dirt, debris, and residue. Ensure the surface is completely dry before applying your State of New Mexico decal. Any moisture or contamination can weaken the adhesive bond and affect long-term durability.

Positioning and Measuring

Before removing the backing, position your State of New Mexico decal on the surface to determine exact placement. Use masking tape to temporarily hold the decal in position while making alignment adjustments. Measure to ensure the decal is centered and level for professional appearance.

Applying the State of New Mexico Decal

Create a hinge by placing tape across the top edge of the decal. Slowly peel away the transfer film from the white backing, then apply the decal starting from the top and working downward. Use a squeegee or credit card to smooth the decal, working from center outward to eliminate air bubbles.

Removing Transfer Tape

Once the State of New Mexico decal is fully applied, carefully remove the transfer tape at a 45-degree angle. If any portions of the decal lift with the transfer tape, press them back down and continue removal slowly. The completed installation should show only the State of New Mexico decal adhered to the surface.
