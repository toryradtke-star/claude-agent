Die cut processing creates State of Vermont decals that follow the exact outline of the design without additional background material. Kiss Cut State of Vermont Decals

Kiss cut State of Vermont decals include the design cut through the vinyl layer while leaving the backing paper intact. This option provides easier handling and application while maintaining design precision. The backing remains as a border around the Vermont shape for simplified positioning.

Transfer Cut State of Vermont Decals

Transfer cut processing removes excess vinyl material around the State of Vermont design while maintaining transfer tape for precise application. This method works well for detailed State of Vermont designs requiring accurate placement on vehicles or equipment.

State of Vermont Decal Installation Guide

Proper installation ensures your State of Vermont decals adhere correctly and maintain long-lasting appearance. Following recommended installation procedures creates strong bonding and professional results on various surface types.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly using warm water and soap, rubbing alcohol, or appropriate cleaning products. Remove all dirt, debris, oil, and moisture that could interfere with adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying your State of Vermont decal.

Smooth, non-porous surfaces including glass, metal, plastic, and painted surfaces provide optimal adhesion for State of Vermont decals. Textured or porous surfaces may require special consideration or alternative installation methods.

Applying the State of Vermont Decal

Position your State of Vermont decal using masking tape to ensure proper alignment before removing the backing material. For larger decals, create a hinge using tape across the top edge, allowing you to remove backing material gradually while applying pressure to prevent air bubbles.

Use a plastic applicator, squeegee, or credit card to apply even pressure from the center outward. Work systematically to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete contact between the decal and application surface.

Removing Transfer Tape

Remove transfer tape slowly at a 45-degree angle to prevent lifting the State of Vermont decal from the surface. If any portion of the decal lifts with the transfer tape, press it back down and continue removal more slowly.
