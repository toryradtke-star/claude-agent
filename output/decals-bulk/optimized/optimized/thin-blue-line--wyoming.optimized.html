State of Wyoming Decal Installation Guide

Proper installation ensures your State of Wyoming decals create strong adhesion and maintain professional appearance throughout their service life. Follow these step-by-step instructions for optimal results on any smooth, non-porous surface.

Preparing the Surface

Clean the application surface thoroughly using warm water and mild soap or rubbing alcohol. Remove all dirt, debris, wax, or residue that could interfere with adhesion. Allow the surface to dry completely before applying your State of Wyoming decal. Clean surfaces are essential for creating the strong bond that keeps decals secure.

Applying the State of Wyoming Decal

Position your State of Wyoming decal on the intended surface and use masking tape to secure one edge, creating a hinge. Slowly remove the backing paper while pressing the decal onto the surface with a squeegee or credit card. Work from the center outward to eliminate air bubbles and ensure complete contact.

For larger State of Wyoming decals, remove backing paper gradually while applying each section. This technique prevents the decal from sticking to itself and allows better control during installation.

Removing Transfer Tape

After the State of Wyoming decal is firmly applied, carefully remove the transfer tape at a sharp angle. Remove the tape slowly to ensure all decal elements stay properly positioned. Press down any loose edges and work out remaining air bubbles using firm pressure.
