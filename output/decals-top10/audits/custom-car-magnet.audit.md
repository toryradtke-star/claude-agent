<h2>Car Magnet FAQs</h2>

<h3>What materials are car magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Car magnets are made from flexible magnetic vinyl material with a strong magnetic backing and protective laminate coating. This construction provides weather resistance and maintains adhesion to metal vehicle surfaces through various driving conditions.</p>

<h3>What vehicle surfaces do car magnets stick to?</h3>
<p>Car magnets adhere to ferrous metal surfaces including steel car doors, truck panels, and other magnetic-receptive vehicle areas. They will not stick to aluminum, fiberglass, plastic, or non-metal vehicle surfaces.</p>

<h3>Can car magnets go through car washes?</h3>
<p>Remove car magnets before entering automatic car washes to prevent damage from high-pressure water and brushes. Hand washing with car magnets in place is acceptable when using gentle pressure and avoiding direct spray on magnet edges.</p>

<h3>How long do car magnets last on vehicles?</h3>
<p>Quality car magnets maintain adhesion and appearance for 2-3 years with proper care and regular cleaning. UV-resistant inks and laminate protection help prevent fading and weather damage during extended outdoor use.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for custom car magnets?</h3>
<p>Car magnet sizes range from small identification magnets to large door panel coverage, with custom sizing available to match specific promotional requirements. Popular sizes include 12"x18", 12"x24", and 18"x24" for standard vehicle applications.</p>

<h3>Can car magnets damage vehicle paint?</h3>
<p>Properly maintained car magnets will not damage vehicle paint when removed regularly for cleaning. Leaving magnets in one position for extended periods without cleaning may cause temporary impressions that typically disappear with normal vehicle washing.</p>
