<h2>How to Apply Custom Refrigerator Magnets</h2>

<h3>Preparing the Surface</h3>
<p>Clean the metal surface thoroughly before applying refrigerator magnets to ensure maximum magnetic adhesion. Remove grease, dust, and residue using a mild cleaner and allow the surface to dry completely. Smooth, flat metal surfaces provide the strongest magnetic hold for long-lasting placement.</p>

<h3>Applying Refrigerator Magnets</h3>
<p>Position your magnet against the clean metal surface and press firmly across the entire back surface. The magnetic backing will create an immediate bond with ferrous metals including refrigerators, filing cabinets, and steel surfaces. Adjust placement as needed since magnets can be repositioned without losing adhesive strength.</p>

<h3>Removing and Repositioning Magnets</h3>
<p>Lift refrigerator magnets by grasping an edge and peeling away from the metal surface. The magnetic bond releases easily without leaving residue or damaging either the magnet or the surface. <a href="https://www.decals.com/clear-stickers">Clear stickers</a> offer permanent adhesive alternatives when magnetic attachment isn't suitable for your application.</p>

<h2>Custom Refrigerator Magnet FAQs</h2>

<h3>What materials are refrigerator magnets made from?</h3>
<p>Our custom refrigerator magnets are made from durable vinyl material with a strong magnetic backing and protective laminate finish. The vinyl surface accepts high-quality digital printing while the magnetic backing provides reliable adhesion to metal surfaces. The laminate coating protects against moisture, fading, and wear for long-lasting promotional use.</p>

<h3>What metals will refrigerator magnets stick to?</h3>
<p>Refrigerator magnets adhere strongly to ferrous metals including steel refrigerators, filing cabinets, metal doors, and appliances. They will not stick to aluminum, copper, brass, or other non-ferrous metals. Test magnetic adhesion on stainless steel surfaces as some grades may have reduced magnetic attraction.</p>

<h3>How long do custom refrigerator magnets last?</h3>
<p>High-quality refrigerator magnets typically last 3-5 years with normal indoor use and 1-2 years in outdoor conditions. The laminate coating protects against UV fading while the magnetic backing maintains strength over time. Proper surface preparation and clean application extend magnet lifespan significantly.</p>

<h3>Can refrigerator magnets be made waterproof?</h3>
<p>Yes, our refrigerator magnets feature water-resistant construction with sealed edges that prevent moisture damage. The laminate coating provides additional protection against humidity and condensation commonly found around refrigerators and outdoor metal surfaces.</p>

<h3>What sizes are available for custom refrigerator magnets?</h3>
<p>Custom refrigerator magnets can be produced in virtually any size from small business cards to large promotional pieces. Popular sizes include 2"x3.5" business card magnets, 4"x6" postcard magnets, and custom die-cut shapes. Contact us for specific size requirements and dimensional limitations.</p>

<h3>How strong is the magnetic hold on refrigerator magnets?</h3>
<p>Our refrigerator magnets use high-strength magnetic backing that securely holds multiple sheets of paper while remaining easy to remove and reposition. The magnetic strength is calibrated to provide reliable adhesion without being difficult to handle or potentially damaging to sensitive surfaces.</p>
