<h2>Feather Flag Questions for Sizing, Materials & Display Options</h2>

<h3>What are feather flags (razor flags)?</h3>

<p>Feather flags (also called razor flags) are tall, curved promotional flags designed for high visibility in a small footprint. They're popular for storefronts, events, and roadside advertising.</p>

<h3>What sizes do feather flags come in?</h3>

<p>Feather flags are available in <strong>7 ft, 9 ft, 13 ft, and 17 ft</strong> sizes.</p>

<h3>Are feather flags single-sided or double-sided?</h3>

<p>Choose <strong>single-sided (single-reverse)</strong> or <strong>double-sided</strong> printing. Single-reverse shows a mirrored image on the back, while double-sided is readable on both sides and is best for text-heavy designs.</p>

<h3>What materials are feather flags made of (and what are the poles made of)?</h3>

<p>Feather flags are made from <strong>polyester-nylon fabric</strong> printed with <strong>dye sublimation</strong> for vibrant color. Poles are flexible carbon composite for strength and wind performance.</p>

<h3>Do feather flags have a matte or glossy finish?</h3>

<p>Feather flags have a <strong>matte finish</strong> to help reduce glare outdoors.</p>

<h3>Do feather flags come with hardware or bases?</h3>

<p>Feather flags require a pole-and-base setup, and you can choose indoor or outdoor base options at checkout depending on where you'll display the flag.</p>

<h3>What is the production time for feather flags, and when does turnaround start?</h3>

<p>Production options typically range from <strong>2-5 business days</strong> depending on what you select at checkout. Turnaround starts after file upload is complete, or after proof approval if a proof is requested or prepress flags the order.</p>

<h3>How long do feather flags last outdoors?</h3>

<p>With continuous outdoor use, typical lifespan is <strong>3-6 months</strong>, and taking the flag down during severe weather can help extend it.</p>

<h3>Do you offer a warranty on feather flags?</h3>

<p>Yes -- feather flags include a <strong>prorated monthly warranty</strong> with proper installation and use. For example, if a flag fails after <strong>2 months</strong>, we cover <strong>33%</strong> of the replacement cost.</p>

<h3>Can I wash my feather flag, and will the sun fade it?</h3>

<p>Yes -- wash on a gentle cycle with <strong>cold water</strong>, avoid bleach, and <strong>air-dry</strong>. Over time, any flag can fade with prolonged sun exposure, but dye-sublimation printing helps color last longer because the ink is absorbed into the fabric.</p>
