<h2>Domain Services FAQ</h2>

<h3>How quickly can my domain be activated for business use?</h3>
<p>Standard domain registration activates within 1-2 hours, with global DNS propagation completing within 24-48 hours. SSL certificates install within 2-4 hours after domain activation. Rush processing available for urgent business requirements.</p>

<h3>What SSL security options are included with domain registration?</h3>
<p>All domain registrations include standard SSL certificates for secure data transmission. Extended validation (EV) SSL, wildcard SSL for subdomains, and multi-domain SSL certificates available for enhanced business security requirements.</p>

<h3>Can you transfer my existing business website and email accounts?</h3>
<p>Yes, we provide complete website migration services including file transfer, database migration, email account setup, and DNS configuration. Zero-downtime migration strategies available for business continuity during transitions.</p>

<h3>What payment options are available for business accounts?</h3>
<p>We accept credit cards, business checking account transfers, PayPal, corporate purchase orders, and wire transfers. Net 30 payment terms available for qualified business accounts with consolidated billing for multiple domains.</p>

<h3>Do you provide technical support for domain configuration?</h3>
<p>Yes, 24/7 technical support included with all domain services. Our team provides assistance with DNS configuration, SSL installation, email setup, website migration, and ongoing technical maintenance.</p>

<h3>Can I register multiple domains for my business locations?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. We specialize in multi-domain business solutions with consolidated billing, centralized management tools, and bulk registration capabilities for franchise operations and multi-location businesses.</p>

<h3>What happens if my domain registration expires?</h3>
<p>Automatic renewal options prevent domain expiration. If expiration occurs, domains enter a grace period allowing renewal without penalty. We provide advance notifications and account management tools to prevent business disruption.</p>

<h3>Are international domain extensions available?</h3>
<p>Yes, we offer country-specific domain extensions and international business registration services. Our team assists with international compliance requirements and multi-country domain portfolio management.</p>
