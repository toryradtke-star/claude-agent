Finishing Options for Double-Sided Banner Installation

Professional finishing options ensure secure installation and long-term durability for your double-sided banner. We offer reinforced hems with grommets or pole pocket finishing to match your specific mounting requirements.

Reinforced Hems with Grommets: Heavy-duty hem reinforcement with brass grommets spaced every 2 feet provides secure anchor points for rope, bungee cord, or zip-tie mounting. Ideal for fence installations and temporary displays.

Pole Pocket Options: Available in 2", 3", or 4" sizes to accommodate different pole diameters. Pole pockets do not increase finished banner dimensions. Recommended to keep text and graphics at least 3 inches from edges when using pole pocket finishing.

We can finish double-sided banners up to 150 square feet. Larger installations require consultation with our production team. Note that pole pockets cannot be combined with reinforced hems, and custom signage applications may require specific finishing recommendations.

Artwork Requirements for Double-Sided Banner Printing

For optimal print quality, submit artwork as .eps files converted to outlines. We also accept high-resolution .pdf or .jpg files with minimum 150 DPI resolution at actual print size.

Include 0.125" bleed on all edges and keep important text and graphics at least 0.25" from finished edges. For pole pocket finishing, maintain 3" safety margin from pocket edges to prevent text cutoff.

Our professional graphic design team can assist with file preparation, color correction, and artwork optimization. Order proofs are available to verify artwork placement and order specifications before production begins.

Color-critical projects should utilize our Color Match Proof System for precise color reproduction. Contact customer service for color matching requirements and Pantone color specifications for business signage brand consistency.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a double-sided banner and how is it constructed?

A double-sided banner consists of two separate vinyl banners printed individually and professionally bonded back-to-back using heat application and reinforced grommets. This construction prevents graphic bleed-through while ensuring both sides display vivid, clear graphics. Each side maintains independent design flexibility for maximum marketing impact.

What sizes are available for double-sided banner printing?

We print double-sided banners up to 6' by 30' in custom sizes to meet your specific requirements. Standard sizes are popular for storefronts and events, while custom dimensions work perfectly for unique installation spaces. Vinyl material can vary up to 1/2 inch per linear foot due to temperature sensitivity during production and finishing processes.

Which vinyl material works best for outdoor double-sided banners?

For outdoor applications, we recommend 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl for maximum durability and weather resistance. The blockout coating prevents light penetration while maintaining excellent print quality. 13 oz matte vinyl also performs well outdoors with good color retention and readability in various lighting conditions.

Can double-sided banners include different graphics on each side?

Absolutely! Each side of your double-sided banner can feature completely different designs, messages, or branding. This flexibility allows seasonal messaging, bilingual content, or different promotional offers on each side. Simply provide separate artwork files for each side during the ordering process.

What finishing options work best for double-sided banner installation?

Reinforced hems with grommets provide versatile mounting options for rope, bungee cords, or zip ties. Pole pockets offer clean installations on banner stands or permanent poles. Choose finishing based on your installation method - grommets for temporary mounting and pole pockets for professional permanent displays.

How do shipping and packaging work for large double-sided banners?

Double-sided banners ship rolled in protective tubes to prevent creasing during transit. Large formats may require freight shipping depending on dimensions. We coordinate appropriate shipping methods based on your banner size and delivery timeline. Expedited shipping options are available for urgent installations.
