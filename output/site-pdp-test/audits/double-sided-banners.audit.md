## FINISHING OPTIONS TAB

<h2>Finishing Options for Double-Sided Banner Installation</h2>
<p>Professional finishing ensures secure installation and extended lifespan for your double-sided banners. Each finishing method accommodates different mounting requirements and installation environments.</p>

<p><strong>Hems & Grommets:</strong> Reinforced edges with brass grommets every 2 feet provide secure mounting points for rope, bungee cords, or zip ties. Standard option for fence installations and temporary displays.</p>

<p><strong>Pole Pockets:</strong> Available in 2", 3", or 4" sizes to accommodate different pole diameters. Pole pockets don't increase finished banner size but require 3-inch margin from text/graphics to pocket edge.</p>

<p><strong>Hems Only:</strong> Clean finished edges without grommets, ideal for custom mounting solutions or applications requiring smooth perimeter finish.</p>

<p>Maximum finishing capacity covers banners up to 150 square feet. Reinforced corners available on banners over 2 feet in height. <a href="/banner-finishing-options">Learn more about banner finishing</a> to select the optimal installation method.</p>

## FAQ

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What is a double-sided banner and how is it constructed?</h3>
<p>A double-sided banner consists of two separate vinyl banners printed individually and heat-sealed together back-to-back with reinforced hems. This construction prevents graphics from bleeding through while providing clear, vibrant images visible from both sides. The dual-layer design ensures professional appearance and eliminates transparency issues common with single-layer double-sided printing attempts.</p>

<h3>Can double-sided banners be used outdoors in all weather conditions?</h3>
<p>Yes, our double-sided banners are rated for both indoor and outdoor use with UV-resistant inks and weatherproof vinyl construction. All three material options withstand rain, wind, and sun exposure, with the 18 oz heavy-duty vinyl providing maximum durability for harsh weather conditions and long-term installations requiring superior weather resistance.</p>

<h3>What's the maximum size available for double-sided banner printing?</h3>
<p>Double-sided banners can be printed up to 6 feet by 30 feet maximum dimensions. Our finishing capabilities accommodate banners up to 150 square feet total area. Larger projects may require multiple banner segments or alternative solutions. Contact our customer service team to discuss oversized requirements and custom installation recommendations for large-format applications.</p>

<h3>How do pole pockets affect the finished banner size and design layout?</h3>
<p>Pole pockets don't increase your banner's finished dimensions but require keeping text and graphics at least 3 inches from the pocket edge to ensure readability. Available in 2", 3", and 4" sizes to fit different pole diameters. Pole pocket finishing cannot be combined with reinforced hems or corners due to construction requirements.</p>

<h3>Do you offer same-day or emergency production for urgent projects?</h3>
<p>Emergency and same-day production may be available depending on current production schedule and project specifications. Contact customer service at (320) 391-5300 to discuss urgent timeline requirements. Our standard 1-day priority production provides reliable next-business-day completion for most emergency needs while maintaining quality standards.</p>

<h3>What file formats work best for double-sided banner artwork submission?</h3>
<p>EPS files converted to outlines provide optimal print quality, though we also accept high-resolution PDF and JPG files minimum 150 DPI at actual print size. Submit separate files when each side features different graphics, or specify identical printing for both sides. Our prepress team reviews all artwork and contacts customers regarding any print compatibility concerns before production.</p>
