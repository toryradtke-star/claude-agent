## 7) FINISHING OPTIONS TAB

Finishing Options for Outdoor Double-Sided Banner Installation

Professional finishing ensures secure installation and extended banner lifespan in outdoor environments. Standard hem and grommet finishing provides reinforced edges with brass grommets spaced every 2 feet along the perimeter, creating secure anchor points for rope, bungee cords, or zip ties during installation.

Pole pocket finishing offers streamlined mounting for banner display systems. Available in 2-inch, 3-inch, and 4-inch widths to accommodate poles ranging from 3/4-inch to 1.5-inch diameter. Pole pockets maintain your specified banner dimensions without size increases, though we recommend keeping text and important graphics at least 3 inches from edges to ensure visibility.

Double-sided banners up to 150 square feet can accommodate professional finishing options. Reinforced corners add extra durability for banners 2 feet or larger in height. Note that pole pocket finishing cannot be combined with reinforced hems, and wind slits are not recommended for double-sided construction as they compromise structural integrity between the adhered panels.

## 14) FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How are double-sided banners constructed to prevent bleed-through?

Double-sided banners are constructed using two separately printed vinyl panels that are professionally adhered back-to-back using heat and pressure. This construction method completely prevents graphic bleed-through while creating a durable, weather-resistant advertising solution. The panels are aligned precisely and sealed around the edges with reinforced finishing to ensure long-term structural integrity and optimal graphic presentation on both sides.

What's the difference between 13oz matte and 18oz vinyl for double-sided banners?

Our 13oz matte vinyl offers excellent durability with rich color reproduction and low glare, making it ideal for most outdoor applications. The 18oz vinyl provides extra heavy-duty performance with enhanced tear resistance and blockout coating for superior opacity. The heavier material is recommended for harsh weather conditions, high-wind areas, or long-term installations where maximum durability justifies the additional investment in premium material construction.

Can I use pole pockets with double-sided banners, and how do they affect sizing?

Yes, pole pockets are available in 2-inch, 3-inch, and 4-inch widths to accommodate poles from 3/4-inch to 1.5-inch diameter. Pole pockets do not increase your banner's finished dimensions – they're created from the specified banner size. We recommend keeping text and graphics at least 3 inches from edges when using pole pockets to ensure visibility and prevent important elements from being hidden within the pocket area during display.

What's the maximum size available for custom double-sided banners?

Double-sided banners can be printed up to 6 feet by 30 feet, with finishing available up to 150 square feet total area. Larger sizes may require special handling or freight shipping. Our production team can accommodate oversized requirements with appropriate lead times and shipping arrangements. For projects exceeding standard size limits, contact our customer service team to discuss feasibility, production scheduling, and delivery options for your specific application needs.

How long do double-sided banners last in outdoor conditions?

Our double-sided banners are engineered for extended outdoor use with premium vinyl materials that resist fading, cracking, and weather damage. Typical outdoor lifespan ranges from 2-5 years depending on material selection, environmental conditions, and installation methods. The 18oz heavy-duty material provides maximum longevity, while proper installation with appropriate finishing options maximizes banner lifespan and maintains professional appearance throughout the display period.
