Frequently Asked Questions

How are double-sided banners constructed to prevent bleed-through?

Our double-sided banners are constructed using two separate vinyl banners that are heat-sealed together back-to-back. This construction method completely prevents bleed-through while maintaining vibrant colors on both sides. The heat-sealed edges and reinforced finishing ensure durability and professional appearance for both indoor and outdoor applications.

What's the maximum size available for double-sided banner printing?

We can print double-sided banners up to 6' by 30' with finishing options available up to 150 square feet. For larger projects exceeding these dimensions, our customer service team can provide custom solutions and recommendations. All banners are manufactured with precise color matching and professional finishing regardless of size specifications.

Can I use pole pockets for hanging double-sided banners?

Yes, we offer 2", 3", and 4" pole pocket options that accommodate poles from 3/4" to 1 1/2" in diameter. Pole pockets don't increase the finished banner size, but we recommend keeping text and graphics at least 3 inches from edges for optimal visibility. What's the difference between your vinyl material options?

Our 13 oz matte vinyl provides low shine with rich colors, ideal for professional signage. The 13 oz gloss option offers high shine and vibrant colors for premium visual impact. Our heavy-duty 18 oz vinyl features blockout coating for maximum durability and is perfect for permanent outdoor installations requiring long-term weather resistance.

How do production times work for custom double-sided banner orders?
