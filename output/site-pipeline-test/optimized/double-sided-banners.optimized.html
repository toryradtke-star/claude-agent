Finishing Options for Secure Double-Sided Banner Installation

Professional finishing ensures your double-sided banners install securely and maintain their appearance throughout their service life. Our standard hem and grommet finishing includes reinforced edges with brass grommets placed every 2 feet for rope or bungee cord attachment. This versatile option works for most hanging and mounting applications.

Pole pocket finishing creates a sleeve along one edge, allowing banners to slide directly onto poles for clean, professional display. Available in 2", 3", or 4" widths to accommodate different pole diameters, this finishing option eliminates visible hardware while providing secure mounting. Note that pole pockets do not increase your banner's finished dimensions.

Due to the double-sided construction process, we can finish banners up to 150 square feet. Larger installations require special handling - contact our production team to discuss requirements for oversized projects. We intentionally avoid wind slits in double-sided banners as they can compromise the sealed construction that prevents bleed-through between sides.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes double-sided banners different from regular banners?

Double-sided banners consist of two separate vinyl banners heat-sealed back-to-back, eliminating bleed-through while providing full-color graphics on both sides. This construction creates opaque signage visible from any direction, unlike single-layer banners where reverse images may show through. The dual-layer design also provides enhanced durability and weather resistance.

Can I use different designs on each side of my banner?

Yes, each side can feature completely different artwork, colors, and messaging. Many customers use one side for primary branding and the other for specific promotions or directional information. Our production process treats each side independently, ensuring optimal print quality regardless of design complexity or color requirements.

How do pole pockets affect the banner's finished size?

Pole pockets do not increase your banner's overall dimensions - they're created by folding and sealing the banner edge to form a sleeve. Keep text and important graphics at least 3 inches from pole pocket edges to ensure they remain visible after installation. Pole pockets are available in 2", 3", or 4" widths to fit different pole diameters.

What's the maximum size available for double-sided banners?

We can print double-sided banners up to 6' by 30' with finishing capabilities up to 150 square feet. Larger projects may require special handling or alternative construction methods. Contact our production team early in your planning process to discuss requirements for oversized installations and ensure we can meet your specific dimensional needs.

Are these banners suitable for outdoor use year-round?

Yes, all three vinyl materials are rated for outdoor use and withstand normal weather conditions including rain, snow, and UV exposure. The 18 oz material offers maximum durability for permanent installations, while 13 oz options work well for seasonal or temporary displays. Proper installation and finishing selection enhance weather resistance and banner longevity.

How should I prepare artwork for the best print quality?

Vector-based EPS files converted to outlines produce optimal results with crisp text and sharp graphics. High-resolution PDFs (300 DPI minimum) also work well for most designs. For color-critical projects, consider our Color Match Proof System to ensure brand colors reproduce accurately. Our design team can assist with file optimization if needed.
