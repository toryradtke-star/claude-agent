Professional Finishing Solutions for Secure Double-Sided Banner Installation

Our double-sided banners accommodate two primary finishing methods: reinforced hems with grommets for versatile hanging options, or precision pole pockets for clean mounting applications. Reinforced hem construction adds durability along banner edges while strategically placed grommets provide secure attachment points every 24 inches.

Pole pocket finishing creates seamless installation with three diameter options: 2-inch pockets accommodate ¾" poles, 3-inch pockets fit 1¼" poles, and 4-inch pockets secure 1½" diameter mounting hardware. Pole pocket construction maintains original banner dimensions without size increases, ensuring precise fit for existing display systems.

Professional finishing accommodates banners up to 150 square feet, with specialized techniques for larger installations available upon consultation. Our finishing department maintains strict quality standards, ensuring consistent results across production runs while accommodating custom specifications for unique installation requirements.

Note that pole pocket finishing requires 3-inch minimum margins from banner edges to prevent text or graphics interference. This finishing method provides clean, professional presentation ideal for trade show displays and permanent installations requiring frequent banner changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes double-sided banners different from regular banners?

Double-sided banners consist of two separate banner panels heat-welded back-to-back, creating a dual-sided display with no bleed-through between sides. This construction allows different graphics or messages on each side while maintaining structural integrity and weather resistance for extended outdoor use in high-visibility applications.

Can I use different designs on each side of the banner?

Yes, each side of your double-sided banner can feature completely different artwork, colors, and messaging. This flexibility allows businesses to target different audiences, display complementary information, or create directional messaging that adapts to various viewing angles and traffic patterns around the installation location.

What's the maximum size available for double-sided banners?

Double-sided banners can be produced up to 6 feet by 30 feet, with finishing capabilities accommodating banners up to 150 square feet. Larger installations may be possible with special consultation. Size variations up to ½ inch per linear foot are normal due to vinyl material properties and heat application during manufacturing.

How long do double-sided banners last outdoors?

Our premium vinyl materials are engineered for extended outdoor exposure, typically lasting 2-3 years under normal weather conditions. The 18 oz vinyl option with blockout coating provides maximum durability for demanding outdoor applications, while proper installation and maintenance can extend banner lifespan in most climates.

What artwork resolution do you recommend for large double-sided banners?

For optimal print quality, we recommend 150 DPI at final print size for photographic images and vector artwork when possible. Our prepress team reviews all files for print compatibility and can provide recommendations for artwork optimization. Professional design services are available for customers needing artwork creation or enhancement.

Do pole pockets affect the finished banner size?

No, pole pockets are created by folding and welding the banner material, so they don't increase the finished dimensions. However, we recommend keeping text and important graphics at least 3 inches from banner edges when using pole pockets to ensure visibility and prevent interference with the pocket construction.
