## 14) FAQ

**How durable are coroplast A-frame signs for outdoor use?**
Coroplast a frames withstand extreme temperatures and weather conditions, lasting up to 2 years outdoors with proper care. The waterproof 4mm corrugated plastic material resists fading, cracking, and moisture damage while maintaining vibrant colors. Due to lightweight construction, we recommend using sandbags or weights to prevent wind displacement and ensure consistent positioning.

**What sizes are available for custom coroplast A-frame signs?**
We offer four standard sizes: 18" x 12", 24" x 18", 28" x 22", and 36" x 24". These dimensions accommodate various business needs from compact sidewalk displays to larger promotional signage. All sizes feature the same durable 4mm corrugated plastic material and full-color digital printing capabilities for professional results.

**Can I get double-sided printing on coroplast A-frame signs?**
Yes, coroplast a frames can be printed single-sided or double-sided depending on your advertising needs. Double-sided printing maximizes visibility by displaying your message to foot traffic approaching from either direction. Both options feature full-color digital printing with unlimited graphics, text, and colors for comprehensive design flexibility.

**What file formats work best for coroplast A-frame artwork?**
Vector-based files like .eps with fonts converted to outlines produce optimal print quality. High-resolution .pdf and .jpg files also work well when properly formatted. Our professional graphic design team can assist with artwork creation or optimization to ensure your files meet technical specifications for best printing results.

**How quickly can I receive custom coroplast A-frame signs?**
Production times range from 1-5 business days depending on your selected timeline. Priority 1-day service handles urgent needs, while Standard 3-day production offers our most popular balance of speed and value. Production begins after artwork approval and payment processing, with shipping times additional based on your location.

**Do coroplast A-frame signs require special installation or setup?**
Coroplast a frames feature simple A-frame construction that requires no tools or special installation. The signs fold flat for easy storage and transport, then open to create a stable display. For outdoor use, we recommend adding sandbags or weights to prevent wind displacement while maintaining professional positioning and appearance.
