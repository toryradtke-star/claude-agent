## 14) FAQ

<div class="faq-section">
<h2>Photo Magnets Photo FAQ</h2>

**Can I use my own photos and artwork for custom photo magnets?**
Yes, you can upload your own photos, artwork, or designs directly through our online ordering system. We accept various file formats including high-resolution JPG, PDF, and vector-based EPS files. Our graphic design team can also help optimize your images for the best printing results or assist with design modifications if needed.

**What material are photo magnets made from and how strong is the magnetic hold?**
Our photo magnets use premium 30-mil magnetic material that provides strong, reliable adhesion to any ferrous metal surface. The magnetic base is flexible yet durable, allowing easy repositioning without losing holding strength. Each magnet includes protective lamination over your printed image for enhanced durability and moisture resistance.

**What surfaces work best with photo magnets and what should I avoid?**
Photo magnets adhere perfectly to refrigerators, filing cabinets, magnetic whiteboards, steel doors, and vehicle panels made of steel. They will not stick to aluminum, plastic, wood, glass, or non-ferrous metal surfaces. Before ordering, test a small magnet on your intended surface to confirm magnetic compatibility.

**How long do custom photo magnets last and can they be used outdoors?**
With proper care, photo magnets maintain their image quality and magnetic strength for years. The protective lamination resists fading, moisture, and everyday wear. While suitable for outdoor use, prolonged exposure to extreme weather conditions may affect longevity. Indoor applications typically provide the longest lifespan and best appearance retention.

**What production and shipping timeframes are available for photo magnet orders?**
Production times range from 1 to 5 business days depending on your selected option and urgency requirements. Standard UPS Ground shipping provides 1-3 day delivery to most US locations. Expedited shipping options include overnight, two-day, and three-day services for faster delivery when needed for special events or urgent projects.
