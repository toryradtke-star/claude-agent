## 14) FAQ

<div class="faq-section">
<h2>Custom Wallpaper FAQ</h2>

<h3>What surfaces work best for custom self-adhesive wallpaper?</h3>
<p>Our custom wallpaper adheres excellently to painted drywall, smooth plaster, and properly sealed surfaces. The medium-tack adhesive bonds well to most latex and acrylic paints that have fully cured. Avoid applying to textured surfaces, unpainted drywall, or freshly painted walls. Clean, dry surfaces produce the strongest adhesion and easiest installation for long-lasting results.</p>

<h3>How do I ensure my artwork prints correctly on wallpaper?</h3>
<p>Submit vector files (.eps, .ai) with fonts converted to outlines for optimal print quality. High-resolution raster files (300 DPI minimum) also produce excellent results. Our prepress team reviews all artwork and contacts you if files need optimization. Consider ordering a proof for color-critical projects or request our Color Match Proof System for precise color reproduction on vinyl material.</p>

<h3>Can custom wallpaper be removed without damaging walls?</h3>
<p>Yes, our wallpaper is designed for clean removal from most properly painted surfaces. The repositionable adhesive allows for adjustments during installation and eventual removal without leaving residue or damaging paint. Removal ease depends on wall surface, paint quality, and application duration. Test a small area first if you're concerned about your specific wall surface or paint type.</p>

<h3>What are the size limitations for custom wallpaper printing?</h3>
<p>We print custom wallpaper up to 12 feet in both height and width, accommodating most residential and commercial wall applications. Larger installations can be created using multiple panels with careful design planning for seamless alignment. Our customer service team can help determine the best panel configuration for your space and provide guidance on installation sequencing for multi-panel projects.</p>

<h3>How long does custom wallpaper production and shipping take?</h3>
<p>Production ranges from 1-5 business days depending on your selected timeline, with 3-day standard being most popular. Shipping typically adds 1-3 business days via UPS Ground to most US locations. Total project time includes artwork approval, production, and shipping duration. Rush options available for urgent installations, and expedited shipping can further reduce delivery time for time-sensitive projects.</p>
