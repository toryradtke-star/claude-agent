## 14) FAQ

**How long do custom yard signs last outdoors?**
Our corrugated plastic yard signs withstand 6-12 months of continuous outdoor exposure while maintaining vibrant colors and structural integrity. The UV-resistant material and waterproof construction provide excellent durability against rain, wind, and temperature fluctuations. Proper storage between uses extends lifespan significantly for recurring campaign applications.

**What file formats work best for yard signs printing?**
Vector EPS files converted to outlines deliver optimal print quality, while high-resolution PDF files (300 DPI minimum) and JPG images also produce excellent results. Our prepress team reviews all artwork and provides recommendations for quality enhancement. Professional design assistance is available for customers requiring artwork creation or modification services.

**Can I get yard signs printed with different designs in one order?**
Yes, multiple design variations are accommodated within single orders, though each unique design is priced individually. This option works perfectly for real estate agents, political campaigns, or businesses requiring location-specific messaging while maintaining consistent branding elements across all signs.

**Do yard signs come with installation stakes included?**
Installation stakes are available as optional accessories during ordering. Choose from standard H-stakes in various sizes (10x15, 10x24, 10x30) or heavy-duty beefy stakes for challenging ground conditions. Stakes are sold separately to accommodate customers with existing hardware or alternative mounting requirements.

**What shipping options are available for large quantity orders?**
Large orders qualify for freight shipping options that reduce per-unit shipping costs significantly. Standard UPS services handle most orders efficiently, while oversized or high-quantity shipments may utilize LTL freight for cost optimization. Contact customer service for volume shipping consultation and bulk pricing information.

**Can yard signs be reused for multiple campaigns?**
Absolutely. The durable corrugated plastic construction supports multiple installations when handled carefully. Proper storage between uses in dry, flat conditions maintains structural integrity and print quality. Many customers successfully reuse yard signs for seasonal promotions, recurring events, and ongoing advertising campaigns.
