How do I store my vinyl banner when it's not in use?

Store vinyl banners rolled with the printed side facing outward in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight. Avoid folding, which can create permanent creases. Retractable banner stands include protective cases that maintain proper banner position during storage while protecting the printed surface from damage and contamination.
