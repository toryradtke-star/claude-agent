Business Magnets Finishing Options

Standard rounded corner finishing provides optimal aerodynamics for vehicle-mounted business magnets, reducing wind resistance that could cause lifting or detachment during highway speeds. This finishing option extends magnet lifespan by preventing corner damage from airflow turbulence.

Square cut finishing delivers crisp, professional edges ideal for indoor applications or stationary displays where wind resistance isn't a concern. This option maximizes the printable area and creates clean geometric presentations for office environments and trade show displays.

Contour cutting allows business magnets to follow specific shapes including logos, text outlines, or custom designs. This premium finishing option creates unique promotional pieces that stand out from standard rectangular formats while maintaining full magnetic functionality.

All finishing options include edge sealing to prevent lamination separation and moisture infiltration. Our precision cutting equipment ensures consistent results across large quantities, maintaining professional appearance standards whether ordering single magnets or bulk promotional sets for comprehensive marketing campaigns.

Business Magnets FAQ

What material are custom business magnets made from?

Our business magnets use premium 30-mil magnetic sheeting with UV-resistant lamination for durability. This professional-grade material provides strong magnetic adhesion while remaining easily removable without surface damage. The lamination protects against fading, scratching, and weather exposure, ensuring long-lasting promotional effectiveness for indoor and outdoor applications.

What surfaces do business magnets stick to effectively?

Business magnets adhere to ferrous metal surfaces including steel vehicle doors, appliances, filing cabinets, and metal display systems. They will not stick to aluminum, plastic, or non-magnetic materials. Before ordering, test your intended surface with a small magnet to confirm magnetic receptivity and ensure secure attachment for your specific application needs.

Can business magnets be cut to custom shapes?

Yes, we offer contour cutting to follow specific shapes including logos, text outlines, and custom designs. Standard options include rounded corners for vehicle applications and square cuts for clean geometric presentations. Rounded corners provide better aerodynamics for mobile advertising, while contour cutting creates unique promotional pieces that enhance brand recognition.

How do I prepare artwork for business magnet printing?

Vector-based files like .eps with outlined fonts produce optimal results, though high-resolution .pdf and .jpg files also print effectively. Our design team provides artwork assistance, and order proofs allow design verification before production. For color-critical projects, our Color Match Proof System ensures accurate brand color reproduction through professional consultation.

What's the maximum size available for custom business magnets?

Business magnets can be printed up to 24" x 24" maximum dimensions, accommodating various promotional needs from compact contact information to large advertising displays. Custom sizing options provide flexibility for specific marketing requirements, vehicle dimensions, or display space constraints while maintaining strong magnetic adhesion across the entire surface area.

How long do business magnets maintain their effectiveness?

With proper care and appropriate surface application, business magnets maintain strong adhesion and vibrant graphics for several years. UV-resistant lamination prevents fading and weather damage, while the durable magnetic material retains holding strength through repeated repositioning. Regular cleaning and storage on flat surfaces when not in use extends their promotional lifespan significantly.
