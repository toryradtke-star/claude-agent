Poster Paper Material Specifications

Our premium poster paper utilizes 10-point (10 mil) thick paper stock specifically engineered for high-quality digital printing. This professional-grade material provides the perfect balance of affordability and visual impact for your custom poster paper projects.

The high-gloss surface finish enhances color saturation and creates vibrant, eye-catching displays that attract attention. Technical specifications include a basis weight of 235 g/m², ensuring substantial feel without excessive thickness. The paper maintains 104.5% whiteness rating and minimum 92% brightness, providing exceptional color accuracy and contrast.

With 99% opacity rating, your poster paper eliminates show-through concerns when displayed against windows or light backgrounds. Poster Paper Finishing and Lamination Options

Enhance your custom poster paper with professional lamination services that extend durability and improve visual appeal. Choose from front-only lamination for basic protection or full front-and-back lamination for maximum durability and professional presentation.

Front lamination provides scratch resistance and moisture protection, making your poster paper suitable for higher-traffic environments. The clear laminate film enhances color vibrancy while protecting against fingerprints and minor abrasions. This option works perfectly for temporary displays and indoor promotional materials.

Double-sided lamination offers comprehensive protection for poster paper that requires handling or extended display periods. This premium finishing option creates a substantial, professional feel while providing maximum protection against wear, moisture, and UV exposure. The lamination process maintains print clarity while adding structural integrity. Consider laminated poster paper for trade shows, retail environments, or any application where your materials need to maintain pristine appearance throughout extended use periods.
