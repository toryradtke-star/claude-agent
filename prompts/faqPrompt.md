FAQ OPTIMIZATION RULE

The FAQ section must be optimized for:

- Google search visibility
- AI answer extraction
- Transactional product decision support

FAQ questions must reflect real search queries that potential buyers ask before purchasing.

Avoid generic informational questions.

--------------------------------------------------

FAQ QUESTION RULES

Each question must:

- Be written as a natural user search query
- Contain the primary product entity when relevant
- Address purchase concerns or product usage
- Be specific and practical

Examples of good FAQ questions:

- How long do clear stickers last outdoors?
- What surfaces work best for clear stickers?
- What file format should artwork be for clear stickers?
- What is the difference between die-cut and kiss-cut clear stickers?

Avoid questions such as:

- What are stickers?
- What is a sticker made of?
- Tell me about stickers.

--------------------------------------------------

FAQ ANSWER RULES

Answers must follow this structure:

1) First sentence must directly answer the question
2) Follow with supporting explanation
3) Include the product entity naturally
4) Provide practical or technical information when relevant

Answers should be approximately 50-120 words.

Avoid overly short answers.

--------------------------------------------------

AI EXTRACTION RULE

The first sentence of every answer must contain the most important information so that search engines and AI systems can easily extract the answer.

Example:

Q: How long do clear stickers last outdoors?

A: Most clear vinyl stickers last 3-5 years outdoors when applied to clean glass or metal surfaces.

Follow with additional explanation after.

--------------------------------------------------

FAQ CONTENT TYPES

FAQ questions should focus on:

- material durability
- installation surfaces
- artwork requirements
- production timelines
- cutting methods
- adhesive types
- indoor vs outdoor use
- customization options

Avoid repeating information already covered in tab content unless the question format adds clarity.

--------------------------------------------------

FAQ COUNT RULE

Generate between 5 and 8 FAQ questions.

Avoid fewer than 5 questions.

Avoid exceeding 8 questions.

--------------------------------------------------

FAQ STRUCTURE RULE

Each FAQ must be structured as:

<h3>Question</h3>
<p>Answer</p>

Do not use bold Q/A formatting.

This improves schema parsing and AI extraction.
